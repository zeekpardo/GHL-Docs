---
title: Creating an Invoice
description: How to trigger automatic invoice creation by tagging a Planning Center calendar event.
---

Once CalendarPay is [set up](/calendar-pay/setup), creating an invoice is as simple as tagging an event in Planning Center.

## Steps

1. Open a calendar event in Planning Center.
2. Make sure the **"Rental Contact Info (MF-XXXX)"** resource is attached and the billing questions are filled in:
   - **Event / resource fee (USD)** — The amount to charge (required).
   - **Pricing type** — "Per event" for a flat fee, or "Per day" to multiply by the event's duration.
   - **Billing contact email** — Where the invoice will be sent (required).
   - **Billing contact name** — The name on the invoice.
   - **Organization / company name** — The organization being billed.
   - **Invoice due in (days)** — Payment deadline (defaults to 30 days if left blank).
3. Apply the **"Send Invoice (MF-XXXX)"** tag to the event.

## What happens next

Within a few minutes (events are checked every 2 minutes):

1. MinistryFlow detects the tagged event.
2. The billing answers are read from the resource request.
3. A contact is created (or matched) in your MinistryFlow account using the billing email.
4. An invoice is generated with:
   - Your church's business details (name, address, logo) pulled from your MinistryFlow account settings.
   - One line item with the event name and calculated amount.
   - The due date based on today's date plus the "due in days" value.
5. The invoice is emailed to the billing contact.
6. The **Send Invoice** tag is removed and replaced with **Invoice Sent** on the event.

## Skipped events

An event will be skipped (no invoice created) if:

- The **fee** is missing or zero.
- The **billing contact email** is missing or not a valid email address.

If an event is skipped, it will not appear in your CalendarPay logs. Make sure the required fields are filled in before applying the Send Invoice tag.

## Invoice details

The invoice includes:

- **Title:** "INVOICE"
- **Number prefix:** "INV-"
- **Line item:** The event name, with a description showing either the flat fee or the per-day calculation (e.g., "$100/day x 3 days").
- **Business details:** Your church's name, email, phone, address, and logo as configured in your MinistryFlow account.
