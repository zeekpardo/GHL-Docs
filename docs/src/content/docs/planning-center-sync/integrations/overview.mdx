The Integrations page is your central hub for managing how MinistryFlow connects to Planning Center and your MinistryFlow account. You can find it under **Settings > Integrations**.

## Layout

The page has three tabs:

### Planning Center

Shows your Planning Center connection status. When connected, you will see:

- The connected Planning Center organization ID
- A **Disconnect** button
- [Sync configuration](/integrations/sync-settings) options (age filter settings)

If your MinistryFlow account is also connected, additional source filter settings will appear here as well.

### MinistryFlow

Shows your MinistryFlow connection status. When connected, you will see:

- The connected location ID
- A **Disconnect** button
- [Source filter settings](/integrations/sync-settings) for controlling which contacts sync
- **Sync stats** showing the status of your last bulk sync, including counts for synced, created, updated, and skipped contacts
- A **Re-sync** button to trigger a fresh bulk sync

### Calendar

Shows your [CalendarPay](/calendar-pay/overview) configuration, including the setup status and event logs.

## Connection status

Each integration card shows one of these states:

- **Not connected** — Click Connect to begin the OAuth flow.
- **Connected** — The integration is active and working.
- **Needs reconnect** — The authorization has expired. A yellow banner will appear at the top of the page prompting you to reconnect. See [Troubleshooting](/integrations/troubleshooting) for details.
