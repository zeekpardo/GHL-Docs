---
title: Connecting Planning Center
description: How to connect your Planning Center account to MinistryFlow.
---

MinistryFlow uses OAuth to securely connect to your Planning Center account. No passwords are stored — MinistryFlow only receives a token that allows it to read and write data on your behalf.

## Prerequisites

- A Planning Center account with administrator access
- A MinistryFlow account with your church already set up

## Steps

1. Open your MinistryFlow settings and navigate to the **Integrations** page.
2. Under the **Planning Center** tab, click **Connect**.
3. You will be redirected to Planning Center's authorization page. Review the permissions MinistryFlow is requesting, then click **Allow**.
4. Planning Center will redirect you back to MinistryFlow. You should see a green "Connected" status on the Planning Center card.

## What happens after connecting

Once connected, MinistryFlow can:

- Read your people, lists, and workflows from Planning Center
- Create and update people in Planning Center when changes happen in MinistryFlow
- Manage workflow cards on your behalf

No data is synced automatically just by connecting. You control what gets synced by enabling specific lists, mapping workflows, or configuring CalendarPay.

## Disconnecting

To disconnect Planning Center, go to the Integrations page and click **Disconnect** on the Planning Center card. This revokes MinistryFlow's access to your Planning Center data. Any previously synced data in MinistryFlow will remain, but no further syncing will occur.
