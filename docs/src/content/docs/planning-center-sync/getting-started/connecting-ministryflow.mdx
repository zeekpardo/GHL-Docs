---
title: Connecting MinistryFlow
description: How to connect your MinistryFlow account to enable contact and pipeline sync.
---

Connecting your MinistryFlow account allows the system to create and update contacts, apply tags, manage pipeline opportunities, and send invoices on your behalf.

## Prerequisites

- A MinistryFlow account (your church's CRM account)
- Administrator access in MinistryFlow settings

## Steps

1. Open your MinistryFlow settings and navigate to the **Integrations** page.
2. Switch to the **MinistryFlow** tab and click **Connect**.
3. You will be redirected to authorize MinistryFlow's integration. Review the requested permissions and click **Allow**.
4. You will be redirected back to the Integrations page. The MinistryFlow card should now show a "Connected" status along with your location ID.

## What happens after connecting

With your MinistryFlow account connected, the system can:

- Create and update contacts in your MinistryFlow account
- Apply tags to contacts (used by list sync and CalendarPay)
- Create and manage opportunities in your pipelines (used by workflow sync)
- Create and send invoices (used by CalendarPay)

## Both connections required

Most MinistryFlow features require **both** Planning Center and MinistryFlow to be connected:

| Feature | Planning Center | MinistryFlow |
|---------|:-:|:-:|
| Contact sync | Required | Required |
| List sync | Required | Required |
| Workflow sync | Required | Required |
| CalendarPay | Required | Required |

Once both are connected, you can [configure your sync settings](/integrations/sync-settings) and start enabling features.

## Disconnecting

Click **Disconnect** on the MinistryFlow card to revoke access. Previously synced data will remain in both systems, but no new syncing will occur.
