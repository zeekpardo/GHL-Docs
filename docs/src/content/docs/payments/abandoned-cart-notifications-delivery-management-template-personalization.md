Abandoned cart reminders are a powerful tool for e-commerce businesses to recover potentially lost sales. By sending timely reminders to shoppers who leave items in their carts without completing the purchase, businesses can re-engage customers and boost conversion rates. This article explains how to take full advantage of abandoned cart reminders, focusing on delivery control, template customization, and more.

**Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Enable/Disable Toggle**: The toggle functionality allows users to easily turn on or off abandoned cart emails with just a click. This provides users with clear control and ensures that it can be activated or deactivated without hassle.
-   **Email Template Selection & Preview**: Users can choose from pre-existing email templates or customize them directly within the reminder settings. The preview button allows users to see exactly how the reminder email will appear, ensuring the content aligns with the brand's voice and aesthetic.
-   **Subject Line Customization**: Personalize the subject line of your abandoned cart reminder emails. This supports custom variables (tags), allowing for dynamic subject lines that cater to each customer’s unique cart, making the email more relevant and engaging.
-   **Custom Reminder Timing**: Set the exact timing of when the abandoned cart email should be sent. You can specify the delay in minutes, hours, days, or even months. The flexibility in timing ensures that reminders are sent at the most opportune moment, increasing the likelihood of conversion.

**How to Use It**

-   Navigate to the **Payment** section in your account.
-   Go to **Settings** > **Notifications** > **Customer Notification** \> **Abandoned Cart**.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769090079145-unnamed - 2026-01-22T215430.798.png>)

-   Toggle the **Abandoned Cart Emails** option to ON.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769090096748-unnamed - 2026-01-22T215450.888.png>)

-   Choose or customize your **Email Template** and **Subject Line**.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769090113605-unnamed - 2026-01-22T215507.329.png>)

-   Select the **Send After** timing (minutes, hours, days, or months) based on your preference.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769090134204-unnamed - 2026-01-22T215525.262.png>)

-   Click the **Preview** button to see how the reminder email will appear.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769090153192-unnamed - 2026-01-22T215546.966.png>)

-   Click **Save** to apply your changes.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769090168573-unnamed - 2026-01-22T215603.835.png>)

When a customer abandons their cart, a unique checkout link is automatically generated. If the abandoned cart email is enabled under Payments > Settings > Notifications, customers will receive the link via email. They can open the link from any device to complete their purchase, with real-time updates on inventory and pricing.

**Pro Tips**

-   **Test Your Reminders**: Before fully implementing, send a test reminder to yourself to ensure the email looks and functions as intended.
-   **Time It Right**: The timing of the abandoned cart email is crucial. If it's too early, the customer may not be ready to buy; if it's too late, they may have forgotten about their cart. Try different timings to see what works best for your audience.
-   **Personalize Your Emails**: Use customer names and other variables in both the subject line and body of the email to make the reminder more personal and engaging.
-   **Use High-Quality Images**: Including images of the abandoned products can make the reminder more visually appealing and remind customers what they left behind.

**FAQs**




**Question:** How do I turn on or off abandoned cart emails?
**Answer:** You can easily toggle abandoned cart emails on or off in the **Settings** section under **Notifications** > **Abandoned Cart**.




**Question:** Can I customize the subject line of the reminder email?
**Answer:** Yes, you can add a custom subject line directly in the reminder setup. This also supports custom variables to personalize the subject.




**Question:** What is the best time to send abandoned cart emails?
**Answer:** You have full flexibility to choose when to send the email. You can set it to send after minutes, hours, days, or months, depending on when you think your customers will be most likely to complete their purchase.




**Question:** Can I use my existing email templates for the abandoned cart reminder?
**Answer:** Yes, you can select from your existing templates or create a new one. The preview button allows you to check how the email will appear before sending.




**Question:** How do I ensure my abandoned cart emails are effective?
**Answer:** Consider personalizing the email with the customer’s name and abandoned products. Include high-quality images and test different timings to determine the best approach for your audience.




**Question:** Will the abandoned cart email link work on any device?
**Answer:** Yes, customers can click the checkout link from any device, and it will take them directly to their cart to complete the purchase.




**Question:** Is there a way to see what the email looks like before it goes live to customers?

**Answer:** Yes, there is a **Preview button** within the settings that allows you to see exactly how the reminder will appear to a recipient, and a "Pro Tip" suggests sending a test reminder to yourself to check functionality.




**Question:** Can I send more than one reminder for the same cart?

**Answer:** The standard notification setting handles the **first** crucial reminder. To send a series (e.g., 1 hour later, 24 hours later, and 48 hours later), you can use the **Workflow Builder**. Using a workflow allows you to build a more aggressive recovery sequence with different incentives in each email.
