The capability to create and customize payment links for products and services, empowers users to configure pricing, billing options, and branding elements, making it easier to facilitate secure and efficient payments without the need for a traditional e-commerce platform.

### Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

**1\. Access Product Creation Section:** Navigate to the product creation section (top right menu) in the payments tab (left menu) and click on the blue ‘create product’ button to create a new product. You can also edit an existing one.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769117700853-image3.png)

**2\. Set Up Product Details:** Provide essential information about your product or service, including title, description, and product type (e.g., physical goods, digital product, service).  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769117730928-image2.png)

**3\. Configure Pricing Options:** Choose between one-time payments or recurring subscriptions and set the price accordingly. Optionally, include a compare-at price to display discounts.  
![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXert8ulIMeLJjPv8K_tqlmi061IEhTrim-3Z-p5ZjYl_oS8CPfuZbAzgUDAIrbfAFOuXjOvw4n9PVhyb7MfcbMAlASI6ZFoOvOQbTkWiZXe8S3mdkBnPetPKSsgPmx0wq8e0e1M-g?key=LStWhNSJfiKk0z9-x2TNVw)

**4\. Customize Additional Options**: Explore settings such as billing frequency, trial periods, and inventory tracking to align with your business needs.  
![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd8bIFa1YldCB29sPBXaQ2FX9HtYVDASTUgV5PMh51AsE3V62YERvQqOnsba89RsoNL0Xfgr89HwK48NRJOffpJz3AZdNSlAHKyxfw9lBEifO9FWTzhpMd1moFaVqDpljRLGp085A?key=LStWhNSJfiKk0z9-x2TNVw)

**5\. Sync with Payment Platforms:** Ensure seamless integration by syncing product details with supported payment platforms like Stripe or PayPal.  

**6\.Create a New Payment Link:** Go to the Payments Links tab within the Payments tab > create a new payment link. Select the product, and pricing details should automatically populate for a new payment link.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769117801919-image4.jpg)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769117819589-image7.jpg)

**7\. Customize Payment Link Settings:** Enhance user experience and branding by configuring options such as **call-to-action buttons**, **payment modes**, and **branding elements**. 

For more information on Payment Links and their features, please see the article “**Payment Links**” 

**8\. Finalize and Test the Payment Link:** Review and preview the payment link to ensure accuracy and functionality before saving.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfZAVuFhHgz8ng6TjwI973Bguy99CrEeJBFBAxrjfFTSekRqxYJoi4zE2DmNVlOR6QPoKs55z4G-aXCd2SBMYoQgMlK9TujIyIcY6HXmQupO2sglrYS5CAi0aAXTLhwfh29EALJ?key=LStWhNSJfiKk0z9-x2TNVw)

**9\. Share the Payment Link:** Copy the generated link and share it with customers via email, text message, or social media for easy access to the payment page.

### **FAQ**  




**Question:** Can I create payment links for both one-time and recurring payments?
**Answer:** Yes, you can create payment links for both one-time payments and recurring subscriptions. Simply configure the pricing options during the product setup to match your desired payment type.




**Question:** How do I ensure that my payment links are secure?
**Answer:** Payment links created within are integrated with secure payment platforms like Stripe or PayPal, which utilize industry-standard encryption and security measures. Always make sure your payment link is in live mode for real transactions and verify compatibility with your specific checkout requirements.




**Question:** Can customers adjust the quantity of products using the payment link?
**Answer:** Yes, you can allow customers to adjust product quantities directly on the payment links. You can set minimum and maximum limits for quantities to control the order range, ensuring that customers cannot exceed or go below your specified limits.




**Question:** How do I share the payment links with my customers?
**Answer:** After finalizing and testing your payment link, you can share it with customers via various channels, including email, text message, or social media, by copying the generated link from your account.




**Question:** Can customers download digital products immediately after purchasing?
**Answer:** Yes, if the product type is set as a digital product and file delivery is enabled. Customers will receive access after completing the payment.




**Question:** Are payment links mobile-friendly?
**Answer:** Yes. Payment links are optimized for both desktop and mobile devices, allowing customers to complete payments from any device.




**Question:** Can I trigger a Workflow when a Payment Link is used?

**Answer:** **Absolutely.** Use the **"Payment Received"** trigger in the Workflow builder and filter by the specific product attached to your link. This allows you to instantly send a receipt, grant access to a membership course, or notify your sales team the second a payment clears.
