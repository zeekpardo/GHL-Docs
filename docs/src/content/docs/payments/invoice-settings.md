**Invoice Settings**

Invoice settings allow you to customize the default title, terms/conditions, and notifications. This article will overview the settings menu and its options.

You can use templates instead of the default option and use the custom values in the design editor.

To edit your invoice settings, Navigate to **Payments** on the left > From the dropdown of **Invoices & Estimates** > Select **All Invoices** > **Settings**

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763991193381-image.png)

### Business Information

This menu will allow you to customize the Business information such as logo, business name, address, and website, and add custom values

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771337572072-image2.png)

### Email Configurations

You can add the From Name and Email address to this section.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771337669721-image8.png)

### Title and Terms

Here you can set the title and the terms/notes for the estimates and invoices created. You can use various formatting options that allow for line breaks, links, and more

-   Estimate Title & Terms/Notes
-   Invoice Title and Terms/Notes

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771337703462-image1.png)

### Payment Settings

Choose when the invoice is due based on X number of days and toggle the auto partial payment option.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771337745534-image11.png)

**Note:** Autopayment is enabled by default for invoices with payment schedules and for recurring invoices created via the dashboard, eliminating the need to manually enable it each time.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775575936513-image1.png)

### Product Settings

When this setting is enabled, the product description will be automatically imported from the product details to the invoice. This allows you to view and edit the description directly within the invoice for easier customization and clarity.

-   Click on the product setting

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771337774276-image6.png)

### Reminder Settings

Increase your payment conversions on invoices by setting payment reminders for your customers directly from settings. You can set up multiple reminders, delete reminders if needed, and customize the reminder frequency and templates.

By default, three reminders will be pre-set. This ensures timely payment follow-ups without requiring manual configuration. You can disable or customize the default reminders as needed.

-   Click on Reminder Settings.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771337811832-image3.png)

-   Configure the details for each reminder, such as Reminder Name, Reminder Template for Email and SMS, reminder frequency, and maximum reminders.
-   Click on Add New Reminder to add another reminder or delete reminders as needed.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763991490582-image.png)

-   Additionally, you can **send invoice reminders** during your **operational/business hours**, improving convenience for both businesses and customers. You can **configure reminders** to use either your **business’s time zone** or the **customer’s time zone**, ensuring timely and relevant communication.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771337841338-image10.png)

**Note:** These settings apply automatically to all invoices **sent after enabling the feature**.

### FAQs  





**Question:** How do I customize the title and terms for my invoices and estimates?
**Answer:** To customize the title and terms, navigate to Payments > Invoices & Estimates > All Invoices > Settings. You can edit the invoice/estimate title and terms/notes here, and use various formatting options like line breaks and links.  




**Question:** Can I schedule multiple payment reminders for my invoices?
**Answer:** Yes, you can set up multiple reminders by going to the Reminder Settings in the Invoice Settings menu. You can customize the reminder frequency, templates, and the number of reminders to send.  




**Question:** Can I add custom fields to my invoices?
**Answer:** Yes, you can add custom fields in the Invoice Settings menu under the "Custom Fields" section, allowing you to personalize invoices with your preferred information.




**Question:** Can I adjust the reminders for specific invoices?
**Answer:** No, the settings apply universally to all reminders once enabled.




**Question:** Will the Business Hours and Time Zone preference reminder settings apply to previously sent invoices?
**Answer:** No, the settings will only apply to invoices sent after the feature is enabled.




**Question:** Can I auto-populate product descriptions on invoices?

**Answer:** Yes. Enabling **Product Settings** allows product descriptions to be automatically imported from your product catalog into invoices. These descriptions can still be edited directly on the invoice if needed.




**Question:** Can I use my own custom templates for customer notifications?
**Answer:** Yes. Under the "Customer Notifications" section, you can either use the available default options or select your own custom templates. You have complete control over the message and formatting for notifications such as "Payment Schedule Received."




**Question:** How do I change the sender name and email address that appear on my invoices?

**Answer:** You can configure the "From Name" and "From Email" address specifically for your billing communications under the **Email Configurations** section in settings.
