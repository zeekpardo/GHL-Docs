**Update Subscription Payment Method**

Updating payment methods for ongoing subscriptions is now simpler and more efficient. A highly requested feature allows business users to send customers a link to update their payment method for subscriptions. This feature supports multiple payment gateways, enhancing its utility and making it accessible to a wider range of users.

**How It Works**

The option to send the payment update link is available through the quick action menu on the subscription list page and the Actions section on the subscription detail page. Business users can email the link directly to the customer or copy it to send through other communication channels. These one-time-use links are valid for 30 days. Customers simply click the link to view and update their subscription details, and upon updating, the new payment method will be charged starting from the next billing cycle. Business users can generate multiple links and deactivate all outstanding links directly from the subscriptions page for any given subscription.

### How to Use

1- Navigate to Subscriptions:

-   Go to the Payments on the left > Subscriptions at the top.

2- Send the Update Link:

-   From the quick action menu or the Actions section, select the option to send a payment update link.

-   Choose to either email the link directly or copy it to send through another method.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763482775353-3.png)

3- Customer Updates Payment Method:

-   The customer receives the link, clicks on it, and updates their payment method.
-   The new payment method will be used for future subscription charges.

4- Manage Links:

-   Generate additional links as needed.
-   Deactivate any outstanding links from the subscriptions page if necessary.

### FAQs




**Question:** How long is the payment update link valid?
**Answer:** The payment update link is valid for 30 days from the time it is generated. After this period, the link will expire, and a new link will need to be generated.




**Question:** Can I send the payment update link through any communication channel?
**Answer:** Yes, you can either email the link directly to the customer or copy it to send through any other communication channel, such as SMS, chat, or other messaging platforms.




**Question:** What happens if the customer does not update their payment method?
**Answer:** If the customer does not update their payment method within the validity period of the link, they may experience service interruptions until the payment method is updated.  




**Question:** How do I deactivate an outstanding payment update link?
**Answer:** You can deactivate any outstanding payment update links directly from the subscriptions page. This option allows you to manage and ensure that only valid links are active.




**Question:** Can I generate multiple payment update links for the same subscription?

**Answer:** Yes, business users can generate multiple payment update links for the same subscription. This allows flexibility in case the initial link is not used or needs to be resent.




**Question:** When does the new payment method take effect?  
**Answer:** The updated payment method is charged starting from the next billing cycle.




**Question:** Where specifically can I find the option to generate the payment update link?
**Answer:** You can find this option in two places: through the **Quick Action Menu** on the main Subscription list page, or within the **Actions** section on the specific Subscription Detail page.




**Question:** Can I cancel a link if I sent it to the wrong person or it's no longer needed?

**Answer:** Yes. Business users have the authority to **deactivate all outstanding links** directly from the subscriptions page to maintain security and organization.
