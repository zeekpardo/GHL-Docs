Product collections are a powerful feature for online stores, allowing you to organize and display products in a user-friendly way. This guide will walk you through setting up and managing product collections to enhance your customers' shopping experience.

## How-to Steps

### 1\. Access the Collections Feature:

-   Navigate to Payments > Products.
-   Look for the "Collections"

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770755392716-image4.png)

### 2\. Create a New Collection:

-   Click on "Create Collection"
-   Name your collection (e.g., "Shirts," "Shoes," "Accessories").

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770755417686-image3.png)

### 3\. Add Products to a Collection:

-   Select the products you want to include in the collection.
-   Assign them to the newly created collection by checking the appropriate boxes or using a drag-and-drop interface.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770755481580-image2.png)

### 4\. Display Collections on Your Website:

-   Go to your website's navigation settings.
-   Add the collection to your menu bar or navigation links.
-   Ensure that each collection appears as a dropdown or a distinct section in your store.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770755499053-image1.png)

### 5\. Configure Collection Settings:

-   Decide if you want the collection to link to a dedicated page or if products should appear directly on the main page.
-   Adjust the display order of collections if needed.

## Benefits

-   **Improved Navigation:** Helps customers easily find related products, enhancing their shopping experience.
-   **Better Organization:** Keeps your product catalog structured and visually appealing.
-   **Increased Sales:** Organized collections can encourage customers to explore and purchase more items.

## Pro Tips

-   **Use Descriptive Names:** Ensure collection names are clear and descriptive to help customers quickly find what they need.
-   **Regular Updates:** Periodically review and update your collections to reflect new products or seasonal changes.
-   **Highlight Popular Items:** Feature best-selling or new items prominently within their respective collections.

## **FAQ's**




**Question:** How do I edit a collection?

**Answer:** Go to the collections section, select the collection you want to edit, and update its name, products, or settings as needed.




**Question:** Can a product be in multiple collections?

**Answer:** Yes, products can belong to multiple collections, making it easier to cross-sell and promote items.




**Question:** What if I need help finding the collections option in my dashboard?

**Answer:** This could be due to your user permissions or the specific eCommerce platform you're using. Check with your admin or platform support for assistance.




**Question:** What if I don’t see the Collections option in my dashboard?

**Answer:** This could be due to user permissions or the specific eCommerce platform version you are using. Check with your admin or platform support to ensure you have access.




**Question:** Can I control the order in which different collections appear?

**Answer:** Yes. Within the collection settings, you can **adjust the display order** to ensure your most important or seasonal collections are shown first to your visitors.
