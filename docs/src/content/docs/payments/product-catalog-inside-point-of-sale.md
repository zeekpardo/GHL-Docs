**Product Catalog inside Point-of-Sale**

The Point-of-Sale (POS) module on Mobile includes the Product Catalog! This feature streamlines the checkout process by allowing users to select products and services directly from a catalog, eliminating the need for manual calculations. Available on the mobile app, this is designed to enhance your mobile POS experience by providing a powerful and convenient tool for instant sales processing, making it beneficial for all businesses, regardless of niche.

### Enhancements Implemented

-   **Innovative Design**: A beautifully crafted screen with all your products available on a single page.
-   **Variants**: Easily select product variants and combinations.
-   **Dynamic Pricing**: Automatically update the final amount based on selected variants and quantities.
-   **Cohesion with Keypad**: Use the existing keypad screen to add custom items alongside catalog items.

### How It Works

-   **Open the Mobile App**: Ensure the preferred account is selected.
-   **Navigate to POS**: Click the third icon (middle) on the bottom navigation bar.
-   **Switch to Catalog Tab**: View all created products for the account.

**Select a Product**: Choose a product to reveal variants, pricing models, and a quantity counter.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763648807174-1.png)

-   **Add to Checkout**: Select the variant combination, choose the quantity, and click "Add."

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763648905955-2.jpg)

-   **Customize Further**: For different variant combinations, click the "+" button and choose "New Customization."
-   **Checkout**: After adding the products, proceed to the checkout page to accept payment via tap to pay.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763648940774-3.jpg)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763648974574-4.jpg)

**Bonus Tip**: You can manually enter the amount from the keypad screen and select products from the product catalog simultaneously. The amount will update automatically.

### Notes

-   **Add Tax**: Taxes linked to a product are automatically accounted for in the Product Catalog. The final amount includes all relevant taxes.
-   **Attach Notes to Items**: Label products as required when making a sale, especially useful for custom items added from the keypad screen.
-   **Integration with Invoices**: The product catalog will soon integrate with the invoice feature, allowing businesses to build and broadcast invoices seamlessly.

### FAQ’s




**Question:** How do I access the Product Catalog in the POS module?
**Answer:** Open the mobile app, navigate to the POS module by clicking the middle icon on the bottom navigation bar, and switch to the Catalog tab to view all created products.




**Question:** Can I add custom items while using the Product Catalog?
**Answer:** Yes, you can manually enter an amount using the keypad screen while selecting products from the catalog. The total amount will be updated automatically.




**Question:** Does the catalog support product variants and pricing adjustments?
**Answer:** Absolutely. You can select different product variants and quantities, and the pricing will dynamically update based on your selections.




**Question:** How does tax calculation work in the Product Catalog?
**Answer:** Taxes linked to products are automatically included in the final amount, ensuring accurate billing during checkout.




**Question:** Can I remove or edit an item after adding it to checkout?

**Answer:** Yes, items added to the checkout can be adjusted or removed before completing the payment.




**Question:** What happens if I have hundreds of products?
**Answer:** Yes! The innovative design includes a search bar at the top of the Catalog tab, allowing you to quickly type the product name or SKU to find exactly what you need without scrolling.




**Question:** Can I apply a discount to a specific product or the entire cart?

**Answer:** Yes. While the catalog calculates the base price and taxes automatically, you can tap on an item in the checkout summary to apply an individual discount, or apply a global discount to the subtotal before proceeding to the final payment screen.
