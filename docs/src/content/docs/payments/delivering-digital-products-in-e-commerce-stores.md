**Delivering Digital Products in E-commerce Stores**

Delivering digital products is now easier than ever for online store owners. This feature enables seamless digital product distribution directly to customers’ emails through secure download links. From e-books and music files to video tutorials, store owners can now manage and deliver digital files efficiently, ensuring a smooth and secure process for both themselves and their customers.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Instant Access for Customers**: Customers receive immediate access to digital products after purchase, boosting satisfaction and reducing wait times.
-   **Secure Delivery**: Digital files are shared through secure download links accessible via email, protecting customer data.
-   **Streamlined Management for Store Owners**: Easily manage and upload digital files from the product settings page, enabling quick setup.
-   **Versatile Product Options**: Store owners can create product variants as physical, digital, or both, making it easy to sell a range of formats, like e-books and physical copies.
-   **User-Friendly Access Center**: Customers log into the Customer Access Center via OTP to download their digital products, adding an extra layer of security.

### **How to Use**

#### **For Store Owners:**

**Setting Up a Digital Product**:

-   Go to: **Payments > Products** and set up your desired product.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773257096222-image2.png)

-   Now, Navigate to **Payments > Inventory Tab** and select the particular product. Under **Shipping and Delivery**, choose “Digital” as the product type.
-   Use the **Click to Upload** option to add the digital files. You can upload up to **10 files (100 MB max per file)**. For **more files, create a ZIP file under 100 MB.**

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762363710958-image.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773257127553-image6.png)

-   Save after uploading files.

**Digital Products Delivery Options**

**Option 1: Setting Up the delivery of digital products through Email**:

-   Enable the “Order Confirmation Email” toggle in **Payments > Settings > Notifications > Customer Notifications** to ensure digital products are delivered via email.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773257172071-image7.png)

-   After checkout, an **Order Confirmation Email** with an “Access Downloads” button will be sent.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762363782437-image.png)

-   Clicking this button directs the customer to the **Customer Access Center,** where they’ll log in using an OTP sent to their email.

-   Customers can also access their digital downloads directly via: <storeurl>/store/account/orders (e.g., myawesomestore.com/store/account/orders).

### **Important Notes**

-   **Order Requirements**: 

-   Digital product delivery is limited to orders through e-commerce stores.
-   When a physical product is bought with Cash on Delivery, digital upsell products require payment through alternative online methods.

-   **Payment Methods**: Cash on Delivery (COD) is not available for digital products, requiring alternative online payment methods for digital upsell products if paired with physical items.
-   When a physical product is bought with Cash on Delivery, digital upsell products require payment through alternative online methods.
-   **No Shipping Fees**: Digital products incur no shipping fees, although physical items will apply standard rates.
-   **Download Restrictions**: Customers can download up to 10 files, with each file limited to 100 MB.
-   **File Downloads:** All the files will get downloaded at once as separate files and not as a zipped file
-   **Customer Login Required**: Customers must log in to the Customer Access Center to access their downloads.
-   **Automated Fulfilment of Orders:** All digital products in an order will be automatically marked as fulfilled, with the fulfillment details left blank.

### **Use Cases**

1.  **Offering Digital Upsells**: Sellers can provide digital upsells alongside physical products, like an e-book paired with a paperback, to enhance product value.
2.  **Selling Virtual Content**: Ideal for stores selling courses, tutorials, or music, where instant access enhances customer satisfaction.
3.  **Reducing Product Handling**: Stores without physical inventory can sell digital-only products, lowering operational costs.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I deliver both digital and physical products in the same order?
**Answer:** Yes, you can offer digital and physical products in a single order, but note that COD is not available for digital items.




**Question:** How secure is the download link sent to customers?
**Answer:** The download link is sent directly to the customer’s email, and customers must log in via OTP to access their files, ensuring secure access.




**Question:** Are there restrictions on file types for digital products?
**Answer:** Supported file types include images, audio, video, documents, and ZIP files. Each file must be under 100 MB.




**Question:** How do customers access their downloads if they lose the email?
**Answer:** Customers can go to <storeurl>/store/account/orders and log in to access their downloads.




**Question:** How do I enable the Download button for digital products?
**Answer:** Navigate to Stores, select your store, edit the Thank You page, and enable the "Download Digital Products" toggle in the settings.




**Question:** Will the Download button show for all products?
**Answer:** The button will appear for all digital products, redirecting customers to the Customer Access Center.




**Question:** Do I need any special requirements to use the download button feature?
**Answer:** Yes, a connected domain and a default page linking to a store page are required for the button to appear at checkout.




**Question:** Can I offer digital upsells with physical products?
**Answer:** Yes. You can sell digital upsells alongside physical products, such as an e-book paired with a paperback, to enhance product value and improve customer satisfaction.




**Question:** Is Cash on Delivery (COD) an available payment option for digital products?

**Answer:** No, Cash on Delivery (COD) is not available for digital products. Customers must complete their purchase using an online payment method. If a digital product is purchased as an upsell alongside a physical product being paid for via COD, the digital portion will still require an alternative online payment method.
