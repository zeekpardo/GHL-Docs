**How to Create Custom Invoice Notifications**

This guide will walk you through the steps to edit your invoice templates for notifications. These notifications include emails and SMS sent to your customers and team members when a payment has failed or succeeded. Customizing these templates ensures brand consistency and improves customer experience. Follow the steps below to learn how to effectively manage and customize your invoice notification templates.

## **Overview of Features**

Editing invoice templates allows you to:

-   Maintain brand consistency across all notification emails and SMS.
-   Customize templates for various scenarios such as payment successes and failures.
-   Ensure team members are promptly informed about payment statuses.

## **Step-by-Step Guide**

### **Accessing Invoice Settings**

**1\. Navigate to Invoice Settings:**

-   Go to the main menu and click on Payments.
-   Select Invoices & Estimates at the top of the screen.
-   Click on the Settings icon located next to the + New button.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763654204868-image.png)

**2\. Manage Notifications:**

-   In the settings, you will see a section for **Notifications**.
-   Click on the dropdown menu to choose between **Customer Notifications** and **Team Notifications**.

**1\. Create a New Invoice:**

-   Go to the invoice section and click **\+ New** to create a new invoice.
-   Enter the customer’s name and add the items.
-   Save the invoice.

**2\. Send Invoice Notifications:**

-   Select **Email Only** or **BCC** options.
-   Click **Edit** to customize the email template.

### FAQs




**Question:** How can I access the invoice settings to edit notification templates?
**Answer:** To access invoice settings, navigate to the main menu, click on Payments, then select Invoices. Click on the Settings icon at the bottom of the page to access and manage notifications.




**Question:** How do I edit the email and SMS templates for customer notifications?
**Answer:** Select Customer Notifications from the dropdown menu under Notifications. Click on Email Template or SMS Template, then use the preview editor to make changes. Save your customized template as a new version and select it from the dropdown menu.




**Question:** Can I create separate templates for payment success and failure notifications?
**Answer:** Yes, you can create and customize separate templates for different payment scenarios, such as Invoice Payment Successful or Invoice Payment Failed. Customize each template to include relevant information and branding.




**Question:** How do I customize templates for team notifications?
**Answer:** Choose Team Notifications from the dropdown menu under Notifications. Edit the email templates similar to customer notifications, ensuring that the templates provide necessary information about payment statuses and are tailored for team members.




**Question:** What should I include in my templates to ensure they are effective?
**Answer:** Include branding elements such as images, logos, and color schemes to maintain consistency. Ensure templates contain all necessary information, like payment details, invoice totals, and due dates. For failed payments, include reminders for customers to update payment details.


**Question:  Do I need to toggle on notifications for them to send?
**Answer:** Yes.  
Ensure all relevant templates are **toggled on** in the settings so they are active and automatically sent when triggered (e.g., when a payment succeeds or fails).




**Question:** Why should I customize my invoice notification templates?
**Answer:** Customizing templates improves customer experience through branded communications, ensures your team is informed about payment statuses, and maintains consistency across your business operations.




**Question:** What happens if no template is selected for a notification?
**Answer:** If no custom template is selected, the system will use the default notification template, provided it is enabled.




**Question:** Where can I find the Invoice custom values to use in my templates?

**Answer:** You can use Invoice custom values within your Email and SMS templates (created as Snippets in the Marketing tab). These allow you to automatically pull in data such as the customer's name, invoice total, due date, and the specific invoice URL.




**Question:** Can I see how my email notification will look on a mobile phone before I send the invoice?

**Answer:** Yes. When sending an invoice, you can click the "Preview" button to check exactly how the email will appear on different devices, ensuring the layout and branding look correct for both desktop and mobile users.
