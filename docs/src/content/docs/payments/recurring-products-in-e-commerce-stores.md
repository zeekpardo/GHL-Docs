**Recurring products in E-commerce stores**

Selling subscription-based services or recurring products in your E-commerce store is now easier than ever. This feature allows store owners to charge customers on a billing cycle, enhancing monthly sales and boosting recurring revenue. This feature provides a seamless way to integrate recurring products into your online store.

**How to Use**

**Navigate to Your Products:**

-   Go to the Payments section on the left.
-   Select Products at the top.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769526111648-image.png)

**Edit or Add a New Product:**

-   Click to edit an existing product or add a new one.
-   You may also choose to duplicate an existing product. You’ll be redirected to the new product’s edit screen, where you can make any desired changes.

**Save and Publish:**

-   Save and publish all pages of your store.
-   This step is crucial to ensure the styles look consistent on the live site.

**_Note: Always save and publish all store pages, regardless of whether you customize the pill colors. If you skip this step, the live site might look different from the builder._**

**Pro Tips:**

-   **Highlight Subscription Benefits:** Clearly communicate the benefits of subscribing, such as discounts or exclusive content, to encourage customers to choose the recurring option.
-   **Set Clear Billing Cycles:** Ensure your billing cycles are clearly defined (monthly, quarterly, annually) to avoid any confusion for your customers.
-   **Test Before Launch:** Before going live, test the recurring product setup to ensure the billing and user experience are smooth and error-free.

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I set up recurring billing for a product?
**Answer:** To set up recurring billing, navigate to the Payments section, select Products, and either edit an existing product or add a new one. Enable the recurring price option and make sure to activate the "Include in online store" toggle.




**Question:** Can I customize the design of my subscription product page?
**Answer:** Yes, within the Website/Store page, you can customize the pill colors, pill text colors, and other UI elements to match your store’s design. The cart icon and "show more" option for product descriptions can also be adjusted for better presentation.




**Question:** Do I need to publish the store after making changes to subscription products?
**Answer:** Yes, it’s important to save and publish all pages of your store after making any changes to ensure that the updates are reflected on the live site. Skipping this step may result in discrepancies between the builder and live site.




**Question:** Can I offer different billing cycles for the same product?
**Answer:** While allows for recurring billing, each product can have one defined billing cycle. For offering multiple billing options (e.g., monthly, or quarterly), you would need to create separate products with different billing cycles.




**Question:** Can recurring products be integrated into funnels or other pages?
**Answer:** Yes. Recurring products can be added to funnels or one-step order forms, allowing for flexible use across your e-commerce ecosystem.




**Question:** Where does the "Continue Shopping" button lead when a customer adds a subscription product to their cart?

**Answer:** The "Continue Shopping" button on the add-to-cart drawer is designed to direct users to the product list page rather than simply closing the drawer. This helps streamline the shopping experience by allowing customers to easily browse more items.
