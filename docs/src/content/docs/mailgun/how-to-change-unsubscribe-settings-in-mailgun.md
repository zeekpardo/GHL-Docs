You may want to update the unsubscribe settings in your Mailgun account, so follow these easy steps to turn the Unsubscribe feature on or off.

### **Step 1:** Log in to your Mailgun account.

### **Step 2:** Navigate to the dashboard on the lefthand side. Click Sending.

### **Step 3:** Within the sub-menu, select Domain Settings. Here you can select from a drop-down list of your domain(s).

### **Step 4:** Scroll down and next to "Unsubscribe" click Edit. You can then toggle on or off the Unsubscribe feature.

### **Step 5:** Click Save to confirm your changes.
