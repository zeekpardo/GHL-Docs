**Practical Use Case:**  

Imagine you need to share a list of your current corporate partners with your accounting team for year-end reporting. Instead of copying details one by one, you can select the specific companies and download a complete spreadsheet in seconds.

**Instructions (Step-by-Step):**

**To export selected companies:**

1.  To view your company list, navigate to **Contacts > Companies**.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772545176648-image1.png)

2\. To choose specific records, click the **Checkboxes** next to the names of the companies you want to export.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772545216628-image3.png)

3\. To begin the download, click **Export** in the action bar that appears at the top of the list.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772545245992-image2.png)

4\. To track the process, watch the progress modal for the record count and percentage completion.

5\. To save the file to your computer, click the link to download the generated **CSV file** once the process finishes.

**To export all companies:**

1.  To view your complete list, navigate to **Contacts > Companies**.
2.  To see more options, click the **3-dot menu** located in the top-right corner of the screen. 
3.  To start a full database download, select **Export** from the dropdown menu.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772545289628-image4.png)

4\. To monitor the status, wait for the export progress modal to reach 100%.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772545318464-image5.png)

5\. To finalize the process, open the **CSV file** that is automatically downloaded to your system.

### Features and Benefits

This provides several improvements to your workflow:

-   **Real-time progress:** You can see exactly how many records are exporting and the percentage of completion.
-   **Flexible selection:** You can choose to export everything or just a hand-picked list.
-   **Universal format:** The system generates a CSV file containing all selected company fields, making it compatible with Excel or Google Sheets.
-   **Efficiency:** This tool eliminates the need for manual data entry and simplifies offline analysis or data sharing with stakeholders.

### FAQs




**Question:** What file format is used for the export?

**Answer:** All company data is exported as a CSV (Comma Separated Values) file, which can be opened by any spreadsheet software.




**Question:** Can I export specific fields or columns?

**Answer:** The bulk export currently includes all available fields for the company records you select.




**Question:** Is there a limit to how many companies I can export at once?

**Answer:** No. You can use the "Export All" feature to download your entire company database regardless of size.




**Question:** Where can I find the file after the export finishes?

**Answer:** Once the progress bar reaches 100%, the file will typically save to your computer's "Downloads" folder.




**Question:** Do I need to stay on the page while the export processes?

**Answer:** For large datasets, it is best to keep the window open until the progress modal confirms the export is complete to ensure your download starts.




**Question:** Will exporting companies delete them from the system?

**Answer:** No. Exporting simply creates a copy of your data in a spreadsheet; all records remain safe within .




**Question:** Do I have to manually click a link to get my file once "Export All" finishes?

**Answer:** When performing a full export via the 3-dot menu, the CSV file is **automatically downloaded** to your system once the progress modal reaches 100%.
