Using the Bulk Actions features in your Contacts/Smart Lists section, you can perform multiple one-off actions to selected or all contacts. Here’s how to do a pipeline change to add or update opportunity statuses, using the bulk action feature:

### Step 1: Choose Your Contacts 

-   Navigate to your contacts.
-   Check the box to select contact records.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768475731904-unnamed \(92\).jpg>)

-   You can also check the box at the top to select all on that page, and you can click the hyperlink to select all contacts in .

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768475763763-unnamed \(93\).jpg>)

### Step 2: Choose Your Action

-   Choose the icon for “Pipeline Change”. Click “More” and then click ”Manage Opportunities.” In the “Add/Update Opportunity” window that pops up, choose a pipeline, stage, opportunity, lead value, opportunity status, and more.
-   Click Add/Update Opportunity to confirm your changes.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768475823803-unnamed \(94\).jpg>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768475837063-unnamed \(95\).jpg>)

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I select contacts for a bulk action?
**Answer:

-   Navigate to your contacts.
-   Check the box next to each contact record you want to select.
-   To select all contacts on the current page, check the box at the top of the page.
-   To select all contacts in , click the hyperlink to select all.




**Question:** How do I perform a pipeline change using bulk actions?
**Answer:

-   Choose the icon for **“Pipeline Change”**. Click **“More”** > **“Manage Opportunities’**’
-   In the **“Add/Update Opportunity”** window, select the desired pipeline, stage, opportunity, lead value, opportunity status, and any other necessary details.
-   Click **Add/Update Opportunity** to apply the changes.




**Question:** Can I cancel a bulk action once it has started?
**Answer:** Depending on your system’s capabilities, you may or may not be able to cancel a bulk action once it has started. Check with your system administrator or consult the help documentation for specifics on your platform.




**Question:** Can I update opportunity statuses for multiple contacts at once?
**Answer:** Yes, using the **Manage Opportunities** bulk action, you can add or update pipeline stages, opportunity statuses, lead values, and more for selected or all contacts.




**Question:** What specific details can I customize when performing an Add/Update Opportunity bulk action?
**Answer:** When the "Add/Update Opportunity" window appears, you can customize several fields including the Pipeline, Stage, Opportunity Name, Lead Value, and Opportunity Status.  




**Question:** How does a bulk pipeline change affect my "Attribution" data?

**Answer:** The system will log the bulk action in the **Audit Logs**. In your Reporting dashboard, the conversion or stage change will be attributed to the user who initiated the bulk action. If the lead was originally from a specific ad campaign, that original attribution is usually preserved on the contact record.




**Question:** Can I use bulk actions to change the financial value associated with my leads?

**Answer:** Yes. In the "Add/Update Opportunity" window, there is a field for "Lead Value" that allows you to update the potential revenue for all selected contacts simultaneously.
