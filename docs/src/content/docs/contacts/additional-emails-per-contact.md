The "Multiple Emails for a Contact"  allows you to add more than one email address to a contact's details. Users can add, manage, and edit multiple email addresses for a contact and choose a primary email that will be used for all actions/interactions with the contact. When sending an email, users can choose from the list of email addresses added for a lead, and the primary email address will be automatically selected. In this article, we will go over how this works.

**Please note:** One primary Email Address and Ten Additional Email addresses are possible at this time, making it a total of eleven email addresses.

This feature can be used while creating a new contact or editing an existing one. Users can specify which email address is the primary one that will be used for all communications and interactions with that contact. This can be helpful in cases where a contact has multiple email addresses for different purposes, such as work and personal email addresses.

### How do you add additional Email Addresses to a contact?

You can add additional email address fields when creating or editing a contact. 

To create a contact, it can be done as follows: 

-   Navigate to the contacts page to edit or create a new contact.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763640670934-unnamed - 2025-11-20T201100.634.jpg>)

-   To edit a contact, click on the contact and ‘+ Add’ > Email.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763640689613-unnamed - 2025-11-20T201119.663.jpg>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763640711053-unnamed - 2025-11-20T201139.834.jpg>)

Once you have a total of 10 additional email addresses for a contact, you won’t be able to add an additional email. 10 additional email addresses are the limit for one contact.  

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763640731774-unnamed - 2025-11-20T201202.702.jpg>)

### How to choose the primary email address?

To make the change, click on the circle next to the email you want to make primary. Once you save, the changes will be applied.

### FAQs




**Question:** How many email addresses can I add for a single contact?
**Answer:** You can add up to one primary email address and ten additional email addresses, totaling eleven email addresses per contact.




**Question:** How do I select a primary email address for a contact?
**Answer:** To set a primary email address, click on the circle next to the email address you want to designate as primary. Save the changes, and the selected email address will be set as the primary one for all communications with that contact.




**Question:** Can I send an email to a contact using any of their multiple email addresses?
**Answer:** Yes, when sending an email to a contact with multiple email addresses, you can select from the list of available email addresses using a drop-down menu. The primary email address will be automatically selected, but you can choose any other email address if needed.




**Question:** How can I add additional email addresses when creating or editing a contact?
**Answer:** When creating a new contact or editing an existing one, navigate to the contact’s details page. You will see an "Add Email" button to add more email addresses. Note that the option to add more email fields will disappear once you have reached the maximum of eleven email addresses.




**Question:** What happens if I try to add more than the allowed number of email addresses for a contact?  
**Answer:** Once you have added ten additional email addresses, you will not be able to add any more. The limit for a single contact is eleven email addresses (one primary and ten additional).




**Question:** Does a contact get "Unsubscribed" from all 11 emails if they click the unsubscribe link?
**Answer:** Yes. For compliance reasons, the **DND (Do Not Disturb)** status is applied to the _Contact Record_ as a whole, not just a specific email address. If a contact opts out, the system will stop sending to any and all email addresses associated with that profile.




**Question:** Can I use different "Email Signatures" for different email addresses on one contact?

**Answer:** No. The email signature is tied to the **User** (the staff member) sending the email, not the recipient's email address. Regardless of which of the contact's emails you choose to send to, your standard user signature will be applied.




**Question:** Can I trigger a workflow if_any_of the 11 emails receives an inbound message?
**Answer:** Yes. If a contact replies to you from any of their 11 saved addresses, the system will recognize the sender and attribute the message to that contact record. This will successfully trigger any "Customer Replied" workflows you have active.
