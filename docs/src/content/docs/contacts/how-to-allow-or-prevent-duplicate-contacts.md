This tutorial will show you how to allow or prevent duplicate contacts within . To learn more about importing contacts, visit the article “**How to import/Export Contacts using a single CSV file**" for step-by-step guidance.

### **Step-by-Step Guide:**

Navigate to Settings > Business Profile> Contact Deduplication Preferences.

This setting allows you to control how your account treats duplicate contacts. 

**_NOTE:_** An error will occur if "Allow Duplicate Contact" is on and you try to import a bulk upload.

If you choose to toggle this setting OFF, you can adjust exactly how your sub-account looks for duplicate contacts. You can look for email or phone numbers first when determining if a contact is a duplicate, and if you want another layer of confirmation, you can also select a secondary determining factor.

**FAQ's**




**Question:** What is the "Allow Duplicate Contact" setting?

**Answer:  This setting controls whether duplicate contacts are allowed in your . When enabled, duplicates can be created; when disabled, duplicate detection is enforced.




**Question:** What happens if I try to import contacts with "Allow Duplicate Contact" turned on?

**Answer:** If "Allow Duplicate Contact" is on, you may encounter errors during bulk import due to the system allowing duplicate entries.




**Question:** How can I configure duplicate contact detection if I turn off the "Allow Duplicate Contact" setting?

**Answer:** You can set your sub-account to detect duplicates based on email addresses, phone numbers, or both. You can choose which factor to prioritize and add an additional layer of confirmation if needed.




**Question:** Where can I find more information about importing contacts?

**Answer:** For detailed instructions on importing contacts, refer to the article "**How to import/Export Contacts using a single CSV file**"  




**Question:** Can I filter down duplicate contacts in my contact list?

**Answer:** At the moment, this is not possible, a workaround for this is to export the list, clean up the list, and import again.




**Question:** What happens if I try to import contacts with "Allow Duplicate Contact" turned on?

**Answer:** Discussion Location:** "An error will occur if 'Allow Duplicate Contact' is on and you try to import a bulk upload."




**Question:** What happens if two contacts have the same name but different emails?
**Answer:** The system will treat them as two completely separate individuals. Because their unique identifiers (emails) are different, they will not be flagged as duplicates, allowing you to manage them independently.




**Question:** If I turn off "Allow Duplicate Contact," what happens to my current duplicates?

**Answer:** Disabling this setting prevents **new** duplicates from being created. It does not automatically merge or delete existing duplicates already in your system. To clean your current list, we recommend exporting your contacts, removing duplicates in a spreadsheet, and re-importing with deduplication active.




**Question:** Why is my bulk import still showing "Duplicates Found" even when the setting is OFF?

**Answer:** This is a safety feature of the Import Wizard. Even with global deduplication off, the importer will ask you how to handle rows in your CSV that share an email or phone with contacts already in the system. You can choose to "Update existing records," "Ignore duplicates," or "Create new" during the mapping process.
