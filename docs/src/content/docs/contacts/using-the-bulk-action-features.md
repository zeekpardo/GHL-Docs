Using the Bulk Actions features in your Contacts/Smart Lists section, you can perform multiple one-off actions to selected or all contacts. From sending an email or SMS to updating the pipeline stage and more, follow the steps below to learn how to use this feature.  

**_NOTE_:** Deleting Contacts / Importing / Exporting / Merging Contacts are only available for Account admins. Account users cannot delete, Import, Export, or merge contacts.

## FAQ’s




**Question:** What happens when I delete a contact using Bulk Actions?


**Answer:** Deleting a contact will remove them from all areas in , including Conversations, Notes, Opportunities, Tasks, Appointments, Manual Actions, and Campaigns. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.




**Question:** Can I schedule a Bulk Action in drip mode?


**Answer:** Yes, you can schedule a Bulk Action in drip mode. When doing so, the “repeat after” option functions as “run every.” For example, if you set it to process 100 contacts every 5 hours, it will continue in batches until all selected contacts have been processed.




**Question:** Can I cancel a Bulk Action once it has started?

**Answer:** Yes. Navigate to **Contacts > Bulk Actions**. Find your active job in the list, click the three-dot menu (⋮) on the right, and select **Pause** or **Cancel**. If you select Cancel, the system will stop processing any remaining contacts in the queue immediately.




**Question:** How can I bulk manage contacts for companies?


**Answer:** In the Contacts/Smart Lists section, you can bulk add, remove, or edit contacts associated with a company. Simply select the contacts, choose the “Add/Edit to Company” option, select the action you want to perform, and apply it to the chosen company.




**Question:** Can I merge multiple duplicate contacts using Bulk Actions?


**Answer:** Yes, you can merge up to 10 duplicate contacts using the Bulk Actions feature. The system will guide you through selecting and merging contacts, ensuring your contact list remains clean and organized.




**Question:** How does the Bulk Export feature work?


**Answer:** The Bulk Export feature allows you to export selected contact records as a .csv file, which will save to your computer. This is useful for backing up data or migrating contact lists. Select the data under Columns for that data to be exported.




**Question:** What are the benefits of using Bulk Actions for managing company contacts?


**Answer:** Bulk Actions for company contacts streamline the management process, allowing you to quickly update large groups of contacts. This saves time, improves efficiency, and ensures your contact lists are always up to date, leading to better customer engagement.


**Question:How can I avoid exceeding the Meta Messaging Limit in Bulk WhatsApp campaigns?


**Answer:** Ensure you use Meta-approved templates and stay within the 24-hour messaging limit. Exceeding the Meta Messaging Limit will prevent remaining messages from being delivered.


**Question:What happens if I don’t filter the workflow/campaign trigger?


**Answer:The bulk automation may run for more contacts than intended. Adding filters ensures better targeting and attribution.




**Question:** What is the difference between "All at Once" and "Drip Mode"?


**Answer:** All at Once** sends your message to every selected contact at the exact same second. **Drip Mode** breaks the list into small batches (e.g., 20 people every 10 minutes). Drip Mode is highly recommended for large lists to prevent your email or phone provider from flagging your account for "spammy" behavior.
