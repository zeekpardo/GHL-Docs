**Reputation Management: Settings**

The review widget is a powerful tool for collecting and showcasing customer feedback, helping businesses improve their online reputation. With an intuitive interface in the Reputation Management section, users can easily manage review settings, requests, and QR codes. The addition of Smart Spam Detection further enhances control by automatically identifying and filtering spam reviews, ensuring only genuine feedback is highlighted.

**Important note:** We have enhanced the privacy of review widgets for **HIPAA-compliant locations** by **displaying reviewer initials instead of full names**, ensuring sensitive information remains confidential. Please ensure your location is HIPAA-compliant.

### FAQs




**Question:** How does the Smart Spam Detection feature work?
**Answer:** Smart Spam Detection automatically flags potential spam reviews to maintain a clean and authentic online presence. You can review flagged content and mark it as "Not Spam" if necessary.




**Question:** Can I customize the review widget for my business?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize your review widget by navigating to the Reputation tab under Widgets. You can adjust its appearance and functionality to fit your brand.




**Question:** How can I use QR codes to collect reviews?
**Answer:** You can create customizable QR codes under the Reviews QR section, place them on business cards or posters, and track scan metrics to optimize your strategy.




**Question:** What is review balancing, and how do I enable it?
**Answer:** Review balancing distributes your review requests across multiple platforms to build a well-rounded online reputation. Enable this feature in the Review Link section.




**Question:** How can I send review requests through WhatsApp?
**Answer:** First, authenticate your WhatsApp Business number under **Settings → WhatsApp**. Then, create a custom template, submit it for Meta approval, and send review requests individually, in bulk, or through workflows.




**Question:** Can I filter which reviews are displayed in the widget?
**Answer:** Yes, you can filter reviews by specific pages and set a minimum rating for displayed reviews to highlight higher-rated feedback and control the content shown.




**Question:** What privacy features are available for HIPAA-compliant locations?
**Answer:** For HIPAA-compliant locations, reviewer initials are displayed instead of full names in the review widget to protect sensitive information. Ensure your location meets HIPAA compliance requirements.




**Question:** How do I select the action for review requests?
**Answer:** In the Review Request section, you can choose to send review requests immediately or follow a custom schedule for sending requests.




**Question:** What is "Reviews AI" and how does it help me?  
**Answer:** Responding to reviews is critical for SEO and customer loyalty, but it's time-consuming. Reviews AI generates response suggestions based on the specific "personality" and "tone" you define. You can review, edit, and post these AI-generated replies with a single click.
