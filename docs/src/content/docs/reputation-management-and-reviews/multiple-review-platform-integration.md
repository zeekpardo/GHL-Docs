This feature allows businesses to **connect and manage reviews from over 40+ platforms** in one place, improving efficiency and customer engagement. With real-time updates and a unified dashboard, businesses can stay on top of their online reputation without switching between multiple review sites.

## **How to Access and Use This Feature**

### **Step 1: Navigate to Review Integrations**

-   Go to **Reviews > Settings > Integrations** in your account.

-   Enter the **URL of your business’s review page** for each platform you want to integrate.

-   **Encourage reviews on multiple platforms** to maintain a well-balanced reputation.

-   **Respond to reviews promptly** to build trust and credibility with potential customers.
-   **Leverage the Review Widget** to display positive reviews on your website.

## FAQ's  




**Question:** Can I integrate reviews from multiple platforms at once?
**Answer:** Yes, you can connect multiple platforms simultaneously by adding the URLs of your business’s review pages.




**Question:** How often are reviews updated?
**Answer:** Reviews sync automatically, ensuring your dashboard stays up-to-date with the latest feedback.




**Question:** Will I be notified of new reviews?
**Answer:** Yes, you can enable notifications to receive alerts when new reviews are posted.




**Question:** Can I delete or edit reviews from my dashboard?
**Answer:** No, reviews are pulled directly from the platforms and cannot be altered. You can, however, respond to reviews within the dashboard.




**Question:** How can I display positive reviews on my website?
**Answer:** You can use the built-in **Review Widget** to showcase selected positive reviews on your website, helping boost credibility and attract new customers.




**Question:** Are responses to reviews tracked in the dashboard?
**Answer:** Yes. You can respond to reviews directly from the dashboard, and your responses will be visible on the original review platform.




**Question:** Can I filter reviews so I only see 1-star or 2-star ratings?

**Answer:** Absolutely. The dashboard includes filtering options that allow you to sort by rating, date, or platform. This helps you quickly identify and address negative feedback that requires immediate attention.




**Question:** Is there a way to see high-level trends in my customer feedback?

**Answer:** Yes. You can use the **Review Analytics** feature to track trends over time and identify specific areas of your business that may need improvement based on recurring feedback.




**Question:** What should I do if a specific review site I use isn’t included in the 40+ supported platforms?

**Answer:** If your preferred platform is missing, you can **submit custom review platform links** directly from your dashboard. This allows you to suggest new integrations, helping the development team prioritize the platforms that matter most to your business.
