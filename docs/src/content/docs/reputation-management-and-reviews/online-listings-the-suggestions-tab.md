In today’s digital world, managing your business’s online presence is crucial for maintaining accuracy, consistency, and credibility. One of the most important aspects of this is ensuring that your business information across multiple online directories, such as Google Business Profile, Facebook, and Yelp, is up-to-date and accurate. That’s where the **Suggestions Feature** inside the Listings Reselling Product comes into play, providing an easy and efficient way to handle updates suggested by publishers.

This feature enhances your control over your business listings, allowing you to review, accept, or reject publisher-submitted updates all from one place. By streamlining this process, businesses can ensure that their online presence reflects the most current and accurate information, helping maintain brand integrity and boosting customer trust.

### **How to Use the Suggestions Feature:**

**1\. Access the Suggestions Hub**:

-   Navigate to the **Reputations** tab on the left > **Listings** at the top.
-   Navigate to the **Suggestions** tab, where you’ll find all suggested updates from publishers like Google, Facebook, and Yelp.

**2\. Review Updates**:

-   In the Suggestions section, you’ll see a list of suggested updates. These could include changes to your business hours, contact information, or business description.
-   Click on each suggestion to review the details.

**3\. Accept or Reject Updates**:

-   After reviewing, you can either **accept** the suggestion to implement it, or **reject** it if it doesn’t align with your business needs.
-   Accepted updates will be synced across all connected platforms automatically.

**4\. Monitor Changes**:

-   Once updates are accepted, check the status of your listings on various platforms to ensure that the changes have been reflected and that your business information is consistent across all directories.

### **Pro Tips for Using the Suggestions Feature:**

-   **Regularly Review Suggestions**: Even though updates are synced automatically once accepted, regularly check the suggestions tab to ensure your business information is always accurate.
-   **Prioritize High-Impact Updates**: Focus on reviewing updates that affect key customer touchpoints, such as your contact details, business hours, and location, to maintain a seamless customer experience.
-   **Use Syncing to Your Advantage**: Leverage the automated syncing feature to save time on manual updates. Once you accept a suggestion, you don’t have to manually update each platform.

### **FAQ**




**Question:** How do I access the Suggestions feature?
**Answer:** The Suggestions feature is available in the Listings management section within the Repuations tab. Simply navigate to the "Suggestions" tab to see all updates from publishers.




**Question:** Can I reject a suggested update?
**Answer:** Yes, you can reject any suggested update. If it doesn’t align with your business, simply choose the "X" option to reject that suggestion.




**Question:** How are updates synced across platforms?
**Answer:** Once you accept a suggestion, it is automatically synced to all connected publisher directories, ensuring consistency across your online listings.




**Question:** Does this feature save time?
**Answer:** Absolutely! By centralizing all updates from different platforms in one place, you save time on managing multiple publisher accounts and can update your listings in just a few clicks.




**Question:** Will I always be notified about new suggestions?
**Answer:** You will be able to see new suggestions directly in the Suggestions section, ensuring that you’re always up-to-date with the latest proposed changes to your listings.




**Question:** Can I prioritize which updates to implement first?
**Answer:** Yes, focus on high-impact updates such as contact details, business hours, and location information to maintain a seamless customer experience.




**Question:** What types of updates can publishers suggest?
**Answer:** Publishers may suggest edits to business hours, categories, phone numbers, website URLs, addresses, photos, business descriptions, or other profile details based on user feedback or automated detection.




**Question:** What happens to my other connected platforms after I accept a suggestion?
**Answer:** You don't need to worry about updating each site individually. Once you accept a suggestion, the feature automatically syncs that update across all of your connected platforms, saving you the effort of manual entry.




**Question:** Why is the Suggestions Feature important for my business’s online presence?
**Answer:** It helps maintain brand integrity and customer trust by ensuring your business information (like hours and contact info) is accurate and consistent across major directories like Google, Facebook, and Yelp.




**Question:** I accidentally accepted a suggestion that was wrong. Can I undo it?

**Answer:** Once accepted and synced, the new data becomes your "active" info. To fix it, navigate to the **Listings > Dashboard** or **Profile** section, manually enter the correct information, and click **Save**. This will push your corrected data back out to all publishers to override the mistake.




**Question:** Where do these "suggestions" actually come from?

**Answer:** Suggestions are typically generated by the publishers (Google, Bing, Yelp) based on several sources: user feedback (e.g., a customer reporting your shop is closed), AI-driven image recognition (e.g., a Google Street View car seeing a new sign), or data scraped from other third-party directories.
