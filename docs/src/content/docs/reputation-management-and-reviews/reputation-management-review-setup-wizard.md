**Reputation Management: Review Setup Wizard**

The **Review Setup Wizard** is a comprehensive tool designed to simplify the process of launching your reputation management strategy. It guides you step-by-step through the necessary actions to ensure that your business is set up for success in collecting, managing, and responding to reviews. With this wizard, you can get started quickly, without the complexity of navigating multiple steps manually.

### **Key Features and Benefits of the Review Setup Wizard**

-   **Step-by-Step Setup Process**: The wizard walks you through the process from start to finish, making it easy for first-time users to get their review collection system up and running.
-   **Integration with Key Platforms**: Easily connect your **Google Business Profile (GBP)** and **Facebook Pages** to enable review collection from major platforms.
-   **AI-Powered Review Responses**: Set up automated, AI-generated review responses to save time and ensure your replies are timely and personalized.
-   **Customizable Review Widget**: Create a fully customizable review widget to showcase your feedback on your website, making it easy to display reviews.
-   **Expandable Reach**: Optionally connect more platforms for review collection, expanding your review strategy to multiple channels.
-   **Clutter-Free Experience**: The wizard disappears automatically once the core steps are completed, ensuring a clean, user-friendly interface.

**Step 1: Connect Your Accounts**

-   Head over to the Reputations tab from the left menu.
-   Navigate to the **Overview Tab** where the **Review Setup Wizard** is located.

### **Pro Tips for Efficient Setup**

-   **Connect Multiple Accounts Early**: When prompted to connect your **Google Business Profile (GBP)** or **Facebook Page**, do so as soon as possible to maximize your review collection from the start.
-   **Customize Review Request Messages**: Personalize your review request messages to increase the likelihood of customer engagement.
-   **Monitor Review Responses**: Utilize **AI-generated review responses** for timely replies, but also keep track of customer sentiment and make adjustments as necessary.
-   **Leverage Custom Widgets**: Use the **Review Widget** on your website to increase visibility for customer feedback, reinforcing trust in your brand.

### FAQs




**Question:** How does the Review Setup Wizard help me?
**Answer:** The **Review Setup Wizard** simplifies the process of connecting accounts, setting up review links, and configuring AI responses, allowing you to launch your review strategy with minimal effort.




**Question:** What happens after I complete the setup?
**Answer:** Once all core steps are completed, the wizard disappears, and you’ll have a fully set-up review collection strategy, with an automated process for requesting and responding to reviews.




**Question:** Can I expand my review collection beyond Google and Facebook?
**Answer:** Yes, after completing the initial setup, you have the option to connect additional platforms for review collection, broadening your reach and feedback opportunities.




**Question:** How do I configure the AI-generated review responses?
**Answer:** You can configure **Reviews AI** by enabling it in the **Review AI Page** under **Settings**, allowing you to automate responses and create personalized Review Agents for different types of feedback.




**Question:** Can I customize the look of the review widget?
**Answer:** Yes, the **Review Widget** is fully customizable, allowing you to match it with your website’s branding and design preferences, ensuring consistency in your user experience.




**Question:** How can I ensure timely replies to reviews?
**Answer:** Enable the AI-generated review responses for automated, timely replies, and personalize them as needed to suit your business's tone.




**Question:** Does the Review Setup Wizard stay on my dashboard permanently?

**Answer:** No. The wizard provides a clutter-free experience by disappearing automatically once you have completed all the core setup steps.




**Question:** How can I tell which steps of the setup I have already finished?
**Answer:** As you progress through the wizard and complete tasks (such as connecting your Google Business Profile or Facebook Page), the system will display **completion bars** to show you exactly which parts of the setup are done.
