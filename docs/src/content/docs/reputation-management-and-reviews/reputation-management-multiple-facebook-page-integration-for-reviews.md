Managing reviews across various social media platforms just got easier! has introduced a new feature to allow businesses to seamlessly integrate multiple Facebook pages into one unified platform. This addition empowers companies managing multiple locations or brands to handle customer feedback more efficiently, saving time and enhancing customer engagement.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Unified Review Dashboard**: Access all your reviews from connected Facebook pages in a single, organized dashboard.
-   **Comprehensive Monitoring**: Monitor and manage reviews across multiple Facebook pages, ideal for businesses with multiple locations or brands.
-   **Showcase Reviews in One Widget**: Combine reviews from various Facebook pages into a single widget, enhancing your brand’s online reputation cohesively.
-   **Enhanced Engagement**: Respond to reviews faster, ensuring efficient communication and improved customer satisfaction.

### **How to Use Multi-Page Facebook Review Integration**

1\. Log in to and navigate to the **Reputation tab**.

2\. Then navigate to the Settings section > Review Link section.

### **FAQ’s**




**Question:** How do I connect multiple Facebook pages?
**Answer:** In the Reputation Management section, choose to add pages, follow the prompts to authenticate, and connect your Facebook pages one by one.




**Question:** Can I view reviews from all my Facebook pages together?
**Answer:** Yes, the unified dashboard displays all connected page reviews in one place for comprehensive monitoring.




**Question:** How do I create a review widget with multiple Facebook pages?
**Answer:** In the Review Management section, click on **Create Widget** and select the pages to display reviews in one cohesive view.




**Question:** Can I share individual reviews on my social pages?
**Answer:** Absolutely! You can select a review and choose **Share** to post it directly from the relevant Facebook page.




**Question:** Are there any limitations on the number of Facebook pages I can connect to?
**Answer:Typically, there’s no limit on connections, but this may depend on your specific platform configuration. Check with your administrator if needed.




**Question:** How does this integration improve engagement?
**Answer:** By consolidating reviews into a **single dashboard**, you can respond more promptly and efficiently, improving customer satisfaction and maintaining a strong online reputation.




**Question:** Will the review widget automatically update with new reviews?
**Answer:** Yes, the widget pulls reviews from all connected pages in real-time, ensuring that your displayed reviews are always up-to-date.




**Question:** What happens if a review is deleted on Facebook? Will it still show in the widget?

**Answer:** The system performs regular syncs with Facebook. If a review is removed from the original Facebook page, it will automatically be removed from your unified dashboard and the review widget during the next sync cycle.
