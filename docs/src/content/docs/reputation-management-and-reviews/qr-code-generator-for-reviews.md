This feature allows you to effortlessly create and customize digital QR Codes that direct customers to your review pages, helping you enhance your online visibility.

### **Key Features & User Benefits of Reviews QR**

-   **Generate QR Codes**: Create unique QR Codes that link directly to your review pages, streamlining the feedback process.
-   **Customize & Edit:** Personalize your QR Codes by adding images, modifying text, and adjusting the design to align with your brand’s identity.
-   **Download & Share**: Once customized, download your QR Code as a PDF, ready for printing or digital sharing.
-   **Track Scans**: Monitor the performance of your QR Codes with real-time tracking, including the total number of scans.
-   **Quick Access**: Customers can easily scan the QR code with their mobile devices, simplifying the review process.
-   **Boosted Reviews**: Simplifying the feedback process encourages more reviews and improves overall ratings.

###  **How to Access Reviews QR:**

-   Go to the **Reputation** section and open **Reviews QR** in **Reputation > Settings**.
-   Pick the review page where you want the QR code to be sent to customers.
-   Click **Create QR Code** to generate a unique code customers can scan to leave reviews or feedback.

**Tip**: You can create multiple QR Codes for different pages, such as Google Reviews, Facebook Reviews, or a custom feedback form.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** How do I create a Reviews QR Code?
**Answer:** Simply go to the 'Reputation' tab, navigate to 'Settings,' and click on "Create QR Code" to generate a new one for your business.




**Question:** Can I customize the QR Code to match my brand?
**Answer:** Yes, you can personalize your QR Code by adding images, changing text, and adjusting the design to align with your brand’s style.




**Question:** How can I use the QR Code once it’s created?
**Answer:** After customizing, you can download the QR Code as a PDF, which you can print or share digitally in various formats.




**Question:** Can I track how many people scan my QR Code?
**Answer:** Yes, the platform provides real-time tracking that shows you the total number of scans and other related metrics.




**Question:** Where can I use the Reviews QR Code?
**Answer:** You can place the QR Code in physical locations, on business cards, within packaging, or include it in digital communications to encourage reviews.




**Question:** What are the benefits of using Reviews QR?
**Answer:** Reviews QR simplifies the review process for customers, leading to increased feedback, improved ratings, and a stronger online presence.




**Question:** Do I need to download the QR image to share it?
**Answer:** No, you can share the link directly without downloading the image.




**Question:** Can I test a QR Code before saving it?
**Answer:** Yes, you can now test your QR Code instantly during the creation process.




**Question:** Can I edit the QR Code content after it’s created?
**Answer:** Yes. You can update the text, images, or linked page, but if you change the link itself, you may need to generate a new QR Code to ensure accuracy.




**Question:** Can I edit the QR Code after it’s created?
**Answer:** Yes, you can update text, images, or styling. If you change the linked review page, you may need to generate a new QR Code to ensure it works correctly.  




**Question:** Can I create more than one QR Code?  
**Answer:** Yes. You can create multiple codes for different platforms, such as Google Reviews, Facebook Reviews, or custom feedback forms.  




**Question:** Can I include my company's visual identity on the QR Code?

**Answer:** Yes. The customization tools allow you to **upload your company logo** or a relevant image and modify the accompanying instructional text to ensure the code aligns with your brand's look and feel.




**Question:** If I change the URL of my review page, do I need to reprint my QR Code?

**Answer:** This depends on the type of QR code generated. If you are using the "Direct Link" method and that URL changes at the source (e.g., you change your Google Business profile name), the old QR code will break. Always test your physical QR codes periodically to ensure the destination link is still active.




**Question:** Can I change the color of the QR code to match my brand?

**Answer:** Yes. In the customization settings, you can change the color of the "eyes" (the squares in the corners) and the data patterns. Just ensure there is high contrast between the code and the background (e.g., dark blue on white) so that mobile cameras can easily read it.
