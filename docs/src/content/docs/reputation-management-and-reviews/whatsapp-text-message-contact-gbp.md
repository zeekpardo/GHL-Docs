**WhatsApp & Text Message Contact for GBP**

Effective customer communication plays a crucial role in building trust and driving business success. To support this, the Google Business Profile (GBP) optimization workflow allows businesses to add **WhatsApp and Text Message contact options** to their listings. This gives customers more flexible, real-time ways to connect, leading to better engagement and improved responsiveness.

### Key Features & Benefits

-   **Multi-Channel Communication:** Add **WhatsApp** and **SMS** as contact options directly on the Google Business Profile.
-   **Prioritized Messaging:** If both options are entered, **Text Message** will automatically display as the primary contact method.
-   **Increased Accessibility:** Businesses become more reachable to customers who prefer messaging over phone calls.
-   **Profile Engagement:** Improves the completeness of the GBP listing, which can positively impact search visibility and customer trust.
-   **Mobile-First Experience:** Caters to users who prefer quick messaging rather than traditional communication methods.

### How to Use 

**1- Navigate to the Reputation Area:**

-   Go to the Reputation section from your account dashboard.

**2- Access GBP Optimization:**

-   Within Reputation, select GBP Optimization.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764350128658-image.png)

**3- Scroll to the Contact Info Section:**

-   Just below the **Phone Number** field, you’ll see two new input fields:  

    -   **Text Message Number**
    -   **WhatsApp Number**

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763039759848-unnamed - 2025-11-13T211550.879.png>)

**4- Enter Contact Numbers:**

-   Input the appropriate numbers into the respective fields.
-   _Note:_ If both fields are filled, **Text Message** will be displayed on the GBP listing.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763039780706-unnamed - 2025-11-13T211611.537.png>)

**5- Save Changes:**

-   Once entered, the details are synced with the GBP listing, making messaging options publicly visible to potential customers.

### FAQs




**Question:** Do I need to verify the WhatsApp or Text Message number before adding it to the profile?
**Answer:** While the system does not enforce verification, it is highly recommended to use active, business-managed numbers to ensure message delivery and customer trust.




**Question:** Can I change or remove the WhatsApp or Text Message contact later?
**Answer:** Yes, you can update or remove these fields at any time by returning to the GBP Optimization section and editing the corresponding fields.




**Question:** Will I get notified when a customer sends a message via WhatsApp or SMS?
**Answer:** Yes, message notifications will be delivered through the respective messaging platform (e.g., WhatsApp or your SMS provider). Make sure the number used is actively monitored.




**Question:** Does this replace the phone call option on Google Business Profile?
**Answer:** No, this does not replace the phone number field. It **adds** messaging as an **additional** contact method for customers who prefer texting over calling.




**Question:** Can I use both WhatsApp and Text Message contact options on my Google Business Profile?
**Answer:** Yes, you can add both WhatsApp and Text Message contact options. However, if both are entered, Text Message will automatically be displayed as the primary contact method.  


**Question:  Will adding WhatsApp and Text Message options impact my search visibility on Google?
**Answer:** Yes, improving your GBP listing by adding messaging options can positively impact your search visibility. A more complete profile enhances customer trust, which can contribute to better search rankings.




**Question:** What happens if the Text Message number is unavailable?
**Answer:** If the Text Message number is inactive or not monitored, messages may fail to deliver. Always ensure your primary messaging number is active and regularly checked.




**Question:** Will my messaging options be visible to the public immediately after I enter them?

**Answer:** Yes. Once you input the numbers and click **Save Changes**, the details are synced with your Google Business Profile, making the messaging options publicly visible to potential customers.




**Question:** If I provide both a WhatsApp and a Text Message number, which one will customers see first?

**Answer:** The system uses **Prioritized Messaging**. If both fields are filled, the **Text Message** option will automatically be displayed as the primary contact method on your Google Business Profile.
