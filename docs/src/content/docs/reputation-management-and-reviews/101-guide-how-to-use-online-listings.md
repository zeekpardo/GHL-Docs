### FAQ's


**Question:How do I create an Online Listing account through ?  **
**Answer:** To create an Online Listing account, navigate to Reputation > Listings within , run a scan, fill out your business information, enter payment details, and confirm your account setup.


**Question:What should I do if my listings are still showing as "Processing"?  **
**Answer:** If your listings are showing as "Processing," wait for up to 48 hours. Your information should update to "Live" once the processing is complete.


**Question:How can I manage my Online Listing listings after setup?
**Answer:** You can manage your Online Listing listings by accessing the Listings page in , clicking on "Manage Listing" next to each listing, and updating details as needed through Online Listing.


**Question:What happens when I cancel an Online Listing listing?
**Answer:** When you cancel an Online Listing listing, Online Listing will stop managing it, but the listing itself remains on the network. The SEO benefits from the listing are preserved, and the minimum commitment period is reduced to 30 days.


**Question:How can I track changes made to my Online Listing information?  **
**Answer:** You can track changes made to your Online Listing information by using the Entity History feature in the Listings management system. This allows you to view a detailed, chronological record of updates, including who made the change, what was changed, and when it happened. This audit trail enhances transparency and accountability in managing your listings.




**Question:** Why did my listing scan show "Errors" before I even started?
**Answer:** It is very common for the internet to have "ghost" data about your business. Old phone numbers, misspelled addresses, or closed locations might be floating around on sites you’ve never visited. The **Scan** finds these errors so the **Online Listing** tool can go out and "overwrite" them with your correct, updated information.




**Question:** If I already have a Google Business Profile (GBP), will this create a duplicate?

**Answer:** No. During the setup, you will connect your existing GBP and Facebook pages. Instead of creating new ones, Online Listing will "lock" your existing profiles to prevent unauthorized third parties (or competitors) from suggesting malicious edits to your business hours or phone number.




**Question:** What is the "Complete Setup" CTA in the Entities view?

**Answer:** After purchasing a subscription, you must click the **"Complete Setup"** button at the top of the Entities view. This opens a modal where you can see which business entities are "Pending" and finalize their individual configurations to push them live.
