This article introduces a powerful feature within Listings that allows businesses in the Food and Healthcare sectors to enhance their online presence by unlocking specialized add-ons. These add-ons provide tailored features, industry-specific fields, and access to exclusive publisher networks, enabling entities to present themselves better and connect with their target audiences.

### **Key Features and Benefits:**

**1\. Eligibility and Upgrade Flow**:  
Businesses in eligible categories (e.g., Food, Healthcare Facility, or Healthcare Professional) are prompted to upgrade, unlocking specialized fields and publishing options tailored to their industry.

**2\. Exclusive Publisher Distribution**:

-   **Food Add-On**: Distribute your listing to Food-related publishers like MenuPix and OpenTable.
-   **Healthcare Add-On**: Reach health-focused publishers such as DocSpot, RatingsMD, Vitals, WebMD, and WellnessCom.

**3\. Dynamic Menu Management for Food Listings**:  
Food entities can manage multiple menus per listing, organize them by sections (e.g., Appetizers, Mains, Desserts), and include detailed information like item names, prices, descriptions, calories, allergens, spice level, and photos.

**4\. Fields for Food Listings**:

-   **Attire**: Indicate dress code (e.g., Business Casual, Formal).
-   **Accepts Reservations**: Yes or No.
-   **Meals Served**: Specify meal types (e.g., Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner).

### **How to Use:**

**1\. Upgrade Eligibility**:  
When your business qualifies for an upgrade, you will receive a prompt. Upon confirming the upgrade, your listing is automatically updated with industry-specific fields and publishing options.

**2\. Listing Update**:  
After the upgrade, you can begin editing your listing. You’ll see new sections available based on the add-ons activated. Food entities will have options for menu management, while healthcare listings will feature extended provider and facility information.

**3\. View & Edit**:  
In the dashboard, you can see which add-ons are active in your listing card view. New fields and sections are automatically made visible for easy editing.

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I qualify for the Food or Healthcare add-ons?
**Answer:** Businesses in specific categories will be prompted to upgrade once they meet eligibility criteria, unlocking specialized fields and features.




**Question:** What fields are available for food listings?
**Answer:** Food listings can include sections for attire, reservation options, and meals served, along with detailed menu information like item names, prices, calories, allergens, and spice levels.




**Question:** How can I manage multiple menus for my food business?
**Answer:** You can organize your menus by categories (e.g., Appetizers, Main Courses, Desserts) and manage them directly within your listing, with options to add details for each item.




**Question:** Can healthcare providers add their qualifications and bio to the listing?
**Answer:** Yes, healthcare professionals can include their NPI, full name, office details, headshot, bio, and other relevant attributes to improve their profile.




**Question:** How do I activate the new add-on features for my listing?
**Answer:** Once you qualify, you will receive an upgrade prompt, and upon confirmation, your listing will be automatically updated with the new features specific to your industry.  




**Question:** Do I need any technical skills to use these add-ons?
**Answer:** No. The upgrade and setup process is entirely guided. Once you accept the prompt, your listing automatically updates with the new fields no coding or external configuration required.




**Question:** How do I activate the add-ons for my listing?
**Answer:** You activate them by accepting the upgrade prompt. Once confirmed, the listing automatically shows newly unlocked fields for editing.




**Question:** Which specific publisher networks will my business be distributed to after upgrading?

**Answer:** It depends on your industry. Food businesses are distributed to networks like MenuPix and OpenTable, while Healthcare entities reach specialized publishers such as WebMD, Vitals, RatingsMD, DocSpot, and WellnessCom.




**Question:** Is there a way to tell customers if they need to dress up for my restaurant?

**Answer:** Yes. The Food Add-On includes an **"Attire"** field where you can specify the dress code, such as Business Casual or Formal, to set clear expectations for your guests.





**Question:** What specific health-related details can I display on my listing to help patients choose my facility or practice?
**Answer:** Beyond basic contact info, you can highlight critical patient-decision factors such as **insurance accepted**, **conditions treated**, whether you are **accepting new patients**, and your **admitting hospitals**. Healthcare professionals can also upload a **headshot** and a **provider bio** to build personal trust.
