In today's digital landscape, customer reviews are more important than ever. They help build trust, foster loyalty, and significantly impact buying decisions. Leveraging the power of customer reviews on your website can go a long way in enhancing your brand reputation. platform offers a robust feature for this very purpose: the Review Widget Customization tool. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create and personalize your review widget using the platform.

## Accessing the Review Widget Customization Tool

-   Navigate to the "Reputation" section and then click on the "Widgets" tab.
-   Click on the "Create New Widget" button, or you can choose from an existing template in the templates tab to get started.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742253729-1.jpg)

### Layout Tab: Configuring Widget Structure

In the "Layout" tab, you'll see several options to customize the basic structure of your review widget:

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773325900078-image4.png)

**AI review summary:** Customize how reviews are summarized and displayed in widgets. Choose from three different summary types:

1.  **Short Summary**: A brief 1–2 sentence overview of the review.
2.  **Detailed Summary**: A more comprehensive 3–4 sentence summary, providing more context and details from the review.
3.  **Action Points**: Bullet points that highlight key takeaways or actionable insights from the review.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773325916572-image1.png)

**Widget Type:** Choose among List, Masonry, carousel, Grid, Slider, Flash, or Legacy options.

**Review Source:** Select where your reviews will come from —Google My Business, Facebook, etc.

**Max Reviews:** Define how many reviews you wish to display.

**Powered By Section:** An optional section that can be enabled or disabled. It displays the name of the platform.  
**Minimum Rating:** This allows you to curate and display the most positive and impactful reviews according to your preferences.This way you can highlight feedback with a minimum rating of your choice, ensuring your best reviews are prominently showcased. 

###   
Content Tab: Editing Widget Header and Description

The "Content" tab allows you to modify the text aspects of your widget:

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773326039297-image3.png)

**Widget Title and Description:** Modify these to align with your brand messaging.

**Header Section:** Choose to display elements like overall rating, review count, and the "Write a Review" button.

###   
Appearance Tab: Customizing Themes and Colors

The "Appearance" tab gives you complete control over the look and feel of your widget:

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773326055599-image5.png)

**Themes:** Choose between Light, Dark, or Custom themes.

**Component Colors:** Fine-tune individual elements like headings, star ratings, and borders to align with your brand colors.

###   
Settings Tab: Customize review elements and embedding.

The "Settings" tab provides advanced customization options:

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773326078321-image2.png)

**Review Elements:** Opt to display or hide elements like review date and reviewer icon.

**Exclude Empty Reviews:** Skip reviews that contain no descriptive text.

To integrate the review widget into your website, simply generate the necessary embed code. Once generated, you'll need to insert this code into your website's HTML where you'd like the widget to appear.

You also have the option to quickly copy the code by clicking on the "Copy Code" icon located at the upper right corner of the widget interface.

###   
Saving, Naming, and Managing Your Widgets

After completing the customization, give your widget a meaningful name and save it. You can choose to make it live immediately or save it for later use. platform also allows you to manage multiple widgets.

## FAQs




**Question:** Can I create multiple widgets for a single account?
**Answer:** Yes, you can now create and manage multiple widgets within the same account.




**Question:** How do I differentiate between widgets when copying the code?
**Answer:** The dropdown menu in the widget module displays all your widgets for easy selection.




**Question:** Are all widgets linked to the same review sources?
**Answer:** Yes, all widgets pull reviews from the same sources but can have unique styles and placements.




**Question:** Can I use these widgets on different websites?  
**Answer:** Yes, as long as the websites are managed under the same account, you can place widgets on multiple sites.




**Question:** Are there any restrictions on the number of widgets I can create?
**Answer:** No, there is no limit to the number of widgets you can create within a .




**Question:** Can I customize individual review elements?
**Answer:** Yes. In the **Settings** tab, you can choose to display or hide elements such as the **review date** and **reviewer icon**, and skip reviews without descriptive text.




**Question:** Can I change the color of the stars to match my brand?
**Answer:** Absolutely. Under the **Appearance** tab, you can fine-tune "Component Colors." This allows you to change the star rating color, heading text, and even the borders to perfectly align with your company’s brand palette.
