**How to Create and Customize QR Codes**

The ability to create and manage QR codes in is a game-changer for enhancing your digital engagement and data collection. This article will guide you through the new QR code feature, highlighting its benefits and providing easy-to-follow steps to make the most of this tool.

**Key Features and Benefits**

-   **QR Code Builder:** Effortlessly create and customize QR codes. Adjust colors, shapes, forms, and logos to match your branding.
-   **Dashboard Management:** View and manage all your QR codes from a central dashboard.
-   **Analytics:** Track how often your QR codes are scanned, visualize trends, and explore detailed data to understand engagement levels.
-   **Versatile Sharing:** Share QR codes across digital channels, websites, or print materials. Direct users to key pages such as forms, surveys, or websites.
-   **Enhanced Aesthetics:** Utilize modern shapes, borders, and instructional text to increase scan rates and brand alignment.

**_Available Formats_**

-   **PNG** – Ideal for digital use with high quality, though it may lose sharpness when resized.
-   **SVG** – Scalable without compromising quality, making it perfect for printing. Users can resize SVG QR codes freely without any loss of quality. To resize, you may use [**_this tool_**](https://products.aspose.app/words/picresize/svg).
-   **PDF** – Suitable for printing on A4 paper, converted from PNG format.

**FAQ**




**Question:** What format should I use?
**Answer:

-   **PNG**: Best for websites and social media, providing good quality for digital use.
-   **SVG**: Ideal for professional printing, maintaining sharpness at any size and allowing easy resizing. Resize your SVG [**_here_**](https://products.aspose.app/words/picresize/svg).
-   **PDF**: Best for printing on A4 paper, making it suitable for official documents.




**Question:** Can I change the physical shape of the QR code?

**Answer:** Yes. In the "Color and Shape" step, you can choose between square, **Rounded**, or **Circular** designs to better fit your layout.




**Question:** How do I add instructions like "Scan Me" to the code?

**Answer:** Use the **Rim Content** feature. This allows you to place text at the top and bottom of the QR code frame to guide users.




**Question:** Can I add my brand colors to the frame?

**Answer:** Yes. When you add a **Border or Frame**, you have the option to customize its color to match your specific branding.




**Question:** Where can I locate my saved QR codes?
**Answer:** You can find your saved QR codes in the QR Codes section of . To access them, navigate to **Sites** on the left > click on "**QR Codes**" from the top menu. Your saved QR codes will be listed with their unique identifiers for easy retrieval and downloading.




**Question:** Are there any limitations on MacBook and Safari?
**Answer:** Some users may experience lower PNG quality when downloading QR codes in Safari due to rendering limitations. In such cases, the SVG format is recommended.  
Older Safari versions may not fully support all customization features, and using Google Chrome or Firefox may offer a better experience.  
Updates are being worked on to improve compatibility and quality for Safari users.




**Question:** Do I need to download the QR image to share it?
**Answer:** No, you can share the link directly without downloading the image.




**Question:** Can I test a QR Code before saving it?
**Answer:** Yes, you can now test your QR Code instantly during the creation process.




**Question:** Can I undo a bulk delete?
**Answer:** No, deletions are permanent at the moment. Be sure to double-check your selection before confirming the action.




**Question:** What are best practices for using QR codes effectively?
**Answer:

-   **Use analytics:** Monitor scans to measure engagement.
-   **Design for impact:** Ensure QR codes are clear and visually appealing.
-   **Update links as needed:** Use dynamic URLs to keep content current without creating new codes.




**Question:** How can I track the performance of my QR codes?
**Answer:** The analytics dashboard shows scan frequency, trends, and detailed engagement data to help you understand how your QR codes are performing.




**Question:** Can I put my company logo in the middle of the QR code?
**Answer:** Yes! Inside the **Customization** settings, you can upload your logo. The builder will place it in the center. **Pro Tip:** Don't make the logo too big, or it might make the code harder for some older phones to scan. Always use the "Instant Test" feature to make sure it still works!




**Question:** If I move a QR code into a different folder, does the link change?
**Answer:** No. Moving your QR codes into folders is purely for your own organization within the dashboard. It does not affect the functionality or the destination of the live QR code.




**Question:** Can I generate a large number of QR codes at once for an event or campaign?

**Answer:** Yes. Use the **Bulk QR Code Creation** feature. Simply upload a **CSV file** containing the names and URLs for your codes. The system will generate them all in real-time, and you can even choose to automatically sort them into a specific folder.
