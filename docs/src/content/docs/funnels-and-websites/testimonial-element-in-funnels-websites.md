The ability to showcase client testimonials is essential for building trust and credibility with potential customers. With the Testimonial element in your page builder, you can display feedback in a way that is both professional and aligned with your brand. In this article, we will guide you through the process of setting up and customizing the Testimonial section to best suit your needs.

### **Key Features**

1.  **Multiple Layouts:**
    -   Choose between grid, stacked, or slider views for displaying testimonials.
    -   This allows flexibility depending on the amount of content and the space on your page.
2.  **Adjustable Columns:**
    -   Control how many testimonials appear per row.
    -   You can set it to display one, two, or more columns, depending on your design preferences.
3.  **Card Design Options:**
    -   Select from clean and modern card styles to showcase each testimonial.
    -   These cards are easy to read and visually appealing.
4.  **Custom Fonts & Colors:**
    -   Customize typography and colors to match your brand’s style.
    -   You can adjust fonts and color schemes to ensure consistency across your website.
5.  **Optional Display Elements:**
    -   Choose whether to display profile photos, names, logos, dates, and star ratings.
    -   This gives you control over the elements that appear with each testimonial.

**How to Use the Testimonial Element**

**1\. Access the Page Builder:**

-   Navigate to Sites on the left > Funnels/Websites/Stores at the top and open the relevant builder.
-   Select the step or page and click edit to open the builder.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763753013384-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 142327.png>)

**2\. Add the Testimonial Element:**

-   Open the page you want to add the testimonials to.
-   Click on the + icon to access the elements section and select the “Testimonial” element.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763753039876-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 142355.png>)

**3\. Select the Layout:**

-   Choose your preferred layout (Grid, Stacked, or Slider).
-   Adjust the number of columns to suit your design.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764874270853-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-04 135056.png>)

**4\. Customize Design:**

-   Select the card style and customize the fonts and colors to match your brand.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763753086716-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 142441.png>)

**5\. Configure Display Options:**

-   Decide if you want to show profile photos, names, dates, and ratings.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763753114001-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 142510.png>)

**6\. Preview & Save:**

-   Preview your testimonials on the page to ensure they look great.
-   Once satisfied, save and publish your changes.

### **Use Cases**

1.  **Product Reviews:**
    -   Display feedback from customers who have used your products or services to build trust.
2.  **Client Success Stories:**
    -   Use testimonials from clients to demonstrate the impact your services have had on their success.
3.  **Service Ratings:**
    -   Showcase ratings and reviews from your satisfied clients to boost your credibility and attract new business.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I use different styles for different testimonials?
**Answer:** Yes, you can choose a different style for each testimonial section, allowing flexibility in your design.




**Question:** How many testimonials can I display per page?
**Answer:** The number of testimonials you can display depends on your layout and the space available on the page.




**Question:** Can I change the order of testimonials?
**Answer:** Yes, you can reorder testimonials within the section by dragging them to your desired position.




**Question:** Is it possible to add videos as part of the testimonial?
**Answer:** Currently, you can add text and images, but video integration may require a custom solution.




**Question:** Can I use this on all pages?
**Answer:** Yes, the Testimonial element can be used on any funnel or website page that supports the page builder.




**Question:** What visual elements can I choose to show or hide for each testimonial?

**Answer:** You have full control over optional elements; you can choose whether to display profile photos, names, company logos, dates, and star ratings to customize the look of each testimonial.




**Question:** What is the ideal image size for the testimonial profile photos?

**Answer:** For the best results, use a square image (e.g., **200x200px**). The builder will automatically crop the photo into a circle or rounded square depending on the style you choose, so ensure the person’s face is centered in the image.




**Question:** What are the three different layout options available for displaying my testimonials?
**Answer:** You can choose between **Grid, Stacked, or Slider** views. This allows you to adjust how the content is presented based on the amount of feedback you have and the available space on your page.
