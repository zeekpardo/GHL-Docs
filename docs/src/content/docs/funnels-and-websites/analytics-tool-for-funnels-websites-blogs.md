Introducing the Funnel, Website, and Blog Analytics tool, a unified dashboard to track views, clicks, and engagement across all your content. Easily monitor performance for funnels, websites, and blogs in one place, helping you make smarter, data-driven decisions and optimize your strategy with ease.

## Key Features

### Holistic Performance Tracking

Gain a complete view of your funnels' and websites' performance. The analytics tool aggregates data from all your funnels, making it easier to visualize changes in performance metrics over time.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775575199009-image3.png)

**External Site & Form Tracking**  

offers native visibility into visitor behavior on externally hosted sites and forms, whether they are built with native code or HTML. By connecting a tracking script, you can monitor external activity directly within your central dashboard.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769202789454-image13.jpg)

### Key Metrics at a Glance

Monitor essential metrics such as page views, opt-ins, sales, and opt-in conversion rates. These metrics are displayed in an easy-to-understand format, helping you quickly assess the effectiveness of your marketing efforts.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775575260772-image2.png)

### Average Time Metrics

Gain a clear understanding of user retention using the **Average Time** metric. This tracks the average duration users spend across your platform during daily, weekly, or custom date ranges. By dividing total time spent by the number of sessions, it provides a vital indicator of how long your content successfully captures and holds visitor attention over time.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775575283361-image12.png)

### Filters in Analytics for Funnels, Websites and Webinars

Advanced analytics filters in allow you to isolate performance data across funnels, websites, and webinars with precision. By segmenting traffic by dimensions like location, source, or behavior, you can move past surface-level stats to uncover granular, actionable insights.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775575342974-image16.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775575397333-image4.png)

### Pie Chart Visualization

Easily track and analyze the breakdown of page views and sales using a pie chart format. The chart dynamically adapts based on filters like date range, funnels, or websites, giving you clear insights to refine your traffic and conversion strategies.

### Flexible Date Range and Filters

Customize your view by selecting specific date ranges and filtering data by individual funnels or websites. This flexibility allows you to pinpoint trends and identify areas for improvement.

### Detailed Distribution Insights

Analyze the distribution of page views, opt-in pages, and sales across various products. The tool provides a detailed breakdown, enabling you to understand the performance of different elements within your funnels.

### Top Browsers & Top Client IPs

The **Top Browsers Breakdown** shows the top 10 browsers your visitors use, allowing you to optimize your site based on real trends.

 The **Client IP Tracking** feature reveals the top 10 IP addresses visiting your site, helping you detect unusual traffic patterns and potential bots.

Shows breakdown of your website's traffic based on device categories: mobile, desktop, and tablet. It includes a pie chart in the Page Views section for both Funnels and Websites, providing real-time insights into device preferences.

**Traffic Source Analytics**

This provides a clear visual breakdown of where your site traffic originates, helping you distinguish between sources like Paid, Organic, Direct, and Referral. This is designed to simplify traffic attribution and highlight patterns over time. 

**To use it,** navigate to the analytics section, select "Traffic Source Analytics," choose a date range, and hover or click on visual bars for detailed channel insights. No additional setup is required, but accurate data depends on your tracking settings and correct tagging of incoming traffic.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768847714150-image10.png)

**Geographic Analytics (State & City Drilldown)**

A deeper understanding of where your traffic originates by allowing you to view data not just by country, but down to specific states and cities. This provides ranked lists, interactive maps, and real-time that make it easier to identify high-performing areas.

**To use it,** navigate to the geographic analytics section, select your desired view from the dropdown (Country, State, City), and hover over regions for instant visitor data. There are no special integrations required, but ensure your data tracking setup is active to utilize full functionality.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768847745264-image13.png)

## FAQ




**Question:** How can I access the new analytics tool?

**Answer:** Navigate to the site section in the left-hand menu and click on the new Analytics tab.




**Question:** What metrics can I track with this tool?

**Answer:** You can track page views, opt-ins, sales, and opt-in conversion rates.




**Question:** Can I filter the data by individual funnels?

**Answer:** Yes, you can filter the data to view metrics for specific funnels or websites.




**Question:** Why is there no "Average Time on Page" data for my external site before January 2026?
**Answer:** Tracking for this specific metric began on January 20, 2026. Data for "Average Time on Page" was not captured prior to this date.




**Question:** What additional features can I expect in the future?

**Answer:** Upcoming enhancements will include card analytics and more detailed performance insights.




**Question:** How can I use the Top Browsers and Client IP data to improve my site?

**Answer:** By analyzing the **Top Browsers Breakdown**, you can optimize your site for the browsers most commonly used by your visitors, ensuring better performance and user experience. Additionally, **Client IP Tracking** helps you identify unusual traffic patterns, allowing you to spot potential security issues like bots or excessive refresh rates from a single IP, enabling you to take proactive actions.




**Question:** What data can I track?

**Answer:** You can track page views, link clicks, engagement by device type, top posts, author clicks, and more.




**Question:** How can I view traffic by device type?

**Answer:** You can view traffic by device type through the "Traffic by Device Type" section, where the data is presented as a pie chart in the Page Views section for both funnels and websites. This breakdown shows real-time insights into traffic preferences based on mobile, desktop, and tablet categories.




**Question:** Can I export analytics data for reporting?

**Answer:** Depending on your platform settings, you may be able to export certain analytics reports. Check the Analytics section for export options or upcoming feature updates.




**Question:** How do I see which specific cities or states my traffic is coming from?

**Answer:** Navigate to the **Geographic Analytics** section and use the dropdown menu to select your desired view (**Country, State, or City**). You can then hover over the interactive map or view ranked lists for instant visitor data.
