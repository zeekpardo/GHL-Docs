Enhancing your website's SEO is crucial for improving visibility and attracting more traffic. With the SEO Suggester tool, you can optimize your website's content more effectively, ensuring better search engine rankings. This guide will explain the key features of the SEO Suggester and provide easy steps to utilize it.

## **Understanding the Basics of SEO**

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is essential for ranking higher on search engines like Google. Ranking organically means your website appears in search results without the need to spend money on ads. Effective SEO helps your content reach the right audience, driving more traffic and enhancing your online presence.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773333591846-image18.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773333634007-image16.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773333663642-image3.png)

-   Click **Validate & Save** – any errors will be highlighted inline if validation fails.

**Pro Tips:**

1.  **Keep Titles and Descriptions Concise:** Aim to keep your titles under 70 characters and descriptions under 155 characters to avoid truncation in search engine results. Always ensure they are unique and relevant to the content of your page.
2.  **Use Long-Tail Keywords:** Incorporate long-tail keywords with high search volume and low competition to improve your chances of ranking higher in search results. Tools like keyword planners can help you identify these keywords.
3.  **Optimize Social Images:** Use click-worthy, high-quality images for your social previews. This not only makes your content more appealing but also encourages clicks when shared on social platforms.
4.  **Utilize Canonical Links Wisely:** Use canonical links to inform search engines of the preferred URL when duplicate content exists. This helps prevent dilution of your ranking signals and improves SEO efficiency.
5.  **Set Correct Language Attributes:** Always set the correct HTML language attribute for your page. This helps search engines understand the primary language of your content, improving your site's accessibility and SEO.
6.  **AI-powered generation:** Analyzes your page to identify the most relevant schema types, structures them at the correct hierarchy level, and automatically fills in fields using real-time page data.

### FAQ's




**Question:** What is the SEO Suggestor used for?

**Answer:** The SEO Suggestor is designed to help optimize your content for search engines like Google. It provides recommendations on titles, descriptions, keywords, and more to improve your chances of ranking organically.




**Question:** How do I set up a social image for my content?
**Answer:** You can add a social image by uploading it in the SEO metadata settings under the Social Image section. This image will be displayed when your content is shared on social media platforms.




**Question:** What are canonical links and why are they important?

**Answer:** Canonical links tell search engines which version of a page to consider as the main one when there are duplicates. This helps avoid confusion and ensures that the correct page is ranked, consolidating your SEO efforts.




**Question:** Can I exclude certain pages from search engine indexing?
**Answer:** Yes, you can exclude pages by adding a tag with robots as the name and noindex, nofollow as the content in the Links and Tags section. This prevents search engines from indexing or following links on that page.




**Question:** Why should I use the HTML language attribute selector?

**Answer:** The HTML language attribute selector helps search engines and browsers understand the primary language of your content. This improves the accessibility of your site and can enhance your SEO for language-specific search queries.




**Question:** Can I use the SEO Suggester for multiple pages or funnels?

**Answer:** Yes. You can repeat the SEO setup process for each page or funnel you want to optimize, ensuring consistent and effective metadata across your website.




**Question:** Why should I avoid putting my domain name in the page title multiple times?

**Answer:** You should avoid repeating your domain name excessively in the title to prevent your website from being flagged as "spammy" by search engines.




**Question:** What happens if I try to generate a schema for a blank page?

**Answer:** The AI Flow requires existing page content to function. If you attempt to generate a schema for a page with no text or data, the system will display an error and will not be able to build the markup. Ensure your page has its primary content in place before launching the **Create with AI** tool.
