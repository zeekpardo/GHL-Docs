Card Authorization, by default, is **not** turned on and will require a few steps to be turned on. The Card Authorization Feature checks the clients bank account with the amount being charged, before crediting it back later. If the account does not have enough funds to pay for your product, they will not be allowed to have access until it is paid. 

**_NOTE:_** If you have any "Trial days" attached to the plan those will also kick in as soon as the card is authorized and Funds may take up to 5 business days before being returned to the clients bank account. This Feature will only work with Subscriptions and Payment plans. 

## **Navigation** 

**Step One:**  Go to the website you would like to set this up for and find the order form step. Click on the three dots.

### **FAQ**



**1\. Can the Card Authorization feature be used for one-time payments or is it only for subscriptions and payment plans?**The feature only works with subscriptions and payment plans, not one-time payments.

**2\. How long does it take for the authorized amount to be refunded to the customer’s bank account?**Funds may take up to 5 business days to be credited back to the customer’s account.

**3\. What happens if a customer’s bank account doesn’t have enough funds during the authorization?**If the account doesn’t have sufficient funds, the customer will not be granted access to the product or service until the payment is successful.

**4\. Will the trial period start as soon as the card is authorized?**Yes, if the plan has any trial days attached, they will begin immediately once the card is authorized.

**5\. Can I customize the amount charged during the Card Authorization process?**Yes, you can set a custom authorization amount in the “Custom Authorization” section of the Product Settings.

**6\. What happens if I don’t see any products in the “Products” section?**If there are no products listed, you need to go back and add them to the order form before proceeding with the Card Authorization setup.

**7\. Can I enable Card Authorization for multiple products within the same order form?**Yes, you can enable Card Authorization for each product individually by following the same steps for each one.
