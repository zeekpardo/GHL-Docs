## **Understanding Email Validation:** 

Email validation is a multi-step process designed to ensure that the email addresses in your mailing list are accurate, authentic, and capable of receiving messages. The validation process involves checking the syntax, domain existence, and risk level of each email address.

### **Why Email Validation Matters:** 

Email validation holds importance for businesses and marketers. It directly influences email deliverability by removing invalid addresses and enhancing your sender reputation. Maintaining a clean and up-to-date email list is essential for optimizing email marketing metrics, including open rates, click-through rates (CTR), and click-to-open ratio (CTOR).

### **Impact on Email Deliverability:** 

Email deliverability is significantly affected by bounced emails. When emails are bounced, it negatively impacts your IP address and domain reputation. Email clients rely on these reputations to decide whether to deliver your messages or mark them as spam. By validating your email list, you minimize bounce rates, preserving your sender's reputation and ensuring your emails reach the intended recipients' inboxes.

## **Email Validation Process:**

-   **Syntax Check:** Ensures the email address format is correct.
-   **Domain Verification:** Validates that the domain is set up to receive emails.
-   **Existence Confirmation:** Verifies the existence of the email address.
-   **Risk Assessment:** Identifies high-risk or role-based addresses (e.g., info@domain.com).

### **When to Validate Your Email List:**

-   New Acquisitions: After acquiring a new mailing list, validating it is essential.
-   Inherited Lists: If you've inherited a list, especially from an unfamiliar source, validation is necessary.
-   Periodic Health Check: Regularly validate your list, especially if it hasn't been verified recently or has undergone significant growth.

### **Manual Re-Validation Process:**

When **Email Verification** is enabled, any unverified email address will be verified before the first email is sent.

**Question:** How do I validate a contact’s email address manually?
**Answer:** 

1.  **Navigate to Contacts:** Go to the Contacts section in your platform.
2.  **Search for Contact:** Find and click on the contact whose email you want to validate.
3.  **Check Verification Status:** Scroll down to the email field and click the envelope icon next to it.
4.  **Review Status:** The status will be indicated next to the email field:
    -   **Green:** Valid
    -   **Red:** Invalid or previously bounced (an error message will specify why)




**Question:** How can I validate emails in bulk?
**Answer:** To validate emails in bulk:

-   Navigate to the Smart-list page within the Contacts Tab
-   Select all the contacts you wish to validate.
-   Click on the "Email Verification Icon".

-   Add an action name and click "Ok, Proceed".
