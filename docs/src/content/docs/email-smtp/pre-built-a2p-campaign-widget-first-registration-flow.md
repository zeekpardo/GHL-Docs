The purpose of this article is to describe the specialized registration experience designed for users to streamline the Application-to-Person (A2P) messaging compliance process. This feature minimizes manual effort and reduces the risk of carrier rejections by enforcing a pre-configured, high-compliance registration path focused on the chat widget as the primary opt-in method.

**Key Features and Benefits**

**Reduced Rejection Rates:** By using pre-built campaign structures that align with carrier and CTIA requirements, the risk of submission rejection is significantly lowered.

**Automatic Compliance Generation:** The system automatically generates required consent language, disclosure blocks, and data rate/frequency notifications, eliminating the need for manual writing.

**Conflict Protection:** Built-in logic prevents high-risk misclassifications, such as blocking the combination of "Marketing" intent with "Financial Services," which is a frequent cause of carrier denial.

**Automated Age Gating:** Mandatory Date of Birth (DOB) fields are automatically enforced and locked into the widget when high-risk or age-restricted content categories are selected.

**Streamlined Selection:** Users are guided through a filtered dropdown of use cases such as Customer Care, 2FA, or Marketing ensuring the messaging intent is clearly defined.

**How to Use the Pre-Built Registration Flow**

**Open Settings:** Click on the Settings located at the bottom of the left-hand sidebar.  

**Access Phone Settings:** From the left-side settings menu, select Phone Numbers.  

**Enter the Trust Center:** Look for the tab at the top of the page labeled Trust Center. This is the primary hub where all A2P registration and compliance tasks are managed.

**Start Your Registration**: Begin the standard process to register your messaging campaign inside .

**Pick the Pre-Built Option**: The system identifies the Sole Proprietor status during the initial onboarding or Chat Widget flow.

**Special Requirements and Limitations**

**Campaign Type Restrictions:** This specific registration flow is not available for "Sole-prop" or "Mixed use-case" campaigns.

**Widget Editing Restrictions:** To maintain compliance, users cannot edit disclosure text, remove required fields, or add custom consent checkboxes once the pre-built flow is active.

**Opt-In Enforcement:** This flow locks the chat widget as the only allowed opt-in method; alternative methods cannot be selected for the same campaign.

**FAQs**




**Question:** Why can't I edit the disclosure text in my chat widget during this process?

**Answer:** The text is locked to ensure it meets strict carrier compliance standards. Allowing manual edits could lead to the omission of required language, such as data rate disclosures or STOP/HELP instructions, increasing your risk of rejection.




**Question:** When will the Date of Birth (DOB) field appear in my widget?

**Answer:** The DOB field is automatically triggered and becomes mandatory if you select "Age gated content" or "Financial services / loan arrangement" as your use case.




**Question:** Can I use this flow if I want to send both Marketing and Informational messages in one campaign?

**Answer:** No, this flow is designed for specific, single-intent campaigns. It is not compatible with "mixed use-case" submissions.




**Question:** What happens if I try to select "Marketing" for a financial services campaign?

**Answer:** The system will block the flow. To proceed with financial or loan-related content, you must switch the message type to "Informational" to align with carrier safety policies.




**Question:** Does this update change how I style my widget?

**Answer:** You can still customize the UI styling, such as colors and header text, but the functional compliance elements and required fields remain locked and non-editable.
