**How to Use the Email Builder and Its In-Line Editor**

Email marketing remains one of the most effective ways to engage with your audience and boost business growth. In this comprehensive guide, we will delve deep into the Email Builder and its Inline Editor, empowering you to streamline your email creation process and create compelling campaigns that resonate with your audience.

##  Understanding the Email Builder Inline Editor:

### Introduction to Email Builder Inline Editor

The Email Builder Inline Editor is a powerful tool embedded within our Templates and Campaigns platform. It eliminates the complexity of email design and formatting, offering a user-friendly interface for effortless customization.

### Leveraging "What You See Is What You Get" (WYSIWYG) Editing

This editor operates on the principle of WYSIWYG, providing real-time previews as you make changes. Whether it's bolding a sentence, inserting an image, creating an image through AI, or changing the color of a heading, the editor displays the final email appearance instantly.

### Customization Features: Fonts, Text Sizes, Colors, Formatting, Hyperlinks, and More

The Inline Editor supports various customization features. Users can choose from multiple heading and paragraph formats, select supported fonts, adjust text sizes, and apply colors using hex codes for precise selection. Formatting options such as bold, italic, underline, and strikethrough, along with hyperlink integration, enhance the email's visual appeal.

Moreover, the inline editor allows for custom values, giving you better control over your content. The tool also supports AI-assisted content generation, which can help you create engaging email content faster.

### Layouts

**![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcXHNskHvrtPRBBJ6uNNS9bwDQicaW12tbQzHaYx0xEVOF424BYLargVWXHow0phdxG6M2SjiMwxVInuDzQzXMxXbfGd8-hTEJpvUSRanaUyUgHk_qOpbpaju9iff39Zn5O1Hnm5s2m1Kf-sjpPfQaNdfCA?key=CaXoWMaQe4Z9h32Sg12mpQ)**

Here's a detailed explanation of each layout:

**1:** This layout represents a single-column structure. All content is placed in one straight vertical line. This is the most basic and one of the most common layouts used in emails. It's great for readability, especially on smaller screens such as mobile devices.

**2:** This layout corresponds to a two-column structure. Your email content is divided into two vertical columns. This layout is ideal for displaying two groups of content side by side, like an image and a text block or two articles from an RSS feed.

**1/3: 2/3 and 2/3 : 1/3:** These layouts represent a structure where the content is divided into two unequal columns. In 1/3: 2/3, one column takes up 1/3 of the width, and the other takes up 2/3. It's reversed in the 2/3: 1/3 layout. These are great for highlighting one piece of content more than the other, like an image and an accompanying description where the image is the main focus.

**1/4: 3/4 and 3/4 : 1/4:** Similar to the above, but here one column takes up 1/4 of the space, and the other takes up 3/4, and vice versa. This layout is useful when one piece of content is significantly more important than the other, such as a small navigation menu alongside a large content area.

**4:** This layout refers to a four-column structure. Your email content is split across four equal vertical columns. This is useful for presenting multiple pieces of content of equal importance side by side, like a grid of images or multiple articles from an RSS feed.

These layouts offer various ways to structure and present your content, depending on your specific needs and the type of email you're sending. By understanding the different options, you can choose the most effective layout for your email.

To add any of these elements to your email, drag and drop them onto your canvas. When you select an element for editing, the Inline Editor will pop up, providing numerous options to modify your chosen element to fit your requirements.

### FAQ’s


**Question:What is the Email Builder Inline Editor, and how does it work?
**Answer:** The Email Builder Inline Editor is a user-friendly tool that allows you to design and format your emails with real-time previews. It operates on a WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) basis, so any changes you make, such as text formatting or image insertion, are instantly visible. This feature simplifies the email creation process and helps you create visually appealing campaigns.


**Question:Can I customize the design and layout of my emails?
**Answer:** Yes! The Email Builder provides various customization options, including font selection, text size adjustments, color application, and formatting (bold, italic, etc.). You can choose from multiple layouts to structure your content effectively, and the default padding settings help maintain consistent spacing around elements.


**Question:How do I add different content elements to my email?
**Answer:** To add elements, navigate to the 'Add Elements' tab in the Email Builder. You can drag and drop various elements like text, images, buttons, dividers, and social media icons onto your email canvas. Each element can be customized further using the Inline Editor to fit your campaign needs.


**Question:Is it possible to save and reuse components in my emails?
**Answer:** Absolutely! You can save individual elements and entire layouts for future use. This feature allows you to quickly access your saved items via the search bar, enabling you to maintain design consistency and save time by reusing popular components without recreating them from scratch.


**Question:What should I do if I need to insert custom HTML into my email?
**Answer:** If you need to include custom HTML, you can use the Code element within the Email Builder. This feature is designed for users familiar with HTML, allowing you to input your custom code. If you're not comfortable with HTML, it’s recommended to review some introductory resources, like W3Schools or Codecademy, before proceeding.


**Question:Can I preview how my email will look on mobile devices?
**Answer:** Yes, the Email Builder provides a responsive preview so you can ensure your design looks great across desktop and mobile screens.  


**Question:How can I add conditional visibility to elements in my email?
**Answer:** To add conditional visibility to elements in your email, navigate to the Email Builder, select the element you want to personalize, and go to the **Visibility** tab. From there, enable the **Conditional Element** option. You can set conditions based on various contact fields, such as single-line text, numbers, radio selects, and drop-down options. However, note that custom fields like multi-select fields are not supported. This allows you to tailor the content of your emails based on specific recipient data.




**Question:** Are GIFs supported in the "Video" element thumbnail?

**Answer:** Absolutely! Using a GIF as a video thumbnail is a "pro-move." It creates the illusion of a playing video inside the inbox, which significantly increases the likelihood of a user clicking the play button to watch the full video on your hosted page.




**Question:** Can I use my own brand colors in the email builder?

**Answer:** Yes. While the editor offers a standard color palette, you can apply your specific brand colors using **Hex codes** for precise selection in the text, background, and button settings.
