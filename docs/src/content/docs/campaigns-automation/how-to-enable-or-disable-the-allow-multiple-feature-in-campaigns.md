**Important Note**: “**Campaigns**” in Automation is a deprecated feature and has been replaced by Workflows. If you want to learn more about how to properly set up Workflows, please check out our article “**How to Create and Use Workflows**”

The "Allow Multiple" feature in campaigns is a versatile setting that determines whether a lead or contact can enter the same campaign more than once. This functionality is essential for managing how contacts engage with your automated sequences, allowing you to tailor the experience based on your specific needs.

**How to Use the "Allow Multiple" Feature:**

**Step 1: Access the Campaign**

1.  Navigate to the campaign you wish to edit.

**Step 2: Adjust Campaign Configuration**

1.  Open the Campaign Configuration Menu.
2.  Locate the "Allow Multiple" setting, which is toggled on by default.

1.  To allow multiple entries, leave the setting as-is.
2.  To prevent contacts from entering the campaign more than once, toggle the setting off.

**Key Features and Benefits:**

-   **Customizable Access:** Decide if a contact can re-enter a campaign based on their interaction history, such as rescheduling appointments or completing a sequence multiple times.
-   **Enhanced Flexibility:** Adapt your campaigns to different scenarios by toggling the feature on or off, ensuring that your automation meets the needs of both recurring and one-time engagements.
-   **Streamlined Management:** Easily adjust the setting to accommodate various campaign requirements, enhancing the overall efficiency of your workflow.

**Pro Tips:**

-   **Assess Campaign Needs:** Consider the purpose of your campaign before adjusting the setting. For example, if the campaign involves appointment reminders, allowing multiple entries might be beneficial if contacts need to reschedule.
-   **Monitor Performance:** Regularly review how toggling the "Allow Multiple" setting impacts your campaign's effectiveness and adjust as needed based on contact interactions and feedback.

**FAQ:**

**1\. What does the "Allow Multiple" feature do?**The "Allow Multiple" feature controls whether a contact can enter the same campaign multiple times. When enabled, contacts can re-enter the campaign; when disabled, they cannot.

**2\. How can I enable or disable the "Allow Multiple" feature?**Access the campaign you want to edit, open the Campaign Configuration Menu, and toggle the "Allow Multiple" setting as needed.

**3\. When should I use the "Allow Multiple" feature?**Use this feature when you need contacts to go through a campaign more than once, such as for appointment reminders or follow-up sequences. Disable it when you want to prevent repeat entries, like for one-time offers or registration processes.

**4\. Can I apply different settings to different campaigns?**Yes, you can enable or disable the "Allow Multiple" feature individually for each campaign based on its specific requirements.

**5\. What happens if I toggle the setting off?**If the "Allow Multiple" feature is turned off, contacts will not be able to enter the campaign more than once, even if they meet the entry criteria again.
