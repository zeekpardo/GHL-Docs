**Custom Fields for Opportunities**

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on utilizing our management system's powerful custom fields feature for opportunities. This versatile tool allows you to capture and organize additional information tailored to your specific business needs, providing you with unparalleled flexibility and convenience. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of creating and using custom fields, ensuring that you can fully leverage this feature to enhance your opportunities management. Let's get started!

### Understanding Custom Fields

-   The custom fields feature empowers you to expand the capabilities of our opportunities management system.
-   Create and add custom fields to capture essential data that goes beyond standard fields.
-   Tailor opportunities to your unique requirements with custom fields designed for your business needs.
-   This interface ensures that you can swiftly access and update custom fields without unnecessary clicks or confusion.
-   You can experiment with different field types, organize custom fields creatively, and build an opportunities management process that aligns perfectly with your business goals.

After creating custom fields, they will be available for all cards within the opportunities area.

Populate the custom fields with relevant information to gain valuable insights into each opportunity.

Tailor your strategies and decision-making process based on the customized data.

For instance, you can use custom fields to track potential revenue, estimated close dates, or specific products/services associated with each opportunity.

### Cleaning Up with "Hide Empty Fields"

Maintain a clear and focused view of your opportunity cards with the "Hide Empty Fields" option.

Enable this setting to display only fields that contain data, reducing visual clutter and distractions.

Enhance the readability of your opportunities and stay focused on relevant information.

By hiding empty fields, you ensure that only the most pertinent details are visible, making it easier to assess the status of each opportunity at a glance.

This setting is visible when you edit an opportunity or after adding a field.

### Customization at a User Level

Customizations made through the "Manage Fields" section are user-specific.  
This means that each user can set up their view for opportunity cards, and these settings will not affect other users unless explicitly shared.  
For example, if you prefer to see certain data points on the card while another user prefers a different layout, each user can set their preferences without interfering with the other.

### Limitations on Opportunity Card Fields

When customizing opportunity cards, there is a limit of seven fields that can be displayed on the opportunity card at a time.  
If you want to add more fields, you must first deselect or remove one of the existing fields.  
This ensures a clean and concise view of the most relevant data for each opportunity.

### FAQs




**Question:** What are custom fields in opportunities?
**Answer:** Custom fields allow you to add tailored data fields to opportunity cards, enabling you to track and organize information specific to your business needs.




**Question:** Can I edit standard fields in the opportunity cards?
**Answer:** No, standard fields are fixed and cannot be edited. However, you can create custom fields to capture additional data as required.




**Question:** How do I reorder custom fields in opportunity cards?
**Answer:** Navigate to **Settings** → **Custom Fields**, select **Opportunity** from the dropdown menu, and drag and drop fields to rearrange them in your preferred order.




**Question:** Can I group custom fields into folders for better organization?
**Answer:** Yes, you can categorize custom fields into folders. Use the “Add Folder” option, set the object to **Opportunity**, and save your folder for easy access.




**Question:** What is the "Hide Empty Fields" feature, and how does it work?
**Answer:** The "Hide Empty Fields" feature allows you to hide fields without data in opportunity cards, reducing visual clutter and ensuring a focused view of relevant information. You can enable this setting while editing an opportunity.




**Question:** Can I manage the data shown on my opportunity cards?
**Answer:** Yes, you can choose which fields to display on your opportunity cards by clicking "Manage Fields" and selecting or deselecting specific data points like opportunity value, source, and tasks.




**Question:** Are the customizations user-specific?
**Answer:** Yes, customizations made to the opportunity card fields apply only to the user who makes the changes. Other users can create their own customizations that won’t impact your settings.


**Question:Is there a limit to the number of fields I can show on an opportunity card?
**Answer:** Yes, you can display a maximum of seven fields per opportunity card. If you need to add more, you will need to remove one of the existing fields.




**Question:** Can I customize the fields displayed on opportunity cards at a user level?
**Answer:** Yes, each user can set up their view for opportunity cards independently. Customizations made through the "Manage Fields" section are user-specific, meaning one user's preferences won’t affect another user's setup unless explicitly shared.




**Question:** Can I customize the fields for all pipelines or just one?
**Answer:** You can choose to apply your field customizations to the current pipeline only or to **all pipelines**, depending on your preference when saving changes.




**Question:** Can I "Lock" the field order so my team can't change it?

**Answer:** The administrative order of fields (set in Settings) is the default for everyone. While individual users can choose which fields to _display_ on the card face, the actual structure and existence of the custom fields are controlled by Admins, ensuring data consistency across the company.




**Question:** What happens if I try to add an eighth field to my opportunity card display?

**Answer:** The system has a **limit of seven fields** for the card face. If you try to add an eighth, you will be prompted to deselect one of the currently visible fields first to maintain a clean and readable layout.
