In this article, you will learn more about how the opportunity tab works, and what you can do. Here is where you can see all your opportunities (which are your leads), and see in which stage of your pipeline they are right now. You can also see their information by clicking on their opportunity card. 

## Part 1: Filtering Opportunities

-   You can filter your opportunities by: Owner, Status, Campaign Type, Last Stage Change, Created on, Updated on, Lead Value, Opportunity Won On, and Opportunity Lost On
-   You can search for opportunities
-   You can select a pipeline
-   You can Sort by Date Added in an ascending/descending option 
-   You can select multiple filters by pipeline, owner, and campaign and toggle “AND” or “OR” relationships with them
-   With the menu dots, you can export and include additional information: Task, Note, Company Name, Calendar Event, Hide Tags, Hide Assigned To

## Settings for Decoupling Contact and Opportunity Owners

With this feature, you can have separate owners for contacts and opportunities in your system.

### How to Enable the Feature:

1.  Navigate to Settings > Opportunities & Pipeline.
2.  Locate the "Allow different owners for contacts and its opportunities" option.
3.  Toggle the setting to "On."

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcZ_MLRnZTsz-Qg4B0hALbHstpT9rUV9P82q0cKeZ_WiMbgpGEVLuygYxDsFyhZpsFF088HVuA205bOv0b51Wtae5f4nViMzN2ese1jCFx-oRYjtrwFPN0uUwA8zgML-PAZOrd4zg?key=NpuiKPQ6K5wnfj7GGKUTHA)

### Functionality:

**Default Opportunity Owner:** When creating a new opportunity, the default owner will be the same as the contact owner. However, you can update this as needed to reflect the appropriate ownership.

### Subsetting:

1.  **Allow updating the contact follower based on the opportunity owner change:** Enable this setting to automatically update the contact follower when the opportunity owner is changed.
2.  **Allow updating the opportunity follower based on the contact owner change:** Enable this setting to automatically update the opportunity follower when the contact owner is changed.

These settings also apply when opportunities and contacts are created or updated via automation and bulk actions.

### Important Notes:

-   Once the setting is enabled, existing automation that updates owners might not update the opportunity owner, so review and adjust all relevant automation accordingly.

**Additional Contacts:**

These additional contacts will also be displayed on their respective contact pages, making it easier to track all opportunities where they play a role.

### **Import Opportunities into Pipelines** 

You can import opportunities directly into your pipelines. If you've been managing your sales pipeline outside of , it's time to transition smoothly and upload your contacts with just a single click.

#### **Navigating to the Import Button**

1.  **Accessing Import Function:** Navigate to the opportunities section and click on the import button.
2.  **Sample Template:** Before importing, download a sample template to understand the required CSV structure.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764007983434-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-24 131255.png>)

#### **Preparing Your CSV File**

The sample sheet should include:

-   Opportunity ID
-   Opportunity Name
-   Contact ID
-   Phone
-   Email
-   Pipeline ID
-   Stage ID
-   Lead Value
-   Source
-   Assigned to Team Member (if any)
-   Followers
-   Notes
-   Tags
-   Status
-   Lost Reason

Once your data is prepared, download your file as a CSV.

#### **Uploading Your CSV File**

1.  **Upload File:** Click the import button and select the opportunity option. Drag and drop your CSV file into the upload area.

#### **Choosing Import Options**

You can choose from:

-   **Create Opportunities:** For moving from another system with no existing CRM data.
-   **Create and Update Opportunities:** To add new and update existing opportunities.
-   **Update Opportunities:** For updating existing opportunities only.

Select "Create Opportunities" for a brand new account and click next.

#### **Mapping Your Data**

-   **Automatic Mapping:** Some fields will automatically map with a green tick.
-   **Manual Mapping:** Manually map unmapped fields such as "Source" and "Status."
-   **Unmapped Columns:** Choose not to import unmapped columns if necessary.

#### **Review and Start Bulk Import**

1.  **Review Data:** Thoroughly check your data. Add tags for better tracking.
2.  **Start Import:** Click "Start Bulk Import." Monitor the process under bulk actions to check the status and handle any errors.

#### **Handling Errors and Validation**

If errors occur, the system will provide error messages and solutions. For example, invalid emails will need verification and updating in your CSV file. Export error details if needed for tracking purposes.

Please see the article “**How to import Opportunities using a single CSV file”** for more details.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** How can I filter opportunities in the Opportunities tab?
**Answer:** You can filter opportunities by various criteria, including Owner, Status, Campaign Type, Last Stage Change, Created On, Updated On, Lead Value, Opportunity Won On, and Opportunity Lost On. Additionally, you can select a pipeline, search for opportunities, and sort by Date Added in ascending or descending order. You can also combine multiple filters with "AND" or "OR" relationships for more refined searches.




**Question:** How do I add a new opportunity?
**Answer:** To add a new opportunity, click on the "+ Add opportunity" button located next to the filters in the Opportunities tab. You can also create a new contact when creating an opportunity. Ensure that you have the necessary settings enabled, such as decoupling contact and opportunity owners if needed.




**Question:** What is the purpose of decoupling contact and opportunity owners, and how do I enable it?
**Answer:** Decoupling contact and opportunity owners allows you to have separate owners for contacts and opportunities, providing greater flexibility in managing responsibilities. To enable this feature, navigate to **Settings > Opportunities & Pipeline**, and toggle the "Allow different owners for contacts and its opportunities" option to "On." This setting allows you to manage ownership changes separately for contacts and opportunities.




**Question:** How can I export opportunity information?
**Answer:** To export opportunity information, click on the menu dots and select the Export option. This will download a CSV file containing the filtered opportunities and any additional information you choose to include, such as tasks, calendar events, notes, and more. The export feature remembers your previous selections, making it easier to include the same details in future exports.




**Question:** What can I do within an Opportunity Card?
**Answer:** Within an Opportunity Card, you can view and manage contact information, such as name, email, phone, tags, and company name. You can also see details about the opportunity, including pipeline stage, status, lead value, owner, and source. Additional features include the ability to book or update appointments, add tasks and notes, manage opportunity custom fields, and add multiple contacts related to the opportunity. You can also use the checkbox to hide empty fields for a cleaner view.




**Question:** What should I do if there are errors during opportunity import?
**Answer:** The system will display error messages, such as invalid emails. You should correct these issues in your CSV file and re-import. Error details can be exported for tracking purposes.




**Question:** How do I hide the fields I’m not using on the Opportunity Card?

**Answer:** Use the **"Hide Empty Fields"** checkbox inside the card. This removes any visual clutter from fields that don't have data yet, allowing your team to focus only on the information that has been filled out.




**Question:** Can I connect more than one person to a single sales opportunity?

**Answer:** Yes. Using the **Additional Contacts** feature within the Opportunity Card, you can associate multiple individuals with a single lead. These opportunities will then also appear on the individual contact pages for every person linked.
