**How to Create and Edit Pipelines**

Pipelines are essential for tracking opportunities, managing customer journeys, and organizing sales processes. This article will guide you through creating, editing and giving, and restricting permissions for pipelines to streamline your business operations effectively.

Pipelines allow businesses to manage and visualize the different stages of their sales process, ensuring each lead is nurtured appropriately. By setting up and customizing pipelines, you can automate workflows, improve efficiency, and enhance your customer journey.

## **How to Create a Pipeline**

### **Step 1: Navigate to Pipelines**

-   **Go to Settings:** Access your account settings from the main menu.
-   **Select Pipelines:**
    -   Navigate to the Opportunities tab on the left and click on the "Pipelines" section on the top ribbon.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256932245-image.png)

### **Step 2: Create a New Pipeline**

-   **Click "Create Pipeline":** Start the creation process by clicking the "Create Pipeline" button.
-   **Name Your Pipeline:** Enter a descriptive name for your pipeline (e.g., "Main Sales Pipeline").
-   **Add Stages:** Define the stages of your pipeline by clicking "Add Stage" and providing a name for each stage (e.g., "New Lead", "Contacted", "Qualified"). You can delete a stage by simply clicking on the delete icon next to a stage.

Note: Pipelines are composed of multiple stages, each representing a step in the customer journey or sales process. Common stages include "Lead Capture," "Qualification," "Appointment Setting," "Follow-up," and "Closing" etc.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256918218-image.png)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1760987094139-create and edit pipeline 1.jpg>)

-   **Reorder Stages:**
    -   Drag and drop stages by clicking the hamburger icon on the left side of each stage to reorder them as needed.
-   **Hide or show in the funnel chart and stage distribution:** Click on the checks next to each stage to either hide or show them in the funnel chart and stage distribution.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256837799-image.png)

-   **Create the Pipeline:** Once all stages are added, click "Create" to create your pipeline.

## **How to Edit a Pipeline**

### **Step 1: Select the Pipeline to Edit**

-   **Go to Pipelines:** Navigate to the "Pipelines" section.
-   **Choose Pipeline:** Select the pipeline you want to edit from the list.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256729717-image.png)

### **Step 2: Edit Stages**

-   **Click "Edit":** Click the "Edit" button next to the pipeline name.
-   **Modify Stages:** Add, remove, or rename stages as required. You can also reorder stages by dragging them.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256711656-image.png)

Note: You can configure your pipeline to appear in the dashboard funnel and pie chart views. This gives you a visual representation of the stages and the overall flow of opportunities through the sales process.   

-   **Edit Permissions:** For that, you will need to select “Sharing & Permissions.” There you will be able edit the permissions for that pipeline.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256655917-image.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256676775-image.png)

-   **Save Changes:** Click "Save" to apply your changes.

### **Step 3: Customize Stage Actions**

1.  **Set Triggers and Actions:** For each stage, define what happens when an opportunity enters or exits that stage. This can include sending emails, updating records, or moving the opportunity to a different stage.
2.  **Automate Workflows:** Use automation tools to trigger actions based on stage changes, reducing manual intervention and ensuring consistency.

**Pro tip:** Regular maintenance is essential. Periodically review and update your pipelines to reflect changes in your business processes or customer preferences. Removing outdated stages and actions can streamline workflows and improve overall performance.

### Preventing Duplicate Pipeline and Stage Names

To improve data accuracy and streamline processes, the platform prevents the creation or addition of pipelines or stages with identical names.

-   **No Duplicate Names**: Users cannot create or add pipelines or stages with the same name.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256559097-image.png)

-   **Existing Pipelines and Stages**: Current pipelines and stages are unaffected by this change.
-   **Renaming Entries**: Renaming an existing pipeline or stage to a name that already exists will result in an error message.  


![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764256210713-image.png)

### Example Use Cases

-   **Creating a New Sales Pipeline**: If you have a sales pipeline named "Sales Q1" and attempt to create another pipeline with the same name, the system will prompt an error, ensuring each pipeline has a distinct name for better data organization.
-   **Renaming an Existing Stage**: If you try to rename an existing stage "Prospect" to "Lead," and a stage named "Lead" already exists, you will be prompted to select a different name to avoid duplicates.

## FAQs




**Question:** Can I have multiple pipelines for different products?
**Answer:** Yes, you can create separate pipelines for different products, services, or customer segments to manage them effectively.




**Question:** How do I handle duplicate opportunities?

**Answer:** You can configure your pipeline settings to allow or prevent duplicate opportunities, depending on your business needs.




**Question:** What happens when I change a pipeline stage?

**Answer:** Changing a pipeline stage can trigger specific actions such as sending emails, updating records, or moving the opportunity to another stage, depending on how you configure your automation.




**Question:** How do I maintain the effectiveness of my pipelines?
**Answer:** Regularly review and update your pipelines to ensure they reflect current business processes. Remove outdated stages and use analytics to identify areas for improvement.




**Question:** What insights can I get from analytics on my pipelines?
**Answer:** Analytics help you understand pipeline performance, identify bottlenecks, and optimize conversion rates. You can use these insights to make data-driven decisions that improve your customer experience and sales processes.




**Question:** Can I choose which stages to display in the dashboard funnel and pie charts?
**Answer:** Yes, you can configure which stages are displayed in the dashboard by toggling the funnel and pie chart icons next to each stage in the pipeline setup.




**Question:** Where are the permissions for pipelines recorded?
**Answer:** They are recorded under Audit Logs




**Question:** Can a user update opportunities in a pipeline they don't have access to?
**Answer:** No. Users can only view or update opportunities in pipelines where they have been granted permission.


**Question:  Can I assign pipelines to specific roles instead of individual users?
**Answer:** Yes. Permissions can be set at both the user and role level for easier management.




**Question:** What happens if I try to rename a pipeline stage to a name that is already in use?

**Answer:** The system will prevent the change and display an error message. To maintain data accuracy, duplicate names for pipelines or stages are not allowed.
