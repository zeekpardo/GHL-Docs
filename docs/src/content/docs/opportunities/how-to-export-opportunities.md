**3\. Exporting Opportunities**

Once you've set your desired filters (if any), click on the three dots> "Export" button, situated at the top right corner of the Opportunities section.

### **4\. Features and Benefits**

-   **Export of Custom Fields:** seamlessly integrates all custom fields associated with opportunities into the exported data. This means if you've created any custom fields tailored to your business needs, they will be included in your exported file.

-   **Filter-Based Export:** Instead of exporting all opportunities, respects the filters you've set. Only the opportunities that match your specified filters will be exported.

-   **No Limit on Opportunities Exported:** Previously, there might have been constraints on the number of opportunities you could export at once. But now, has removed all such limits. All visible opportunities on the board can be exported in one go.

### **5\. Wait and Download**

After clicking the "Export" button, a progress bar will appear, showing the status of your export. It's important to avoid reloading the page or navigating away from the "Opportunities" section during this process. Once the export is complete, you'll receive a downloadable file containing all the exported data.

**_Note on Permissions_**

_It's essential to note that to access and utilize the export feature, one needs admin permissions. Always ensure you have the required access rights before attempting to export data._

**FAQ**




**Question:** How can I access the "Opportunities" section for exporting data?

**Answer:** To access the "Opportunities" section, navigate to the sidebar menu in dashboard and select "Opportunities." This section lists all your business opportunities, including potential sales and leads.




**Question:** What should I do if I want to export only specific opportunities?

**Answer:** Use the filter options available in to narrow down the opportunities you want to export. Click on the filters or search bar, select your criteria (such as lead status or date range), and then proceed to export only the filtered opportunities.




**Question:** Are there any limitations on the number of opportunities I can export at once?

**Answer:** No, there are no longer any limits on the number of opportunities you can export in one go. You can now export all visible opportunities on your board without any constraints.




**Question:** What permissions do I need to use the export feature?

**Answer:** To utilize the export feature, you need to have admin permissions. Ensure that you have the required access rights before attempting to export any data from .


**Question:Where can I download the exported file?
**Answer:** After the export completes, a **download link** will appear. Click it to save the exported file to your device.




**Question:** How do I export opportunities from a specific date range?
**Answer:** Click **Add Filter** and select "Date Created" or "Date Updated." Once you define your start and end dates, the board will refresh. When you hit **Export**, only the deals from that specific window will be included in the file.




**Question:** Is it safe to browse other parts of the platform while my export is processing?

**Answer:** No. It is important to **avoid reloading the page or navigating away** from the Opportunities section while the progress bar is active. Staying on the page ensures the export completes successfully and generates your download link.




**Question:** Will my Opportunity Tags and Notes be included in the export?

**Answer:** Yes. The export is comprehensive and includes standard fields like Opportunity Name, Value, and Stage, as well as associated Tags and any Notes attached to the opportunity.
