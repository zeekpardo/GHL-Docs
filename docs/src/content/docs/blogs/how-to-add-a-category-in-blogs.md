Categorizing your blog posts is not only an important way for you to stay organized, but it can also help your readers find the posts and content they are looking for. Follow these simple steps to learn how to create and add categories to your Blog section:

## Navigation

-   Navigate to Sites on the left > Blogs at the top
-   On the Blogs page, you’ll see a gear icon in the top right corner.
-   Click on the gear, or Settings feature.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762961367811-1.png)

-   Go to Categories
-   Click “+Add new category” to create a new category

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762961449190-2.png)

-   Follow the instructions in the pop-up.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762961502288-3.png)

-   You can also view a list of existing categories - if you wish to edit or delete an existing Category

## **FAQ’s**




**Question:** Can I edit or delete existing categories?

**Answer:** Yes, you can view a list of your existing categories from the Categories section. From there, you can choose to edit or delete any category as needed.




**Question:** Where can I find the category settings for my blog?

**Answer:** You can find the category's settings by navigating to **Sites > Blogs** and clicking on the gear icon in the top right corner. The Categories section is located within the settings.




**Question:** What happens if I delete a category?

**Answer:** If you delete a category, the blog posts associated with that category may become uncategorized or may need to be reassigned to another category.




**Question:** Can I have multiple categories for a single blog post?

**Answer:** This depends on how your blog platform is set up. While some platforms allow multiple categories, others may only support one category per post. Check your blog settings to see if multiple categories are supported.




**Question:** How can I use categories to improve the visibility of my blog posts?

**Answer:** Well-organized categories make it easier for readers to find related content, increasing engagement and time spent on your site. Categories also help search engines understand your content better, which can improve SEO.




**Question:** Can I rearrange the order of my categories?

**Answer:** Some blog platforms allow you to reorder your categories based on priority or relevance. Check the Categories section in your settings to see if this option is available.




**Question:** How do I create a new category for my blog?

**Answer:** You can create a new category by navigating to Sites > Blogs, clicking on the gear icon in the top right corner, and then going to the Categories section. From there, click on the “+Add new category” button and follow the instructions in the pop-up to create your new category.




**Question:** Will adding categories affect existing blog URLs?
**Answer:** Usually, no categories are primarily for organization. However, if your blog’s URLs include category slugs, changing or deleting categories may impact URLs and SEO. Always check before making changes.




**Question:** Can I hide certain categories from the public?
**Answer:** A category only appears on your live site if it has at least one published post assigned to it. If you create a category but only have "Draft" posts inside it, the category will remain hidden from your visitors until you go live with the content.




**Question:** Is there a way to modify a category name after it has been created?

**Answer:** Yes. From the list of existing categories in the Settings menu, you have the option to **edit** any category if you need to update its name or details.




**Question:** If I create a new category, do I need to navigate to a different page to finish the setup?

**Answer:** No. After clicking "+Add new category," a **pop-up window** will appear with instructions, allowing you to complete the creation process without leaving the page.
