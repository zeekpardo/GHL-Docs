**How To Add Authors in Blogs**

Adding Authors to your Blog section allows you to appropriately give credit to your contributors. Follow these simple steps to learn how to add authors to your Blogs.

## Navigation

-   Navigate to Sites on the left > Blogs at the top
-   On the Blogs page, you’ll see a gear icon in the top right corner.
-   Click on the gear, or Settings feature.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763069726047-auth blog 1.jpg>)

-   Toggle to Authors
-   Click “+Add new author” to set up a new author

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763069785127-auth blog 2.jpg>)

-   Follow the instructions in the popup.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763069824486-auth blog 3.jpg>)

-   You can also view a list of existing authors - if you wish to edit or delete an existing one, click the pencil icon and the trash icon respectively and you can make changes from the menu that will pop up.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763069860584-auth blog 4.jpg>)

It’s as easy as that to add authors to your blog section, allowing you to give credit to those who create your content.

### FAQ 




**Question:** How do I add a new author to my blog?

**Answer:** To add a new author, navigate to the Blogs section, click the gear icon in the top right corner, toggle to Authors, and click “+Add new author.” Follow the instructions in the popup to set up the new author.




**Question:** Can I edit an existing author’s details?

**Answer:** Yes, you can edit an existing author’s details by clicking the pencil icon next to the author’s name in the list of authors. A menu will appear, allowing you to make changes.




**Question:** How do I delete an author from my blog section?

**Answer:** To delete an author, click the trash icon next to their name in the list of authors. A confirmation will appear, allowing you to remove the author from the list.




**Question:** Can I set author bios or profile pictures?

**Answer:** Yes, when adding or editing an author, you can usually include a bio, profile image, and other relevant details to showcase the contributor.




**Question:** Is there a limit to how many authors I can add?
**Answer:** No. Whether you have a single writer or a large team of fifty guest contributors, you can add as many authors as you need to support your content strategy.




**Question:** How do I manage my list of existing contributors?

**Answer:** The Authors tab provides a comprehensive list of all creators currently in your system. From this view, you can see everyone at a glance and choose to either update their information or remove them entirely.




**Question:** How do I assign a specific author to a blog post?

**Answer:** Once you have created your authors in the Settings menu, open any blog post in the editor. In the post settings sidebar, you will find a dropdown menu labeled "Author." Simply select the desired name from your pre-configured list before publishing or updating the post.
