**Content AI for Blogs**

Content AI is your assistant to generate your content 10X faster so that you can invest your efforts in other things. 

## **Overview**

**Content AI** can help you create blog posts or even write the full post for you.

-   **Outlines** - Let AI create the angle and structure of your blog post.
-   **Introduction** - AI can help you start your post with an opening paragraph or section.
-   **Full Sections** - AI can create full sections of copy under each headline.

The Content AI option will appear in the editor; the user must click on the button to activate it.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763754229822-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 144344.png>)

The Content AI model will ask for the following things:

-   **Brand Voice -** (Enabled only if you have Brand Voice)
-   **Type** - Outline, Introduction, and Section
-   **Product Title**
-   **Description**
-   **Keywords**
-   **Tone of writing**
-   **Variations**

Content AI will allow you to select the content of the example it gave you. Once ready, click on Continue.

Optionally, click on the Thumbs Up/Thumbs Down icons to give feedback. Your feedback helps us improve generation quality and tune the AI to match your expectations.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764176777242-image7.png)

**Please Note:** If you select multiple variations and the AI is only able to produce fewer images than desired, you will not be charged. 

**_Please Note:_** _Whenever the user has generated content, when they click on Content AI again it will retain the old content and the user can generate new content for the outline, introduction, and specific section by clicking “Generate new content”_

### Using Brand Voice with Content AI:

**Brand Voice Toggle**: Easily enable/disable **Brand Voice** within the **Content AI modal**. It’s **ON by default** if a Brand Voice exists.  

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763754356696-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 144550.png>)

**Smart Autofill**: When **Brand Voice is ON**, the **Writing Tone** field is automatically filled based on your selected Brand Voice.  

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763754378063-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 144613.png>)

**Metadata Shaping**: **Target Audience**, **CTA**, **Brand Purpose**, **Values**, and **Promise** silently influence the AI prompt behind the scenes.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763754396742-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 144632.png>)

**Brand Voice Selector Dropdown**: Choose your preferred Brand Voice from saved options, with your default pre-selected.

## FAQs




**Question:** What is Content AI, and how does it help with content creation?
**Answer:** Content AI is a tool designed to assist you in generating content up to 10 times faster, allowing you to focus your efforts on other tasks. It can help create blog posts, outlines, introductions, and full sections of content, making the writing process more efficient.




**Question:** What options does Content AI provide for creating content?
**Answer:** Content AI offers several options for creating content:

-   **Outlines:** Generates the angle and structure for your blog post.
-   **Introduction:** This helps you start your post with an opening paragraph or section.
-   **Full Sections:** Creates complete sections of copy under each headline.




**Question:** How do I activate Content AI in the editor?
**Answer:** To activate Content AI, click on the Content AI button that appears in the editor. This will open the model where you can input your content requirements.




**Question:** What information does Content AI require to generate content?
**Answer:** Content AI requires the following inputs:

-   **Type:** Choose whether you need an Outline, Introduction, or Section.
-   **Product Title:** Provide the title related to your content.
-   **Description:** A brief description of the content or product.
-   **Keywords:** Key terms to guide the AI in generating relevant content.
-   **The tone of Writing:** Specify the tone, such as casual, formal, or friendly.
-   **Variations:** The number of content variations you would like to generate.




**Question:** Can I generate multiple variations of content, and what happens if fewer variations are produced?
**Answer:** Yes, you can request multiple variations of content. If the AI produces fewer variations than requested, you will not be charged for the missing variations.




**Question:** How can I update or generate new content for my blog post using Content AI?
**Answer:** After generating content, if you wish to update or create new content, click on the Content AI button again. The previous content will be retained, and you can generate new content for outlines, introductions, or specific sections by selecting “Generate new content.”




**Question:** Can I use Content AI to write entire blog posts?
**Answer:** Yes, Content AI can assist in writing entire blog posts by generating outlines, introductions, and full sections, allowing you to assemble a complete post efficiently.




**Question:** What happens if I want to regenerate specific sections of my content?
**Answer:** You can regenerate specific sections of your content by clicking on the Content AI button and selecting the section you want to update. Then, choose “Generate new content” to refresh that section.




**Question:** Does Content AI retain the previously generated content?
**Answer:** Yes, Content AI retains previously generated content when you click on it again. This allows you to compare or continue refining your content without losing any progress.




**Question:** How does Content AI determine the tone of the content?
**Answer:** Content AI uses the tone specified in your input, such as casual, formal, professional, or friendly, to generate content that matches your desired style and engages your target audience appropriately.




**Question:** How can I manage the Content AI and view its history?
**Answer:** To manage the Content AI and view its history, navigate to the **Marketing** section on the left and click on the **Content AI** tab at the top.




**Question:** How do I use Brand Voice with Content AI?

**Answer:** When you have a Brand Voice set up, you can enable it within the Content AI modal using the Brand Voice Toggle. By default, if you have a Brand Voice, it will be turned on. The Writing Tone field will be automatically filled based on your selected Brand Voice. Additionally, the AI's prompt is influenced by your Brand Voice settings, including your Target Audience, CTA, Brand Purpose, Values, and Promise.  




**Question:** How can I provide feedback on the content generated by the AI?
**Answer:** You can give feedback by clicking on the Thumbs Up or Thumbs Down icons within the tool. Providing this feedback helps improve the overall quality of future generations and tunes the AI to better match your specific expectations.




**Question:** If I leave the "From Name" empty but fill in the "From Email," what will the recipient see?

**Answer:** If the "From Name" is empty, many email clients will simply display the first part of your email address (the part before the @) or show "Unknown Sender." For the best engagement, always provide a clear From Name, such as "Sara from \[CompanyName\]."




**Question:** What does the "Smart Autofill" feature do when using Brand Voice?

**Answer:** When the Brand Voice toggle is ON, Content AI automatically fills the "Writing Tone" field based on your specific Brand Voice profile. This ensures your content remains consistent with your established brand identity without you having to manually type in the tone every time.




**Question:** Can I turn off the Brand Voice feature if I want to write in a completely different style for one post?

**Answer:** Yes. There is a **Brand Voice Toggle** inside the Content AI modal that allows you to easily enable or disable the feature. If you turn it off, you can manually enter a different "Tone of Writing."
