Keeping your blog content optimized for search engines is crucial for increasing visibility and driving traffic to your site. Enhancing SEO in the blog builder has become even easier and more efficient. This article will guide you through the key features and steps you need to follow to ensure your blog posts are fully optimized for search engines like Google.

## **How to Use SEO Features**

### **1\. Simplify Your URLs**

When creating a new blog post, ensure that your URLs are short and contain relevant keywords. Avoid using unnecessary characters or long strings of text in the URL. The update automatically simplifies URLs, but you can further adjust them for maximum SEO benefit.

**Steps:**

-   After creating your blog post, navigate to the URL section.
-   Edit the URL to remove any irrelevant words or characters.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761770719296-image9.jpg)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761770800972-image2.jpg)

-   Ensure the URL includes primary keywords related to the content.

### **2\. Automate Sitemap Submission**

The blog builder now automatically submits your updated or new blog posts to search engines. This feature ensures that your content is indexed promptly, improving your chances of ranking higher in search results.

**Steps:**

-   Once you publish or update a blog post, the sitemap is automatically updated.
-   No further action is needed on your part, making the process seamless and efficient.

### **3\. Customize Canonical URLs**

Canonical URLs are important for preventing duplicate content issues. By setting a canonical URL, you tell search engines which version of a page you want to be indexed.

**Steps:**

-   In the blog post settings, find the option to set a canonical URL.
-   Enter the URL you want search engines to prioritize.
-   Save your settings to apply the canonical URL to your blog post.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXf2c19KhfZMN02ZA2mz0_ymIIaCmJ7CI1pL311ic7B0FXy_GGFzr3j4UBpjfzCNX_gJ7PijLCgXvCxoeML26PBFf7R4Mj3Pqnm-0pEfnFYTq2kfNBy5E3zPhAkGjaCC0Pe-63bEUQ?key=3FHppw5-jft4eTuBMYA3VA)

**Dynamic Custom Values for Canonical URLs:**You can also define canonical URLs dynamically using custom values. This ensures that canonical links remain consistent and accurate even as your blog content evolves.

**Steps:**

1- Navigate to the settings menu and create custom values under the "Custom Values" tab.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761771206565-image5.jpg)

2- Use braces ({{custom\_value}}) to dynamically insert custom values for categories, authors, or other blog attributes.

3- Save your settings and the canonical URLs will adapt automatically.

### **4\. Optimize Meta Data**

SEO metadata, including titles, descriptions, and keywords, plays a crucial role in how your content is indexed and ranked by search engines. The blog builder allows you to easily manage this metadata for each blog post.

**Steps:**

-   Create a new blog post (if you haven’t already) and add a title, description, and image.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761771365893-image4.jpg)

-   After writing your blog post, navigate to the metadata section.
-   Add a relevant title that includes your primary keywords.
-   Write a concise and keyword-rich description.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761776219949-image10.jpg)

-   Enter keywords separated by commas to target specific search terms.

## **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Simplified URLs:** The update simplifies URLs by removing unnecessary characters, making them cleaner and more SEO-friendly.
-   **Automated Sitemap Submission:** Your blog posts are now automatically submitted to search engines whenever they are published or updated, eliminating the need for manual resubmission.
-   **Customizable Canonical URLs:** You can now specify canonical URLs, allowing you to tell search engines which version of a page should be indexed, helping to prevent duplicate content issues.
-   **Enhanced Blog Layout and Design:** The new customization options allow you to create different layouts and designs, improving user experience and SEO performance.
-   **Meta Data Management:** The blog builder provides tools for adding SEO metadata, including titles, descriptions, and keywords, ensuring that your content is optimized for search engines from the start.

## **Pro Tips**

-   **Regularly Update Old Content:** Search engines favor fresh content. Regularly updating older blog posts with new information or SEO improvements can boost your rankings.
-   **Use Long-Tail Keywords:** Incorporating long-tail keywords in your metadata and content can help attract more targeted traffic.
-   **Optimize Images:** Ensure your images have descriptive alt text and are compressed for faster load times, which can improve your SEO.

## FAQs




**Question:** How can I ensure my blog post is optimized for mobile devices?
**Answer:** Use the mobile preview option in the blog site editor to check how your blog appears on different devices. Adjust the layout, font sizes, and image placements to ensure a seamless mobile experience.




**Question:** What should I do if my old blog links break after migrating to the new builder?
**Answer:** If you migrate old blogs to the new builder, update any external backlinks to point to the new URLs. This prevents loss of traffic and maintains your SEO rankings.




**Question:** How often should I update the metadata for my blog posts?
**Answer:** It’s a good practice to review and update your metadata periodically, especially if there are changes in your content strategy or keyword focus.




**Question:** How do dynamic custom values improve canonical URLs?
**Answer:** Dynamic custom values allow canonical URLs to adapt automatically to changes in categories, authors, or other blog attributes. This ensures consistency and minimizes manual updates, supporting better SEO.




**Question:** How does the blog layout affect SEO?

**Answer:** A clean, mobile-friendly layout improves readability, engagement, and user experience, all factors that contribute to better search visibility.




**Question:** Do I need to manually submit my sitemap to Google every time I publish a new post?

**Answer:** No. The blog builder includes an automated sitemap submission feature. Once you publish or update a blog post, the sitemap is automatically updated and submitted to search engines without any further action required on your part.




**Question:** How can I keep my search rankings high for older blog posts?

**Answer:** Regularly update your old content with fresh information, new links, or improved metadata. Search engines favor "freshness," and a quick update can often boost a post back up in the search results.




**Question:** What is a "Long-Tail Keyword," and why should I use it?

**Answer:** Long-tail keywords are longer, more specific phrases that users search for. Using them in your metadata and content helps attract more targeted, high-intent traffic to your blog.
