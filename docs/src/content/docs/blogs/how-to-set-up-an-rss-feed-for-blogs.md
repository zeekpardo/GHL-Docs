RSS feeds are an easy way for you to keep up with news and information that's important to you and help you avoid the conventional methods of browsing or searching for information on websites. The URL within blog settings provides the ability to have category feeds for blogs.

Once you have set up your categories and added the blog list element to the desired site builders, you will retrieve the RSS Feed URL. See our other articles on blogs for more information.

## **How to get started with RSS Feeds for Blogs**

1\. Navigate to Sites on the left > Blogs at the top.

2\. Once Inside Blogs, go to the Settings (gear icon) of Blogs.

3\. After going to Settings, there will be an option to go to the 'RSS' tab.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeBWXz2NHDubtC7z8T5KaRwAdU4Z6fF28CkzTc5Lrq3oi_OpB6ktX7wYzS1KbsdKNYB84iJkCzdMN4ouUz2aSxwU85OW23iE91hNEV0BHk3w5FIQ82b0r2jwSVO4MDGDBEosnP4?key=lW05WA-kr1xmmPvS8sij9Q)

4\. Select the option to add the feed name and choose the blog (ensure you select blogs that are live, not in draft).

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfOPkwRYKPkucCI0gOLCviiUqOG8BYbPoVUVoHFD3yfkxY3-SBN9hFOSh_l1EafsvdEMsbiMH1rmbJEaUlbHImyYPcCgMS5hWq1GZO0kJ-hhhGpRb4wItBclShi6XB-ICd26JqVEw?key=lW05WA-kr1xmmPvS8sij9Q)

5\. You can control how many blog posts you would like included in your RSS Feed in the RSS tab. You can adjust the number accordingly for more or fewer posts.

**Please Note:**

The maximum number of allowed blog posts will be 100.

6\. Toggle the option to enable or disable full content in the RSS feed.

7\. Click "Add" to save the details.

## **FAQ’s**




**Question:** How do I get started with RSS feeds for blogs?
**Answer:** To get started, navigate to Sites > Blogs, then go to the Settings (gear icon) of Blogs. From there, you can access the RSS tab to configure your feed.




**Question:** Can I select which blogs are included in the RSS feed?
**Answer:** Yes, you can choose which blogs to include in the feed. Ensure you select blogs that are live, not in draft mode.




**Question:** How many blog posts can I include in my RSS feed?
**Answer:** You can include up to 100 blog posts in your RSS feed. Adjust the number according to your needs in the RSS tab.




**Question:** Can I control the content in my RSS feed?
**Answer:** Yes, you can toggle the option to enable or disable full content in the RSS feed based on your preferences.




**Question:** Can I adjust the number of blog posts in my RSS feed?
**Answer:** Yes, you can control how many blog posts to include in your RSS feed by adjusting the number in the RSS tab. The maximum number of allowed blog posts is 100.




**Question:** Do draft blogs appear in the RSS feed?
**Answer:** No, only blogs that are **published and live** will appear in your RSS feed. Draft blogs are excluded.




**Question:** Can I have different RSS feeds for different blog categories?

**Answer:** Yes. The system allows you to generate category-specific feeds. This is extremely useful if you have a multi-topic blog (e.g., "Marketing Tips" and "Product Updates") and want to allow users to subscribe only to the topics that interest them.




**Question:** Where do I find the specific URL for my RSS feed?

**Answer:** After setting up your categories and adding the blog list element to your site builder, the RSS Feed URL becomes available. You can retrieve this link to share with your audience or plug into feed readers.




**Question:** Why is my RSS feed showing old posts instead of the newest ones?

**Answer:** This is usually due to "caching" in the RSS reader app or browser you are using. To verify the live feed, try opening the RSS URL in an Incognito/Private window. If the new posts appear there, the feed is working correctly, and the reader app simply needs time to refresh.
