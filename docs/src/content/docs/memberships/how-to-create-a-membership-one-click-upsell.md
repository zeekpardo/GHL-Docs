In the Membership section, you can customize settings and features to improve the user experience. You can also increase revenue by adding upsell offers at checkout, giving users extra options while they’re already completing a purchase.

**What is an upsell?**  
An upsell lets you offer an additional product or service right after a customer completes a purchase. They can choose to accept or decline the offer, and if accepted, they will be redirected to the checkout page for the upsell.

## Follow these steps to create and use a membership upsell:

### Part 1: Creating an Upsell Offer

**Step 1: Create An Upsell Offer**

-   Create an offer to use as your upsell. Go to Memberships on the left > Courses at the top > From the dropdown select Offers

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389316265-unnamed \(38\).jpg>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389324828-unnamed \(39\).jpg>)

-   Save it and name it “Upsell” or “Upsell Offer” (or something similar) so you can easily find it in Step 2.

To learn more about how to build an offer, check out our article **“How to Build and Edit an Offer in Memberships”**

**Step 2: Add the Upsell to your Existing Offer**

-   Navigate to the offer you wish to add the upsell to.
-   Navigate to the Upsell section and click the "Add Upsell Offer" button.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389347929-unnamed \(40\).jpg>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389361803-unnamed \(41\).jpg>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389374449-unnamed \(42\).jpg>)

-   From the dropdown, choose the Offer you want to use as the upsell (this would be the upsell offer you created in Step 1).

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389391362-unnamed \(43\).jpg>)

-   Customize the Upsell page as you wish.
-   Activate and save your changes.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389409952-unnamed \(44\).jpg>)

### Part 2: Customizing, Editing, or Removing Your Upsell Checkout Page

**Step 1: Customizing or Editing an Upsell page** 

-   Click the customization/edit (pencil icon) button from your offer list to access your Upsell Offer.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389429675-unnamed \(45\).jpg>)

-   Make any edits or changes you wish.
-   You can apply any customizations you wish on the Checkout page by using the menu on the left-hand side dropdowns.
-   Save to confirm your changes.

**_NOTE:_** If you’re already within the offer that includes an Upsell, you can navigate to the Upsell section and edit the Upsell from within the Upsell tab. Click the edit (paint palette) icon to edit the upsell offer that’s already added to your Membership offer.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389455809-unnamed \(46\).jpg>)

**Step 2: Removing an Upsell Page**

-   Navigate to Offers.
-   Click on the Offer you wish to remove an Upsell for.
-   Click on the Upsell section.
-   Click on the the pencil icon to delete the offer
-   Scroll down and click "delete" to remove the Upsell offer.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389476545-unnamed \(47\).jpg>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389491764-unnamed \(48\).jpg>)

### Part 3: Previewing Your Upsell Page

-   Click the preview (eyeball)  button from your offer list. This will take you to the Offer Builder.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389523072-unnamed \(49\).jpg>)

-   This will open a new page allowing you to preview what the page looks like for your users.

### FAQs




**Question:** What is an upsell offer?
**Answer:** An upsell offer is an additional product or service presented to customers immediately after their initial purchase. They can choose to accept or decline this offer. If accepted, they are redirected to the checkout page for the upsell.




**Question:** How do I create an upsell offer?
**Answer:** To create an upsell offer, go to Memberships > Courses > Offers, create and save your offer as "Upsell" or similar. Then, add it to your existing offer by navigating to the Upsell section and selecting it from the dropdown menu.




**Question:** How can I customize or edit my upsell page?
**Answer:** Click the customization/edit (pencil) icon from your offer list to access and make changes to your upsell page. Customize using the left-hand dropdown menu and save your updates.




**Question:** How do I remove an upsell offer?
**Answer:** To remove an upsell, go to Offers, select the offer, navigate to the Upsell section, click the three dots icon next to the upsell you want to delete and select "Delete."




**Question:** Will adding an upsell disrupt the original checkout flow?
**Answer:** No. The upsell is presented immediately after the initial purchase and is seamlessly integrated into the checkout process.  




**Question:** Can I edit the upsell offer directly from within the main membership offer?

**Answer:** Yes. If you are already inside the main offer that includes an upsell, you can navigate to the Upsell tab and click the edit (paint palette) icon to modify the upsell offer without having to go back to the main offer list.




**Question:** Can I add more than one upsell to a single Membership Offer?

**Answer:** The current membership checkout flow supports a primary upsell offer directly following the initial purchase. If you need a multi-step "Upsell Path," you may consider using a dedicated **Funnel** with multiple OTO (One-Time Offer) steps, which can then grant access to various membership products.
