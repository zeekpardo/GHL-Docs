Within the Membership section,  you can create posts under your categories. Follow these simple steps to learn how!

-   Navigate to Memberships > Products > All Products.
-   Click into a product, and under your category, you can manage your post(s).

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/3YYY0XZu5XjwE9TU4w7rXz71zIjPBZxL35iBo5BUSKB95wnoij2RU2Hpc1YQMhsfLkr05nWoX9-6j716stiPFHEYuOX2P3kV5LvUBkjWLU9SuBtTHk5NhcqZlmxq1_lQUARX-3I=s0)

-   Click the Edit icon next to the “blank post” placeholder.
-   In the new window, you give the post a title, add any attachments, insert body text, and add a thumbnail image. 

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/jTbCQzp5ANAhirPBEDV_NjYmunsiQsFQIcpIdIt9sO0dO4KuLyZ3bZZiEJOyi7poRsyk9CleDcbyaa4EfjqxX9F5jXeZYuEVgEWFu9l8gf-2T5TNWZnybJimQVV_N5cYJFCfDgo=s0)

-   Click Save to confirm your changes.

-   Repeat this process for any additional posts you wish to add.
