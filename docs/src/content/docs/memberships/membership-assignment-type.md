Membership products have an assessment type called assignments; these are also known as Offline Quizzes, Homework, Paragraph Quiz, and Subjective Quiz. 

This Assignment type can be used to create offline Assignments, which allow course creators to upload a questionnaire with instructions and manually grade them. The student can write their answers in a paragraph area online or upload answer sheets.

**Key Features:**

-   **Quiz Explanations:** Students can view detailed explanations for quiz answers, helping them learn from mistakes and prepare for future attempts.
-   **Single Choice vs. MCQ Tags:** Clear labels for single choice and multiple choice (MCQ) questions eliminate confusion, so students know what type of question they’re answering.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389714714-unnamed - 2026-01-14T192145.028.png>)

-   **Course Progress Bar:** A progress bar on the Courses screen helps students track their learning journey, showing how much they’ve completed and what’s left.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389782250-unnamed \(50\).jpg>)

-   **Locked & Drip Lesson Indicators:** More distinct indicators for locked and drip lessons make it easy for students to identify which sections are accessible or need to be unlocked.
-   **Enhanced Status Pages for Quizzes & Assignments:** Status pages display results like "In Review," "Pass," or "Fail," along with instructor notes for additional guidance.

-   **Full Quiz Review:** After completing a quiz, students can review all attempted questions, correct answers, and their total score, providing comprehensive feedback for improvement.

### Step 1: Create an assignment post

-   Within a product, you would click on the plus sign next to a category and select Add Assignment

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389885666-unnamed \(52\).jpg>)

-   This will pull up a menu where you can choose the Title

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389904067-unnamed \(53\).jpg>)

### Step 2: Fill in the details

-   Add visual appeal to your course assignment by incorporating thumbnails for quizzes and assignments.
-   Fill in the instructions, upload the template for the assignment, and upload the required files

**Note:** Supported Files: jpeg, jpg, pdf, xls

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389927925-unnamed \(54\).jpg>)

### Step 3: Preview the Assignment

-   Click on the preview button to view the assignment draft where you can start the assignment as if you were a member of the course

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389946796-unnamed \(55\).jpg>)

-   The contact would upload the assignment and provide answers in the answer box.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768389973108-unnamed \(56\).jpg>)

### Step 4: Publish the Assignment

-   Click on the Draft Button and Publish it

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768390004585-unnamed \(57\).jpg>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768390018905-unnamed \(58\).jpg>)

### FAQs

**What file types can be uploaded for assignments?**
**Answer:** You can upload files in formats such as jpeg, jpg, pdf, and xls.

**Can I preview the assignment before publishing it?**
**Answer:** Yes, you can use the "Preview" button to see a draft of the assignment before it goes live.

**How do students submit their assignments?**
**Answer:** Students can submit their assignments by uploading answer sheets or writing responses in the provided text box.

**Is the assignment interface accessible on mobile devices?**
**Answer:** Yes, the assignment interface is optimized for mobile browsers and PWA for easy access and submission.




**Question:** Can I make changes to the assignment after it is published?
**Answer:** Yes, you can update and republish the assignment as needed.  




**Question:** Can I edit an assignment after it’s published?
**Answer:** Yes. You can make updates to the assignment content and **republish** it at any time.




**Question:** Where can I learn more about creating membership courses?
**Answer:** For additional guidance, refer to our other articles on **creating membership courses**.




**Question:** Can I provide feedback without giving a final "Pass" or "Fail" grade?

**Answer:** **Yes.** The "In Review" status allows you to leave instructor notes and feedback. If a student needs to make corrections, you can use the notes to guide them and leave the assignment in a pending state until they upload a revised version.
