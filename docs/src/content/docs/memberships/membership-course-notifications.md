**Membership Course Notifications**

Stay connected with your courses through our notification system. You will receive updates for various events, including comments, unlocked content, timely access to drip content, and interactions with instructors.

Stay Connected with Comments

**Receive Instant Notifications:**

Never miss a beat in your learning journey! You will receive instant notifications whenever someone comments on a course you're enrolled in or replies to one of your comments. This ensures you're always in the loop and can participate in discussions promptly.

**Facilitate Smoother Interactions**

This feature is all about fostering a vibrant learning community. By keeping you informed of new comments and replies, interactions within courses become more dynamic and engaging, enhancing your learning experience.

Unlocked Content Notifications

**Never Miss Out on New Content**

When a post or category that was previously locked becomes accessible, learners will receive a notification right away. This means you're always updated on the latest content available to you, ensuring you don't miss out on valuable learning opportunities.

Timely Access to Drip Content

**Stay Informed on Content Releases**

Drip content helps pace your learning, but waiting for it without knowing when it’ll be available can be frustrating. With course notifications, learners will be notified the moment new drip content is released in their courses, allowing you to access fresh materials right when they're available.

Enabling Instructor-Learner Interactions

**Enhanced Communication with Instructors**

Instructors will receive notifications for learner comments, making it easier for them to respond and engage. This fosters a better connection and communication flow between learners and instructors.


**FAQs**




**Question:** What specific events will trigger a notification for me as a learner?

**Answer:** You will receive notifications for four main events: when a new comment is posted on a course you’re in, when someone replies to your comment, when previously locked content becomes accessible, and when "drip" content is released.




**Question:** How do notifications help with "Drip" content schedules?

**Answer:** Drip content is released on a set schedule to pace your learning. Instead of manually checking the app to see if new lessons are open, the system sends a notification the moment the material becomes available, ensuring you can start your next lesson immediately  




**Question:** Where will I see these notifications?

**Answer:** Notifications are typically delivered in two ways: via an **In-App Notification center** (usually a bell icon) and through **Email**. Depending on your account settings, you may also receive push notifications if you have the mobile app installed and active.




**Question:** Do instructors also benefit from this notification system?

**Answer:** Yes. Instructors receive notifications for **learner comments**, which makes it much easier for them to respond quickly and stay engaged with their students' progress and questions
