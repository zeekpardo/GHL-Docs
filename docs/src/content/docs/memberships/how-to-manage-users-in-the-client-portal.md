In this guide, you'll learn how to manage users within the client portal using features that make setting passwords, generating login links, and inviting users easier than ever. Whether you’re managing a course, a community, or an affiliate program, this guide will walk you through every step with clear instructions and helpful tips.

### Key Features of the Client Portal

1.  **Client Portal URL**:  
    This is the primary link to your client portal. You can customize this URL based on your domain settings and share it with your users to access the portal.
2.  **Invited Users vs. Joined Users**:
    -   **Invited Users**: These are individuals you have invited to join the client portal but have not yet accepted.
    -   **Joined Users**: These are users who have successfully joined the client portal.

### How to Use Magic Links

Magic links are unique, one-click login links that allow users to access the client portal without needing a password. These links enhance the user experience by providing quick and secure access.

**How to Generate Magic Links:**

1.  **Access the Client Portal Dashboard**: Navigate to the Sites and then Client Portal dashboard where you manage your users.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcs-bCRs7qfy-EjupWkrlSQuesa3XSAQ824LNqvWhAXyFmCBknsZ0ZnTV8796Is66itCzcXoNmUl05P83BtjboFLkjFjkvqt0KmgHWY6lL7iIYUGzsv2LWzhVhgDUJK_mA4OZ8_uA?key=6FMkPiuTfPFP6FZd25QUhQ)

1.  **Select a User**: Click on the desired user from the list.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfDowNWjZ9WOzXVHHoCURegRB-9_IXmul7A7WtPuc5SQEJaFS-rLtQBoGQMg4FJfNsNKYCOEnOVJyoTg5qb1Mh5kUpi_a6RL960wh3s-e7C26Dh44ua7UoBKp7WqvufQQ5KFmoHLBZZpJDNp8VXoBSanwJ9?key=6FMkPiuTfPFP6FZd25QUhQ)

1.  **Generate Magic Link**: Click on "Generate Magic Link" and choose to send the link via email or copy it directly.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfEm5wuTtGTdmXh1nSL3U_HaSaXnYipGYoVoQE59AxJ0zPqegc9-WAlr1SY32VHVAmPep6RefNM2IryE5pGQPFMymbFRQs10BVuLMt1_XxWW6WAluaNOG_QvQuilkpq9xJmVA-PA5N-M-dyaTgR23KasLI?key=6FMkPiuTfPFP6FZd25QUhQ)

**_Note:_**

_You have the option to send magic links directly via email, providing users with immediate access to the client portal. Additionally, magic links can be generated for specific sections of the portal, such as the courses, affiliates, or community sections. This allows you to tailor the access experience for users, directing them precisely where they need to go within the portal._

_![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXehZLBjTWXv-Ka5N99uAjq932Yg-OVd6MsCPqb-mIrX8JFDuLIa5UUgkRlEnFrjqizSD-iIbXjqN6yF-XAcjZo39gTRrCsKgY0sJHuGPy32aHZrb_yw63RM__f1TnbVTie0lrNvx7kBAZqU48sINqmkeyn6?key=6FMkPiuTfPFP6FZd25QUhQ)_

1.  **Use Magic Links in Automation**: If using automation, like email campaigns or workflows, use the custom values available to auto-generate and send magic links tailored to each user. Simply navigate to automations>workflows>create/edit a workflow.
2.  **Global Magic Links:**

Global Magic Links allows creators to add contact-level login links to membership portals, giving them more flexibility. These links are no longer dependent on course-specific triggers, enabling seamless integration across all template structures.

           **Integration Areas:**

Global Magic Links are integrated with workflow email actions, workflows, custom email templates, and smart list conversations, providing seamless automation and communication within the portal.

**For workflows**, navigate to Account → Automation → Workflows → Create New → Email Action → Select from Client Portal Contact Custom Values.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771422263795-image11.png)

**For email templates**, navigate to Account → Marketing → Emails → Templates → Select from Membership Contacts Custom Values.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771422303110-image13.png)

**For contact emails**, navigate to Account → Contacts → Contact Details → Email → Select from Client Portal Contact Custom Values.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1771422323642-image5.png)

**_Note:_**

_When sending an SMS in a blank workflow, the login URL for a magic link may not appear by default. This occurs because the system requires confirmation that the user is associated with the client portal. To ensure the login URL field is available, use a trigger related to the client portal, such as 'Offer Access Granted.' This action informs the system of the user’s involvement with the portal, enabling the proper generation of the magic link._

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764770118286-image11.png)

**_Special Note:_**_Magic links are created at the contact level. Ensure the correct contact is selected when generating these links._

### How to Invite Users to the Client Portal

Inviting users to the client portal is a simple way to onboard new contacts or give access to specific sections of your portal.

**Steps to Invite Users:**

1.  **Navigate to the Invite Action**: In the client portal dashboard, click on the "Invite" button.
2.  **Choose or Enter Contact**: Select an existing contact or enter a new email address.
3.  **Send Invite**: The invitation will be sent from your default domain, and the user will receive an email to join the client portal.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd7306JNuZuLFnv7DzdkSuQJmHl-BfKJ_0TXIy1k8R3UDgUGcyfGJaEwas3VtX8D6AB6QPq6Wwn2-lZQ2c2-HW9JbxS2ef-ZNsYEbf9Q0NzMtO8Mcc6G7eA5cFqZA29i3Q0d01-vSa-RI6mJzNgj7VX2wA?key=6FMkPiuTfPFP6FZd25QUhQ)

**Tip:**You can invite multiple users at once by selecting several contacts and sending invitations in bulk.

### How to Send Login Emails

Login emails provide a quick and straightforward way for users to access the client portal via a login button embedded in the email.

**Steps to Send Login Emails:**

1.  **Select Users**: In your client portal, choose the contacts you want to send login emails to.
2.  **Send Login Email**: Click on "Send Login Email." The selected users will receive an email with a login button, allowing them to access the portal without entering a password.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdfu4aQphoVhbAE-2VP9DbNruEUabCxxvFt16S0FpVX9hCilNiotcdS3NdKCgEq2pTH4uGo6lKxzrQNQUAT1t7pqZ4vcsqsKXbxnUTEQXiqLfX-HechMzeNwxDQuieZAkqBjvJWySPO3mhv6evpG7xoQRcA?key=6FMkPiuTfPFP6FZd25QUhQ)

**Note:**Login emails can be sent to multiple contacts simultaneously, streamlining the onboarding process.

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I generate a magic link for a specific section of the client portal?
**Answer:** To generate a magic link for a specific section, like courses or communities, go to the client portal dashboard, select the user, and choose the desired section when generating the link.




**Question:** Can I automate the sending of magic links through workflows?
**Answer:** Yes, magic links can be sent automatically via workflows, emails, or SMS by using custom fields for the login URL available in your ’s automation settings.




**Question:** What should I do if a user can’t access the client portal through the magic link?
**Answer:** Ensure the magic link was correctly generated and sent to the right contact. If issues persist, check the user’s contact information and regenerate the link.




**Question:** How can I manage password resets for users in the client portal?
**Answer:** Users can reset their passwords through the client portal login screen by selecting "Forgot Password." They will receive a reset link via email to set a new password.




**Question:** Is it possible to invite multiple users to the client portal at once?
**Answer:** Yes, you can select multiple contacts in the client portal dashboard and use the invite action to send out invitations in bulk, simplifying the onboarding process.




**Question:** Why isn't the magic link login URL appearing when I try to send an SMS in a workflow?
**Answer:** This usually happens because the system needs confirmation that the user is actually associated with the client portal. To fix this, ensure your workflow uses a trigger related to the client portal, such as "Offer Access Granted." This informs the system of the user's involvement and enables the magic link field to be generated.




**Question:** What is the difference between an "Invited User" and a "Joined User" in the dashboard?

**Answer:** **Invited Users** are individuals you have sent an invitation to who have not yet accepted or logged in. **Joined Users** are those who have successfully completed the onboarding process and accessed the portal.
