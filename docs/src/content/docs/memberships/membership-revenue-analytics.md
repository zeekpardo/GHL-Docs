**Membership - Revenue Analytics**

Revenue Analytics, a sub-feature of Analytics accessible through the main menu, has been expanded to provide users with comprehensive insights into one-time purchases and membership transactions. To access these valuable insights, follow these steps:

## **Accessing Revenue Analytics:**

This feature allows users to filter their data by date and channel of purchase and view the distribution of the top four offers in the given date range. Additionally, users can compare multiple offers and see the revenue from these offers.

## **How to use this feature?**

To access Member Analytics, follow these steps:

1.  Navigate to the Membership tab
2.  Hover over the ‘Courses’ section and select ‘Analytics.
3.  Go to the Analytics tab.
4.  Access Member Analytics: Click on Revenue Analytics

**FAQS**




**Question:** What is Revenue Analytics?
**Answer:** Revenue Analytics is a feature that provides users with comprehensive insights into both one-time purchases and membership transactions. It allows for detailed filtering and comparison of revenue data across different offers and time periods.




**Question:** How do I access Revenue Analytics?
**Answer:** To access Revenue Analytics, follow these steps:

1.  **Navigate to the Analytics Section:** Go to Sites.
2.  **Select Membership:** Click on Membership.
3.  **Choose Analytics:** Click on the Analytics tab.
4.  **Access Revenue Analytics:** Click on Revenue Analytics.




**Question:** What can I view in the Net Revenue Tab?
**Answer:** The Net Revenue Tab is the default view in Revenue Analytics and displays data related to one-time purchases. You can filter this data by date and purchase channels, such as Membership, Funnel, or Upsell.




**Question:** What are the filtering options available in Revenue Analytics?
**Answer:** You can filter data by:

-   **Date:** Including specific date ranges.
-   **Purchase Channel:** Membership, Funnel, and Upsell.




**Question:** How can I track membership transactions in Revenue Analytics?
**Answer:** Membership transaction tracking is now included in Revenue Analytics. All transactions within the Memberships section can be viewed under:

-   **Payments -> Transactions:** Filter these transactions using the Memberships filter.
-   **Payments -> Subscriptions:** View any subscription created.
-   **Payments -> Products:** View corresponding products/prices for offers created within the Memberships section.




**Question:** What is Offer Distribution in Revenue Analytics?
**Answer:** Offer Distribution provides insights into the distribution of the top four offers within the selected date range. You can see the number of units sold and total revenue for these top offers based on your filters.




**Question:** How does the Compare Offers Tab work?
**Answer:** The Compare Offers Tab allows users to compare the performance of up to four offers simultaneously. This feature helps in understanding the revenue generated by each offer and comparing their performance.




**Question:** Can I compare more than four offers at once?
**Answer:** No, currently you can compare up to four offers at a time in the Compare Offers Tab.




**Question:** What is the benefit of tracking both one-time and recurring transactions?
**Answer:** Tracking both one-time and recurring transactions provides a comprehensive view of revenue sources, helping you understand the financial impact of both immediate sales and long-term membership income.




**Question:** How can I get further assistance with Revenue Analytics?
**Answer:** For additional support, refer to the help documentation within the Analytics section or contact customer support for personalized assistance.




**Question:** Can I compare more than four offers?
**Answer:** No, currently you can compare up to **four offers** at a time.




**Question:** How can I get help or additional support with Revenue Analytics?
**Answer:** Refer to the help documentation within the **Analytics** section or contact **customer support** for personalized guidance.




**Question:** Is there a way to see a "unified" view of how often members are logging in?

**Answer:** Yes, by selecting **Login Analytics** within the Membership Analytics section, the tool provides a unified login count. This helps you understand general activity trends across your entire membership base.




**Question:** Can I export the charts and data from the Revenue Analytics tab?
**Answer:** While the visual charts are currently for in-app viewing, you can export the raw transaction data by navigating to **Payments > Transactions** and using the "Export" button. You can apply the same date filters there to match your Analytics view.




**Question:** Can I manually adjust a member's progress if they have completed a lesson outside of the platform?

**Answer:** Yes. Within the Member Analytics interface, you have the ability to manually mark specific categories or lessons as "Complete" for your members. This allows you to accurately reflect their engagement and progress even if the system hasn't automatically triggered a completion.




**Question:** If I delete a Membership Offer, will the historical data disappear from Revenue Analytics?

**Answer:** No. The revenue data is tied to the transaction record. Even if an Offer is deleted or archived, the historical units sold and revenue generated will still appear in your analytics for the date range during which those sales occurred.
