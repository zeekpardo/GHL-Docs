Introducing the Facebook - Comment(S) On A Post workflow trigger, this feature enhances sales and conversations on Facebook by automating interactions with customers. It enables businesses to attract and engage with customers in real-time, ensuring no lead slips away and facilitating re-engagement without costly retargeting ad campaigns.

##   
**Prerequisite:**

To utilize this feature, integration of a Facebook account is necessary. Additionally, the actions in workflows require the availability of triggers like Facebook User comments on a post.

## How to integrate a Facebook account:

To integrate your account, follow these simple steps:

1.  Go to settings on the bottom left.
2.  Navigate to integrations.
3.  Locate the Facebook and Instagram sections.
4.  Connect your account.

### **FAQ's**  




**Question:** Do I need to connect a Facebook account before using this trigger?
**Answer:** Yes, you must integrate your Facebook account with the system. Navigate to Settings > Integrations and connect your Facebook account under the Facebook and Instagram sections.




**Question:** Can I target comments from specific posts only?
**Answer:** Yes, you can specify posts using the "Published" or "Custom" filters. For published posts, select from the list of existing posts on your page. For custom posts, paste the URL or ID of the post you want to target.




**Question:** What criteria can I use to filter comments?
**Answer:** You can use criteria such as "Contains Phrase" or "Exact Match" to filter comments. These options allow the workflow to trigger only when specific phrases or keywords are included in the comment.




**Question:** Can I reply to comments directly via Messenger?
**Answer:** Yes, you can use the "Facebook Interactive Messenger" action to send a reply either as a direct message (DM) or as a comment reply via DM.




**Question:** Is it possible to automate replies using AI?
**Answer:** Absolutely! You can use the AI action to generate and automate responses to social media interactions, ensuring timely and relevant engagement.



**Question:** Can I attach files or buttons to Messenger replies?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize messages by attaching files and adding interactive buttons for enhanced engagement.




**Question:** Can I "Undo" a change if I delete a section by accident?
**Answer:** In the Website and Email builders, there is an **"Undo" arrow** at the top. For Workflows, if you delete a step and save, it cannot be undone manually; however, you can check the **"Restore Version"** history (if available) to revert to a previous save.
