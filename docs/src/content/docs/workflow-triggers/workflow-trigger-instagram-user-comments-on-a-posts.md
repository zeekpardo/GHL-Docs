**Workflow Trigger: Instagram - User Comments On A Post**

A workflow trigger has been introduced to enhance engagement and drive sales on social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram. With this capability, users can automate responses to comments on their posts, ensuring no potential lead slips away and conversations with customers are instantly initiated and maintained. In this article, we'll delve into the specifics of leveraging the Instagram - User Comments On A Post trigger within 's automation workflow.

## Prerequisites:

Before utilizing this feature, ensure the following prerequisites are met:

1.  Integration of with your Facebook account.
2.  Availability of the "Facebook - User comments on a post" or "Instagram - User comments on a post" triggers in the automation workflow.

## How To Use:

Follow these steps to integrate Instagram - User Comments On A Post trigger into your automation workflow:

**1\. Access Settings:** Go to settings in platform.

**2\. Navigate to Integrations:** Within settings, locate and click on the integrations section.

**3\. Connect Instagram Account:** Under the Facebook and Instagram section, connect your Instagram account by following the prompted steps.

### Important Points

-   **Page Filter:** The "Page is" filter is the initial field to be selected in triggers.
-   **Interactive Messenger Actions:** You can add up to 3 buttons in the Interactive Messenger action.
-   **Call Button Limitation:** No additional actions can be added after the "Call" button.
-   **Default Wait Time:** A default wait time of 10 seconds is included, which can be edited by the user.
-   **Default Timeout:** If no button is selected, contacts will proceed to the "Default Timeout" branch.
-   **Reply to DM:** If selected, the system will check for recent conversations.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I use this trigger for posts on Facebook as well?
**Answer:** Yes, you can use the trigger “Facebook - User comments on a post”




**Question:** How do I know if the trigger is set up correctly?
**Answer:** Use the Test Workflow feature to simulate comments and verify that the workflow functions as intended.




**Question:** What should I do if the workflow is not triggering as expected?
**Answer:** Double-check your filter settings, ensure that the Instagram account is correctly connected, and review the post details. Test the workflow again if necessary.




**Question:** Can I add more actions to the workflow after it is published?
**Answer:** Yes, you can modify the workflow and add or adjust actions even after it is published




**Question:** Is there a limit to how many comments can be processed at once?
**Answer:** The system is built to handle high volume. However, Instagram has rate limits to prevent spam. Our platform manages these limits automatically, but we recommend avoiding overly repetitive messages to keep your account safe.
