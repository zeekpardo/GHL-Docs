One Workflow Trigger that is helpful in creating a more personalized experience for your clients is the Birthday Reminder trigger. This can be used in all sorts of ways from simple birthday celebration emails to special birthday promotions. This article will cover exactly how to create a Birthday Reminder Workflow Trigger and what customization options are available for this trigger.

### **Key Benefits of the Birthday Reminder Workflow Trigger**

-   **Automates Customer Engagement**: Automatically sends birthday messages to customers without the need to manually track dates, ensuring consistent and timely engagement.
-   **Enhances Personalization**: Customize messages with contact names, personalized greetings, and special offers to create a more individualized experience for your customers.
-   **Boosts Customer Loyalty**: Celebrating a customer's birthday helps strengthen the relationship between the brand and the customer, increasing the chances of repeat purchases.
-   **Increases Sales & Conversions**: Birthday discounts or special incentives can encourage customers to make a purchase, leading to higher revenue and conversions.
-   **Customizable Execution Timing**: Utilize time delays and filters to send messages at the most effective times, such as a few days before or on the customer’s birthday itself.

**_Tip:_** _Usually, you would want to send a reminder every year, so for this workflow please make sure to enable “Allow re-entry” in the workflow settings._

-   The _Birthday Reminder_ is set up by default to add a contact to your workflow on the day of their birthday, provided that information is listed in their contact information. You can, however, adjust the timing of when your contact is added to the workflow and filter which birthday months, and days will trigger this workflow.
    -   Under **Workflow Trigger Name** you can change the display name for this workflow trigger within the Workflow builder. 
        -   Note that this will not impact any functionality of the workflow, it will only be shown within the builder
    -   Under **Filters,** there are multiple options available to you
        -   **Before no. of days** and **After no. of days** impact the timing of when this workflow will trigger. Respectively, this will trigger on the selected number of days before or after the contact’s listed birthdate.
        -   **Month is** and **Day is** allow you to specify which dates or months can trigger this workflow. For example, you can make this workflow only trigger for birthdays in March, or for birthdays that coincide with a holiday.
        -   **Custom Field** allows you to filter which contacts can be added to this workflow based on custom fields you have created.

### **FAQs**  




**Question:** What is the Birthday Reminder Workflow Trigger?
**Answer:** The Birthday Reminder Workflow Trigger is used to automate actions based on a contact’s birthday. It allows you to send birthday greetings, promotions, or other personalized messages on or around a contact’s birthday.




**Question:** How do I set up a Birthday Reminder Workflow Trigger?  
Answer:**

1.  **Navigate to Automation**:
    -   Start a new workflow or edit an existing one.
2.  **Add New Trigger**:
    -   Click “Add New Trigger.”
    -   Select “Birthday Reminder” from the Contact section or use the search feature.
3.  **Configure Trigger**:
    -   You can adjust the timing of when the contact is added to the workflow (default is on the birthday).
    -   Use filters to specify which months or days should trigger the workflow, or set custom field filters.
4.  **Save Trigger**: Click “Save Trigger” to finalize the setup.
5.  **Set Up Actions**: Define the actions that will occur when the trigger is activated.
6.  **Save Workflow**: Click “Save” and ensure the workflow is published and tested.




**Question:** What customization options are available for the Birthday Reminder trigger?  
Answer:**

-   **Timing Adjustments**: You can set the trigger to occur a specific number of days before or after the birthday.
-   **Month and Day Filters**: Specify particular months or days that should trigger the workflow.
-   **Custom Field Filters**: Use custom fields to further refine which contacts are included in the workflow.




**Question:** How do I ensure the workflow triggers every year?
**Answer:** To ensure the workflow sends a reminder every year, enable the “Allow re-entry” option in the workflow settings. This allows the workflow to be re-triggered annually for each contact.




**Question:** Can I use the Birthday Reminder trigger for special promotions?
**Answer:** Yes, you can use the Birthday Reminder trigger to set up special promotions, offers, or personalized messages. Customize the workflow actions to fit your promotional needs.




**Question:** What should I do after setting up the trigger?
**Answer:** After setting up the trigger:

-   Configure the actions you want to be triggered (e.g., send an email, apply a tag, etc.).
-   Save and publish the workflow.
-   Test the workflow using the Test Workflow button to ensure it functions as expected.




**Question:** Can I filter contacts based on custom fields?
**Answer:** Yes, under the Filters section, you can use custom fields to filter which contacts are added to the workflow based on the criteria you have set.




**Question:** Can I filter the trigger to specific months or days?
**Answer:** Yes. Use the **Month is** and **Day is** filters to target birthdays in certain months or on particular days, such as during holidays or special events.




**Question:** How can I collect birthdays from my customers automatically?
**Answer:** You can add a "Birthday" field to your **Forms** or **Surveys**. When a customer fills out the form, their birthday is saved directly to their contact profile, which will then automatically qualify them for any active Birthday Reminder workflows.




**Question:** Is it possible to trigger a workflow after the birthday has already passed?
**Answer:** Yes. By using the "After no. of days" filter, you can trigger actions (like a "Hope you had a great day!" follow-up) a specific number of days after the listed birthdate.
