**How to Use**

-   _This will pull up a list of selections of triggers on the right-hand side of the screen_

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768858966720-image3.png)

    -   Select the “**Abandoned Checkout**” button

-   **Please Note**: _this will only work if you have a Shopify account and it is integrated (To learn more about integrating your Shopify account please continue)_

-   Click the “**Connect**” button

-   Please note that you can open the “**Learn More**” option (in red) this will give you step-by-step instructions on integrating your Shopify account

-   **For Example:** if the customer has not checked out their items for (30 Minutes) you have the option to send a reminder email to them

## **FAQs** 




**Question:** Can I set different abandoned checkout triggers for different product categories in my Shopify store?
**Answer:** No, the abandoned checkout trigger is based on cart activity and filters like cart value, but it does not allow customization by specific product categories.




**Question:** What happens if the customer completes the purchase before the trigger duration ends?**If the customer completes the purchase before the specified duration (e.g., 30 minutes), the automation will not be triggered, and no reminder email will be sent.




**Question:** Is there a way to track the performance of the abandoned checkout trigger?
**Answer:** Yes, you can monitor the performance of your abandoned checkout trigger by reviewing automation analytics, such as email open rates and conversion rates, within your dashboard.




**Question:** Can I customize the reminder email content sent after the abandoned checkout trigger?
**Answer:** Yes, you can fully customize the email content, including text, images, and offers, to align with your brand and encourage the customer to complete their purchase.




**Question:** Can I set multiple triggers with different time durations for abandoned checkouts?
**Answer:** No, each abandoned checkout trigger is limited to one specific duration per automation. If you need multiple reminders, you can create additional workflows with different time settings.




**Question:** Do I need a Shopify account to use the abandoned checkout trigger?
**Answer:** Yes, this trigger only works if you have a Shopify account integrated with your account.




**Question:** How does the "Cart Value" filter work in conjunction with the Abandoned Checkout trigger?
**Answer:** You can add the "Cart Value" filter to your trigger to make your automation more specific. This allows you to set conditions so that the workflow only fires if the abandoned cart reaches a certain monetary threshold, ensuring you prioritize high-value recovery opportunities.
