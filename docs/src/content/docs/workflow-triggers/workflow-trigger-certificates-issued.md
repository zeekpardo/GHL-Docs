**Workflow Trigger: Certificates Issued**

The Certificates Issued trigger enhancement allows users to trigger workflow actions based on the issuance of certificates, offering a more tailored approach to automation.

## **How to Use this Workflow Trigger:**

Implementing the Certificates Issued trigger is a straightforward process, designed to streamline your workflow automation efforts. Follow these steps to leverage this feature effectively:

**1- Access Workflow Builder:** Log in to your account and navigate to **Automation** > **Workflows** and click on **\+ Create Workflow**.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762954715594-image4.png)

**2- Select Certificates Issued Trigger:** Within the Workflow Builder interface, locate the Certificates Issued trigger among the available options  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762954880292-image1.png)

**3- Add Filters:** Users can add filters based on certificate templates and different conditional operators to further customize the workflow trigger.  
![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXct4rE-bCuJdf29V4eMv-JAGN8AAbm59Hi95th4iC8SzYasvbZU-YFq1YL87C55lHlgzRc28yNpYfK1ycwvGKY5BKMoSCon0qxrmlUriXjJbP6oG8jso60awnBWI9s_E4WmO2tU51Q5XkyaxVZKVUilUMI0?key=tt9KBNeJbWp695Jac7X_cg)

**4- Define Trigger Conditions:** Specify the conditions under which you want the trigger to activate, such as the issuance of a specific certificate template.  
![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeQ4Mm2EhPs8f-Bt6e952tR2MyzLrl0KmYxlkWWqdcc6YA0AxhLWvNDtD6o35qwV7jK1vo7fHW8pWfxBwRuS-iliY9OLUAjA-RLGOilowfG0_cvZn6Fw58Ebm5AZnKzQOZb2_BDLLVectfrNOTIua40AYk?key=tt9KBNeJbWp695Jac7X_cg)

**5- Choose Certificate Templates:** Select certificate templates for actions, ensuring that the appropriate template is issued as a certificate in response to the trigger. Save, publish and/or test the article after completion.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773987754859-image1.jpg)


**_NOTE: For an in-depth overview of Workflow Testing see our article, “Using the Testing Features in Workflows”_**

**6- Customize Workflow Actions:** Define the actions you want the workflow to undertake in response to certificate issuance, such as sending notifications or updating records.  

## **Benefits of Certificates Issued Workflow Trigger:**

The introduction of the Certificates Issued trigger offers several significant benefits to users:

1.  **Tailored Automation:** Users can trigger workflow actions precisely when certificates are issued, enabling tailored responses to certificate issuance events.
2.  **Enhanced Customization:** With the ability to add filters based on certificate templates and conditional operators, users can customize workflow triggers to meet specific business needs.
3.  **Efficient Certificate Issuance:** By choosing certificate templates for actions, users can streamline the issuance process, ensuring that the appropriate certificate is generated automatically in response to the trigger.

### FAQ’s




**Question:** What is the Certificates Issued workflow trigger?
**Answer:** The Certificates Issued trigger allows users to automate actions when a certificate is issued, enabling more tailored workflow automation.




**Question:** How do I enable the Certificates Issued trigger in my workflow?
**Answer:** Navigate to **Automation > Workflows**, click **\+ Create Workflow**, and select the **Certificates Issued** trigger from the available options.




**Question:** Can I customize when the trigger activates?
**Answer:** Yes, you can define trigger conditions and add filters based on certificate templates and conditional operators to tailor the workflow to your needs.




**Question:** What actions can I automate with this trigger?
**Answer:** You can automate tasks like sending notifications, updating records, or triggering other workflow actions when a certificate is issued.




**Question:** How can I test my workflow after setting up the trigger?
**Answer:** You can use the workflow testing feature to verify functionality. For more details, refer to the article **“Using the Testing Features in Workflows.”**




**Question:** Do I need to publish the workflow after setting up this trigger?

**Answer:** Yes, after configuring the trigger and defining workflow actions, you should **save, publish, and test** the workflow to ensure it functions correctly.




**Question:** Will this trigger fire if I manually issue a certificate?  
**Answer:** Yes. Regardless of whether the certificate is issued automatically via a course completion or manually by an administrator, the "Issuance" event will still activate the workflow.




**Question:** How does this trigger help with the actual issuance of the certificate?

**Answer:** It streamlines the process by ensuring that once the trigger criteria are met, the appropriate certificate is **generated automatically**, reducing the need for manual administrative work and ensuring members receive their credentials instantly.
