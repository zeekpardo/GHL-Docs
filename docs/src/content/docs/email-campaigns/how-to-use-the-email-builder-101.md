**How To Use the Email Builder-101**  

Email marketing remains one of the most effective ways to engage with your audience and boost business growth. In this guide, we will delve deep into the Email Builder, empowering you to streamline your email creation process and create compelling campaigns that resonate with your audience. In it, you can create and save templates and schedule email campaigns. The Email Builder contains two sections: Email Campaign and Templates. The Email Templates are where you can customize the branding, layout, and design of the email, and the Email Campaigns are for adding a message and delivery options for sending to customers and leads.

Read on to learn how to navigate to and use the email builder:

## **Navigating Email Builder: An Overview**

The Email Builder, located within , is a versatile platform divided into two core sections: Email Campaigns and Templates. Here’s an introduction to these sections:

### **Email Campaigns:**

Create personalized messages tailored for specific audiences.

Fine-tune delivery options to optimize engagement.

Snippets included: Attachments from the snippet are automatically included.

### **Templates:**

Customize branding, layout, and design with ease.

Choose from existing templates, modify campaigns, add snippets, or create from scratch using intuitive tools.

Snippets: Attachments won’t be added (not supported).

### **Creating Email Templates**

-   Click +New to get started.
-   Choose the option of the template you wish to create. You can create a template using the Template Library, reuse a Campaign to send or schedule, or you can use a Blank Template. If using the Blank Template, you can choose between “Design Editor” and “Code Editor”.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764005443429-image.png)

**NOTE:** The plain text editor offers a basic, clean, and no-frills approach to text editing, making it ideal for simple, straightforward content creation. Design Editor has a drag and drop elements, while Code Editor refers to HTML

### **Design Options:**

-   **Template Selection:** Choose from Template Library, Campaign Reuse, or Blank Template (with Plain text editor, Design Editor, or Code Editor options).
-   **Visual Editing:** Drag and drop elements, resize images effortlessly, and enhance colors for a visually appealing template.

### **Part 1: Element Management**

On the left side, you can see five icons: The Add Element, the Manage Element, the Appearance icons, A/B Testing, and Saved Items

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764005455130-image.png)

-   **Add Elements:** The Add Element icon will allow you to add the needed elements to your template. Select from a range of elements including Text, Image, Button, Logo, and more.
-   **Manage Elements:** The Manage Elements icon provides an overview of all the elements within your email. Scrolling through the list of sections lets you see what elements it contains.
-   **Appearances:** In the Appearance icon, you can tweak the aesthetics of your template, you can customize your button, add dividers, add custom CSS, and set the mobile formatting. 
-   **Layout Customization:** Drag and drop layouts, ensuring a seamless arrangement of elements.
-   **A/B Testing:** Enable A/B testing to maximize engagement by sending up to 6 variations of your subject line or email content to a portion of your users, and then automatically sending the winning variation to the remaining audience.
-   **Saved items:** Drag and drop your saved elements into the builder, rename them, or delete them if not needed.
-   **Flexible Editing Layout:** To improve multitasking, the edit panel can be moved to the right side of the screen. This allows for a side-by-side view when using AI tools alongside manual editing. You can toggle the panel between the left and right positions at any time using the switch control.

**Text Snippets in Email Builder**

You can insert pre-saved snippets directly into your email templates or campaigns. Open any email editor, click "Insert Snippet," and choose from your saved snippets. Note that attachments are supported in campaigns but not in templates.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764788040446-image5.jpg)

**Mobile Optimization**:

The Email Builder also supports better mobile optimization, allowing users to adjust how content displays on mobile screens. You can enable or disable stacking for two or more columns without needing custom code. Users can also adjust image alignment to ensure full-width images display correctly on mobile devices

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764788056596-image1.jpg)

**Visibility Controls for Layouts in Email Builder**

Managing responsive email design is more streamlined with the ability to control visibility for entire layout sections, not just individual elements. This allows users to show or hide full content blocks based on device type, ensuring cleaner mobile and desktop views while saving editing time. A major benefit is the ability to create mobile-optimized emails without duplicating effort across views.

**How to Use:**While editing an email template, hover over a layout block and use the device icons to toggle visibility. Alternatively, use the “Visibility” tab in the left panel to manage preferences. Preview in both desktop and mobile views or send a test email to confirm the layout visibility.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764005687771-image.png)

## **Creating Email Campaigns**

### **Part 1: Creating your campaign**

-   Go to Marketing - Emails - Email Campaigns
-   Click on +New

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764788161306-image7.jpg)

-   Hover over the template, you will see two icons: the check mark icon (to select) and the preview icon. 

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764005728449-image.png)

-   When you click on the checkmark icon, you will be redirected to the email builder again, where you can make the last edits or additional edits as needed. 
-   Save the changes made, ensuring the updated template is accurately reflected in the campaign.

### **Editing and Refinement:**


If you click on the 3 dots icon, you will see the options: Test Email, See Version History,  and Add Attachments.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764788245445-image2.jpg)

### **Sending and Scheduling:**

-   Once everything is ready, you can click on Send and Schedule. This will open up the Sending Options where you can choose how and when you want to schedule the Email Campaign to be sent, and determine who will receive it. You can completely customize this section, you can send emails immediately, schedule them for a specific time, use batch scheduling for large groups to send emails gradually, or choose a smart list of contacts to send to, and more. This helps manage sending volume and prevents overloading recipients.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764788265818-image8.jpg)

**Using Automations:**Email campaigns can be integrated with automations for enhanced functionality. You can add an email within an automation, select from the existing templates, and set it to be sent as part of a broader marketing sequence. This allows for consistent branding and messaging across multiple automations without needing to recreate emails each time.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764788363048-image3.jpg)

Adding emails into automations ensures consistency and leverages existing templates efficiently. If a template is used in multiple automations, any updates made to the template will automatically apply across all associated workflows, simplifying maintenance and updates. This approach optimizes your email marketing strategy, ensuring your content remains up-to-date and relevant across all automations.

**Question:** If I update an Email Template, will it automatically update the emails already inside my active Workflows?

**Answer:** Yes. This is a major time-saver. If your Automation is set to send a specific "Template," any design or text changes you save to that template will be reflected the next time the Workflow triggers for a contact. You do not need to manually update each individual Workflow.
