Research indicates that a significant number of emails are overlooked in crowded inboxes. This modification aims to address this issue by allowing users to resend their emails to non-openers, ensuring that carefully crafted messages reach their intended audience. Users can resend campaigns to non-openers with just a few clicks. Additionally, they can modify the subject line to refresh the message and reduce the chance of user complaints. Furthermore, the statistics feature provides detailed insights into how subscribers engage with your email content, allowing for more effective campaign optimization.

## How to Use

### Steps to Resend Emails to Unopened Recipients

1\. Navigate to Email Marketing:

-   Go to the Marketing > Email

2\. Select Your Campaign:

-   Click on the "Campaigns" tab to view all your sent campaigns.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769002255981-unnamed - 2026-01-21T213049.355.png>)

3\. Choose the Campaign to Resend:

-   Select the campaign you want to resend. Click on the three dots next to the campaign name.

4\. Resend to Unopened:

-   Choose the "Resend email to Unopened" option.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769002272139-unnamed - 2026-01-21T213107.620.png>)

5\. Update Settings:

-   Update the email settings, including the subject line if desired.
-   Schedule the resend time.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769002295783-unnamed - 2026-01-21T213127.336.png>)

6\. Confirm and Schedule:

-   Confirm the details and schedule the resend campaign.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769002326430-unnamed - 2026-01-21T213159.875.png>)

**_Note: The resend feature is available within 12 hours to 10 days after the original email was sent. This timing minimizes complaints and aligns with industry norms. Resending is not supported for Batch schedules and RSS campaigns._**

## Pro Tips

-   Crafting Effective Subject Lines: Change the subject line when resending to make it more attention-grabbing. Ensure it aligns with the email content to avoid being perceived as spam.
-   Timing Matters: Consider the optimal time for resending. If your initial email was sent during off-peak hours, schedule the resend for a more active time.
-   Monitor Engagement: Keep an eye on engagement metrics to understand the impact of your residents and adjust your strategy accordingly.

**FAQs** 


**Question:What is the purpose of resending emails to unopened recipients?
**Answer:** Resending emails to non-openers helps ensure that important messages reach as many subscribers as possible, especially in crowded inboxes where emails can easily be overlooked.


**Question:How can I resend an email to recipients who didn’t open the first email?
**Answer:** Go to the Email Marketing section, select the campaign, click on the three dots next to it, and choose “Resend email to Unopened.”


**Question:Can I modify the subject line when resending to unopened recipients?
**Answer:** Yes, you can update the subject line to make it more attention-grabbing and relevant, helping to reduce the risk of being marked as spam.


**Question:Is there a time limit for resending emails to non-openers?
**Answer:** The resend option is available within 12 hours to 10 days after the initial email was sent, aligning with best practices to minimize complaints.


**Question:Can I resend batch-scheduled or RSS campaigns?
**Answer:** No, resending to non-openers is not supported for batch-scheduled or RSS campaigns.




**Question:** Is resending emails to non-openers compliant with email marketing laws?
**Answer:** Yes, as long as the original email was sent to subscribers who opted in and you respect unsubscribe requests, resending is compliant. However, always follow your local laws and platform terms of service.




**Question:** Can I schedule the resend for a specific time when my audience is more active?
**Answer:**  Absolutely. You can pick any time within the allowed window (12 hours – 10 days). Choosing a high-engagement time can significantly increase opens and clicks.




**Question:** How can click tracking help improve email performance when resending?
**Answer:** Click tracking shows which links subscribers interact with, allowing you to optimize content, link placement, and engagement strategies for better campaign performance.
