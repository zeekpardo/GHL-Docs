**Email Campaigns: The Statistics Tab**

This article will explain the importance of tracking your email campaign statistics. By understanding what is working and what is not in your campaigns, you can improve your marketing efforts and get a better return on your investment.

How to Access the Campaign Statistics Dashboard:

-   Navigate to the Marketing tab.
-   Hover over the Emails section and go to Campaigns.
-   Click on the Statistics section.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764343840328-image.png)

The dashboard provides a variety of insights into your email campaign performance, including:

-   **Conversion Summary:** This shows you how much revenue you've generated, the average order value, the order rate, and the total orders.
-   **Engagement Summary:** This displays the number of emails delivered, opened, clicked, and orders placed.
-   **Top Performing Emails**: Displays a list of top-performing emails based on opens, clicks, and revenue generated.
-   **Open Rate:** This is the percentage of recipients who opened your email.
-   **Unsubscribed**: This indicates the number of people who unsubscribed from your email list.
-   **Click-Through Rate:** This is the percentage of recipients who clicked on a link in your email.
-   **Emails Sent:** This is the total number of emails sent.
-   **Top Performing Emails:** This shows the emails that generated the most revenue.
-   **All in one Place:** Email marketing dashboard shows stats for all emails including Workflows, Bulk Actions, and Campaigns

Date Range Selection:

You can select the date range for the statistics from the options available at the top of the dashboard:

-   Last 7 days
-   Last 30 days
-   Current Week

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764343857532-image.png)

Detailed Insights:

**Engagement Summary:** This section provides detailed metrics on the number of emails delivered, opened, clicked, and orders placed from email links.

**Performance Analysis:** Track the performance of your email campaign over time. You can select different email statuses from the drop-down menu on the right side and track their performance through graphs and percentage metrics. This helps identify trends and allows for necessary changes to campaigns. For instance, if the open rate is declining, you might need to change the subject line or email content.

**Identify Top Performing Emails:** This feature highlights the top-performing emails that have generated the most revenue, helping you understand what works well and replicate successful strategies in other campaigns.

-   You can view the **top 5 email campaigns** based on open rate, click rate, and revenue.

**Note**: For SMTP, delivered counts are not tracked. We recommend using click rate and revenue as key metrics for performance analysis.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764343956442-image.png)

**Most Recent Emails:** View statistics for the most recent email campaigns sent, providing immediate insights into their performance.

By utilizing these features, you can effectively monitor and optimize your email marketing efforts, ensuring better engagement and higher returns on investment.

**Dummy Data View:** The Email Marketing dashboard includes a **dummy data view**, allowing new users to explore campaign statistics without the need to send any campaigns.

The dashboard will display the Statistics page. New users will be prompted to view the data with dummy data by clicking on the “Try Sample Data” option.

## **Introduction to Click Tracking**

This feature is incredibly useful, especially if you're running a launch and sending out a lot of emails within a short period, such as one or two weeks. Instead of sending all the emails to everyone on your list, you can track who clicked on which link, allowing you to retarget those most interested in your content. By segmenting your list and identifying quality leads, you can tailor your content more effectively.

### **Setting Up Your Campaign**

To set up click tracking in your email campaigns, follow these steps:

**1\. Navigate to the Email Marketing Section**:

-   Click on **Marketing** on the left, then **Emails** at the top, and select **Campaigns**.
-   Create a new campaign and click on send or schedule.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764344205207-image.png)

**2\. Create or Clone a Campaign**:

-   You can create a new campaign by clicking the **Add New** button or clone an existing campaign. For this example, we'll clone an existing campaign.
-   Edit the cloned campaign by clicking on it. The regular email builder will appear, where you can add elements like images, videos, and text.

**3\. Adding and Tracking Links**:

-   To add a link, click on the text you want to hyperlink, then click on the hyperlink icon. Add your desired link and click **Save**. You can also add buttons with hyperlinks.
-   Once your email is ready, click **Send** or **Schedule**. Before sending, make sure to toggle on the **Track Clicks** option. This ensures the system will track every click on the links you added.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764344227786-image.png)

Please note that the current implementation of click tracking does not support A/B testing or RSS campaigns. It also does not work with workflows, but is available under the marketing and campaigns sections. Custom values are supported, but trigger links are not.

### Tag Contacts Directly from Campaign Statistics:

Segment and personalize your audience based on campaign interactions, right from the campaign statistics page. You can tag contacts who opened, clicked, or interacted with your campaign, and even set up auto-tagging for future interactions, streamlining your segmentation process.

-   Open the campaign statistics by clicking the 3-dot menu on a sent campaign.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764344247746-image.png)

-   Click Load More and scroll to interactions like Opened, Clicked, etc.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764344261085-image.png)

-   Select contacts and click Add Tags to assign tags.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764344275148-image.png)

-   To auto-tag future interactions, select **All**, then **Add Tags**, and enable **Auto-Tagging**.
-   Track your tagged contacts in the **Tags** and **Bulk Actions** sections.

## **FAQ's**




**Question:** What insights can I get from the Campaign Statistics dashboard?

**Answer:** The dashboard provides insights such as conversion summary (revenue, order value, order rate), engagement summary (delivered, opened, clicked emails), top-performing emails, and more. These help you analyze your campaign's performance and make data-driven decisions.




**Question:** How can I track the performance of my email campaigns?
**Answer:** You can access the statistics for your campaigns by navigating to the Marketing tab, selecting Emails, and then clicking on Campaigns. From there, go to the Statistics section to view key performance metrics like open rate, click-through rate, and revenue generated.




**Question:** What is click tracking, and how does it work?
**Answer:** Click tracking allows you to monitor which recipients clicked on specific links in your emails. By enabling click tracking during campaign setup, the system tracks all clicks on hyperlinks, helping you identify engaged leads and retarget them accordingly.




**Question:** How do I enable click tracking in my campaigns?
**Answer:** To enable click tracking, make sure to toggle on the "Track Clicks" option while setting up or editing your campaign. This will allow you to track each click on the links within your email.




**Question:** Can I use click tracking with workflows?
**Answer:** Currently, click tracking is not supported in workflows. However, it is available in the Email Marketing section under Campaigns for better tracking of your marketing emails.




**Question:** How can I view email statistics for my workflows?
**Answer:** To view email statistics for workflows, go to the Email Marketing section, then select Campaigns > Workflow Campaigns. From there, you can access detailed statistics for each email action within the workflow, including open rates, click-through rates, and other key performance metrics.




**Question:** Can I track individual email performance within a workflow?
**Answer:** Yes, you can track individual email action performance within a workflow. Simply use the dropdown menu under the Workflow Statistics section to view stats for each email action separately, allowing you to analyze and optimize the performance of specific emails within the workflow.




**Question:** What should I focus on when using SMTP for sending emails?

**Answer:** Delivered counts are not tracked with SMTP. Focus on **click rate** and **revenue generated** as key performance metrics.  




**Question:** How can I use campaign statistics to improve future emails?
**Answer:** By analyzing trends such as declining open rates or low click-through rates, you can optimize subject lines, content, timing, and audience segmentation to improve future performance.




**Question:** If I delete a Workflow, will I lose the email statistics for those messages?

**Answer:** No. One of the best features of this update is that workflow email stats are now unified in the Email Marketing section. Even if a workflow is paused or edited, the historical data remains accessible for analysis.




**Question:** Why are my "Delivered" statistics not showing up?

**Answer:** If you are using an external SMTP provider, the "Delivered" status is often not passed back to the dashboard. In this scenario, you should focus on **Open Rate** and **Click-Through Rate** as your primary indicators of success, as these metrics confirm the recipient interacted with the email.
