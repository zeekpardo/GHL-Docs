**Content AI in the Email Builder**

Writing assistant Content AI helps generate 10X faster, more effortless, and better Content. Content AI in Emails generates outlines, introductions, specific sections, and variations for different email contexts, including promotional emails, introduction emails, coupons/discounts, recurring newsletters, testimonials, cold outreach, and content/giveaways. It can also be used to create subject lines and variations. Using Content AI in Emails makes creating email content faster and more efficient while allowing for tailored Content based on specific business needs.

### Who is this feature going to be useful for?

The Content AI feature in emails can be helpful for businesses of all sizes that engage in email marketing. It can benefit individuals, small businesses, and larger organizations by providing a fast and efficient way to create email content. The feature can help companies with limited resources, allowing them to create professional-quality emails without hiring a dedicated content writer or marketing specialist. 

### The benefits of using Content AI for Emails.

-   Faster and more efficient content creation: Content AI can generate outlines, introductions, and specific sections for emails, which can save time and reduce the effort required to create content from scratch.
-   Tailored Content: Content AI can help businesses tailor their email content to specific contexts, such as promotional emails, introduction emails, coupons/discounts, recurring newsletters, testimonials, cold outreach, and content/giveaways.
-   Variations for A/B testing: Content AI can create variations of email content, including subject lines, which can be used for A/B testing to determine the most effective Content.
-   User-friendly interface: Content AI is easy to use, with a drag-and-drop interface that allows users to create emails quickly and efficiently.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can Content AI generate content for any email context, or is it limited to specific types?
**Answer:** Content AI can generate content for a variety of email contexts, including promotional emails, introduction emails, coupons/discounts, recurring newsletters, testimonials, cold outreach, and content/giveaways. If your specific context is not listed, you can select "Other" for broader content generation.




**Question:** Is there a limit on the number of variations I can create using Content AI?
**Answer:** You can choose the number of variations you want Content AI to generate. If the AI is unable to produce the desired number of variations, you will only be charged for the variations that are successfully generated.




**Question:** Can I edit the existing text using Content AI, and what options are available for editing?
**Answer:** Yes, you can edit existing text using Content AI. Available options include improvising writing, making text longer or shorter, checking spelling and grammar, and simplifying writing. These tools help refine and enhance your content to better suit your needs.




**Question:** How can I manage the Content AI and view its history?
**Answer:** To manage the Content AI and view its history, navigate to the **Marketing** section on the left and click on the **Content AI** tab at the top.




**Question:** What is the Brand Voice toggle in Content AI?
**Answer:** It enables or disables Brand Voice, which automatically adjusts the tone and content based on your pre-defined brand settings.




**Question:** How does the Brand Voice feature work in Content AI?
**Answer:** Content AI allows you to enable or disable the Brand Voice feature. When enabled, it automatically adjusts the tone of the generated content based on the pre-defined settings of your brand’s voice. You can also choose from saved Brand Voice options through a dropdown menu.




**Question:** Where exactly do I find the Content AI tool while I am building an email?

**Answer:** Inside the **Email Builder** (Marketing > Emails), you can find the Content AI icon within the text editor of any **Text element**. Additionally, you can find the icon on the **Send and Schedule** screen specifically for generating subject lines.




**Question:** How does the AI incorporate my business goals when "Brand Voice" is enabled?
**Answer:** When Brand Voice is toggled on, the system uses **Metadata Shaping** to influence the AI prompt behind the scenes. This means it silently considers your saved Target Audience, Call to Action (CTA), Brand Purpose, Values, and Brand Promise to ensure every generated variation is aligned with your specific brand identity.
