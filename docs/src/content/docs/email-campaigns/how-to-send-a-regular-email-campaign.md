In this article, you will learn how to send a regular email campaign. This email can be sent right away or scheduled to many contacts at once. 

## Part 1: Create the email

-   Head to Marketing > Emails > Campaigns - Create Campaign

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557067438-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003058.876.jpg>)

-   Choose the email template you want to use for your marketing email campaign.
-   Design your email and content by adding all the necessary elements.
-   **Streamlined UI:** The builder features a cleaner, more intuitive interface designed to reduce friction and speed up your creation process.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557084472-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003117.059.jpg>)

-   You can save your email campaign and edit it if needed later until you are ready to send it

### How to Verify Your 'From' Email Address to Avoid Being Sent to Spam

Verifying emails before sending campaigns helps prevent identity spoofing and ensures your messages reach the intended recipients. **To use this feature, follow these steps:**

1\. **Setting Up an Email Campaign:** Begin by setting up your email campaign in the campaign builder. Once done, click on the send or schedule icon:

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557102918-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003135.729.jpg>)

2\. **Verifying the Sender Email:** During setup, ensure the sender email is verified by following the verification prompts.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557119591-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003152.826.jpg>)

Enter the 6-digit code that you will receive after putting in your email address:

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557138176-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003210.697.jpg>)

3\. **Handling Unverified Emails:** Address any unverified emails by correcting them and re-verifying them.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557158938-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003230.177.jpg>)

4. **Completing the Verification Process:** Finalize the verification for all email addresses.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557176117-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003247.543.jpg>)

5\. **Managing Verified Emails:** Review and manage your list of verified emails.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557196059-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003307.689.png>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557208198-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003319.719.png>)

6\. **Sending the Verified Campaign:** Once all emails are verified, proceed to send your campaign.

_Note_: _Ensure all emails are verified before sending to maintain a strong email reputation and avoid deliverability issues._

## Part 3: Send Email

-   Click on “Send or Schedule”
-   Fill out the information in the **To** and **From** sections
-   Add your subject line in the Subject section
-   Select the option to Send Now or Schedule (Depending on when you want to send the email, now or later)
-   If you choose the schedule option, you will need to add the date and time (please note that the email will be sent in your account's timezone)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557318899-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003510.870.jpg>)

-   You also have the ability to choose the sender domain for your campaigns. If using a public or shared email, the system will automatically adjust to ensure compliance, avoiding campaign failures. _Note: This is for LC Email only._

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765892617070-image16.jpg)

-   The system will automatically pre-select the correct sender domain, and users will be alerted to any issues with their selected domain. _Note: This is for LC Email only._

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765892684283-image25.jpg)

-   You can choose a single domain or all domains (based on domain configuration rules) for their campaign. _Note: This is for LC Email only._

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765892738864-image21.jpg)

**Note:** You can reschedule your campaign one hour before the execution date/time. To do this, click on the reschedule button and set the date/time of the campaign. It is suggested that you apply drip/batch schedule mode when sending large quantities of emails to avoid high bounced rates and thus avoid suspensions.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557340159-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003529.646.png>)

You can also send the campaign via email as a test. This will help you to see how your email will be seen by your leads. 

Click on the 3 dots on the left side of the Save button and click on Test Email. Add the details and click on “Send Test Email”. 

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557358551-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003550.574.jpg>)

**Note:** You can restore older versions by clicking on the 3 dots next to the Save button. It will show you the dropdown option to see the version history and restore the older version. 

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765557379151-unnamed - 2025-12-13T003609.613.jpg>)

## FAQ




**Question:** How do I create and send a regular email campaign?
**Answer:** To create and send a regular email campaign, navigate to Marketing > Emails > Campaigns, select your desired email template, design your email, and then either send it immediately or schedule it for later.




**Question:** Can I change the position of the settings panel while I work?

**Answer:** Yes. The edit panel is flexible and can be switched between the left and right sides of the screen to accommodate your preferred workflow, especially when using AI tools.




**Question:** Is it possible to add borders to my images or backgrounds to specific sections?

**Answer:** Yes. The builder includes overall border controls for image elements and background image settings for individual sections.




**Question:** Why is it important to verify my 'From' email address?
**Answer:** Verifying your 'From' email address is essential to ensure your emails are delivered successfully and not marked as spam, which helps maintain a strong email-sending reputation.




**Question:** How can I add product elements with direct checkout links to my email campaign?
**Answer:** You can add product elements with direct checkout links by navigating to the email marketing section, creating a new campaign, and selecting the direct checkout option for the product element. Customize the checkout page as needed.




**Question:** What are the key features of the inline editor for customizing emails?
**Answer:** The inline editor allows you to easily style your email with various heading options, font styles, colors, text formatting, and more, making it simple to create high-quality emails efficiently.




**Question:** How do I schedule or send my email campaign after it's created?
**Answer:** Once your email campaign is ready, click on "Send or Schedule," fill out the recipient details, add a subject line, and choose to either send the email immediately or schedule it for a later date and time.




**Question:** How do I manage Sender Domain settings in my campaigns?
**Answer:** You have control over the sender domain for their campaigns. If using a public or shared email address, the system will automatically adjust it to ensure compliance. You can also choose the domain you wish to use for each campaign from the available options




**Question:** Can I undo a change I made to my email design?
**Answer:** Yes. Click the three dots next to the **Save** button to access **Version History**. This allows you to view previous iterations of your design and restore an older version if you made a mistake.
