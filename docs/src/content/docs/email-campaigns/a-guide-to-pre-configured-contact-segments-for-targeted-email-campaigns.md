Pre-built segments are predefined groups of contacts that help you target specific audiences based on their engagement with your emails. These segments are automatically created based on key behaviors such as recent interactions or lack of engagement, eliminating the need to manually sort through contacts. Let’s dive into how you can leverage this powerful tool to enhance your email marketing strategy.

### **Examples of Pre-Built Segments:**

-   **Active Contacts:** Reach contacts who have engaged with your emails in the last 7, 30, or 60 days.
-   **Recently Engaged:** Target contacts who interacted with your last 5 email campaigns.
-   **Re-engagement:** Focus on contacts who have not interacted with your recent email campaigns.

By using these segments, you can send tailored messages that are more relevant to your audience, increasing the chances of engagement and retention.

## **Key Features and Benefits**

### **Targeted Messaging**

Deliver more relevant content to your most engaged contacts, enhancing the effectiveness of your campaigns.

### **Customer Retention**

Create specific campaigns aimed at re-engaging contacts who may be at risk of losing interest, helping to maintain a strong relationship with your audience.

### **Cost Efficiency**

Save time and resources by avoiding sending emails to disengaged users, allowing you to focus your efforts on contacts who are more likely to engage.

## **How to Use Pre-Built Contact Segments**

### **Step-by-Step Guide**

1.  **Navigate to Campaigns:**
    -   Go to Marketing > Emails > From the Dropdown select Campaigns.

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/hlpt-support-widget.appspot.com/o/covers%2F12345%2F1731943679994_image.png?alt=media&token=a627d1e3-8291-4f04-b7bc-5e27d8695848)

**2\. Create a New Campaign:**

-   Click on "Create Campaign" and proceed to the send or schedule screen.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762206148782-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-03 164215.png>)

**3\. Select Pre-Built Segments:**

-   In the recipient list, choose “Pre-Built Segments” from the available options.

**4\. Choose Your Segment:**

-   Use the drop-down menu to select your preferred segment, such as contacts engaged in the last 7 days or those who have interacted with your last 5 email campaigns.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762206273100-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-03 164418.png>)

**5\. Send or Schedule:**

-   After selecting your segment, click “Send” to send the email immediately or “Schedule” to send it at a later time.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762206328493-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-03 164518.png>)

**Note:** Segmentation is done at the time of scheduling, not during execution.

## **FAQs**




**Question:** Are Subscribers and Contacts Different?
**Answer:** No, subscribers are the same as contacts. The terms are used interchangeably in this context.  




**Question:** What Defines an Engaged Contact?
**Answer:** A contact is considered engaged if they have opened or clicked on any marketing email sent through workflows, bulk actions, or email campaigns.  




**Question:** Can I Customize My Segments?
**Answer:** Currently, Pre-Built Segments are predefined based on common engagement metrics. Custom segmentation options may be available in future updates.  




**Question:** How Often Are Segments Updated?
**Answer:** Segments are updated at the time of scheduling. They are not dynamically updated during the execution of the campaign.  




**Question:** What Happens if I Use Multiple Segments?
**Answer:** If multiple segments are selected, the system will merge them to include contacts that fall into any of the selected categories, maximizing your reach.  




**Question:** How Are Pre-Built Segments Created?
**Answer:** Pre-built segments are automatically generated based on key behaviors, such as recent interactions or lack of engagement with your emails, eliminating the need for manual sorting.  




**Question:** Do these segments include contacts I manually added or imported yesterday?
**Answer:** New contacts will only appear in these segments once they have had a chance to engage. Since these segments are behavior-based (requiring an open or click), a brand-new contact won't appear in an "Active" segment until they interact with their first email from you.




**Question:** If a contact is in two segments I’ve selected, will they get the email twice?

**Answer:** No. The system automatically "deduplicates" the list. Even if a contact qualifies for three different segments, they will only receive one copy of your email.




**Question:** When exactly does the system determine which contacts fall into a specific segment for a campaign?
**Answer:** The segmentation of your contact list occurs at the time you schedule the campaign, rather than at the moment the emails are actually sent or executed.
