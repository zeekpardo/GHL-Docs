Creating and managing customer list custom audiences is a crucial aspect of optimizing your advertising campaigns. By leveraging custom audiences, businesses can target the most relevant individuals based on specific customer data. This guide explains how to create a customer list custom audience using smart lists or CSV file uploads.

#### **Key Features & Benefits:**

-   **Flexible Audience Management:** Allows the creation of custom audiences using static or dynamic updates, offering greater flexibility for targeting.
-   **Targeted Campaigns:** Dynamic audiences are updated automatically, ensuring campaigns always reflect the most current data.
-   **Improved Control:** Static audiences remain consistent, ideal for controlled tests and compliance.
-   **Simple Setup:** Easy integration with existing smart lists and the ability to upload customer lists via CSV files for seamless audience creation.

#### **How to Create a Customer List Custom Audience:**

1.  **Navigate to Audience Creation:**
    -   Start by clicking on the **‘Create a Custom Audience’** or **‘Create new audience > Custom Audience’** button within the Ads manager settings tab:

1.  **Select Your Source:**
    -   In the modal dialog that appears, choose **Customer List** as the source for your custom audience.
    -   Click **Next** to proceed to the next step of the process.

#### **Pro Tips:**

-   **Always Validate Your CSV File:** Make sure your CSV file follows the provided formatting guidelines to avoid errors during the upload process.
-   **Utilize Smart Lists for Dynamic Audiences:** Smart Lists are ideal for dynamic audience creation as they automatically update when new contacts are added or removed.
-   **Be Mindful of Audience Size:** Ensure that your audience meets the minimum size requirement for your campaigns to ensure effective targeting.

### **FAQ's**




**Question:** What is the difference between a static and dynamic audience?
**Answer:** A static audience includes only the contacts present in the smart list at the time of creation. A dynamic audience automatically updates with any changes to the contacts in the smart list.




**Question:** How can I upload a customer list via CSV?
**Answer:** Click on the **‘CSV Upload’** option, select your CSV file, and ensure it follows the required formatting guidelines for optimal results.




**Question:** Can I use multiple smart lists to create a single custom audience?
**Answer:** Yes, you can select one or more smart lists to create a custom audience, allowing for broader targeting.




**Question:** How do I know if my custom audience is ready to use?
**Answer:** Once the audience is created, it will appear in the audience list and be marked as **Ready** once it meets all necessary requirements.




**Question:** Are there any special terms or conditions for creating custom audiences?
**Answer:** Yes, you need to accept the customer list custom audience terms of service before creating the audience. You’ll be prompted to do so during the audience creation process.




**Question:** Can I edit the details of my custom audience after it's been created?

**Answer:** Yes, you can edit the details of your custom audience after it has been created. Simply navigate to the audience list in the Audience tab, select the audience you wish to edit, and update the audience name, description, or the selection of smart lists associated with it.  




**Question:** How can I ensure that my customer list CSV file is correctly formatted for upload?
**Answer:** Always validate your CSV file before uploading by ensuring it follows the provided formatting guidelines. This will help avoid errors during the upload process and ensure your custom audience is created correctly.




**Question:** If I delete a contact from my Smart List, will they be removed from my Facebook/Google ad audience?

**Answer:** Yes, if you have selected the **Dynamic** audience type. The system will sync the removal to the ad platform, ensuring you aren't wasting ad spend on someone who is no longer a relevant lead or has unsubscribed.




**Question:** Is there a minimum number of contacts required for the audience to work?

**Answer:** Yes. While the guide allows you to create the audience, you must ensure it meets the **minimum size requirement** of the specific ad platform (like Facebook or Google) for your campaigns to target effectively.




**Question:** Why is it important to include a description when setting up the audience?

**Answer:** Adding a description, along with a unique name, allows for **easier identification** and better organization within your Ads Manager, especially when managing multiple target groups.
