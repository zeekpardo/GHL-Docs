**How to leverage extra assets within a Google Ads campaign in the Ad Manager**

In , you can streamline the process of managing Business Logos, Call Extensions, and Sitelinks by setting them up at the campaign level. This ensures consistency across all Ad Groups and Ads within a campaign, saving time and reducing redundant configurations.

### **1\. Adding a Business Logo**

-   **Navigate** to the **Additional Assets** section when setting up or editing a Google ad campaign.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXegXO0Wdr19rrftnYZ-UPdLESojG1fHZ0Rh9zJzVH4pl1aIxIeWT-iM1ZOQP-x8TEI20uJvSRbHndeUaqQ6hCI9F5e3mal9Mk7zDl9OJczpo2hdoCgu_o46tVtw7MzGccyJhlFz?key=QMxxCFVKTh0QtWB2aEzjuA)

-   **Upload your logo** by selecting **Upload Logo** and choose an image from your media library. Ensure the logo is a **1:1 (square)** image for proper display.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfE0eCzoSrlkNNpOqSnWAwlaVs6x7iQZj19hCkL3HY-YzJuiYK2TtI_w4hex7FDMYNpuwKkXLYpnlAVFv06EzyrPk0p7FXCPKY42xBsfd6_VZkfneJcFhYXalgLXNoAlgi4ORKjSg?key=QMxxCFVKTh0QtWB2aEzjuA)

-   Once uploaded, you can **crop** or adjust the aspect ratio to fit the design of your ad.
-   After making the necessary adjustments, **save** the logo. It will be applied across all placements, including **Search**, **Display**, and **YouTube**.

**2\.Add Image Assets**

Image assets can be added to search ad campaigns to enhance visual appeal and boost engagement across various ad placements. To use this, In the Additional Assets section, select **Upload Images** to begin.  

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1758312418751-how to leverage modal 1.jpg>)

-    Choose images from the media library or upload new ones from your device.  
    Each campaign supports up to 20 image uploads.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1758312481312-Leverage Modal2.jpg>)

### **3\. Setting Up Call Extensions**

-   **Click** on the **Call Extensions** dropdown.
-   **Select** one or more existing phone numbers from your account. 

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfwK1joM_uB-5GbhZ4i_8wTL1_jYioSOtJ4k-2-Gj6W4aoHT6tKMl8y6OGoQW2Fa_dXcZvHGgCJBsXK0iBLm4RwL-MOLJr-tPZztRVsE49H0D9YeRK1P64APltdwrvN0u13re11aQ?key=QMxxCFVKTh0QtWB2aEzjuA)

-   If you need to add a new phone number:  

    -   Click **Create a new call**.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfH4SEWHm-2OWO3223xSkrrYG-MqGgvPY_tN9OTl-ydBkbqOp2kOWEMTLFuGuV08DbYyJ3AZD_WfMbOncHxj1DKgomos1WhxY1e8Ej4HuQy0gyFJ1sDrBGOIRQSlJjOnIeY3nbh4g?key=QMxxCFVKTh0QtWB2aEzjuA)

-   **Enter** the phone number and select the appropriate **Conversion Action Category** (e.g., Calls from Ads).
-   Click **Create** to add the number to your call extensions.

-   **Tip:** Call extensions enhance **mobile experience** and **CTR** by enabling users to dial your business directly from the ad.

### **4\. Configuring Sitelinks**

-   **Click** the **Sitelinks** dropdown.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeyleidLgUI5Gee8641o5uLQz6Zeu-q0sYw_VfF4umSk4JSA7k0G6L0QV9woJYhhu-SYAmjnpvyddF7dNQSuQ7opLP47CT8BnmsFE_8r-v6IS2XUR4_TSCD5pbSZuMO70hkQpp6?key=QMxxCFVKTh0QtWB2aEzjuA)

-   **Select** from existing sitelinks or click **Create a new sitelink** to add one.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdum9_OgWE4LG3P6aLdpZVp11iecWl020pkr7uXppVts81JSYPI_VdmrgcFQc3DJ9gWH8feRHYYt1uwIQNKKqIx24CZAnaTM31Ty7dCqUd6tuQLyMMupsxcwwNLq41s8d10AgXvfA?key=QMxxCFVKTh0QtWB2aEzjuA)

-   Enter **Sitelink Text** (max 25 characters), and optional **Description Line 1 & 2**.
-   Provide the **Final URL** where the user should be directed.
-   Click **Create** to finalize the sitelink.

-   **Recommendation:** Adding **at least 4 sitelinks** optimizes your ad’s performance and visibility.

### **Fallback Logic**

If no assets are set at the campaign level, the system will default to the **account-level assets** (if available). If there are no account-level assets, the ad may run without these enhancements. For more information about account-level assets, please refer to ’s help documentation.

### FAQs




**Question:** What happens if I upload a non-square logo?
**Answer:** Only **1:1 (square)** images are accepted. Any non-square logo will be discarded without notice. Use the **crop** tool to convert your image into a square format.




**Question:** Can I add multiple phone numbers for call extensions?
**Answer:** Yes, you can add multiple phone numbers. These will rotate based on factors like user **location** and **device**.




**Question:** Why is my call extension marked as "Disapproved"?
**Answer:** Common reasons for disapproval include:

-   Invalid or **unverified number**
-   **Country code mismatch**
-   Formatting issues  
    Make sure the phone number complies with the required policies and resubmit after making corrections.




**Question:** How many sitelinks can I add to my campaign?
**Answer:** You can add **2-20 sitelinks**. For optimal performance, it's recommended to add at least **4 sitelinks**.




**Question:** Can I edit an existing sitelink or phone number?
**Answer:** Yes, you can edit any existing sitelink or phone number by clicking the **edit icon** next to the entry.




**Question:** What are the benefits of using campaign-level assets?

**Answer:

-   Saves time by applying assets across all **ads and ad groups**.
-   Ensures **brand consistency**.
-   Automatically includes features like clickable sitelinks and call buttons, enhancing ad performance.




**Question:** Will campaign-level settings override account-level assets?
**Answer:** Yes, **campaign-level assets** take priority over **account-level assets**. If no campaign-level assets are configured, account-level settings will be used as fallback.




**Question:** Can I add image assets to my Search campaigns?
**Answer:** Yes, Search campaigns now support image assets. You can upload up to **20 images per campaign** to make your ads more visually engaging and improve click performance.




**Question:** What happens if I don’t set campaign-level assets?
**Answer:** If no assets are set at the campaign level, the system will use account-level assets if available. If neither is configured, ads may run without logos, sitelinks, or call buttons, which could impact performance.  




**Question:** Where will my Business Logo be displayed once it is uploaded?

**Answer:** Once the logo is saved at the campaign level, it will be applied across all ad placements, including **Search, Display, and YouTube**.




**Question:** Do Image Assets appear every time my Search Ad is shown?

**Answer:** No. Google uses machine learning to decide when to show images based on the user's search intent, the device they are using, and the likely performance boost. To increase your chances, upload high-quality, relevant images and ensure your "Optimization Score" is healthy.




**Question:** What is the specific limit for the number of image assets I can upload to a single campaign?

**Answer:** Each campaign supports the upload of up to **20 images** to enhance visual appeal and engagement.
