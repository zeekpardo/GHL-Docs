**Ad Manager: Settings**

Ad Manager settings provide enhanced options for managing Meta Ad accounts, connected Facebook pages, and conversion pixels, allowing more control over ad assets and campaign data. By understanding these settings, users can optimize their campaigns, manage Facebook pages, and track conversions seamlessly.

## **How to Access Ad Manager Settings**

1\. Navigate to the **Ad Manager** home page located within the **Marketing** tab.

2\. Click the **Settings** icon to open the Ad Manager settings section.

3\. Explore the three main tabs: **Integrations**, **Pages**, and **Conversions**, for specific functionalities.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcnLoBKjiKMeEl1VOgF-nPm42Syi75_wqK8ZRLKxnDObBS-IYUREv3cn-PbpN01FAgnatVyE9ivcD4-DSxrDWRbHpiJ2xaDNGIxGaZBLZK66dUsGW0AzC85bO8rVXkiN7Iv6RXG6g?key=aUS5i7p6Pc8fqRv4TwcfyGmC)

### **Ad Account Tab**

The **Ad account** tab under Ad Manager settings lets you manage the Meta Ad account connected to your campaigns. **Currently, only one Meta Ad account can be connected.**

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcXOLBnjPM8Knec4a_3YlSc37DiIssiWTfysM45na0oBQgCmGw1uriYDNGE0jyr4DmI-JJUkyA_0hUBcpC_Sh2NiNHYuRHGm6FoxlrJ4IYkgMYVxFohvROBIneBM_aTd6CY14gYrw?key=aUS5i7p6Pc8fqRv4TwcfyGmC)

#### **Key Functions**

-   **Add or Remove Ad Account**: Connect your Meta Ad account when you first set up Ad Manager, or remove it to link a different account. When removing an ad account, you’ll be prompted for confirmation to remove it and all the campaigns which are present in Ad Manager will no longer be visible.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeDJZbpIqjsNq0KYax6Bj78JwZckS-4d7y3QeBTJyQ0irDkqsDp_uU8S2FaJIfKZS8Nhun57tt9ZMwuhbqO8LwelwBTyFbONMi84CptvUaM6-ya_15bwIPpnqGIf4LpyG5CQ5CFIQ?key=aUS5i7p6Pc8fqRv4TwcfyGmC)

-   **Restoring Campaigns on Reconnection**: If you reconnect the same ad account, all associated campaigns and pages will reappear without data loss.

### **Pages Tab**

The **Pages** tab lists all Facebook pages linked to your Ad Manager. You can add new pages or remove existing ones.

#### **Key Functions**

-   **Connect Facebook Pages**: Click **Connect Facebook Page(s)** to link pages associated with your Meta Ad account.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfko1GdWkDX8jDFT6QLmBZVj_hKbJL6247aJarEgjfDkfo_BUh0ZueMgux768Ev8pn1sXgbUKoLYPXA6nTPkfmZ5WgOQgJxNY_xdJKBeFpCFKkhQg9BCmGEO9iNE0AAMpf3EXs2Fw?key=aUS5i7p6Pc8fqRv4TwcfyGmC)

-   **Delete Pages**: To remove a page, click the delete icon. Note that published ads remain on Facebook but are no longer editable from Ad Manager.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfpoX2-hy4r-KzTQbOBrzI8mcvDmRaAyF8EEjejona-SLxklCSnKaL-KQ9M03-ZMmM05Ait9N3HzzZgVH2QawGFlL57WaKR83MPgwZndR6FoWTOx3xASfJy-T2b97WiLoBsYkZiKQ?key=aUS5i7p6Pc8fqRv4TwcfyGmC)

If you reconnect a previously removed page, all related campaigns will reappear in your Ad Manager dashboard, ready for continued use.

### **Conversions Tab**

The **Conversions** tab displays all conversion pixels, including those created within Ad Manager or brought in from your Meta Ad account.

#### **Key Functions**

-   **Create New Pixels**: Add new conversion pixels with the **Create New Conversion Pixel** option to track specific actions for your campaigns. The pixel ID and code are automatically generated, simplifying workflow creation for sending CAPI events back to Facebook.
-   **View Existing Pixels**: Monitor and manage all pixels connected to Ad Manager, including those from the Meta Ad account.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXc18MOs7eejyWq3ORSB3KFgMOUNEoQgilQgPElhbHCQddJDm28R2zliJMy2bMam0SW0hyOZiJHtE-h9JHa1Dr4EVfgAWUDlYokRASBRGc4EWxoCjS8puuZnFbx6yccrjstLRXbG?key=aUS5i7p6Pc8fqRv4TwcfyGmC)

**Note:** Currently, Ad Manager allows the creation of only one pixel, which can be utilized for both Lead and Funnel events without any limitations from Facebook.

## FAQs




**Question:** What happens if I delete the connected Meta Ad account?
**Answer:** Deleting the Ad account removes access to associated campaigns in Ad Manager. Reconnecting the same Ad account restores all data and campaigns without loss.




**Question:** Can I manage multiple Meta Ad accounts?
**Answer:** Currently, only one Meta Ad account can be integrated with Ad Manager. Support for multiple accounts may be introduced in future updates.




**Question:** What happens if I delete a Facebook page from the Ad Manager?
**Answer:** Deleting a page prevents future ads from being published through Ad Manager, but existing ads remain active on Facebook. Reconnecting the page restores the campaigns in Ad Manager.




**Question:** How many pixels can I create in Ad Manager?
**Answer:** As of now, Ad Manager supports creating one pixel, which can be used for both Lead and Funnel events.




**Question:** Do I need to configure the pixel manually after creating it?
**Answer:** No, the pixel ID and code are automatically generated, simplifying the process for integration with workflows and Facebook’s Conversions API.


**Question:  Do I need to manually configure the pixel after creation?
**Answer:** No, the pixel ID and code are automatically generated, simplifying integration with workflows and sending CAPI events to Facebook.


**Question:  What happens if I remove a Meta Ad account from Ad Manager?

**Answer:** Removing a Meta Ad account will unlink it from your campaigns and prevent access to any associated campaigns. However, if you reconnect the same account later, all associated campaigns and pages will be restored without any data loss.




**Question:** What happens if I remove a Facebook page from Ad Manager?

**Answer:** Removing a page prevents future ads from being published through Ad Manager. Existing ads remain live on Facebook. Reconnecting the page restores campaigns in Ad Manager.




**Question:** What happens to my data if I disconnect and then reconnect the same Meta Ad account?

**Answer:** If you reconnect the same ad account, all associated campaigns and pages will reappear in Ad Manager without any data loss.




**Question:** What is the primary benefit of the automatic code generation in the Conversions tab?

**Answer:** It simplifies the technical workflow for sending **Conversions API (CAPI)** events back to Facebook, ensuring your tracking is accurate without requiring manual coding or external pixel installations.
