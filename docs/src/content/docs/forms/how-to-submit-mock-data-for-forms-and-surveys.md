**How To Submit Mock Data for Forms and Surveys**

Submitting mock data for your survey or form will allow you to test the form/survey and see how the information looks in the contact profile. Mock data for your survey or form is essential to understanding how the information flows. Follow these 4 simple steps to input mock data for survey or form completion.

### **Step 1: Creating the Form/Survey**

**To access and use the Form or Survey Builder, follow these steps:**

1.  **Navigate to the Builder:**
    -   Go to **Sites > Forms/Surveys > Builder**.
2.  **Select Your Form or Survey:**
    -   Choose the specific form or survey you want to work on.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764618338997-image1.png)

1.  **Add Fields and Customize:**
    -   **Left Panel:** Use the left panel to add both standard and custom fields, giving you the flexibility to create mock data scenarios.
    -   **Right Panel:** Utilize the extensive style and design options on the right to tailor the appearance of your form or survey.
2.  **Add Elements and Style:**
    -   Incorporate the necessary elements into your form or survey and apply your preferred styles.
3.  **Save Your Work:**
    -   Once you’ve added and styled your elements, click **Save** to preserve your changes.

### **Step 2: Add the Form/Survey to your funnel**

-   Go to the page editor where you want this form/survey to be

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763754665044-image.png)

-   Add the form element

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763754702704-image.png)

-   Click on the form element to open the settings on the right, and edit your submit options as needed, then save.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775255429400-image4.png)

### **Step 3: Submit Mock Data**

-   Use the **Eye icon** to **preview** the funnel step containing the form.
-   Fill out the form as if you were a real user. Use dummy data (e.g., test@example.com) for testing.

**Note:** After submitting the form, will also create a contact within your contacts tab.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763754746784-image.png)

### **Step 4: See the results**

-   Go to the Forms/Surveys - Submissions - Filter by the Forms/Surveys - Check your test.
-   Click on the **Submitted At** to reveal detailed information on the submission on the right.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763754765662-image.png)

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I delete mock data after testing the form or survey?
**Answer:** Yes, mock data can be deleted by locating the contact created in your contacts tab and removing it manually.




**Question:** Will submitting mock data affect live submissions?
**Answer:** No, submitting mock data is only for testing purposes and does not impact live submissions.




**Question:** Can I test multiple forms or surveys simultaneously?
**Answer:** Yes, you can test multiple forms or surveys by submitting mock data for each one separately.




**Question:** Do I need to publish the form or survey to test it with mock data?
**Answer:** No, forms and surveys can be tested in the preview mode without publishing them.




**Question:** Can I use the same email address to test the form multiple times?

**Answer:** If you use the same email address, the system will usually update the _existing_ test contact rather than creating a new one. If you want to see how a brand-new contact looks, use a different "dummy" email for each test.




**Question:** What is the fastest way to "act like a user" and submit test data?

**Answer:** The most effective way is to use the **Eye icon** in the builder or page editor to "Preview" the live step. This opens the page in a functional state where you can fill out the fields and click the submit button just as a real lead would.
