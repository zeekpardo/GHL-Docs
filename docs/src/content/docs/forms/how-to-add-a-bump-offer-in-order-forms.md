A Bump Product is a Pre-checkout offer that presents an additional product to your customer. The Bump Product is shown in the checkout and can be added to the order with just a click. 

### Step One:

-   Navigate to the Sites menu on the left, Funnels at the top, open the particular Funnel, and select the funnel step where you would like to add a Bump Product.  
-   In the right corner, click on ‘Add Product.’ This is the same process you would use to add a regular product to the checkout. 

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768473614766-unnamed - 2026-01-15T184011.154.png>)

### Step Two: 

-   Choose the product you want to add and its price. 
-   Give it a name, and in **Additional Settings,** select the Bump Product option and click Save.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768473643495-unnamed - 2026-01-15T184041.359.png>)

### Step Three:

-   Now, edit the funnel page and add a two-step order form. 
-   Click “General” under the settings on the right-hand side. 
-   Scroll down to find the Order Bump option and toggle it on.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768473663706-unnamed \(78\).jpg>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768473676005-unnamed - 2026-01-15T184108.298.png>)

-   Save the changes and preview to make sure it is added.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768473699405-unnamed \(79\).jpg>)

### FAQ


**Question:What is a Bump Product?
**Answer:** A Bump Product is an additional offer presented to customers during the checkout process. It allows customers to add an extra product to their order with a single click, making the checkout process more convenient and potentially increasing overall sales.


**Question:How do I add a Bump Product to my funnel?
**Answer:** To add a Bump Product, navigate to the Sites menu, open the Funnel, and select the funnel step where you want to include the Bump Product. Click on ‘Add Product,’ choose the product and its price, then name it and select the Bump Product option in Additional Settings. Click Save to add it.


**Question:How do I set up the Bump Product on my funnel page?**Edit the funnel page and add a two-step order form. Go to “General” settings on the right-hand side, find the Order Bump option, and toggle it on. Save your changes and preview the page to ensure the Bump Product is properly added.**


**Question:Can I customize the name and price of the Bump Product?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize both the name and price of the Bump Product during the setup process. When adding the product, enter the desired name and price before saving the changes.


**Question:How can I verify that the Bump Product is correctly added to the checkout?
**Answer:** After saving the changes, preview your funnel page to check that the Bump Product appears in the checkout. Ensure it can be added to the order with a single click and that all settings are applied correctly.




**Question:** What happens if a customer doesn’t select the Bump Product?

**Answer:** If the customer does not click to add the Bump Product, it will not be included in the order and they will only be charged for the main product.




**Question:** Where can I find the setting to enable the Order Bump within the funnel page editor?

**Answer:** Within the funnel page editor, you must add a two-step order form, then navigate to the "General" section under the settings menu on the right-hand side and toggle the Order Bump option to "on.  




**Question:** Can I add more than one Bump Product to a single checkout page?

**Answer:** No, the system supports **one Order Bump** per checkout form. If you want to offer multiple additional products, we recommend using an **Upsell (OTO)** page, which appears _after_ the initial checkout is completed.




**Question:** At what stage of the customer journey is a Bump Product presented?

**Answer:** It is a "Pre-checkout" offer, meaning it is presented to the customer while they are still on the checkout page, before they finalize their primary purchase.
