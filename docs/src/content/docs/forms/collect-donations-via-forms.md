**Collect Donations Via Forms**  

You can now collect payments and donations through a form. These forms allow you to add a payment element, enabling you to collect either a specific donation amount or the client's desired donation amount.  
**  
How to Set It Up:**  

1\. Go to the Sites on the left.  

2\. Navigate to Forms at the top > From the Dropdown select Builder  

3\. Click on the + Add Form button in the top right corner.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764769490747-image3.png)

4\. Add the Collect Payment element to the form  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764769508943-image2.png)

Click on the options on the right side to customize your payment settings.  

**Features:**  
    **1. Live Mode:**

-   Toggle on the live mode to make payments live. For test payments, toggle off the live mode.  
    **  
    2\. Payment Type:**
-   Collect Payment: Choose this option to collect a specific amount.
-   Sell Product: Select this option to sell a product you have created. Note: You must have a product created under the Payments tab > Products.  
    **  
    3\. Suggest an Amount:**
-   Enter specific donation amount options and add as many as you want. This allows users to select a suggested amount directly.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1758916285440-03.png)

-   If the suggested amount option is toggled off, users will need to enter their desired donation amount manually, without any suggestions or options.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1758916496041-04.png)

**4\. Include Other Amount:**

-   This option allows users to select and enter their desired donation amount.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1758916700450-05.png)

**5\. Select Currency:**

-   Choose the desired currency in which you want to receive the payment.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1758916787660-06.png)

## FAQs




**Question:** What is "Live Mode"?  
**Answer:** Live Mode allows real payments. Toggle it off to test payments without processing real transactions.




**Question:** Can I collect a specific donation amount?  
**Answer:** Yes, select "Collect Payment" and set a fixed donation amount.




**Question:** What is the "Suggest an Amount" feature?  
**Answer:** It allows you to offer suggested donation amounts. Users can select one or enter their own amount if this feature is off.




**Question:** What is "Include Other Amount"?  
**Answer:** This option lets users enter a custom donation amount, even if suggested amounts are provided.




**Question:** Can I choose the currency for payments?

**Answer:** Yes, select your desired currency during form setup.  


**Question:How can I sell a specific product through the form instead of just a donation? Answer:** Select the "Sell Product" option under Payment Type. Note that you must first create the product under the Payments tab > Products for it to be available here.**




**Question:** Can I set up a "Recurring" donation or payment through this form?

**Answer:** No. The "Collect Payment" element on forms is designed for one-time transactions  




**Question:** How can I verify that my payment form works without actually charging a credit card?

**Answer:** You should toggle **Live Mode to "Off."** This allows you to run test transactions to ensure the form logic and redirection are working correctly before you begin accepting real money.
