**Rating Element for Forms, Surveys & Quizzes**

Collecting meaningful feedback is crucial for understanding customer satisfaction, preferences, and engagement. The Rating Element provides an easy and visually appealing way to gather ratings within Forms, Surveys, and Quizzes. This article explains the key features, benefits, and usage instructions for this versatile tool.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Universal Compatibility**The Rating Element works seamlessly across Forms, Surveys, and Quizzes, making it a flexible choice for various feedback and data collection needs.
-   **Multiple Icon Options**Choose from stars, hearts, thumbs-up, and other icons to suit the tone and style of your survey or quiz.
-   **Customizable Scale**Set your own rating scale, such as 1 to 5 points, and add custom labels like “Poor” to “Excellent” for clearer respondent guidance.
-   **Style and Layout Controls**Adjust icon alignment to left, center, or top. Customize selected and unselected icon colors to match your branding and improve visual clarity.
-   **Flexible Output Formats**Ratings can be stored as absolute values (e.g., 4), percentages (e.g., 80%), or fractions (e.g., 4/5), allowing integration with various reporting and automation tools.
-   **Comprehensive Submission Output**Captured ratings are visible in submission tables, contact records, PDF exports, and contact timelines, enabling easy review and analysis.

### **How to Use the Rating Element**

1\. Open the builder for your Form, Survey, or Quiz.

2\. Drag and drop the Rating Element into your desired position.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764275412693-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-27 153005.png>)

3\. Customize the icon style (stars, hearts, etc.), rating scale, and labels.

4\. Adjust the alignment and colors to fit your design.

5\. Select the preferred output format for storing ratings: absolute, percentage, or fraction.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764275432259-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-27 153023.png>)

6\. Save and publish your Form, Survey, or Quiz.

### **Pro Tips**

-   **Choose icons that fit your audience’s expectations** — for example, hearts may work well for customer satisfaction, while stars are more traditional and widely recognized.
-   **Use custom labels** to clarify the meaning behind each rating point, reducing ambiguity in responses.
-   **Integrate ratings with your automation system** via custom fields to trigger workflows based on specific feedback.
-   **Test on mobile devices** to ensure the rating element displays correctly and is easy to interact with across all platforms.
-   **Consider combining the Rating Element with open-ended questions** to capture qualitative insights alongside quantitative ratings.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I use the Rating Element in all types of Forms, Surveys, and Quizzes?
**Answer:** Yes, the Rating Element is universally compatible and can be added to any Form, Survey, or Quiz layout.




**Question:** How many icons can I include in the rating scale?
**Answer:** You can customize the scale to any number of points, such as 1 to 5 or 1 to 10, depending on your preference.




**Question:** Is it possible to change the icons from stars to something else?
**Answer:** Absolutely. The Rating Element supports multiple icon types, including stars, hearts, thumbs-up, and more.




**Question:** How is the rating data stored and displayed?
**Answer:** Ratings can be saved as absolute values (e.g., 3), percentages (e.g., 60%), or fractions (e.g., 3/5), and are visible in submissions, contact records, PDFs, and timelines.




**Question:** Will the rating element work well on mobile devices?
**Answer:** Yes, it is fully responsive and designed to provide a smooth experience on smartphones and tablets.




**Question:** Can I combine the Rating Element with other types of questions?
**Answer:** Yes, you can combine the Rating Element with open-ended questions to capture both quantitative and qualitative insights from your respondents.  




**Question:** Can I customize the colors of the Rating Element icons?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize the selected and unselected icon colors to match your branding, enhancing visual clarity.  




**Question:** Can I adjust the alignment of the Rating Element icons?
**Answer:** Yes, you can adjust the alignment of the icons to the left, center, or top to match your design preferences.




**Question:** Does the rating update the contact's profile automatically?
**Answer:** Yes, provided you have mapped the element to a specific Custom Field in the field settings. Each time the contact submits a new rating, the value in their profile will update to reflect their most recent feedback.




**Question:** Will the rating icons appear in the PDF export of a submission?

**Answer:** The PDF export will display the **stored value** (the number, percentage, or fraction) rather than the physical icons. This ensures the data remains clear and legible for internal reporting and administrative reviews.




**Question:** Can I adjust the alignment of the Rating Element icons?
**Answer:** Yes, you can adjust the alignment of the icons to the left, center, or top to match your design preferences.
