**How to Add or Remove Logos for Forms (Agency Branding)**

You may want to add or remove logo from a form. We will walk you through the simple steps of adding or removing a logo from your forms using the New Form Builder. Follow these simple steps to learn how:

## Accessing Form Builder and Editing Form:

-   Navigate to the Form Builder.
-   Click "Edit" for the form you wish to update.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764864071669-image2.png)

-   From the menu on the right-hand side, select Styles.
-   Scroll to the bottom, and under Miscellaneous, you can toggle on or off "Agency Branding" to hide or show the 's logo.

### **Short Notes**

-   **Logo Display Control**: The ability to control the display of the logo provides flexibility in branding your forms while maintaining the required professional appearance.
-   **Quick Changes**: The toggle feature allows for quick and easy changes to the form’s branding without needing extensive customization or technical skills.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I customize the logo appearance on my form?
**Answer:** No, the Form Builder allows you to only show or hide the logo. Customization of the logo appearance is not supported.




**Question:** Will hiding the logo affect form functionality?
**Answer:** No, hiding the logo will not affect the functionality of the form. It only changes the visual branding.




**Question:** Can I add my own logo instead of the logo?
**Answer:** Currently, you can only toggle the logo on or off. Adding a custom logo is not supported.




**Question:** What if I don't see the "Agency Branding" option?
**Answer:** Ensure that you are using the New Form Builder. If the option is still not visible, check with your system administrator or support team.




**Question:** How do I access the "Agency Branding" option to show or hide the logo?

**Answer:** To access the "Agency Branding" option, navigate to the Form Builder, click "Edit" for the form you want to update, and then select "Styles" from the right-hand menu. Scroll to the bottom, and under "Miscellaneous," you'll find the toggle to control the display of the logo.




**Question:** Where exactly in the Styles menu can I find the toggle for the logo?

**Answer:** The "Agency Branding" toggle is located at the very bottom of the Styles menu, specifically under the **Miscellaneous** section  




**Question:** If I hide the logo on one form, does it hide it on all my forms?
**Answer:** No. This setting is form-specific. You must go into each individual form’s "Styles" menu to toggle the branding on or off. This allows you to keep the logo on some forms while hiding it on others.




**Question:** Will the logo reappear if I update other parts of my form's design?
**Answer:** No. Once you have toggled "Agency Branding" off and saved your changes, the logo will remain hidden even if you modify other styles or fields later.
