**Hyperlink Text Fields In Forms & Surveys**

You can add clickable links to text fields in surveys. This is designed to improve the customer experience by giving users easy access to more information or resources directly through these links. It's all about making interactions smoother and more informative for everyone involved.

### Why Does It Matter To Your Business?

-   **Increased Engagement:** By embedding hyperlinks in text fields, businesses can guide respondents to additional resources, increasing engagement and potentially driving more informed interactions.
-   **Improved Customer Experience:** Businesses can enrich their surveys by providing direct access to privacy policies, terms of service, or other important information through hyperlinks

### Steps to Add a Hyperlink in Surveys Builder

#### 1\. Access Sites and Surveys

-    Navigate to "Sites" from the left navigation menu.
-    Click on "Surveys" from the upper navigation menu.
-    Create a new survey or edit existing ones.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764014563950-image.png)

#### 2\. Add Text Element

-   In the surveys builder, click on the "+" icon to add a text element by drag and drop.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769445480036-image2.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769445497759-image6.png)

#### 3\. Insert Hyperlink

-    Select the text where you want to add the hyperlink.
-    Click on the three dots (ellipsis) to open additional options.
-    Choose the hyperlink icon.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769445524832-image4.png)

#### 4\. Enter URL

-   Enter the URL you want to link to in the provided field.
-    Click the "Confirm" button to add the hyperlink.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769445546199-image3.png)

#### 5\. Save and Preview

-   Save your changes.
-   Preview the survey to see the added hyperlink in action.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769445565862-image1.png)

Adding hyperlinks in surveys enhances the customer experience by offering seamless access to important resources. This feature creates a more professional and engaging interaction for the business and its audience within the platform. It allows businesses to easily direct users to additional information, external websites, or relevant documents, thereby improving navigation and usability. By integrating hyperlinks, businesses can effectively enhance communication and provide valuable resources to their customers, ensuring a more streamlined and informative experience.

### FAQ’s




**Question:** Why is this feature important for my business?
**Answer:** Embedding hyperlinks in your surveys can significantly improve customer engagement by directing users to relevant resources. This not only provides more information but also helps create a smoother and more informative interaction for your audience.




**Question:** How do hyperlinks improve the customer experience?
**Answer:** By including hyperlinks, you can provide direct access to essential documents like privacy policies, terms of service, or any other important resources. This makes your surveys more informative and user-friendly.




**Question:** Can I add multiple hyperlinks in a single text element?
**Answer:** Yes, you can add multiple hyperlinks by selecting different portions of text and linking each to a different URL.




**Question:** Can I use hyperlinks to send respondents to a "Thank You" page?

**Answer:** While you can add a link to the final text element of a survey, the best practice for a "Thank You" experience is to use the **Survey Settings** to define a **Redirect URL**. This automatically sends the user to a new page the moment they click "Submit," rather than requiring them to click a manual link.
