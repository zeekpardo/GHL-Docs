Incorporating opportunity custom fields into forms and surveys is a game-changer for data collection. This feature enables users to gather opportunity-specific information seamlessly, optimizing the efficiency of tracking and managing opportunities.

**How-to Steps:**

**1\. Access the Form or Survey Builder:**

-   Navigate to the form or survey builder in Sites > Forms/Surveys
-   Add a form/survey or edit an existing one.

**2\. Navigate to the Custom Fields Section:**

-   Go to the 'Add Form/Survey Element' section by clicking on the + icon on the top left corner.
-   Go to the Add Object Fields section.
-   Select the 'Opportunity' category from the object fields dropdown.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767956481427-unnamed \(32\).jpg>)

**3\. Add Desired Opportunity Custom Fields:**

-   Choose the specific opportunity custom fields you want to include in your form or survey.
-   Drag and drop these fields into your form or survey layout.

**4\. Configure Opportunity Settings:**

-   In the settings modal, configure the opportunity settings by selecting a pipeline, stage, status, and opportunity value.
-   Utilize the 'Create Pipeline' option if you need to set up a new opportunity pipeline.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767956499361-unnamed - 2026-01-09T190132.405.png>)

**5\. Save and Submit:**

-   Save your form or survey.
-   Upon submission, an opportunity will automatically be created and mapped to the contact generated from the submission.

**Benefits:**

-   **Efficiency:** Streamlines the process of tracking and managing opportunities directly from form and survey submissions.
-   **Customization:** Offers flexibility in configuring opportunity settings to match your specific needs.
-   **Automation:** Reduces manual effort by automatically creating opportunities upon form or survey submission.This way you will be able to seamlessly move data inputted by the contact directly into your opportunity-related custom fields. 

**Pro Tips:**

-   **Organize Custom Fields:** Utilize the collapsible format to keep your custom fields neatly organized under their respective parent folders.
-   **Optimize Pipelines:** Regularly update your opportunity pipelines to reflect the current sales process, ensuring accurate data collection and tracking.

**FAQs**




**Question:** How do I ensure all necessary data is captured?
**Answer:** Include all relevant custom fields and configure the settings appropriately to capture comprehensive opportunity data.




**Question:** Can I create multiple opportunity pipelines?
**Answer:** Yes, you can create and configure multiple opportunity pipelines to suit different sales processes or product lines.




**Question:** Can I automate data mapping to opportunity fields?
**Answer:** Yes, when a contact submits the form or survey, the data entered is automatically mapped to the associated opportunity fields, streamlining your workflow and reducing manual entry errors.  




**Question:** Is it possible to set up a brand-new pipeline directly within the form or survey builder?
**Answer:** Yes. Within the opportunity settings modal, you can utilize the 'Create Pipeline' option if you need to establish a new opportunity pipeline without leaving the builder.




**Question:** How can I keep my custom fields organized if I have a large number of them?

**Answer:** You can utilize the **collapsible format** within the builder to keep your custom fields neatly organized under their respective parent folders.




**Question:** What happens automatically once a contact submits a form containing these fields?

**Answer:** An **opportunity is automatically created** and mapped to the contact. Any data entered into the opportunity-specific custom fields is moved directly into their opportunity record.
