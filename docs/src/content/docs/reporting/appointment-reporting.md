**Appointment Reporting**

Efficient appointment management is a cornerstone of successful business operations. Understanding how your appointments are booked, which days are most popular, and how your team performs can provide critical insights that drive business growth. This article will walk you through the enhanced Appointment Reporting feature, designed to offer a more comprehensive view of your appointment data, streamline your workflow, and optimize your scheduling efforts.

## **How to Use the Appointment Report Tab**

-   **Access the Appointment Report:**
    -   Navigate to the Reporting section in the left-hand menu.
    -   Click on "Appointment Report" at the top of the page to access your appointment data.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764714039811-image4.png)

-   **Explore the New Charts:**

Review the bar charts to see metrics like:

-   **Numeric Charts by Appointment Status:** View the count of appointments grouped by status, such as booked, confirmed, cancelled, new, and more.
-   **Top Performing Calendars:** Identify the most popular calendars based on booking volume, helping you understand which ones are performing best.
-   **Top 5 Users by Appointments:** Find out which users are securing the most appointments, making it easier to acknowledge and reward top performers.
-   **Most Popular Appointment Days:** Discover which days of the week have the highest number of appointments to optimize your scheduling and resources.
-   **Top 5 Calendars with Cancellations:** Track the calendars with the highest cancellation rates, helping you identify potential issues and improve retention.
-   **Top 5 Calendars with Reschedules:** Monitor the calendars with the most reschedules to help improve scheduling efficiency.

## FAQ


**Question:How do I access the Appointment Report?

**Answer:** You can access the Appointment Report by navigating to the Reporting section in the left-hand menu and selecting "Appointment Report" from the top menu.


**Question:What are the benefits of the bar charts in the Appointment Report?

**Answer:** The bar charts provide clear, visual insights into key metrics such as the most booked calendars and popular appointment days, helping you make informed decisions about scheduling and resource allocation.


**Question:How does the API latency reduction improve my experience?

**Answer:** The API latency reduction means that the Appointment Report page now loads 80% faster, allowing you to access your data more quickly and efficiently.


**Question:Can I customize the Appointment Report to suit my needs?

**Answer:** While the Appointment Report offers detailed insights, for more customization, you can use custom dashboards to add and modify widgets that display the specific metrics you need.




**Question:** What is the significance of detailed channel information in the report?
**Answer:** Detailed channel information helps you identify which platforms or methods are most effective in driving appointments, enabling you to optimize your marketing and outreach strategies.




**Question:** Can I export my appointment data from the Appointment Report?

**Answer:** Yes, you can export your appointment data by clicking the “Export” button in the Appointment Report section. This allows you to download a list of your appointment data for further analysis or record-keeping.




**Question:** If a user deletes an appointment from the calendar, does it disappear from the report?

**Answer:** If an appointment is deleted, it is removed from the active "Booked" counts. However, if you are looking for historical data on why appointments were lost, we recommend using the **"Cancelled"** status instead of deleting, so you can still track those metrics in your "Top 5 Calendars with Cancellations" chart.




**Question:** Can I see if my appointments are coming from my website vs. my social media?

**Answer:** Yes. The **Detailed Channel Information** breaks down the sources of your bookings, allowing you to see which platforms (like a specific landing page or a social media link) are driving the most traffic to your calendars.
