**Advanced Data Points in Call Reporting**

This feature has a range of enhancements and features designed to streamline the call reporting experience and empower the user with deeper insights into their customer interactions.

-   **Streamlined Call Reporting Flow**: We’ve reimagined the reporting interface to provide a separate view of the incoming and outgoing call reports, making it easier than ever to analyze and compare call data.

-   **Call Status breakdowns**: We understand the power of seeing information about the progress and outcome of a call and that’s why we include the breakdown of different call statuses.
    -   Missed/No Answer
    -   Declined/Cancelled
    -   Failed
    -   Voicemail statuses
    -   Incoming and Outgoing calls.
    -   Busy

-   **First-time callers chart** - We’ve included charts that showcase first-time calls status breakdown to give insights about leads who reached out to the business for the very first time

-   **Introduced call duration stats** - Average call duration and total call duration as two stats for all-time calls and first-time calls to help determine the quality of conversations.

-   **Uncover Top Call Sources:** With our interface, you can effortlessly explore the top sources of incoming calls. Gain insights into which source is performing better for you and optimize your marketing strategies.
-   **Uncover Top Call Agents:** Top Agent chart for outgoing calls, helping the user identify their most productive team members and replicate their success.

-   **Enhanced Table Functionality:** Allows the user to switch between different tabs, including All, Incoming, and Outgoing, ensuring they have the flexibility to focus on the specific data needed. Furthermore, we’ve introduced column selection, empowering the user to customize the table view by selecting the columns that matter most.

## FAQ’s




**Question:** How do I apply filters in the Call Reporting feature?

**Answer:** To apply filters, click on the Filters button within the Call Reporting interface. From there, you can select the desired filters, such as date range, call status, source, and more, to customize the data view according to your needs.




**Question:** Can I customize the table view in Call Reporting?

**Answer:** Yes, the enhanced table functionality allows you to customize the table view by selecting the columns that are most relevant to you. This feature provides flexibility in focusing on the specific data needed for your analysis.




**Question:** What can I learn from the average and total call duration stats?

**Answer:** Average and total call duration stats help you evaluate the quality of customer interactions. Longer or shorter call durations may indicate the need for adjustments in your approach, training, or customer engagement strategies.




**Question:** Why is it important to identify top call sources?

**Answer:** Identifying top call sources helps you understand which marketing channels or campaigns are most effective in driving calls to your business. This allows you to optimize your marketing efforts by focusing on the sources that generate the best results.




**Question:** Can I track calls by their outcomes using this feature?

**Answer:** Yes, you can track calls by their outcomes using the call status breakdown, which includes categories like missed, declined, failed, and voicemail. This breakdown provides valuable insights into the progress and results of your calls.




**Question:** How can I use the insights from the Top Call Agents chart?

**Answer:** Insights from the Top Call Agents chart can be used to identify and reward high-performing team members, replicate successful strategies across your team, and improve overall productivity and customer service quality in your call center or business.


**Question:Does the system show call recordings or transcripts?
**Answer:** If call recording is enabled in your account, you can access recordings directly from the Call Reporting table. For compliance reasons, ensure you have permission to record and review customer calls.


**Question:How can I use the insights from Call Reporting to improve my business?
**Answer:** Use metrics like call duration, call outcomes, top sources, and top agents to optimize marketing campaigns, improve team performance, and enhance customer service quality.




**Question:** If a call is "Redirected," how is it reported?|
**Answer:** The report will show the initial source of the call and the final outcome. If a call rings through an IVR (Press 1 for Sales) and is then answered, it will show as an **Incoming Call** that was successfully answered by an agent.




**Question:** What specific insights can I gain from the "First-time callers" chart?
**Answer:** The First-time callers chart provides a status breakdown specifically for leads who are reaching out to your business for the very first time. It includes data on their call outcomes and duration stats (average and total), helping you determine the quality of initial conversations with new prospects.
