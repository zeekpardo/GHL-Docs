### Understanding The Attribution Report

The Attribution Report feature empowers business owners to gain deeper insights into the lead sources and understand their marketing efforts. With this report, you can track where your leads are coming from, whether it's through the UI (Manually made), third-party sources (like Zapier or other inbounds), or other avenues.

You can track if your lead comes from: 

-   UI (Manually made)
-   Third-party (Zapier or via any other inbounds)
-   Others

To Access the attribution reports:

-   Navigate to the Reporting tab.
-   Click on the Attribution Report section.

General Overview: 

-   **Revenue Numbers:** This toggle will help you see the revenue numbers alongside the count of numbers.

### FAQ: 




**Question:** What is the Attribution Report used for?

**Answer:** The Attribution Report helps business owners track and analyze the sources of their leads, providing insights into the effectiveness of their marketing efforts. It shows where leads originate from, including UI entries, third-party integrations, and other sources.




**Question:** How can I see revenue numbers in the Attribution Report?

**Answer:** There is a toggle within the Attribution Report that allows you to view revenue numbers alongside the count of leads. This feature helps you correlate lead sources with revenue generation.




**Question:** What does the "Lead by Source" chart show?
**Answer:** The "Lead by Source" chart provides a visual representation of the various sources of leads, helping you identify which sources are driving the most traffic to your business.




**Question:** What information does the "Lead by Medium" graph provide?

**Answer:** The "Lead by Medium" graph breaks down leads based on the medium through which they entered the database, allowing you to evaluate the performance of different mediums.




**Question:** Can I use this report to compare multiple lead sources at once?
**Answer:** Yes, the report provides a comprehensive overview, allowing you to compare multiple lead sources and mediums side by side to identify top-performing channels.




**Question:** What should I check if lead data appears incomplete?

**Answer:** Ensure that lead sources and integrations are properly configured and that leads are being captured with the correct attribution details.




**Question:** How are leads from external apps like Zapier classified in the report?

**Answer:** These are categorized under **Third-party** sources. This group includes leads coming in via Zapier or any other inbound integrations.
