Consistency and efficiency are the cornerstones of a successful content strategy. Enter **Category Queues**, an innovative feature that allows you to organize, schedule, and repurpose your content seamlessly. With Category Queues, you can maximize the reach of your evergreen posts, maintain a consistent online presence, and save time by automating the management of your content.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

#### **1\. Streamlined Content Organization**

-   **Categorized Management**: Group your posts into specific categories, such as Blog Posts, Tips, Promotions, or Announcements, for tailored scheduling and easy management.
-   **Simplified Workflows**: Access and manage your evergreen content with intuitive tagging and categorization.

#### **2\. Optimized Scheduling**

-   **Targeted Timing**: Assign specific posting days and times to each category, ensuring your audience sees the right content at the right moment.
-   **Automated Consistency**: Let Category Queues handle your posting schedule, keeping your content pipeline active without constant manual effort.

#### **3\. Content Repurposing Made Easy**

-   **Revive Valuable Posts**: Republish high-performing evergreen content to extend its lifecycle and reach.
-   **Focus on Innovation**: Free up your time for new content creation while your existing posts continue working for you.

#### **4\. Flexibility and Control**

-   **Pause and Resume**: Manage your queues on your terms, pausing or resuming categories as needed.
-   **Editable Content**: Update and refine previously published posts before re-queuing them for maximum impact.

### **How to Use Category Queues**

Follow these steps to get started with Category Queues:

#### **Step 1: Prepare Your Content (Pre-requisite)**

Navigate to the Marketing tab on the left> Social planner on the top right>Create a new post or edit an existing post:  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764174708709-111.png)

Ensure your existing posts are tagged with appropriate categories. Categories act as the foundation for organizing and scheduling your posts.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764174735589-112.png)

#### Step 2: Navigate to the Category Queue Option

-   Access the New Post section.
-   Choose the Category Queue option to begin setup.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764174815024-113.png)

#### Step 3: Select a Category

Select or create a new category on the first screen via the dropdown. Choose the relevant category for which you want to create a queue. For example, you can have categories like “Promotions” or “Tips.”

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770060912396-image8.jpg)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770060929137-image5.jpg)

#### **Step 4: Enable Preferences**

Customize your queue with the following options:

-   **Future Posting**: Automatically includes new posts tagged to the category in the queue.
-   **Prioritize Content**: When checked, new content is added to the top of the queue; when unchecked, it is appended to the end.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770060981902-image3.jpg)

#### **Step 5: Set a Posting Schedule**

-   Select specific days and times for your posts to go live.
-   Ensure your schedule aligns with audience engagement patterns for optimal visibility.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770061051263-image1.jpg)

#### Step 6: Review and Edit

-   You can visualize your queue in a **Weekly or Monthly Calendar View**.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770061094237-image13.jpg)

-   Use the drag-and-drop feature to change the post order or use the **Reschedule Queue** tool to shift the entire timeline to a new start date.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770061190940-image4.jpg)

-   While creating a new post you can also add it to the existing queue

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770061247736-image10.jpg)

-   Address any flagged errors before finalizing the queue.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770061274396-image11.jpg)

#### **Step 7: Finalize the Queue**

-   Click **Queue Post** to schedule posts or save them as drafts for future editing.
-   Confirm your queue to activate consistent posting.

#### **Step 8: Manage Your Queue**

Pause or resume the queue anytime using the toggle in the content management tab.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764175097356-119.png)

-   Delete queues when no longer needed, or adjust your strategy by creating new ones.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764175190500-120.png)

**Important Notes:**

-   The created category queue cannot currently be edited or rescheduled for a different timeframe.
-   Category queues can be created for previously published content that is assigned to a specific category.
-   The content of previously published posts can be edited or updated during the creation of a category queue.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** Can I edit posts in a Category Queue?

**Answer:** Yes, you can update the content or media of posts before finalizing the queue.




**Question:** What happens to new posts tagged to a category?

**Answer:** New posts automatically get added to the queue, in case the new posts need to be added to the front of the queue, please select the option '**Enable Future Posting**' and '**Prioritise Content**'. 




**Question:** Can I change the schedule for an existing queue?

**Answer:** Currently, the schedule for a queue cannot be edited once created. If adjustments are needed, you’ll need to create a new queue.




**Question:** Can I pause or delete a queue?
**Answer:** Yes, you can pause or resume a queue at any time. If a queue is no longer needed, it can also be deleted.




**Question:** What if there’s an error in my queue?
**Answer:** Errors will be flagged during setup, allowing you to address them before confirming the queue.




**Question:** Do Category Queues work with all social media platforms?

**Answer:** Yes, any platform supported by the Social Planner will follow the queue’s schedule, as long as the post meets platform-specific requirements.




**Question:** If I pause a queue, does it lose its place?
**Answer:** No. When you toggle a queue to **Pause**, it simply "freezes" exactly where it is. When you click **Resume**, it picks up with the very next post in line according to your schedule.




**Question:** Can I change the date of just one single post within my queue?
**Answer:** No, while you can reorder posts or reschedule the entire queue's timeline, **single-post rescheduling** within a queue is not currently supported.
