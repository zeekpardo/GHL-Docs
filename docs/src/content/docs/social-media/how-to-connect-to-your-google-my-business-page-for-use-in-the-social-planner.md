The Social Planner tool allows you to connect multiple social media accounts and manage them in one area. By connecting your Google My Business (GBP), you can use the Social Planner to schedule, create, and push out posts to your Google My Business pages, along with Facebook Pages and Instagram as well.  

**Step 1: Connect your Google Business Profile**

-   Navigate to Marketing on the left → Social Planner at the top → Settings (or gear icon on top right)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770754388280-image4.png)

-   Click “Connect a new Google My Business profile”.
    -   **NOTE_:_** If you're connecting a profile for the first time, give permissions to by choosing the Gmail Account associated with your GBP page.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770754420092-image6.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764175051279-image4.jpg)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762963990766-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-12 111259.png>)

-   If connecting any profile for the first time, you will have enabled permissions for .

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762964020128-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-12 111327.png>)

-   Select the page(s) you wish to connect. You’ll see a list of all the pages connected to your profile. Select the one(s) you wish to connect to the Social Planner.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762964057226-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-12 111401.png>)

-   Choose the GBP Location(s) for the Social Planner.
    -   **_NOTE:_** If the GBP location is already added to the planner via , it will show the message "location already added". If the GBP account has more than 10 locations, it will show an error that _"This location belongs to a chain. The Local Post API is disabled for this location"._

You can manage all of your accounts from the Settings icon, anytime. Add, change, or remove connected pages and groups anytime.

### **FAQ's**




**Question:** How do I connect my Google Business Profile account to the Social Planner?
**Answer:** To connect your Google Business Profile account, go to Social Planner > Settings, then click "Connect a new Google Business Profile account." Follow the prompts to give permissions and select the GBP pages you wish to connect.




**Question:** What should I do if I receive a "location already added" message?
**Answer:** This message indicates that the GBP location is already connected to the Social Planner via . You cannot add the same location again.




**Question:** Why do I see an error saying, "This location belongs to a chain"?
**Answer:** If your GBP account has more than 10 locations, you may see this error because the Local Post API is disabled for chain businesses. Unfortunately, you cannot post to such locations using the Social Planner.




**Question:** Can I manage multiple GBP accounts within the Social Planner?
**Answer:** Yes, you can connect and manage multiple GBP accounts, as well as Facebook and Instagram accounts, from the Social Planner settings. You can add, change, or remove connected pages anytime.




**Question:** Where can I find help if I encounter issues with publishing to GBP?
**Answer:** If you experience any issues with GBP publishing, refer to our troubleshooting article, ‘Google Business Profile Publishing Troubleshooting,’ for detailed assistance.




**Question:** Does the Social Planner support specific GBP post types like "Offers" or "Events"?
**Answer:** Yes. When creating a post for GBP in the Social Planner, you can select specific post types. This allows you to add "Call to Action" buttons (like _Book_, _Order Online_, or _Learn More_), date ranges for events, and coupon codes for special offers.
