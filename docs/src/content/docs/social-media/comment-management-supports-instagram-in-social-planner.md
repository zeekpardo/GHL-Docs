**Comment Management Supports Instagram in Social Planner**

Effective social media management is all about timely engagement and organized communication. The **Comment Management** feature in the **Social Planner** now supports **Instagram**, allowing you to handle all your Instagram interactions directly from one unified dashboard. This functionality centralizes engagement, enabling faster responses, improved audience connection, and simplified workflow management, all without switching between multiple platforms.

### Key Features and Benefits

**1\. Unified Comment Management**Manage Instagram comments alongside other supported platforms from the same interface, keeping all your social interactions in one place.

**2\. Direct Replies**Respond to comments on Instagram posts directly within the Social Planner, reducing response time and boosting engagement.

**3\. Threaded Conversations**Maintain context and clarity in your discussions with threaded replies, allowing smooth and organized conversations.

**4\. Comment Filters**Easily toggle between **Latest** and **Top** comments to prioritize engagement where it matters most.

**5\. Mentions and Tagging**Mention or tag public Instagram profiles while replying to increase visibility and encourage interaction.

### How to Use Instagram Comment Management

Navigate to **Marketing → Social Planner → Comments.**

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764012075019-image4.png)

Select the **Comments** tab to view all your Instagram post comments.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764012091642-image3.png)

 Click **Reply** to respond directly to individual comments or continue existing threads.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764012107281-image2.png)

Use **Mentions** to tag other users in your replies for better visibility.Apply **Filters** to switch between **Latest** and **Top** comments based on your engagement strategy.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764012125873-image1.png)

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I manage comments for multiple Instagram accounts?
**Answer:** Yes, as long as each Instagram account is connected under your dashboard, you can manage comments for all of them from the Social Planner.




**Question:** Can I reply to private messages from this section?
**Answer:** No, this feature is limited to managing public comments on posts. Direct messages are handled separately under the messaging or inbox section.




**Question:** Are mentions supported for all types of Instagram accounts?
**Answer:** Mentions are available for public profiles only. Private profiles cannot be tagged using this feature.




**Question:** Can I view deleted or hidden comments here?
**Answer:** No, once a comment is deleted or hidden on Instagram, it will not appear in the Social Planner interface.




**Question:** Will other social media platforms be supported soon?
**Answer:** Yes, additional platform support is planned for future releases to provide a fully unified comment management experience.




**Question:** Can I respond to multiple comments at once?
**Answer:** No. Each comment must be replied to individually to maintain proper threading and context.




**Question:** Do I get a notification within the platform when a new Instagram comment arrives?

**Answer:** Yes, new comments will appear in your **Comments** tab. You can also set up internal notifications or workflows to alert your team whenever a new interaction is detected on your social posts.




**Question:** How do I prioritize which comments to answer first?

**Answer:** You can use the **Comment Filters** to toggle between **Latest** and **Top** comments. This helps you focus on the newest interactions or the most popular ones based on your current engagement strategy.
