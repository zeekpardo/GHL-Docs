**Using The Image Editor in the Social Media Planner**

## **Enhanced Visual Appeal**

The Image Editor allows for significant enhancements in an image's visual aspects. Users can adjust various parameters such as brightness, contrast, saturation, and color balance. This functionality is crucial for making photos vibrant and eye-catching, which is especially important in a medium where visuals are the first point of contact with the audience.

## **Correction of Imperfections**

One of the critical features of the Image Editor is its ability to correct imperfections. Red-eye, blemishes, scratches, and other common issues can be easily fixed, ensuring your images look their best. This is particularly useful in portrait photography or when restoring old photos.

## **Creative Freedom**

The Image Editor offers a wide range of creative tools, including filters and effects, for those looking to add a unique touch to their images. These features allow users to experiment with different styles and artistic expressions, thereby creating truly unique images that stand out on social media.

## **Cropping and Resizing**

The Image Editor also provides the ability to crop and resize images. This is crucial when creating content for various platforms, as each has its specific dimension requirements. Whether it's for social media, websites, or print materials, the Image Editor ensures that your images are perfectly tailored to the medium.

## **Advanced Features for Professionals**

For professionals who require more advanced features, platform’s Image Editor doesn’t disappoint. It offers tools akin to those found in high-end software like Adobe Photoshop or Lightroom, including advanced retouching and color grading. These capabilities are invaluable for photographers, designers, and artists who use CRM for their social media management.

**Question:** Does the Image Editor have advanced features for professional use?

**Answer:** To access the Image Editor, go to 'Marketing > Social Planner' and create a new post using the post composer. Upload an image, hover over it, and click on the editing options to begin making adjustments.




**Question:** What are some of the future enhancements planned for the Image Editor?
**Answer:** Future** enhancements for the Image Editor include AI-driven editing to simplify the process with smart recommendations, text support for adding custom text to images, and options to personalize images further with stickers and emojis.




**Question:** Can I adjust basic visual settings like brightness and contrast in the Image Editor?
**Answer:** Yes, you can fine-tune basic visual settings such as brightness, contrast, saturation, and even apply blur effects to your images, allowing for a high degree of customization to match your creative vision.




**Question:** How can I use the filters in the Image Editor?
**Answer:** To use filters, access the editing options after uploading your image in the Social Planner. Choose from various filters like black and white, duotone, or vintage to give your images a distinct look and feel.




**Question:** Will the Image Editor allow for text additions in the future?
**Answer:** Yes, adding custom text to images is one of the planned enhancements for the Image Editor, which will expand the creative possibilities and allow for more personalized content creation.




**Question:** Can I undo or revert changes made in the Image Editor?
**Answer:** Yes, you can undo recent edits or revert to the original image at any time before saving, ensuring you don’t lose your base file.




**Question:** What specific editing options can I use to enhance my images in the Image Editor?

**Answer:** The Image Editor provides a variety of tools to enhance your images, including adjustments for brightness, contrast, saturation, and color balance. Additionally, you can crop, resize, apply filters (like black and white, duotone, or vintage), and apply blur effects to achieve your desired visual effect.




**Question:** Can I use the Image Editor for images I’ve already scheduled?  
**Answer:** Yes. You can edit a scheduled post, hover over the image to open the editor, make your adjustments, and save. The updated image will be the one that goes live at the scheduled time.




**Question:** Does using an Image Slider affect my page load speed?

**Answer:** Image sliders can impact performance if the images are very large. To maintain a fast load time, we recommend compressing your images before uploading and keeping the total number of slides between 3 and 5.




**Question:** Does the Image Editor offer specific presets for social media cropping?
**Answer:** Yes. Within the editing mode, users can adjust their images using various presets specifically designed for different social media platforms, including square, landscape, and portrait orientations, as well as custom sizes.
