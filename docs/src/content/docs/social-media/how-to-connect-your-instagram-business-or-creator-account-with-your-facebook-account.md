**How To Connect your Instagram Business or Creator Account with your Facebook Account**

To successfully post to the Instagram Business Profile, including direct publishing, your Instagram Business profile must be connected to a Facebook Page or Creator Profiles. 

You don't need to publish to the Page when you use Instagram, but the permissions to manage your Instagram profile are granted through Facebook. This is a requirement from Facebook (which possesses Instagram).

###  **Before you connect, you need to:**

-   Confirm that your Instagram profile is a Business or Creator profile.
-   Confirm that you have an Admin or Editor Page role for the Facebook Page if it's a Classic Page type or Facebook access with full or partial control on a New Page type.

## **How to use** 

### **Step 1: Go to Facebook**

-   In a new browser tab, sign in to your Facebook account.
-   On the home page, please click the "Accounts Icon" located on the top right side of the screen.

## FAQs 




**Question:** Why do I need to connect my Instagram Business profile to a Facebook Page?
**Answer:** Connecting your Instagram Business profile to a Facebook Page is required to grant the necessary permissions for managing your Instagram account through Facebook. This connection allows for features like direct publishing and enhanced account management capabilities.




**Question:** Do I need to publish content on my Facebook Page if I connect it to my Instagram Business profile?
**Answer:** No, connecting your Instagram Business profile to a Facebook Page does not require you to publish content on the Facebook Page. The connection is solely for permissions and management purposes.




**Question:** What types of Instagram profiles can be connected to a Facebook Page?
**Answer:** Only Instagram Business profiles or Creator profiles can be connected to a Facebook Page. Ensure your Instagram account is set to one of these profile types before attempting to connect.




**Question:** Can I connect multiple Instagram Business profiles to a single Facebook Page?

**Answer:** Yes, you can connect multiple Instagram Business profiles to a single Facebook Page, provided you have the necessary permissions on that Page.




**Question:** Does this connection allow me to schedule Instagram posts?

**Answer:** Yes. Once connected, direct publishing and scheduling features for Instagram Business profiles are enabled through the linked Facebook Page.




**Question:** How can I tell if my Instagram is already a Business or Creator profile?

**Answer:** Go to your Instagram profile, tap the three lines (menu), and select **Settings and Privacy**. Look for **"Account type and tools"** or **"Business tools and controls."** If you see options to "Switch to Personal Account," you are already on a professional profile. If it says "Switch to Professional Account," you are currently on a personal one and need to upgrade it first.




**Question:** Where is the "Accounts Icon" located to switch to my Facebook Page?

**Answer:** The Accounts Icon is located in the **top right corner** of your Facebook home page. Clicking this allows you to select the specific Page you want to manage and connect to Instagram.
