Welcome to our guide on connecting your TikTok personal account to the Social Planner. This feature allows you to easily manage and schedule your TikTok content directly from within the Social Planner. While support for TikTok business accounts is on the roadmap, this guide will walk you through the steps to connect your personal TikTok account seamlessly.

### Why Connect TikTok to the Social Planner?

Connecting your TikTok account to the Social Planner can streamline your social media management by allowing you to:

-   Schedule and plan your TikTok content.
-   Manage your content from a single dashboard, reducing the need to switch between apps.
-   Keep your social media strategy organized and consistent.

## How to Connect Your TikTok Personal Account to the Social Planner

Follow these simple steps to connect your TikTok personal account:

### Step 1: Access the Social Planner

-   Log in to your account.
-   Navigate to the **Marketing** section in the main menu.
-   Click on **Social Planner**.
-   Go to social planner settings or click on the gear icon at the top right.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764189991657-image8.jpg)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769740552145-image3.jpg)

### Step 2: Open the Connection Wizard

-   Upon visiting the Social Planner, you will see a connection wizard with an option to connect your social media accounts.
-   Alternatively, you can click on the **Settings** icon (gear icon) in the Social Planner overview to start the connection process.

### Step 3: Add Your TikTok Account

-   Click on **Add Account**.
-   You will see options to add both TikTok individual and business accounts. Select the **TikTok Individual Account** option.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763041977297-unnamed - 2025-11-13T215234.956.png>)

### Step 4: Log into Your TikTok Account

-   You will be prompted to log into your TikTok account. Enter your TikTok credentials to proceed.
-   Follow the on-screen instructions to authorize the Social Planner to access your TikTok account.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763042066456-unnamed - 2025-11-13T215414.221.png>)

### Step 5: Choose Your TikTok Account for Integration

-   After logging in, choose the TikTok personal account you want to connect with the Social Planner.
-   Confirm your selection to complete the integration.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763042109629-unnamed - 2025-11-13T215453.019.png>)

### Step 6: Manage Your Connected Accounts

-   Once connected, you can manage your TikTok account from the **Settings** icon in the Social Planner.
-   Here, you can view your connected accounts, manage settings, and add or remove accounts as needed.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764190036524-image3.jpg)

### Step 7: Compose Posts with Both Image and Video content

-   During post creation, Users can create a single post that includes both images and videos, allowing simultaneous scheduling across platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok, saving time by avoiding multiple individual posts.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764190071720-image9.jpg)

**Enhanced Preview and Slider**

Preview posts in high resolution, view them in a slider format and edit directly within the composer for a more streamlined experience.

## FAQ's




**Question:** What should I do if I encounter login issues while connecting my TikTok account?
**Answer:** Ensure that your TikTok credentials are correct. If the issue persists, check your internet connection or try restarting the connection process from the Social Planner settings.




**Question:** How do I disconnect my TikTok account from the Social Planner?
**Answer:** To disconnect your TikTok account, go to the **Settings** icon in the Social Planner, select the account you want to disconnect, and follow the prompts to remove it.


**Question:  Can I connect multiple TikTok accounts to the Social Planner?
**Answer:** Yes, you can connect multiple TikTok Accounts. Just click on Add Socials in Social Media Settings and select TikTok.


**Question:Does the Social Planner post-composer create posts with both images and videos in one go?
**Answer:** Yes, it allows users to create posts with both images and videos in one go, schedule them across platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok, preview posts in high resolution, manage errors with color-coded alerts, use custom values for personalized content, and adjust the text input area for easier content management.




**Question:** What if my TikTok account doesn’t appear after logging in?
**Answer:** Ensure that you are logged into the correct TikTok personal account. If the account still doesn’t appear, try logging out of TikTok and logging back in through the Social Planner.




**Question:** Is there any watermark or certification notification when scheduling TikTok posts?
**Answer:** Yes, a watermark and certification notification appear during scheduling to ensure transparency and clarity within the Social Planner or Planner calendar.




**Question:** What is the benefit of the "Calling From" feature when searching for accounts?
**Answer:** While the article primarily focuses on social connections, it highlights that the "Calling From" feature allows you to search for accounts or phone numbers using their **friendly names** rather than just the raw digits/ID. This makes identifying the correct integration much faster and more intuitive.
