**How To Connect LinkedIn for the Social Planner**  

The Social Planner tool allows you to connect multiple social media accounts and manage them in one area. By connecting your LinkedIn account, you can use the Social Planner to schedule, create, and push out posts to your LinkedIn, along with Facebook Pages and Groups, Instagram, and Google My Business.

## **Navigation**

**Step 1:** Navigate to the Marketing tab on the left and click on the Social Planner at the top.

**Step 2:** Click on the icon below the Social Planner tab, and click on Add Account, then on LinkedIn.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I connect both my LinkedIn personal profile and LinkedIn business pages to the Social Planner?
**Answer:** Yes, you can connect both personal profiles and business pages to manage them simultaneously in the Social Planner.




**Question:** What permissions do I need to grant when connecting my LinkedIn account?
**Answer:** You need to allow permission for the Social Planner to access your LinkedIn account, which typically includes permissions to post, manage pages, and access analytics.




**Question:** Can I schedule posts for LinkedIn using the Social Planner?
**Answer:** Yes, once your LinkedIn account is connected, you can schedule posts for your LinkedIn profile or pages, just like with other connected social media accounts.




**Question:** What happens if I don’t see my LinkedIn account as an option when adding accounts?
**Answer:** If your LinkedIn account doesn’t appear, make sure it’s integrated properly and that you have granted all necessary permissions. You may need to log in to LinkedIn and try reconnecting.




**Question:** Can I connect multiple LinkedIn pages to the Social Planner?
**Answer:** Yes, you can connect and manage multiple LinkedIn pages from the Social Planner, provided you have admin access to those pages.




**Question:** Are there any limitations when posting to LinkedIn through the Social Planner?
**Answer:** Yes, posts created from third-party apps on LinkedIn pages may not have the “Share” and “Repost” options due to LinkedIn’s restrictions on page posts.




**Question:** How do I select which LinkedIn profile or page to post from?
**Answer:** After connecting LinkedIn, choose the desired profile or page within the Social Planner interface when creating a new post.  




**Question:** Why don't I see the "Share" or "Repost" options on the posts I make to my LinkedIn page via the Social Planner?
**Answer:** This is a specific limitation set by LinkedIn for all third-party applications. Posts created on LinkedIn pages through external tools do not include the native Share or Repost buttons.




**Question:** Do I need to provide any special permissions during the setup?

**Answer:** Yes. LinkedIn requires a "handshake" permission. When prompted, you must click **"Allow"** to enable the Social Planner to communicate with your LinkedIn account.




**Question:** Can I connect a LinkedIn Group to the Social Planner?

**Answer:LinkedIn’s API primarily supports **Personal Profiles** and **Business Pages**. Direct posting to LinkedIn Groups through third-party tools is restricted by LinkedIn to prevent spam. To share content in a Group, we recommend posting to your Page first and then manually sharing that link within your specific LinkedIn Groups.
