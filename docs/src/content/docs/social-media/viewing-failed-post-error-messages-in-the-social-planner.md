**Viewing Failed Post Error Messages in the Social Planner**

There are a variety of reasons when a social post may fail to successfully post to Facebook, GMB, Instagram, or LinkedIn. One of the most common examples is a Social Account token expiring. This can happen after a time period (set by the social media program itself - such as LinkedIn account tokens expiring after 60 days and Facebook tokens expiring after 90 days), or if an account password is changed.

Below is a list of common reasons and solutions, along with the relevant error codes. This will help you troubleshoot any issues you may experience. When you navigate to the Social Planner and view “Failed” posts, you can see the error code, along with a message about the failure reason and the proposed solution.

### FAQs




**Question:** Why did my social post fail to publish?Answer**: Social posts may fail for various reasons, such as expired tokens, insufficient permissions, or content violations. Specific error codes and messages provide details on the failure and how to resolve it.




**Question:** What is a Social Account token, and why does it expire?Answer**: A Social Account token is an authorization key that allows the Social Planner to post on your behalf. Tokens expire after a set period, such as 60 days for LinkedIn and 90 days for Facebook, or if the account password is changed.




**Question:** What should I do if I see an "OAuthException" or error codes 102, 109, 463, or 467 for Facebook?Answer**: These errors indicate that the Social Account token has expired. Please reconnect your Facebook account to resolve this issue.




**Question:** How can I resolve a temporary error from Facebook, such as error codes 1, 2, or "Unexpected Error"?Answer**: Perform a hard refresh on your browser to clear the cache and retry posting after a few minutes.




**Question:** What does Facebook error code 3 mean, and how can I fix it?Answer**: This code means the token has expired, or the account lacks posting permissions. Reconnect the account with the necessary posting permissions.




**Question:** What should I do if I encounter a “Temporary Block” on Facebook?
**Answer**: Facebook temporarily restricts posting for certain periods. Wait for the block to lift before retrying. Avoid posting duplicate content or violating community standards to prevent recurring blocks.




**Question:** What causes duplicate post errors?
**Answer**: Most platforms restrict posting identical content within a short time frame (usually 12 hours). Modify your post copy or wait before reposting.  




**Question:** Why am I getting a "Location Disabled" error when trying to post to Google Business Profile?

**Answer:** This usually occurs if the location is part of a brand chain. Google often restricts local post API access for chain locations. Additionally, ensure your GBP setup is complete and the account is reconnected in the Social Planner.




**Question:** Why did my Instagram carousel post fail to publish?

**Answer:** Instagram Business Accounts require carousel posts to have more than 2 images or videos and fewer than 10 images or videos. If your post does not meet these specific quantity requirements, it will result in error code 400-2207028.
