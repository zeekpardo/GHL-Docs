**Setting Up Email Notifications for Expired Social Media Accounts in the Social Planner**

The Social Planner is where you can host your social accounts such as GBP, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram. Each social account has a ‘token’ which maintains its integration to . Various platforms have ranging token expiration dates - for example, Facebook has a 90-day limit while LinkedIn has a 60-day limit. A token may also expire if a password is reset for a social media account.

**Note:** To maintain the tokens, accounts may need to be reconnected from time to time. This can be done based on a notification or by manually visiting the Social Planner settings section.

### **Email Notifications in Social Planner**

Users will receive email notifications for the following events in the **Social Planner**:

-   **Account Expired:** Notification for social accounts whose tokens have expired.
-   **Account Pre-Expiry:** For accounts like Facebook pages with a 60-day expiry, a reminder is sent around the 50th day to refresh the token.
-   **Request for Approval:** Alerts users when a scheduled social post requires approval.
-   **Post Approved - Creator Update:** Notifies the post creator when their scheduled post is approved.
-   **Post Rejected - Creator Update:** Notifies the post creator if their scheduled post is rejected.
-   **Post Failed:** Alerts selected users when a scheduled post fails to publish.

## **Navigation** 

Follow along to learn how to create email notifications for expired accounts in order to stay on top of the connections in your Social Planner:

-   Navigate to the Social Planner
-   Click into the Manage Social Accounts section (using the gear icon)
-   Once in the Social Planner Settings > navigate to the Notifications tab

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1760985571876-image.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1760985591508-image.png)

-   Click the pencil icon and a window will appear, then choose the User(s) to notify, the frequency of notification, and the email template.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1760985704496-image.png)

-   Save your changes and you’re all set!

**NOTE:** If you change the # of day reminders, this will be applied to the next time the account expires.

**Question:** How to change the default email template for notifications? 
Answer**: To change the default email template for notifications, go to Emails > Template, click on New, and select Email Marketing Template. In the template library, find the System Template option on the left, select the desired template, and load it for editing. Navigate to the Social Planner notification section, modify the template as needed, and save the changes.

**Question:** When does the Pre-Expiry Notification trigger?  
Answer**: If enabled, the pre-expiry email notification is sent when a social account, such as a Facebook page, has a token set to expire in 90 days. The notification will be triggered on the 80th day, reminding the user to refresh the token.




**Question:** What happens when a social media account token expires?Answer**: If a token expires, the account may disconnect from the Social Planner, causing scheduled posts and other activities to stop until the account is reconnected.




**Question:** How do I know when a token is about to expire?Answer**: You can receive notifications via email if you set up notifications for expired accounts, or you might see alerts within the Social Planner settings.




**Question:** What should I do if a social account disconnects due to an expired token?Answer**: You will need to reconnect the account manually by navigating to the Social Planner settings and reauthorizing the account.




**Question:** Can I change how often I receive email notifications about token expiration?Answer**: Yes, when setting up notifications, you can choose the frequency of reminders, as well as customize the email template.




**Question:** Does changing the number of day reminders affect the current token expiration cycle?Answer**: No, the change in reminder settings will only apply for the next expiration cycle after you make the change.




**Question:** Can I receive notifications for social media accounts other than Facebook or LinkedIn?  
Answer**: Yes, notifications can be set up for all social accounts connected to the Social Planner, including Instagram, GBP, and other supported platforms.




**Question:** Who receives an email notification when a scheduled social media post is approved or rejected?
**Answer:** The creator of the post will receive an update notification if their scheduled post is approved or rejected.




**Question:** If my token expires, will my already scheduled posts be deleted?

**Answer:** No, your scheduled posts are not deleted. However, they will stay in a **"Failed"** or **"Paused"** status because the system no longer has permission to push them to the platform. Once you reconnect the account and refresh the token, you can manually retry or reschedule those posts.
