Connecting your YouTube account to a social planner can significantly streamline your content creation and scheduling process. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you link your YouTube account effectively.

## **Steps to Connect Your YouTube Account**

**1- Access the Social Planner:**

-   Navigate to the marketing section of your dashboard.
-   Select the option labeled “Social Planner.”
-   Click Settings or click the gear icon on the top right to go to social planner settings. From there, click on ‘Connect YouTube’

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742204429-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 112257.png>)

**2- Authenticate Your Account:**

-   Once you click on ‘connect YouTube’, you will be prompted to log into your Google account. Make sure to use the credentials associated with your YouTube channel.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742234609-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 112348.png>)

-   Grant the necessary permissions for the social planner to access your YouTube data.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742263030-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 112415.png>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742292683-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 112447.png>)

**3- Select Your YouTube Account:**

-   Once connected, you will see a list of your YouTube accounts (if you have multiple).
-   Choose the account you want to connect for posting content.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742323842-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 112516.png>)

**4- Confirmation:**

-   After selecting your account, confirm the connection. You should now see your YouTube account listed within the social planner.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742384230-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 112618.png>)

**Create a New YouTube Post:**

1- Navigate to "Create New Post."

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742406810-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 112642.png>)

2- Choose the YouTube channel where you want to post.

3- Enter your video description in the caption, including any hashtags and emojis.

4- Select either the "Video" or "Shorts" option.

5- Add a title for your YouTube Video or Short.

-   Note: When the Shorts option is selected, "#shorts" is automatically appended to the title.

6- Set the privacy level: Public, Private, or Unlisted.

7- Optionally, add a follow-up comment to your post.

8- Schedule your YouTube Video or Short.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742437028-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 112710.png>)

9- Alternatively, Users can also create a single post that includes both images and videos, allowing simultaneous scheduling across the entire platforms, and saving time by avoiding multiple individual posts.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763742468670-unnamed.png)

**Schedule your YouTube posts and shorts in the social planner** 

This enables users within to schedule both YouTube videos and Shorts directly through the Social Planner, offering a streamlined way to manage content and maintain a consistent publishing schedule. This supports improved workflow efficiency by allowing creators and teams to prepare and organize video content in advance.

To use this, simply open the Social Planner, select your connected YouTube channel

Upload your video or Short, set its details, adjust visibility preferences, and schedule the post.

**Video and Shorts Dimensions for YouTube:**

-   Users can upload videos and shorts up to 1GB in size.
-   Thumbnails can be PNG or JPEG and up to 2MB for verified users.
-   Shorts must be 60 seconds or less and should have a vertical aspect ratio, ideally 9:16.

## **Pro Tips**

-   **Check Permissions:** Ensure that the social planner has all necessary permissions for posting, managing videos, and accessing analytics to fully utilize its features.
-   **Keep Your Credentials Secure:** Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on your Google account to keep your YouTube channel safe.
-   **Regularly Update Content Strategy:** Use the analytics provided by the social planner to refine your content strategy based on viewer engagement and trends.

### **FAQ**




**Question:** How do I connect my YouTube account to the Social Planner?
**Answer:** To connect your YouTube account, go to the Marketing section > Social Planner. Click on "Connect YouTube," log into your Google account, and grant the necessary permissions. Select the YouTube account you want to connect to and confirm the connection.




**Question:** What permissions are needed for the Social Planner to post on YouTube?
**Answer:** The Social Planner needs access to post content, manage videos, and view analytics. Make sure to grant these permissions during the connection process to fully utilize all features.




**Question:** What are the video requirements for uploading to YouTube via the Social Planner?
**Answer:** You can upload videos up to 1GB in size. For shorts, ensure they are 60 seconds or less and have a vertical aspect ratio (9:16). Thumbnails can be PNG or JPEG and up to 2MB for verified users.




**Question:** Can I schedule both YouTube videos and shorts?
**Answer:** Yes, you can schedule both YouTube videos and shorts. Simply create a new post, select the "Video" or "Shorts" option, and follow the steps to schedule your content.




**Question:** What should I do if my YouTube connection fails?
**Answer:** First, ensure you have entered the correct credentials and granted all necessary permissions. If issues persist, try reconnecting your account or checking for any updates or changes in your Google account settings


**Question:Does the Social Planner post-composer create posts with both images and videos in one go?

**Answer:** Yes, It allows users to create posts with both images and videos in one go, schedule them across platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok, preview posts in high resolution, manage errors with color-coded alerts, use custom values for personalized content, and adjust the text input area for easier content management.




**Question:** How does the Social Planner help manage content more efficiently?

**Answer:** It provides features like high-resolution preview sliders, color-coded error/warning messages, custom values for dynamic content, adjustable text input areas, and watermark/certification notifications, allowing smoother content management and scheduling.




**Question:** If I schedule a video for multiple platforms (e.g., YouTube and Instagram), can I have different captions for each?
**Answer:** Yes. After creating the main post, you can use the **"Customize for Each Channel"** feature to tweak your YouTube title and description while keeping a different caption for Instagram or TikTok, all within the same scheduling window.




**Question:** What happens if I delete a video in the Social Planner after it has been posted?

**Answer:** Deleting a post within the Social Planner after it has gone live will **not** delete the video from your YouTube channel. To remove a live video, you must log into your YouTube Studio directly.




**Question:** What happens to the title of my video if I select the "Shorts" option in the Social Planner?
**Answer:** When you select the **Shorts** option, the system automatically appends **"#shorts"** to your video title. This helps ensure your content is correctly categorized by YouTube's algorithm as a Short.
