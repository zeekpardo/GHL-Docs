**How to Share PDFs as Images in a LinkedIn Carousel Post**

In today’s social media-driven landscape, visually engaging posts can make all the difference in reaching and engaging an audience. LinkedIn’s PDF Carousel format has emerged as a powerful way to capture attention, as research shows that PDF Carousel posts can achieve over double the reach of standard image or text posts. Now, with the Social Planner, users can easily create LinkedIn Carousel posts, upload PDFs as images, and streamline content sharing across social channels.

## **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **PDF to Carousel Conversion**: Upload PDFs to LinkedIn, converting each page into individual images for a dynamic and informative Carousel post.
-   **Photo to PDF Conversion**: Import images into the Social Planner, merge them into a single PDF, and post directly to LinkedIn.
-   **Expanded Reach**: Carousel posts typically gain more visibility, making them ideal for sharing expertise or promoting content that resonates with audiences.
-   **Effortless Editing**: Rename PDF files, add watermarks, and delete images from the preview easily for a customized post.

## **Notes and Tips**

-   **File Limitations**: LinkedIn allows up to 300 pages and a maximum size of 100 MB per document.
-   **Image Cropping**: When posting to Instagram, images are automatically resized to fit Instagram’s aspect ratio.
-   **Media Deletion**: You can remove media by hovering over the preview and clicking the “X.”

### FAQs




**Question:** How many images can I convert into a PDF for LinkedIn posting?
**Answer:** LinkedIn supports up to 300 pages per PDF, with a maximum file size of 100 MB.




**Question:** Can I add multiple LinkedIn accounts to the Social Planner?
**Answer:** Yes, you can connect multiple LinkedIn pages or profiles, selecting the desired one during post-creation.




**Question:** What happens if I upload an oversized PDF?
**Answer:** You will be prompted to reduce the file size to meet LinkedIn’s 100 MB limit.




**Question:** How do I ensure images fit Instagram’s aspect ratio?
**Answer:** When selecting Instagram, the Social Planner automatically crops images to meet platform specifications.




**Question:** Can I cross-post Carousel content to other platforms?
**Answer:** Yes, LinkedIn Carousel content can be shared across platforms that support similar formats; otherwise, individual images will be displayed as standard posts.




**Question:** Can I edit a PDF after uploading it to the Social Planner?

**Answer:** Yes, you can rename the PDF, delete specific pages/images, and add watermarks before posting to LinkedIn.




**Question:** Is it possible to schedule LinkedIn Carousel posts?

**Answer:** Yes. After uploading and customizing your PDF or images, you can choose “Schedule” to publish the post at a later date.




**Question:** Can I add branding or custom labels to my PDF before it goes live?

**Answer:** Yes, the Social Planner allows for customization by letting you rename your PDF files and add watermarks to your content for branding consistency.




**Question:** How exactly does a PDF turn into a carousel on LinkedIn?

**Answer:** When you upload a PDF to the Social Planner for a LinkedIn post, the system automatically converts each page of that PDF into individual images to create the dynamic carousel format.
