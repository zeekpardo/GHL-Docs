### **FAQs on Duplicating Triggers**

**1\. Why should I duplicate a trigger instead of creating a new one from scratch?**

Duplicating a trigger is useful when you need to run the same action repeatedly but with slight modifications. It saves time by allowing you to start with an existing trigger and make necessary adjustments instead of setting up a new trigger from scratch.

**2\. How do I duplicate a trigger?**

Follow these steps to duplicate a trigger:

1.  Navigate to **Triggers**.
2.  Click the **Edit** dropdown menu on the right side of the trigger you want to duplicate.
3.  Select **Duplicate**.
4.  Enter a **New Trigger Name** and choose your account as the **Location**.
5.  Click **Copy**.
6.  Access the new trigger created. Make any necessary adjustments and click **Save** in the top right corner.
7.  The new trigger will be in draft mode. Activate it when you’re ready to use it.

**3\. What should I do after duplicating a trigger?**

After duplicating a trigger:

-   Click into the new trigger to make any additional adjustments.
-   Save your changes by clicking **Save** in the top right corner.
-   The trigger will initially be in draft mode. Activate it when you’re ready to deploy it.

**4\. Can I edit the duplicated trigger immediately?**

Yes, after duplicating a trigger, you can immediately click into the new trigger to make any necessary adjustments. Don’t forget to click **Save** to apply your changes.
