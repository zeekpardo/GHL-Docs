Unlock seamless communication with your clients through outbound phone numbers. In this comprehensive guide, we'll walk you through the steps of acquiring a digital phone number within the app. Get ready to enhance your call and messaging experience effortlessly!

### **Step 1:** Navigate to Settings > Phone Numbers:

-   Begin your journey by clicking on Settings and selecting Phone Numbers from the dropdown menu. Here, you'll find the gateway to your enhanced communication setup.

### **Step 2:** Click Add Number:

-   Initiate the process by clicking on the "Add Number" button. This action sets the stage for acquiring your customized phone number tailored to your preferences.

### **FAQs:**

**What are the benefits of Toll-Free Numbers over Local/Phone numbers?**

Toll-free numbers offer increased message throughput and the ability to send messages even while the registration is in progress. They are ideal for businesses aiming for efficient communication.

**What advantages do Local/Phone numbers offer for businesses?**

Local/Phone numbers provide a localized presence, fostering trust and familiarity among local customers. They are perfect for businesses looking to establish a strong local connection.
