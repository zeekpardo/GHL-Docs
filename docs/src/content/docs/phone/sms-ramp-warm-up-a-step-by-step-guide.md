**SMS Ramp/Warm up: A Step-by-Step Guide**

The SMS Messaging Ramp Card is designed to help users track and understand their SMS sending limits and progression. This progress card provides a clear, visual representation of the user's current level, the actions required to reach the next level, and an overview of future messaging capabilities. The goal is to provide transparency, making it easier for users to see where they stand and how they can progress to higher SMS limits. By following the step-by-step guidance, users can easily unlock higher levels and increase their daily SMS allowance.

### **Key Features and Benefits:**

-   **Clear Progression Overview**: Users can see their current level and the required actions to move to the next level.
-   **Visual Representation**: The card displays locked and unlocked levels, providing a clear roadmap to higher messaging limits.
-   **Step-by-Step Guidance**: Provides actionable steps, such as sending a specific number of messages, to unlock higher levels.
-   **Motivation and Transparency**: Helps users stay on track and understand the ramp-up process, reducing confusion.

### **How to Use:**

**1\. Access Messaging Limits**: Within your account, navigate to Settings on the bottom left > Phone Numbers > Click on the Advanced Settings tab at the top and select the "Messaging Limit".

### FAQs




**Question:** What is the SMS Messaging Ramp Card?
**Answer:** It’s a feature that helps you track your SMS sending limits and shows you the steps to unlock higher levels for more messaging capacity.




**Question:** How do I unlock the next level?
**Answer:** Complete the required action for your current level, such as sending a set number of messages, and then wait for the upgrade.




**Question:** What happens when I reach the highest level?
**Answer:** Once you reach the highest level, you’ll receive a congratulatory message and be guided to contact your agency or support team for further upgrades.




**Question:** What kind of activities help unlock levels faster?
**Answer:** Sending legitimate, high-quality messages (not spam or bulk unsolicited content) that comply with SMS best practices helps you progress smoothly through levels.




**Question:** Is the progress card visible to all users?
**Answer:** Yes, each user with SMS permissions in their account can access the Messaging Ramp Card to track their progress and required actions for leveling up.




**Question:** Will my level "reset" if I don't send any messages for a few weeks?
**Answer:** No. Once you have unlocked a level, you keep that status. You do not need to "re-warm" your number unless you stop using the service for an extended period of several months.




**Question:** What happens if I exceed my daily limit before I reach the next level?

**Answer:** If you reach your daily limit, the system will prevent further outgoing messages for the remainder of that 24-hour period. Any scheduled messages will be queued and sent once your limit resets or your level increases.  




**Question:** What happens to my account immediately after I hit the message limit required to unlock a new level?

**Answer:** Once you reach the limit required for the next level, your account will be **temporarily blocked for 24 hours** before the next level is officially unlocked.
