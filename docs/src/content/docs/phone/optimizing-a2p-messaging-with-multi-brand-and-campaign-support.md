**Optimizing A2P Messaging with Multi Brand and Campaign Support**

This feature introduces robust support for managing multiple A2P (Application-to-Person) brands and campaigns, enhancing your messaging capabilities. This allows better organization and optimization of your sending strategies, making it easier to manage and scale your campaigns effectively.

**How-to Steps:**

**1\. Accessing the Trust Centre:**

-   Navigate to the Trust Centre section.
-   You will see tabs for listing the brands and campaigns.
-   Trust Centre section with new tabs for brands and campaigns.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764359711437-image.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764359781666-image.png)

**2\. Upgrading A2P Brand:**

-   Go to the Brands tab.
-   Click on the "Upgrade Brand" CTA to change from Sole Prop to Standard/High-volume Standard brands.
-   You can maintain multiple brands for one subaccount.
-   Brands tab with the 'Upgrade Brand' CTA highlighted.

**3\. Creating Multiple Campaigns:**

-   Open the Campaigns tab.
-   Click on the "Create Campaign" CTA to start a new campaign using an approved brand.
-   Specify different use cases for each campaign.
-   Campaigns tab with the 'Create Campaign' CTA highlighted.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764359803959-image.png)

**4\. Linking Phone Numbers:**

-   Go to the Manage Numbers page.
-   Link different phone numbers to different campaign use cases.
-   Manage the Numbers page with linked phone numbers.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764359819341-image.png)

**5\. Auto-Submission of A2P Campaigns After Brand Approval**

-   Once a brand is approved, the associated A2P campaign is automatically submitted, removing the need for manual action and speeding up the process. This reduces confusion, delays, and manual effort, ensuring a smoother registration experience.

**Benefits:**

-   **Improved Organization:** Manage multiple brands and campaigns from a centralized location, making it easier to keep track of your messaging efforts.
-   **Scalability:** Easily scale your operations by creating multiple campaigns with different use cases under the same brand.
-   **Flexibility:** Link different phone numbers to specific campaigns, allowing for targeted communication strategies.
-   **Simplifies** the registration process and reduces manual effort, making the entire campaign submission more efficient.

**Pro Tips:**

-   **Brand Upgradation:** Regularly review and upgrade your A2P brands to ensure they meet your messaging needs and regulatory requirements.
-   **Campaign Management:** Utilize the ability to create multiple campaigns under one brand to segment your audience effectively and tailor your messages.

**FAQ/Use Cases:**




**Question:** Why can't I create a new campaign?
**Answer:** Ensure your brand is approved and not older than five days. If it is, you need to manually initiate the campaign submission.




**Question:** How do I manage phone numbers for different campaigns?
**Answer:** Use the Manage Numbers page to assign phone numbers to specific campaign use cases, ensuring accurate and efficient message delivery.




**Question:** How does auto-submission work?
**Answer:** After your A2P brand is approved, the associated campaign is automatically submitted, eliminating the need for any manual action.




**Question:** Do I need to take any action to enable this?
**Answer:** No action is required. The auto-submission process is triggered once your brand approval is completed.




**Question:** What should I do if my brand approval is older than five days?
**Answer:** If the brand approval is older than five days, you will need to manually submit the campaign from the Campaigns section.




**Question:** Can I disable auto-submission if I prefer manual control?
**Answer:** Auto-submission is applied by default after brand approval. Manual submission is only required in specific cases, such as expired approval windows.




**Question:** How often should I check my A2P brand status?

**Answer:** It is recommended to **regularly review** your brands in the Trust Centre to ensure they still meet your current messaging needs and remain compliant with the latest regulatory requirements.




**Question:** Can I have more than one A2P brand within a single ?

**Answer:** Yes. The system allows you to maintain and manage **multiple brands** for one providing better organization for diverse business needs.




**Question:** Can I move a phone number from one Campaign to another?

**Answer:** Yes. On the **Manage Numbers** page, you can unbind a number from an existing campaign and link it to a different one. Note that it may take a few minutes for the mobile carriers to recognize the change in use-case association.
