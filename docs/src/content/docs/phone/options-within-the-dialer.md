**Options within the Dialer**

This article will guide you through the various options available while making or receiving calls within the app. Whether handling outbound or incoming calls, the dialer offers several features to streamline your communication experience.

### **Key Features**

**Mute**: Mute yourself during a call, preventing the caller from hearing you while you can still hear them.  
**Dial**: Opens the dial pad for making selections when prompted, such as navigating IVR systems.  
**Hold**: Puts the call on hold, muting both parties, and is triggered automatically when initiating a Warm Transfer.  
**Warm Transfer**: Speak to the recipient before transferring the call. You can switch between calls and merge them if needed.  
**Blind Transfer**: Instantly transfers the call without a prior conversation with the recipient.  
**Quick Dialing**: Starts dialing immediately after opening the dialer, searching for contact matches as you type.  
**Add Notes After Calls**: Capture important details post-call from the Recents screen.  
**Tag Contacts After Calls**: Apply tags to contacts directly after calls from the Recents screen.

## **Options Within the Dialer**

Once you place or receive a call and it connects, you will have the following options:

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770661200811-image10.png)

### **Mute**

-   Place yourself on mute.
-   The caller cannot hear you, but you can still hear them.

### **Dial**

-   Opens the dial pad to make selections when prompted (e.g., "Press 1 to reach Joe Smith").
-   Now available for **navigating IVR systems** (Beta feature).

### **Hold**

-   Puts the call on hold, muting both parties.
-   Calls are automatically placed on hold when initiating a **Warm Transfer**.

### **Warm Transfer**

-   Allows you to **speak to the recipient before transferring** the call.
-   **How to use:**

1\. Click **Warm Transfer**.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770661231622-image6.png)

-   Search for an existing contact or enter a new number.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764082830380-image.png)

-    A new call tab will open. You can:
    -   Switch between the two calls.
    -   Click **Patch Call** to merge both calls.
-    If you hang up while on two calls, you will remain on the original call.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764082855561-image.png)

**Note:** You can transfer both **incoming and outbound calls**, providing seamless call management across all interactions.

### **Blind Transfer**

-   Instantly transfers the call **without a prior conversation** with the recipient.
-   Simply **dial the number** or **select a contact** to transfer.

### **Quick Dialing**

-   Start dialing immediately after opening the dialer.
-   Automatically searches your contacts for matches as you type.
-   **Calling From** feature allows you to search by the friendly name of the phone number, whereas previously, searches were only possible using the phone number itself.
-   **Quick access to Contacts and Recent Calls** with a single tap.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764082887792-image.png)

### Auto-minimize, Pin & Drag 

The dialer automatically minimizes when you click outside of it, allowing you to quickly view contact details or pipeline info without manually closing it.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775486998174-image1.png)

**Voicemail Tab on the Dialer:**  

The voicemail feature streamlines voicemail management by consolidating messages into a single tab with searchable lists, audio playback, transcriptions, and one-click follow-ups. Users can access voicemails directed to them or across the sub-account, allowing for quick responses like callbacks or SMS.  
**  
How to Use:**  

Open the dialer and click the Voicemail tab. View voicemails under the "For Me" tab and click any voicemail to play audio and read the transcription. Use the call or SMS buttons to respond, or search by phone number for specific voicemails.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764082911938-image.png)

**Note:** Access to the "All" tab is permission-based, and transcriptions are available if enabled. This feature is currently available on web browsers, with mobile app support coming soon.  

### **Call Scripts**

**Important:** Only Account Admins can create Call Scripts. Non-admins can't create Call Scripts but they can use them when on a call. This feature is currently unavailable on the mobile app.

Call Scripts are designed to enhance the consistency and productivity of calling agents by providing them with a structured and easy-to-follow guide during calls. This feature ensures that agents adhere to the necessary communication flow, leading to more efficient and effective calls.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764082931981-image.png)

**To use this** feature, navigate to Settings > Phone Numbers > Advanced Settings > Call Scripts to create or select a script. 

Once created, agents can access the script during calls by clicking on "Scripts" and selecting the appropriate one.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764082952699-image.png)

## **FAQs**




**Question:** What happens when I put a call on hold?  
Answer**: Both parties are muted until the call is resumed. Calls are automatically placed on hold during a warm transfer.




**Question:** What is the difference between a warm transfer and a blind transfer?  
Answer**: Warm Transfer: You connect with the recipient before transferring the call.  
Blind Transfer: The call is instantly transferred without prior notice to the recipient.




**Question:** Can I transfer a call to a specific number during a warm transfer?  
Answer**: Yes, you can transfer the call to another user, a specific number, or forward it.




**Question:** What happens when I initiate a blind transfer?  
Answer**: The call is immediately sent to the recipient’s number, and your connection ends.




**Question:** Is the caller notified when I put them on hold?  
Answer**: No, they experience a silent pause but are not explicitly notified.




**Question:** Can I use call scripts while on a call?

**Answer:** Yes, call scripts can be used by agents during calls, but only if the script has been created by an Account Admin. Non-admin users can access and use the scripts, but they cannot create them. This feature is currently unavailable on the mobile app.




**Question:** How do I manage voicemail messages?

**Answer:** Open the **Voicemail** tab in the dialer to access messages. You can play audio, read transcriptions, respond via call or SMS, and search by phone number. Access to the **All** tab and transcriptions may require specific permissions.




**Question:** How does the "Calling From" search feature differ from previous versions?
**Answer:** Previously, you could only search for a phone number using the digits themselves. Now, the "Calling From" feature allows you to search using the **friendly name** of the phone number, making it much easier to identify and select the correct outbound line.
