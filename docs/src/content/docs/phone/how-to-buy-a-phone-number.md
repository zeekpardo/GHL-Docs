**How to Buy a Phone Number**

Whether you're purchasing toll-free, local, or mobile numbers, this comprehensive guide will walk you through the entire process. Stay ahead of the communication game with our stylish upgrade introduced on August 22, 2023. From streamlined searches to enhanced categorization, we've got it all covered. Let's dive in!

### Enhanced Number Organization:

To simplify access, different numbers are now categorized under distinct tabs. Plus, we've introduced a singular **Call to Action (CTA)** for adding numbers, making the process quicker and hassle-free.

### Number Type Field for Easy Identification:

Every number now includes a **“Number Type”** field, aiding easy recognition of their respective purposes. No more guesswork, just clear identification.

### Purchase Date Visibility:

Clients can now view the **purchase dat**e for each number, providing additional information at a glance. Stay informed and stay in control.

### Search and Filters:

Search results now show Number Type and Capabilities, and we've added filters based on **Number Type, Capabilities, and Number Strings.** Finding the perfect number is now a breeze.

### Refresh Results Feature:

Easily reload new numbers with the **“Refresh Results”** feature, ensuring a smoother acquisition process. No more waiting, just instant access to the numbers you desire.

## How to Buy Different Types of Numbers:

### Step 1: Navigate to Your Location/Account:

-   Head into your Location/Account where you want to purchase the number.

### Step 2: Access Settings and Click on Phone Numbers:

-   Click on Settings, then navigate to Phone Numbers.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764011842838-image.png)

### Step 3: Add a New Number and Choose Country:

-    Go to manage number then Click on Add Number/Add Phone Number, and select the country you want to buy the number for from the Country dropdown.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764011348972-image.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764012069838-image.png)

### Step 4: Apply Filters and Select the Number Type:

-   Set the Phone Number Type in the filters according to your preference (Toll-Free, Local, or Mobile) and hit search.

**Note:** Toll-free numbers begin with one of the following three-digit codes: 800, 888, 877, 866, 855, 844, or 833. If it's not available for the one you are looking for, please reach out to support

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764012159820-image.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764012168936-image.png)

### Step 5: Choose a Number and Complete the Purchase:

-   Select one of the available numbers and click on Proceed to Buy. Complete the purchase process, and you're all set!

### Step 6: Configure and Register Your Number:

-   Once you've completed the purchase, configure and register your number according to your business needs. Ensure it's set up to handle the desired message throughput and other specific requirements.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764012204464-image.png)

### FAQs:




**Question:** What should I do if I can’t find the toll-free number I’m looking for?
**Answer:** If the toll-free number you’re seeking isn’t available, you can reach out to support for assistance. They may be able to help you find an alternative number or provide guidance on the best available options.




**Question:** How do I register and configure my newly purchased number?
**Answer:** After purchasing your number, configure and register it through the account settings under Phone Numbers. Ensure that it’s set up to handle your specific needs, such as message throughput, and confirm any additional settings required for your business operations.




**Question:** What are the benefits of Toll-Free Numbers over Local/Phone numbers?

**Answer:** Toll-Free numbers offer increased message throughput and the ability to send messages even while the registration is in progress. They are ideal for businesses aiming for efficient communication.




**Question:** How can one register their US/Canada Toll-Free numbers?

**Answer:** For Accounts on LC phone, sign up for Toll-Free Number Registration for LC - Phone (US/Canada) from the application.




**Question:** What advantages do Local/Phone numbers offer for businesses?  
**Answer:** Local/Phone numbers provide a localized presence, fostering trust and familiarity among local customers. They are perfect for businesses looking to establish a strong local connection.




**Question:** How long does it take to activate a purchased number?  
Answer**: Numbers typically activate immediately after purchase. In some cases, additional verification or configuration may be required, which can add a short delay.




**Question:** Can I search and filter numbers before purchasing?

**Answer:** Yes. You can use filters for **Number Type, Capabilities, and Number Strings**, and see the purchase date and number type for easier selection.  




**Question:** Is the verification code valid for a limited time?

**Answer:** Yes, for security purposes, each OTP (One-Time Password) is valid for a specific window (usually 5–10 minutes). If the user enters the code after it has expired, they will be prompted to request a new one.




**Question:** How can I see more number options if I don’t like the initial search** results?

**Answer:** You can use the **“Refresh Results”** feature. This allows you to instantly reload a new list of available numbers without having to restart your search from scratch.
