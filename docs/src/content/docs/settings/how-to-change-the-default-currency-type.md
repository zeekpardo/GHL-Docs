**How to Change the Default Currency Type**

You can select or update the currency in your account by choosing the country associated with that specific currency. If your country is set to the United States, the default currency will be USD. To change the currency, simply update the country setting.

**Follow these steps to update the currency:**

-   Go to the Settings tab and select Business Info.
-   On the right side, under Business Physical Address, update the country.
-   Click "Update Address" to save the changes.

**Set a Default Currency for Seamless Payments**

Set a default currency to streamline invoicing, product pricing, and checkouts—reducing manual changes and minimizing errors. The chosen currency auto-applies across payment pages but remains adjustable at checkout.

**How to Set a Default Currency:**

1- Go to **Settings**

2- Click on **Business Profile**

3-Select **Currency**

4- Choose your preferred default currency from the dropdown

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfhd1m_G90PqtkRXbfIs8cs9QtV1ZgMoEUnIcieJayLOAJVCOAgD0W2m_WQGqQ3Yhwetfnq8nuS_Ko-srJtOn8Gxz7bcJfsjCc2lIQczqAikOA8Ml6RwsXBVCj6sksPbMwHu_hJ3w?key=esg3c8mPr3tR9u5KqrR50Q)

5- Save your changes

**Note:** If no currency is set, the system defaults to the business's country-based currency.

**Multi-Currency Support for Product Creation and Payments**

With multi-currency support, you can create products and accept payments in your preferred currency. This feature allows you to:

-   Select any currency while creating products, invoices, or Text2Pay links, regardless of your business's country setting.
-   Target products towards specific geographies.
-   Send invoices to customers in different countries.
-   Create products in the preferred currency, send one-time or recurring invoices, and use Text2Pay links based on the contact’s country and currency.

### **FAQs: Updating Currency and Multi-Currency Support**




**Question:** How do I update the currency in my account?
**Answer:** To update the currency associated with your account:

1.  Navigate to the **Settings** tab and select **Business Info**.
2.  On the right side, under **Business Physical Address**, update the country to the one associated with your preferred currency.
3.  Click **Update Address** to save the changes.




**Question:** Why is my currency not updating immediately?
**Answer:** It might take some time for the currency update to reflect. Logging out of your account and logging back in can help refresh and update the currency settings.




**Question:** Can I choose a different currency for each product I create?
**Answer:** Yes, with multi-currency support, you can select any currency when creating products, invoices, or Text2Pay links, regardless of your business's country setting. This allows you to target products and services towards specific geographies.




**Question:** How does multi-currency support benefit my invoicing?
**Answer:** Multi-currency support allows you to:

-   Create products and send invoices in different currencies based on your customers' locations.
-   Send invoices in the currency preferred by the customer, facilitating easier transactions and improving customer satisfaction.




**Question:** Can I use Text2Pay links in different currencies?
**Answer:** Yes, you can create Text2Pay links in the currency that corresponds to your contact’s country. This feature supports smooth payment transactions across different currencies.




**Question:** What if my country is not listed?
**Answer:** If your country is not listed or available, you may need to contact customer support for further assistance or to check if additional currencies or countries can be added to the system.




**Question:** Is there a limit to how many currencies I can use?
**Answer:** There is no specific limit to the number of currencies you can use. You can select and work with multiple currencies based on your business needs and customer locations.




**Question:** What happens if I don’t set a default currency?
**Answer:** The system will automatically use the currency based on your business’s country.




**Question:** Can I change the currency for an existing invoice or product?
**Answer:** Yes, you can edit the product details or invoice settings and select a different currency, provided the product supports multi-currency options.




**Question:** Can I set a default currency for my business?
**Answer:** Yes, you can set a default currency for your business to streamline invoicing, product pricing, and checkouts. This default currency is automatically applied across payment pages but can still be adjusted at checkout.  




**Question:** How long does it take for the country and currency update to take effect?
**Answer:** It may take some time for the country and currency updates to take effect. Logging out of your account and logging back in can help refresh and update the currency settings.




**Question:** Does the system automatically calculate exchange rates?

**Answer:** The platform does not currently perform live currency conversions between your products. For example, if you set a price at $100 USD, the system will not automatically know that equals roughly £80 GBP. You should manually set the price for each currency based on your preferred exchange rate.




**Question:** How do I ensure my invoices reflect the correct currency for my products?
**Answer:** Navigate to the Payments Tab and enter the Invoices section. When you add a product to an invoice, the currency you previously selected for that specific product will automatically be reflected on the invoice.




**Question:** What happens if I don't manually select a default currency in my Business Profile?
**Answer:** If no default currency is manually set in the Business Profile settings, the system will automatically default to the currency associated with the country listed in your business's physical address.
