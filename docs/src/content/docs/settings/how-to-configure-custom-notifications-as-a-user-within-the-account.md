**How to Configure Custom Notifications as a User within the Account**

allows you to customize your notification preferences to suit your work style, giving you flexibility and control over how you receive reminders. This article walks you through setting up and customizing your notifications within an account, ensuring you never miss a task or important message again.

## **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Customizable Reminder Times**: Schedule task reminders based on your preferred time zones, ensuring you and your team receive timely alerts.
-   **Multiple Notification Channels**: Notifications can be sent via in-app messages, SMS, and email, allowing you to choose the channel that works best for you.
-   **Daily Notifications**: Set up reminders for due and overdue tasks, helping you manage priorities efficiently.

## **How to Use**

### **Step 1: Accessing Notification Settings**

1.  Navigate to the **Settings** section in the bottom left of your screen.
2.  Select **My Profile** from the left-hand side menu.
3.  Click on the **Notifications** tab at the top of the screen.

### **Step 2: Customizing Notification Preferences**

In the **Notifications** tab, you will see three categories: In-app, Email, and SMS. Each category represents the method through which you can receive notifications.

### **1- In-app Notifications:**

-   Available for both mobile and desktop versions.
-   Ensure that the desktop notification bell is visible and mobile notifications are set for prompt delivery.

### **Step 3: Set Task Reminders**

1.  Scroll down to **Task Notifications**.
2.  To enable reminders for tasks assigned to you, check **In-app**, **Email**, or **SMS** based on your preference.
3.  Set the time interval for receiving reminders (e.g., 15, 30, or 60 minutes before the task is due).
4.  Enable daily summaries of both due and overdue tasks at your preferred time (e.g., 8:00 AM).

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXekphxghLBSQ4J0pQQEb4iDN6a6sYcTUMAlJUCJNEVsgfN_OqCsF57UpM50vUougXJsMyfHyi8Bs9EeLBdLv1I7VsyWXQQI22MPmCVkXsoUikVlf4xGvzpOe30Cizr_v9JiwkggcGBjTLzzjdJZhR3RyHpU?key=qr2lsoXhC5sEJNzo-RoQtw)

### **Step 4: Sending and Receiving Notifications**

-   **Daily Summaries**: Activate daily summaries to ensure team members receive a list of all due and overdue tasks.
-   **Multiple Channels**: Ensure notifications are sent through all relevant channels (e.g., In-app, Email, SMS) to cover all touchpoints.
-   **Task Alerts**: Customize when and how notifications for specific tasks will be sent based on your team’s time zone and daily schedule.

### **Step 5: Save Your Settings**

Once you've configured your notification preferences, click the **Save** button at the bottom right to apply the settings.

## **Pro Tips**

-   **Set Notifications Based on Work Hours**: Configure notifications to align with your team's working hours to avoid sending reminders when they're unavailable.
-   **Daily Task Summaries**: Use daily summaries to help team members focus on their most important tasks and ensure overdue tasks are addressed.
-   **Experiment with Channels**: Test different combinations of notification channels (in-app, SMS, or email) to find the best way to stay informed without feeling overwhelmed.

## **FAQ**




**Question:** How does the mark all as read button in the notifications panel work?
**Answer:** The "Mark All as Read" button in the notifications panel updates the status of all notifications from unread to read.

**Question:** How many notifications can I scroll through before additional ones need to load?
**Answer:** You can scroll through 25 notifications before the panel loads the next set of 25.  


**Question:What does the "Clear All" notification option do?
**Answer:** Clicking the "Clear All" button removes all notification records currently fetched for the logged-in user at the page level. Any new or additional notifications will appear after the clearing process is complete. **Please note that once cleared, these notifications cannot be restored.**




**Question:** Can I customize the time interval for task reminders?
**Answer:** Yes, you can set the time interval for task reminders to 15, 30, or 60 minutes before the task is due.

**![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXf7hJAp0rpFUzdL3AfOsAD2ZgHAxPio3gqBTPVOWOyDAxVoUGzNF7Op6yTw3_7Qt7Y5zMZj45IBUkJhVrWhWW3EMXNjNBQTnNA2cgVJRvnC89WssgeZ_ZRpLULlchvfQCQVjPaXv2r2d_8ggVR8GouWYwoM?key=qr2lsoXhC5sEJNzo-RoQtw)**




**Question:** Can I adjust notifications based on my work hours?
**Answer:** Yes. You can configure notifications to align with your team’s working hours, ensuring reminders are sent only when relevant.




**Question:** What happens if I change my time zone?
**Answer:** Task reminders and daily summaries automatically adjust based on your updated time zone, ensuring notifications are delivered at the correct local time.




**Question:** Why am I not receiving desktop notifications even though I enabled them in the app?

**Answer:** You must also ensure your browser settings allow the website to push notifications. If you use Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox, you may need to grant permission in the browser's privacy or site settings for the alerts to appear when you are outside the application.




**Question:** What happens if I click on a notification pop-up in the web app?

**Answer:** The web app features "Click-to-Redirect." Clicking a notification will automatically take you to the origin of that alert, such as the specific Conversation tile, Task list, or Calendar entry.
