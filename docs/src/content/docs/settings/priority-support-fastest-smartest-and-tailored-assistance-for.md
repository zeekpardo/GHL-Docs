# Priority Support

Priority Support offers the **fastest, most** personalized assistance to customers, with direct access to expert agents and **quickest** resolutions. This guide covers key features, how to enable it, and how to leverage its benefits.

## What is Priority Support?

Priority Support is a premium feature designed to provide the **fastest and most** personalized support. Customers are connected with highly skilled agents who offer immediate assistance, ensuring the **quickest** resolutions and the **best** possible service.

## What Are the Benefits of Priority Support?

-   **Queue Jumping:** Be placed at the front of the support line.
-   **Direct Tier 3 Assignment:** Get routed to the most experienced agents.
-   **Human-Only Support:** No AI, just real agents.
-   **Video Call Priority:** Ability to immediately request a video call.
-   **Quickest Resolutions:** The quickest wait times and **highest tiered** experts combine to result in the fastest resolution.

## What does personalized support mean in Priority Support?

Personalized support means that **since you are always assigned to our Tier 3 agents, you are more likely to be assigned to someone who has worked on your account previously.** This enables them to offer more personalized assistance, resolving issues more efficiently and providing the **best** overall experience.
