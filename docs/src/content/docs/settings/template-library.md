**Template Library**

The Template Library offers a diverse range of professionally designed templates for websites, funnels, social media posts, email campaigns, and more. It helps users quickly create high-quality marketing materials with customizable content and stock images. This tool is ideal for businesses and marketers looking to save time and effort while maintaining a polished brand image.

**Benefits of Using the Template Library**

-   **Time and Effort Savings:** Quickly create marketing materials using ready-made templates, saving valuable time.
-   **Premium Designs:** Access high-quality templates with top-notch stock images and content, ensuring a consistent brand look.
-   **Easy-to-Use Interface:** Easily filter and preview templates to find the ones that best fit your business needs.
-   **Customizable:** Personalize templates by adjusting images, colors, and content to match your brand.
-   **Affordable:** Eliminate the need for custom designs or hiring a designer, reducing costs.
-   **Universal Access:** The Template Library is available to all users at no extra cost, with no additional subscriptions required.

**Download Templates to a Folder**

Users can download email and funnel templates directly into designated folders, enhancing organization and accessibility.

**Mark and Access Favourite Templates**

This feature allows users to easily mark templates as favorites, making it quicker and more efficient to find them later. To use this feature, simply hover over a template and click the heart icon to mark it as a favorite.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763751913592-image.png)

You can also do this from the preview screen. If you want to remove a template from your favorites, just click the star again. Access all your favorite templates by using the Favourites filter located on the left panel.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763751964277-image.png)

**Search for Templates**

-   Enter a tag, name, or category in the search bar.
-   The results will display matching templates, even if the word is not in the template name.

### FAQs




**Question:** Is there a cost associated with using the Template Library?

**Answer:** No, the Template Library is available to all users without additional fees.




**Question:** How do I organize my downloaded templates?
**Answer:** After downloading email and funnel templates, you can save them directly into designated folders. This feature helps keep your templates organized and easily accessible for future use




**Question:** Can I customize the templates for my brand?
**Answer:** Yes! All templates in the Template Library come with customizable content, images, and layouts. You can adjust them to match your brand’s colors, messaging, and style.




**Question:** Can I mark multiple templates as favorites?

**Answer:** Yes, you can mark as many templates as you like as favorites.




**Question:** How do I search for specific templates?

**Answer:** Enter a tag, name, or category in the search bar. The results will display all matching templates, even if the keyword isn’t in the template name.


**Question:Does the Template Library support templates for paid advertising campaigns?

**Answer:** Yes. The Template Library supports both Google and Meta Ads. It includes an objectives dropdown that automatically adjusts based on the platform you are using, helping you organize and manage your ad templates effectively.  




**Question:** Is there a way to see what a funnel or website template looks like before I add it to my account?

**Answer:** Yes. When you select a template in the Funnels or Website section, you can view a **preview**, read a detailed description, and check for any **prerequisites** (such as specific forms or assets required) before clicking "Choose Template."




**Question:** How do I remove a template from my "Favourites" list if I no longer want it there?
**Answer:** To remove a template from your favorites, you can either hover over the template and click the star icon again, or perform the same action from the template's preview screen.
