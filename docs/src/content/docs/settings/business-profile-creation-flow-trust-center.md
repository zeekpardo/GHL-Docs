**Business Profile Creation Flow (Trust Center)**

On the Phone Numbers Page, we have the Trust Center. This will be visible to Account admins. Accounts with the US as the country will see this. Trust Center provides products that can improve customer engagement through brand recognition.

Carriers are becoming increasingly sensitive to spam and AUP enforcement when it comes to SMS and Calls. To keep spamming in check and to protect the end customer, they have created various regulations & compliance frameworks.

This is sometimes required to utilize SMS and Power Dialer features in the system. However, when successfully verified, the likelihood of your business number being marked as “potential spam” when making outbound calls or your SMS messages not making it past the phone carrier (AT&T, Verizon, etc.) spam filters is greatly reduced.

The review process can be very detailed, and even seemingly small deviations can cause the review to fail.

## **Opt-In Guidelines**

When preparing your campaigns, it's crucial to follow Opt-In Guidelines to ensure compliance and engagement with your audience. Opt-ins play a pivotal role in establishing customer consent and maintaining transparency. Following these guidelines will improve your campaign's chances of success.

**Enhanced Brand Registration Overview**

Starting from August 2023, the registration process for an enhanced version of the standard brand offering is available, designed to comply with new industry guidelines for registered messaging services. This registration is particularly suited for customers who require a higher daily sending limit, which is approved by industry regulatory bodies.

### **Who is Eligible?**

Eligibility for standard brand registration is limited to businesses that meet specific criteria:

-   Tax Identification: Businesses must have a valid Tax ID (EIN for US-based businesses).
-   Valid Business Information: Registration requires accurate business details, including the business name, contact information, and physical address.

### **Voice Integrity: Improve Your Phone Number's Reputation**

The Voice Integrity feature helps improve your phone number's reputation by allowing you to register it with major caller-ID analytics firms. This process removes “spam likely” labels from legitimate calls, increasing answer rates, particularly for numbers that may have been blocked in the past. By enabling this feature, you can fix bad reputations and clear existing spam flags, leading to a higher trust factor for your calls.

**Important:** This is only for USA-based users.

**How to Use It:**

1- Navigate to Settings > Phone Numbers > Trust Center > Voice Integrity.

2- Fill in the required form and submit.

## **Toll-Free Messaging as an Alternative**

Toll-free messaging presents a viable alternative to A2P 10DLC, offering a verification process without relying on Brand and Campaign Registration. While A2P 10DLC provides benefits, Toll-Free messaging is another avenue worth exploring for your messaging needs.

## **Additional Notes and Best Practices**

In addition to the core registration process, there are some additional notes and best practices to keep in mind for a successful campaign. These key points and best practices will help you navigate the A2P 10DLC ecosystem more effectively:

-   **Understanding Charges**: Be aware that SMS, MMS, and calls may incur charges even before they are delivered, based on responses from carriers. It's important to plan your messaging strategy with these potential charges in mind.

-   **Account Availability:** The feature is available for Phone and Accounts. Ensure that your account and device are eligible for the A2P 10DLC capabilities to make the most of the benefits.

-   **Submission Duration:** After submitting your registration, please note that the approval process may take up to 2 weeks depending on the volume of applications that have been received in the same area but sometimes it only takes up to 72 hours if the Information provided is accurate. Review your customer business profile information before submitting it to avoid potential delays.

-   **Maintain Consistency:** Consistency in branding, website information, and sample messages is crucial. Ensure that your brand identity is accurately represented across all platforms and materials to avoid potential campaign rejection.

-   **Toll-Free Messaging:** Consider Toll-Free messaging as an alternative to A2P 10DLC. While A2P 10DLC offers benefits, Toll-Free messaging provides an option that doesn't require Brand and Campaign Registration.

-   **Global Considerations:** For information about sending messages in countries other than the US, refer to our country-specific SMS Guidelines pages. These guidelines provide insights into any unique rules or restrictions for each country.

-   **Staying Updated:** The A2P 10DLC ecosystem continually evolves, and regulations may change over time. Stay informed about the latest compliance standards and best practices to ensure ongoing success.
-   **Opt-Out Language:** Including opt-out language in your sample messages is recommended. Adding language such as "Please reply STOP to opt-out" provides recipients with an easy way to unsubscribe from your messages.

### FAQs




**Question:** What is the pre-requisite for A2P Standard registration?  
**Answer:** The prerequisite to A2P registration is to create a business profile providing the EIN and additional business registration information.  




**Question:** What information would be required from the account to move with the registration?  
**Answer:** The account will need a business registration EIN and some additional info like address, website and etc.  




**Question:** Is messaging to Canada or any other countries also affected by these changes?  
**Answer:** No. Canadian mobile carriers may introduce registered A2P 10DLC solutions in the future, but at this time carriers have not announced any plans to do so.  




**Question:** I have been sending SMS forever, what has changed?  
**Answer:** A2P 10DLC (Application-to-Person 10-Digit Long Code) is a messaging framework introduced by the wireless industry in the United States to regulate and improve the delivery of Application-to-Person text messages. It aims to address issues related to spam and unwanted messages on long codes while ensuring legitimate businesses can continue using text messaging for communication with their customers.  




**Question:** Does this apply to only marketing campaigns or does it apply to all SMS?
**Answer:** A2P 10DLC is applicable for all Application-to-Person texting like marketing SMS, reactivation campaigns, appointment reminders, prospecting, billing alerts, 2FA login codes, etc.




**Question:** What is the Voice Integrity feature?
**Answer:** **Voice Integrity** helps improve your phone number’s reputation by registering it with major caller ID analytics firms. This can remove “**Spam Likely**” labels, restore trust, and increase call answer rates. It is available **only for U.S.-based users**.




**Question:** Can I use a DUNS number for my business profile registration?

**Answer:** No. You must use a valid Tax ID (EIN for US-based businesses) to register your business profile. Using a DUNS number should be avoided as it can lead to brand registration failures.




**Question:** How long does the approval process take after I submit my registration?

**Answer:** The approval process typically takes between 72 hours and 2 weeks. To avoid delays, ensure that your business profile information is reviewed for accuracy before submission.




**Question:** Why was my Brand registration rejected even though my EIN is correct?

**Answer:** The most common reason for rejection is a mismatch between your **Legal Business Name** and your **Tax ID (EIN)**. The name must match your CP 575 or SS-4 confirmation letter _exactly_, including punctuation like commas or "Inc." Carriers verify this data against a real-time IRS database.
