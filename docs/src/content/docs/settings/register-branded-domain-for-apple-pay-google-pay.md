**Register Branded Domain For Apple Pay & Google Pay**

Apple Pay and Google Pay seamlessly integrate with branded domain names for Invoice, Text2Pay, and Payment Link functionalities. This addresses previous challenges and ensures a smooth payment process for your customers using branded domains.

## **How to Use**

**Step 1: Access Stripe Integration Settings**

-   Navigate to your dashboard.
-   Go to Payments on the left > Integrations at the top > Stripe.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773332858747-image4.jpg)

**Step 2: Enable Domain Registration**

-   Locate the "Register domains for Apple Pay" toggle.
-   Ensure this toggle is enabled to allow automatic domain registration with Stripe.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764359217169-image.png)

**Step 3: Manually Resync Domain (if needed)**

-   If you encounter issues, use the "Resync Domain" button on the Stripe integration page to manually register your branded domain name with Stripe.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764359232171-image.png)

**Step 4: Implement Payment Links**

-   When creating payment links, invoices, or Text2Pay requests, ensure you are using your branded domain names.
-   Apple Pay and Google Pay will now be available as payment options for your customers.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764359248966-image.png)

## **Pro Tips**

-   **Regular Checks**: Regularly check your Stripe integration settings to ensure the "Register domains for Apple Pay" toggle is enabled.
-   **Manual Sync**: Use the manual resync button if you experience any issues with payment processing.
-   **Stay Updated**: Keep your payment integration settings updated to leverage new features and enhancements.

## FAQs




**Question:** Do I need to do anything special to enable Apple Pay and Google Pay on my branded domain?
**Answer**: Ensure that the "Register domains for Apple Pay" toggle is enabled in the Stripe integration settings. The system will handle the rest.




**Question:** What if the payments aren't working?

**Answer:** Use the "Resync Domain" button on the Stripe integration page to manually register your branded domain with Stripe.




**Question:** Are there any additional costs for using Apple Pay and Google Pay?

**Answer:** There are no additional costs for enabling Apple Pay and Google Pay through branded domain names beyond the standard transaction fees.




**Question:** Will enabling Apple Pay and Google Pay affect other payment methods?
**Answer:** No. Enabling these methods will not interfere with existing payment options like credit/debit cards; they simply add additional ways for customers to pay.




**Question:** Does this integration support recurring payments?

**Answer:** Yes. Apple Pay and Google Pay through this branded domain integration are fully compatible with both **one-time charges and recurring subscription invoices**.
