This article will go over how to find and setup a Regulatory bundle in .

**Step 1**. Head into Settings (bottom left menu) > Phone numbers > Regulatory Bundle/Address

**Step 2**. You will need to fill out this page with the information for the Business:

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/hlpt-support-widget.appspot.com/o/covers%2F12345%2F1682106613077_image6?alt=media&token=f46cd73b-9f3b-4ea1-a26b-552b4b80ae91)

**Step 3**. Once all the information is submitted click on 'SAVE'. 

**Step 4**. We will coordinate and get the regulatory bundle approved.
