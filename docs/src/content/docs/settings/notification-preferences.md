Notification preferences allow you to establish your preferences for both the web and mobile versions of the platform. The design of this part of allows you to:

-   Set notification preferences for web and mobile (can be same or different)
-   Use multiple types of notifications including: “assigned to” notifications for both conversations & tasks
-   Distinguish between system and custom notifications within the notification screen
-   Clear Notifications

**Note:** Control which notifications you want to receive by enabling or disabling them based on your preferences.

###  **Steps to Access Notification Settings**

1.  Go to **Settings**.
2.  Select **Profile**.
3.  Click on the **Notifications** tab.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763747880172-image2.png)

Customize your Notification preferences to make this feature work for you and your particular business needs.

**FAQs**




**Question:** What are notification preferences?
**Answer:** Notification preferences allow you to customize the notifications you receive on both the web and mobile versions of , helping you control what alerts you get and how you stay informed.  




**Question:** Can I set different notification preferences for web and mobile?
**Answer:** Yes, you can set separate preferences for web and mobile, allowing you to tailor notifications based on your specific needs for each platform.  




**Question:** What types of notifications can I customize?
**Answer:** You can customize various types of notifications, including "assigned to" notifications for conversations and tasks, as well as system and custom notifications.  




**Question:** How can I manage which notifications I receive?
**Answer:** You can enable or disable notifications based on your preferences, ensuring that you only receive the alerts that are most relevant to your needs.  




**Question:** How do I access notification settings?
**Answer:** To access your notification settings, go to the Settings menu, select Profile, and click on the Notifications tab to make adjustments.  




**Question:** Do notification settings sync between web and mobile?
**Answer:** No. Preferences for **web and mobile are managed separately**, so you can fine-tune alerts for each platform based on your usage patterns.  




**Question:** Can I clear notifications from the notification screen?
**Answer:** Yes, the notification screen allows you to clear notifications as needed, helping keep your alerts organized.  




**Question:** I'm getting too many alerts. How do I make them stop?
**Answer:** You can "mute" specific types of activity. Just go to your Notification settings and toggle off the switches for things you don't need to see immediately. This helps you focus only on the most important work.




**Question:** Why should I customize my preferences instead of using the default settings?

**Answer:** Customizing your preferences allows the feature to work for your "particular business needs." Since every role is different, adjusting these settings ensures that a salesperson only sees new lead alerts while an admin might only see system-level updates, preventing "notification fatigue."




**Question:** How can I tell the difference between a system-generated alert and a notification from a custom automation?
**Answer:** Within the notification screen, the platform is designed to help you distinguish between system and custom notifications. This allows you to easily identify which alerts are coming from core platform events versus those you have created specifically within your own custom workflows.
