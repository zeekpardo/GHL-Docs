### FAQs




**Question:** Who can access the Audit Logs?
**Answer:** Audit Logs are only visible to Account Admins. Account Users do not have access to this feature.




**Question:** What filters can I apply in Audit Logs?
**Answer:** You can filter logs by Contact or Opportunity ID, Selected Users (staff members), Actions (created, updated, deleted, restored), Modules, and Date.




**Question:** How do I track changes to a specific calendar?
**Answer:** Copy the calendar ID from Calendar Settings, go to Settings > Audit Logs, paste the ID in the 'Document ID' search box, and view all details related to that calendar.




**Question:** Can I track refund actions in Audit Logs?
**Answer:** Yes, you can track all successful and failed refund actions, including details such as refund status, notes, type of refund, and the user who processed it, under Settings > Audit Logs.




**Question:** What details can I view in the Audit Logs for Calendar settings?

**Answer:** You can view edits made to calendars, including timestamps, the user who made changes, and specific modifications to calendar settings.




**Question:** How do I retrieve details for a specific contact or opportunity?
**Answer:** Use the Contact or Opportunity ID to filter logs under Settings > Audit Logs and view all associated records and changes.




**Question:** Can I view audit logs for communication channels like WhatsApp?
**Answer:** Yes. Logs include actions such as adding phone numbers, updating templates, and modifying flows, with timestamps and user identification.




**Question:** How far back can I view events in the Audit Logs?
**Answer:** Audit logs will display events from the last 60 days.




**Question:** If I delete a user, will their past actions disappear from the Audit Logs?

**Answer:** No. Even if a staff member is removed from the account, the historical records of their actions remain in the Audit Logs for the 60-day retention period. Their name will still appear in the "Selected Users" filter to ensure accountability.
