**Customizable 404 Error Page**

With the customizable 404 error page feature, you can create a personalized webpage that users will see when they encounter an error on your site. This page functions like any other funnel step or website page, allowing you to include branding, messaging, and links to keep users engaged with your content.

**Setting Up Your Customizable 404 Error Page:**

1.  **Navigate to Domain Settings:**
    -   Go to your domain setting by navigating through Settings > Domain & URL Settings > Domains.
2.  **Connect Your Domain:**
    -   Ensure your domain is connected to your site

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762534309525-1.jpg)

1.  **Set Up Your 404 Page:**
    -   Under additional options, locate the setting for the 404 error page.
    -   Customize the page with your preferred branding, messaging, and links to other areas of your website.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762534333725-2.jpg)

2) If you already have a domain setup, go to edit domain

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I create a custom 404 error page?

**Answer:** To create a custom 404 error page, navigate to **Sites** and create your 404 page. Customize it with your preferred branding, messaging, and links. After that, go to **Settings** → **Domain & URL Redirect** and manage your domain. Click the three dots next to your domain, select **Edit**, and then choose your 404 page you have created in the Sites tab.




**Question:** Why should I have a unique 404 error page for each domain?

**Answer:** A unique 404 error page for each domain ensures that users receive a tailored experience, enhancing engagement and maintaining consistent branding across your site.




**Question:** Will users automatically be redirected to the custom 404 page?

**Answer:** Yes, once you’ve selected your custom 404/error page, users who encounter a 404 error on your site will be automatically redirected to your personalized page.




**Question:** Do I need a connected domain to use a custom 404 page?

**Answer:** Yes. Ensure your domain is connected to your site before assigning a custom 404 page.




**Question:** Can I edit my 404 page after it’s been set?

**Answer:** Yes. You can update the page anytime in the **Sites** tab, and changes will reflect automatically once saved.




**Question:** Can I set one 404 page to work across multiple domains?
**Answer:** Yes. While we recommend unique pages for different brands, you can manually assign the same 404 page to multiple domains by editing each domain’s settings individually in the **Domain & URL Settings** tab  




**Question:** Can I use a video or a background image on my 404 page?
**Answer:** Absolutely. Since the 404 page is built just like any other page in the Builder, you can add videos, animations, or creative imagery to match your brand’s personality.




**Question:** What happens if I delete the page I assigned as my custom 404?

**Answer:** If the assigned page is deleted, the system will revert to the generic default error page. To avoid this, always ensure a new page is created and assigned in the **Domain & URL Settings** before removing an old version.
