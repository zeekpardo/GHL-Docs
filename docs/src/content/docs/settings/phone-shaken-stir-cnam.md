**Phone SHAKEN/STIR & CNAM**

SHAKEN/STIR refers to a system in the United States that allows businesses to send Application-to-Person (A2P) type messaging via standard 10-digit long code (10DLC) phone numbers. This will help pre**vent the calls from showing up as SPAM or getting filtered**.

**NOTE:If you register for A2P to send SMS, all your phone numbers are automatically registered for SHAKEN/STIR so that your calls are more likely to get answered.**

**What is Shaken/STIR?** It is a set of protocols designed to verify caller ID information and ensure that it comes from a genuine source. This system uses digital certificates to authenticate the caller’s identity. When a call is made, the caller’s credentials are confirmed through these certificates, which are then recorded in an online database. This process helps recipients trust that the call is indeed from the expected source.

### **Identity Verification for Phone Number Purchases (US/CA/PR)**

To ensure security and prevent fraud when purchasing phone numbers in the United States, Canada, and Puerto Rico, Persona identity verification is required for certain phone number purchases and services. This is particularly necessary when your sub-account has not previously completed the Persona verification process.

Once verified, you won't need to repeat the verification process for future purchases, including A2P registrations, toll-free number verifications, CNAM setups, and SHAKEN/STIR authentication.

### **How to Register for CNAM (For the United States Only)**

CNAM is a feature in the United States public telephone network that identifies an incoming caller by a personal or business name associated with the calling party. 

**Note** that CNAM registration does not guarantee that your business name will show up on your contact's phone—this depends on whether their carrier plan supports CNAM. 

-   Navigate to Settings > Phone Numbers > Trust Center > CNAM (Voice).

### FAQs




**Question:** How will the numbers be linked to the SHAKEN/STIR Profile?
**Answer:** All numbers will be Linked Automatically. All currently available numbers and future bought numbers will automatically be linked with the registered SHAKEN/STIR.




**Question:** Why does the business name not display when making calls, even though the client is A2P and SHAKEN/STIR approved with a verified caller ID setup?
**Answer:** The business name does not display when making calls because the verified caller ID feature only changes the number to display as the actual business number or the client's cell phone. To display the business name, the CNAM Registration feature will be required.




**Question:** How can I prevent future mislabeling of my calls?
**Answer: 

-   Register all new numbers immediately.
-   Monitor call patterns for irregularities.
-   Follow industry best practices for call frequency and messaging content.


**Question:  What should I do if my international calls are marked as spam?
**Answer:** If your international calls are flagged as spam, contact the terminating carrier directly to address the issue, as there is no centralized system for international spam label removal.




**Question:** Is Persona identity verification required for all phone number purchases?
**Answer:** No, Persona identity verification is only required for certain phone number purchases in the United States, Canada, and Puerto Rico, especially if your account has not previously completed the verification process. Once verified, you won't need to repeat the process for future purchases.  




**Question:** I registered for CNAM, so why do some people still only see my phone number?
**Answer:** CNAM display depends on the **recipient’s carrier and mobile plan**. Some carriers require the recipient to have a specific "Caller ID" feature enabled on their account to see business names instead of just the phone number and location.




**Question:** Does registering for SHAKEN/STIR guarantee that my calls won't be marked as spam?

**Answer:** No. While SHAKEN/STIR confirms your ownership of the number and improves answer rates by verifying the call's source, it does not entirely eliminate the possibility of calls being flagged as spam by individual carrier algorithms or user reports.  




**Question:** If I have already registered for A2P to send SMS, do I need to manually register for SHAKEN/STIR?
**Answer:** No. If you register for A2P to send SMS, all your phone numbers are automatically registered for SHAKEN/STIR. This ensures your calls are verified and more likely to be answered without requiring a separate registration process.
