Purchasing a domain is essential for businesses looking to establish a professional online presence. With the domain purchase feature in , you can effortlessly search, buy, and manage domains from one central platform. This simplifies domain management for subaccount users, providing a seamless, integrated experience. This guide will walk you through the steps to purchase and manage a domain while highlighting the benefits of using this feature.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Centralized Domain Management:** Purchase and manage your domains directly from the platform, avoiding the hassle of external services and multiple logins.
-   **Seamless Integration:** Connect your domain effortlessly to websites, funnels, blogs, email services, WordPress, and other products.
-   **Easy DNS Management:** You can modify DNS records (A, CNAME, MX, etc.) with a single click, streamlining configurations without technical complexities.
-   **Wide Selection of TLDs:** Choose from an extensive list of top-level domains (TLDs), such as .com, .net, .io, .health, and more, to match your brand’s identity.
-   **99.99% Uptime:** Benefit from high server uptime, ensuring your domains are always accessible and secure.

### **How to Purchase a Domain**

Follow these easy steps to purchase a domain in .

#### **Step 1: Access Domain Purchase**

1.  Log in to your account and navigate to the **Settings**.
2.  Select **Domains** from the menu.
3.  Click on ‘purchase a domain’.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769460370979-image9.png)

#### **Step 2: Check Domain Availability**

1.  Use the **search bar** to type in the desired domain name.
2.  The system will show the availability of your selected domain along with pricing.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769460396899-image5.png)

#### **Step 3: Purchase the Domain**

1\. If the domain is available, click on the **Buy** button.

2\. You can also purchase .ai domains directly through the domain purchase flow in your account. This feature is perfect for businesses and individuals launching AI-driven products or services, providing a modern and recognizable online identity.

**Important Notes for AI domains:**

-   .ai domains require a minimum purchase of 2 years.
-   Terms are only available in 2-year increments (2, 4, 6, 8, or 10 years).
-   Renewals are supported in 2-year increments.
-   Pricing is based on the selected term and is displayed before confirmation.

3\. After clicking Buy, a confirmation popup will show a "Pricing Option" dropdown menu, allowing users to select a registration term ranging from 1 to 10 years, depending on TLD availability. The default term is typically set to 1 year for most domains, although certain domains like .ai may have a different default term. The final price will dynamically update based on the selected term.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** What types of DNS records can I modify?
**Answer:** You can modify A, CNAME, MX, AAAA, and TXT records directly from the platform.




**Question:** Can I purchase multiple domains for my business?
**Answer:** Yes, you can purchase and manage multiple domains from the platform, all under one centralized system.




**Question:** Do I need any external services to manage my domain?
**Answer:** No, everything from purchasing to DNS management can be done within , eliminating the need for external domain management services.




**Question:** How do I connect my domain to a website or funnel?
**Answer:** Once you purchase a domain, simply navigate to the **Domains** page, select **Configure**, and follow the instructions to connect the domain to your website, funnel, or any other product.




**Question:** What happens if my domain is not available?
**Answer:** If the domain you want is already taken, the platform will offer alternative suggestions based on your search.




**Question:** Can I renew my domain before it expires?
**Answer:** Yes. You can renew domains through the platform. Note that certain TLDs, like .ai, have specific renewal terms (in 2-year increments).




**Question:** Are there limitations on registration terms for certain domains?
**Answer:** Most TLDs allow registrations up to **10 years**, but some, like the **.co family**, have a maximum registration term of **5 years**. Renewal terms and prices may vary depending on the TLD.




**Question:** Can I purchase AI domains through the platform?
**Answer:** Yes, you can purchase .ai domains directly through the domain purchase flow in your account. However, please note that AI domains require a minimum purchase of 2 years, and terms are available only in 2-year increments  




**Question:** What is the difference between an "A Record" and a "CNAME Record"?

**Answer:** An **A Record** points your domain to a specific IP address (useful for WordPress or external hosting). A **CNAME Record** points your domain to another URL (used for connecting to our Funnels and Websites). Our "Connect" tool usually handles these settings for you automatically.




**Question:** Can I transfer a domain I bought here to another registrar later?
**Answer:** Absolutely. While we provide centralized management for your convenience, you own the domain. Under ICANN regulations, you can request an authorization code to transfer the domain to another provider after the initial 60-day registration period has passed.
