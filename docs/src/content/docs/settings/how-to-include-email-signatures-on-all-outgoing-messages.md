**How to Include Email Signatures on All Outgoing Messages**

You can add your email signature to all outgoing emails and replies. You can also choose to place it before the quoted text in email replies. Here's how to select one or both options.

### **Step 1:** Navigate to Settings > My Staff > Team Management.

### **Step 2:** Choose the User you wish to edit and click Edit.

### **Use Cases for Email Signature Settings**

**1\. Professional Branding**: Ensure that every outgoing email from your team includes a consistent and professional email signature, reinforcing your company’s brand.

**2\. Consistent Communication**: Attach the email signature to all outgoing messages to maintain a uniform appearance and provide essential contact information in every email.

## FAQ’s




**Question:** How do I navigate to the email signature settings?
**Answer:

-   Step 1: Go to Settings > My Staff > Team Management.
-   Step 2: Select the user you wish to edit and click Edit.
-   Step 3: In the popup, expand the User Info tab.
-   Step 4: Scroll down to Email Signature to make your changes.




**Question:** What options can I select for attaching the email signature?
**Answer:

-   Attach to All Outgoing Messages: Check this box to automatically include the email signature in every email you send.
-   Attach Before Quoted Text in Replies: Check this box to add the email signature before the quoted text in email replies.




**Question:** Can I customize the content of the email signature?
**Answer:Yes, you can customize the email signature by entering your desired text and formatting in the customization box provided.




**Question:** How do I save my changes?

**Answer: Click the Save button to confirm and apply your changes.




**Question:** Will my email signature be applied to only new emails, or will it also be added to existing email threads?

**Answer:** The email signature settings will apply to both new outgoing messages and replies, according to the options you selected. If you choose to attach the signature before quoted text in replies, it will only apply to future replies and not retroactively to existing threads.




**Question:** Can I use different signatures for different users?

**Answer:** Yes, each user can have their email signature set up individually in the Team Management section.




**Question:** How can I include images or links in my email signature?
**Answer:** You can include images or links in the email signature by using HTML formatting in the customization box, if the platform supports it. Ensure any images are hosted online and use proper HTML tags to embed them.




**Question:** What should I do if the email signature isn’t appearing as expected?
**Answer:** Ensure you have checked the appropriate boxes to include the signature in the desired locations.

-   Verify that the signature content is correctly formatted and saved.
-   Test by sending a test email to confirm the signature appears as intended.




**Question:** Can I set up multiple email signatures?
**Answer:** Typically, you can set up only one email signature per user. However, some systems may allow multiple signatures for different purposes or departments. Check your platform’s capabilities for more details.




**Question:** Where can I find more information or support if I encounter issues?
**Answer:** For additional help, consult your platform's help documentation or contact their support team for assistance with email signature setup.




**Question:** Will updating my signature affect previously sent emails?

**Answer: No, any updates you make to your email signature will only apply to **future emails**. Past sent messages will retain the original signature used at the time of sending.




**Question:** Where can I get more help if I encounter issues with signatures?

**Answer:Consult the platform’s help documentation or contact support for guidance on signature setup and troubleshooting.




**Question:** Does changing my signature update it on emails I already sent?

**Answer: No. Like a physical letter, once an email is sent, the content is permanent. Updating your signature in settings will only apply to new emails and replies sent _after_ the "Save" button is clicked.




**Question:** How does this feature help with team-wide branding?

**Answer:** The system allows for **Professional Branding** by enabling you to set unique signatures for each staff member. This ensures that every team member maintains a consistent, uniform appearance that reinforces your company’s brand and provides essential contact information in every interaction.
