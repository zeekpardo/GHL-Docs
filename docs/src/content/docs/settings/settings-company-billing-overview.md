This article provides a clear and actionable guide to effectively managing billing within your account. Whether you're overseeing subscriptions, handling payment methods, or monitoring transaction history, this guide offers step-by-step instructions to streamline financial management and ensure seamless operations.

## How to Use the Billing Features

### Step 1: Access the Billing Dashboard

Navigate to Settings (bottom left) and access the billing section within your account. This dashboard serves as your one-stop location for all financial management tasks.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765450711683-unnamed - 2025-12-11T185819.493.png>)

### Step 2: Manage Subscriptions

-   Select the subscription type you want to manage (SaaS, reselling, or marketplace apps).
-   Modify or cancel plans as needed.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765450732072-unnamed - 2025-12-11T185846.098.png>)

### Step 3: Add or Update Payment Methods

-   Go to the payment methods section.
-   Add a new card, delete unused ones, or set a primary card.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765450753156-unnamed - 2025-12-11T185908.046.png>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765450769377-unnamed - 2025-12-11T185921.229.png>)

### Step 4: Update Billing Information

-   Open the billing information tab.
-   Modify your billing address to reflect accurate and up-to-date details.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765450795797-unnamed - 2025-12-11T185949.474.png>)

### Step 5: Review Transaction History

-   Access the charges and transaction history tab.
-   Download invoices or review past spending for better budget management.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765450821475-unnamed - 2025-12-11T190015.010.png>)

### Step 6: Monitor and Recharge Wallet Balances

-   Check your current wallet balance.
-   Add funds in bulk or set up automatic recharges to avoid service interruptions.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765450846436-unnamed - 2025-12-11T190036.739.png>)

## Pro Tips

**1\. Set a Default Payment Method:** Ensures seamless automatic payments and minimizes failed transactions.

**2\. Schedule Regular Reviews:** Check your subscription list and transaction history monthly to stay updated.

**3\. Enable Notifications:** Opt for alerts when wallet credits are running low or a transaction fails.

**4\. Keep Billing Information Accurate:** Outdated details can result in compliance issues or interrupted services.

**FAQs**




**Question:** How do I add a new payment method?
**Answer:** Go to the Payment Methods section in the Billing area, select "Add New Card," and provide the required card information.




**Question:** Can I manage multiple subscriptions at the same time?
**Answer:** Yes, you can oversee multiple subscriptions, including SaaS, Reselling, and Marketplace apps, directly from the Subscriptions section.




**Question:** What steps do I follow to update my billing address?
**Answer:** Navigate to the Billing Information section within Billing, click "Edit," and make the necessary changes to your billing address.




**Question:** How can I access my past invoices?
**Answer:** Visit the Charges and Transaction History section in Billing to view or download your previous invoices.




**Question:** What’s the process for adding funds to my wallet?
**Answer:** Head to the Wallet section, select "Add Funds," and follow the instructions to deposit the desired amount into your wallet balance.




**Question:** How do I set up automatic recharges for my wallet?
**Answer:** To set up automatic recharges, go to the "Wallet" section, select "Automatic Recharges," and follow the prompts to specify the amount and frequency for auto-recharging.  



**Question:** How do I monitor my wallet balance?
**Answer:** Go to the Wallet section in the Billing area to check your current wallet balance. This will display the available funds, helping you track your financial status and plan accordingly.  




**Question:** Am I able to cancel my subscription plan through the dashboard?

**Answer:** Yes. Within the "Manage Subscriptions" section, you have the option to both modify and cancel your plans as needed to suit your requirements.




**Question:** Are my credit card details stored securely within ?

**Answer:** Security is a priority. All payment information is encrypted and handled through secure, industry-standard payment gateways. does not store your full card numbers on internal servers.




**Question:** Can I receive alerts if my wallet balance is getting low or if a payment doesn't go through?
**Answer:** Yes, you can stay informed by enabling notifications. This allows you to receive alerts when your wallet credits are running low or if a transaction fails, helping you avoid potential service interruptions.
