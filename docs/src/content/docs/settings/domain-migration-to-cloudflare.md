**Domain Migration To CloudFlare**

The One-Click Migration feature via Domain Connect is designed to make migrating your existing domains to CloudFlare effortless. It eliminates the need for deletions and re-additions, allowing you to upgrade your domain management with a single click. It streamlines the process, ensuring a quick and seamless transition to CloudFlare.

## Benefits of Migrating to CloudFlare

-   Speed Upgrade: Experience a 15% speed boost for your funnels and websites.
-   Enhanced Security: Improved protection against DDoS attacks and other threats.
-   Smart Caching: Faster loading times for visitors due to optimized caching.

## How to Switch 

## Automated Flow with One-Click Migration

1.  Initiate Migration:
    -   Click the "Migrate Domain" button after going to Settings>Domains and URL Redirectors.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763575233020-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-19 130026.png>)

2\. Follow Domain Connect Flow:  

-   Follow the prompts provided by the Domain Connect flow. This automated process will handle your DNS updates seamlessly.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763575280268-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-19 130114.png>)

3\. Completion:  

-   Once the prompts are completed, your DNS updates will be applied automatically.
-   Your domain is now powered by CloudFlare.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763575321901-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-19 130156.png>)

### Manual Migration (For Unsupported Providers)

1.  Initiate Migration:
    -   Click the "Migrate Domain" button in your domain settings.
2.  Update DNS Records:
    -   Update your CNAME records to point to sites.ludicrious.cloud.
    -   Update your Apex record to point to the IP address 162.159.140.166.
3.  Authorize Domain:
    -   Click "Authorize Domain" to verify your DNS updates.
4.  Completion:

    -   Your domain is now successfully migrated to CloudFlare.

    FAQ:   
    


**Question:** What is the One-Click Migration feature via Domain Connect?
**Answer:**  The One-Click Migration feature simplifies the process of migrating your existing domains to CloudFlare. It automates DNS updates, eliminating the need to delete and re-add domains manually.  

    


**Question:** What benefits does migrating to CloudFlare offer?
    
**Answer:** Migrating to CloudFlare provides a 15% speed boost for your websites and funnels, enhanced security against DDoS attacks, and smart caching for faster loading times.  

    


**Question:** How do I initiate the One-Click Migration?
    
**Answer:** To start the migration, go to Settings > Domains and click on "Migrate Domain." Follow the prompts provided by the Domain Connect flow to complete the process automatically.  

    
**Question:  What should I do if my domain provider is unsupported by the One-Click Migration?
**Answer:** For unsupported providers, you can manually update your DNS settings. Click "Migrate Domain" in your domain settings, update your CNAME records to point to sites.ludicrious.cloud, update the Apex record to the IP address 162.159.140.166, and click "Authorize Domain" to verify the updates.  

    


**Question:** How long does the migration process take?
    
**Answer:** The migration process is generally quick and should be completed within a few minutes, depending on your domain provider's response time for DNS updates.  

    


**Question:** Will migrating to CloudFlare affect my existing website or funnel settings?
**Answer:** No, migrating to CloudFlare will not affect your existing website or funnel settings. The migration only updates DNS records to route your domain through CloudFlare for improved speed and security.  

    


**Question:** Can I revert the migration if needed?
**Answer:** Yes, if you encounter any issues or need to revert the migration, you can update your DNS settings back to the original configuration through your domain provider's dashboard.  

    


**Question:** Can I migrate multiple domains at once?
**Answer:** Yes, you can repeat the One-Click Migration process for each domain you manage. Each domain’s migration is independent and will follow the same streamlined process.  

    


**Question:** Will my SSL certificate be affected?
    
**Answer:** No. CloudFlare provides an automatic, high-level SSL certificate once the migration is complete. Your site will remain secure with the "padlock" icon in the browser, and in many cases, the security handshake will become even faster.  

    


**Question:** Do I need to manage my own DDoS settings after migrating?
    
**Answer:** No. Once the migration is complete, your domain is automatically protected by Cloudflare's enterprise-grade security, which includes **enhanced DDoS protection** as a default benefit.  

    


**Question:** Will migrating my domain affect my business email (e.g., Google Workspace or Outlook)?
    
**Answer:** No. The One-Click Migration via Domain Connect is designed to identify and preserve your existing MX (Mail Exchange) records. Your email flow should remain uninterrupted during and after the transition.
