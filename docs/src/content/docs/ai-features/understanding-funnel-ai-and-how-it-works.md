Creating high-converting funnels has never been easier with **Funnel AI**. This tool simplifies the funnel-building process by automating content generation, layout structuring, and industry-specific customization. By inputting a few details about your business, **Funnel AI** generates a fully functional funnel tailored to your objectives, saving you time and effort while ensuring a professional and effective design.

## How to Use Funnel AI to Build a Funnel

### Step 1: Navigate to the Funnels Section

-   Go to the **Funnels** section located within **Sites**.
-   Click on **"+ New Funnel"** to begin creating your funnel.

### Step 2: Select Funnel AI

-   A pop-up window will appear.
-   Choose **"Funnel AI"** and click **"Continue"** to proceed.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764329626654-unnamed - 2025-11-28T193338.074.png>)

### Step 3: Enter Your Business Name

-   Type the name of your business in the provided field.
-   This helps AI personalize the funnel based on your business identity.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766600512177-image2.jpg)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770063686070-image6.png)

### Step 4: Select Your Industry

-   Use the dropdown menu to choose your industry.
-   Example: If you run a **hair salon**, select "Hair Salon" from the list.
-   Click **"Next"** to move forward.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770063718550-image8.png)

### Step 5: Define Your Funnel Objective

-   Choose the **goal of your funnel** (e.g., lead generation, appointment booking, sales conversion).
-   Selecting the right objective ensures that the AI builds a funnel aligned with your needs.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764329690634-unnamed - 2025-11-28T193437.386.png>)

### Step 6: Customize Content Tone

-   Select how the content on your funnel should sound (e.g., professional, engaging, conversational).
-   This helps ensure your funnel resonates with your audience.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764329710414-unnamed - 2025-11-28T193459.183.png>)

### Step 7: Generate Your Funnel

-   Click **"Generate"** and wait a few seconds while AI builds your funnel.
-   AI will automatically design the layout, structure, and content for your selected industry and objective.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764329730501-unnamed - 2025-11-28T193523.082.png>)

### Step 8: Review and Customize

-   Once the funnel is generated, review its structure and content.
-   Make any adjustments if needed before publishing.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770063767627-image2.png)

## FAQ's




**Question:** Can I edit the funnel after AI generates it?
**Answer:** Yes! You can manually edit the funnel layout, text, images, and call-to-action buttons after AI generates it.




**Question:** What industries are supported?
**Answer:** Funnel AI supports a variety of industries, including **healthcare, beauty, real estate, education, fitness, and more**.




**Question:** How long does it take for AI to generate a funnel?
**Answer:** The process takes only a **few seconds**. Once generated, you can review and customize the funnel as needed.




**Question:** Can I change the tone of the content after generating the funnel?
**Answer:** Yes! You can adjust the **tone and messaging** of the funnel at any time to better match your brand voice.




**Question:** Is the AI-generated content optimized for conversions?
**Answer:** Yes, Funnel AI structures the content based on best practices for conversion optimization, ensuring that the funnel aligns with your business goals.




**Question:** Does Reviews AI work across all social media platforms?
**Answer:** Reviews AI is compatible with major review platforms like Google and Facebook, ensuring your responses remain consistent across different channels.




**Question:** Do I need to know coding to use Funnel AI?
**Answer:** No, Funnel AI is designed for non-technical users. It provides pre-built structures that can be easily customized without coding.




**Question:** What if my specific business goal isn't just "making a sale"?

**Answer:** Funnel AI allows you to **Define Your Funnel Objective** in Step 5. You can select goals such as **lead generation** or **appointment booking**. The AI then designs the layout and structure specifically to drive users toward that specific action.




**Question:** Will the AI-generated funnel include my logo?

**Answer:** If you have already uploaded a logo to your **Business Profile** settings, the AI will often attempt to place it in the header. If not, you can simply click the image placeholder in the header and upload your logo in one click.
