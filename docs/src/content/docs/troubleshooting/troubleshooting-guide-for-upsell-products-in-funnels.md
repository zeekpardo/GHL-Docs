This troubleshooting guide helps you resolve common issues that may arise when setting up **upsell products** in your funnels. From product configuration and button actions to payment processing errors, it provides clear, step-by-step solutions to ensure your upsell flows function correctly and deliver a seamless purchasing experience for your customers.

**FAQs: Troubleshooting Upsell Products in Funnels**

**Questions: Why is my upsell product not appearing in the funnel after I create it?**
**Answer:** If your upsell product isn’t showing up in the funnel, it could be due to the product not being correctly linked to the funnel or not being saved properly.

**How to Fix:**

-   After creating the product, ensure that you have **added the product to the funnel** at the appropriate upsell step.

**Steps to Fix:**

1.  Go to your funnel settings.
2.  Navigate to the funnel step where the upsell product should appear.
3.  Click **Add Product** and select the created upsell product.

**Save** the changes to the funnel.

**Questions: Why is the upsell button not triggering the payment for my product?**
**Answer:** This issue could arise if the **button settings** aren’t configured correctly to trigger the upsell product payment.

**How to Fix:**

-   Ensure that the **button action** is set to **One-Click Upsell/Downsell** in the button settings.

**Steps to Fix:**

-   Go to the **upsell page** and edit the button you’ve added for the upsell.
-   In the button settings, look for the **Button Actions** section.

Select **One-Click Upsell/Downsell Product** from the available actions.

-   Choose the correct **upsell product** and configure the post-purchase action (e.g., redirect to another page).
-   **Save** the button settings.

**Questions: Why is my upsell product not reflecting the correct price when the customer clicks the button?**
**Answer:** If the product price is not showing correctly, it might be due to an issue with the **price override** or the **product pricing settings** not being properly updated.

**How to Fix:**

-   Double-check the price settings for your upsell product and ensure that any price override is set correctly.

**Steps to Resolve:**

1.  Go to the **Products** section and find the upsell product.
2.  Verify that the **price** is set correctly (e.g., $7 per month or $27 one-time).
3.  Ensure that no conflicting price settings exist in the upsell funnel step.

Revisit the **upsell funnel step**, select the correct price, and **save** the changes.

**Questions: Why is the customer not being redirected after purchasing the upsell?**
**Answer:** If the customer isn’t being redirected after completing the upsell purchase, the **post-purchase redirection** might not be set correctly in the button settings.

**How to Fix:**

-   Ensure the button is configured with a **redirect action** after the upsell is completed.

**Steps to Resolve:**

1.  Edit the **upsell button** in the funnel.
2.  In the **Button Actions**, check if the **post-purchase redirection** is enabled.
3.  Set the redirection to the next page or confirmation page after the purchase is completed.

**Save** your changes to the button settings.

**Question:** Why isn’t the upsell product automatically charging the customer’s credit card?
**Answer:** The issue may arise if the **payment action** for the upsell product is not properly linked to the customer’s credit card details.

**How to Fix:**

-   Ensure the **payment settings** are correctly configured in the product settings for automatic charging.

**Steps to Resolve:**

1\. Go to the **Payments tab>integrations.**

**![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeXixFWDKfWtOmu1iWJU2oAYn_EOiVTzC-bGz7uIqhFOMmWBQaA_ohMoOvm5ut2gLkGEeYuGiJgPNMIxZeSxx6kBYmRMY4inY_Pcd0aKh_Uih5aaG-5Zt-2O8PTJHYc7gb9RKMF?key=z6X79Pu0JiKki7LmF4Vcv2ZL)**

2\. Check the payment method for the product (ensure **credit card payment** is selected).

3\. Verify that the **One-Click Upsell** action is correctly set for automatic processing.

**4\. Save** the settings and test the upsell again.




**Question:** My upsell button is not functioning as expected. What could be the problem?
**Answer:** The upsell button may not be functioning correctly if the **link** for the button is not set up properly or the button’s **action** is not linked to the correct product.

**How to Fix:**

-   Check that the **button link** is configured to the correct **upsell product** and the button action is set to the correct payment processing option.

**Steps to Resolve:**

1.  Go to your funnel and **edit the upsell page**.
2.  Click on the upsell **button** and check the **button action** settings.
3.  Ensure the button is set to **One-Click Upsell/Downsell** and that the **correct product** is selected.
4.  Save and test the button again.




**Question:** How do I set up a down-sell product in my funnel?
**Answer:** Setting up a **downsell product** follows the same process as an upsell, with an additional option to offer a discounted alternative if the customer declines the initial upsell.

**How to Set Up:**

-   Create a **down-sell product** and link it to the **down-sell button** in your funnel.

**Steps to Set Up:**

-   Create the **down-sell product** in the **Products** section.
-   Go to the **down-sell page** in your funnel and edit the **down-sell button**.

In the **Button Actions**, select **One-Click Downsell Product**.

**Question:** Why is my upsell product not showing the correct quantity or availability?
**Answer:** If the upsell product shows incorrect availability or quantity, it may be due to an issue with the **inventory** or **product settings**.

**How to Fix:**

-   Verify that the **inventory settings** for the upsell product are configured correctly, especially if the product is quantity-limited.

**Steps to Resolve:**

1\. Go to the **Payments tab> Products> Inventory** section.

2\. Open the **upsell product** and check if **inventory management** is enabled.

3\. Ensure that the **quantity available** is correct and adjust as needed.

**Question:** Will the upsell product be added to the customer's original order, or is it processed separately?
**Answer:** If a customer chooses to purchase an upsell product, it will **create a separate order** rather than being bundled into the initial checkout transaction.




**Question:** Can I offer a different product if a customer declines my initial upsell?

**Answer:** Yes, this is handled through a "Downsell" step. If a customer clicks a "No" link or declines the first offer, you can redirect them to a downsell page featuring a different or lower-priced product.




**Question:** Why might an upsell product reflect an incorrect price when a customer clicks the button?

**Answer:** This is often due to a **conflicting price override** or incorrect pricing settings. You should verify the price in the Products section and ensure the funnel step is using the correct price point.
