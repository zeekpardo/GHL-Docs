**How are payouts generated?**

Payouts get generated in the '**Payouts**' screen under the '**Pending**' tab on the 1st of every month and the column '**Due**' reflects the date on which the payout should be done to the affiliate based on the configurations done for the campaign they were enrolled into.  

Payouts are generated for a specific interval and the ability to make actual payments at a later date. This is mostly used to cover refunds and other issues that might occur.

**How do Commissions work? How are they linked to Payouts?**

Commissions are how payouts are essentially earned. The affiliate earns a commission each time someone makes a purchase through the unique link associated with their Affiliate Campaign.  

An Affiliate’s Commissions are based on the percentage of commission fixed for the campaign which the Affiliate is marketing.

**PRO TIPS:**

-   Payments are completed manually. The Affiliate Manager owner/admin must complete them.
-   Affiliate commissions can be recurring or one-time, depending on the Stripe product being sold and how it’s set up.
-   Affiliate commission is tracked by sales happening in Stripe. doesn’t track refunds for subscriptions or one-time products.
-   Subscription cancellations should be effectively monitored - the Affiliate Manager administrator can choose to pay out affiliates 15, 30, or 60 days post-sale, allowing for the commission to be denied for a canceled subscription.
-   Commission can be set up to be a flat rate commission.

### FAQ




**Question:** How do I approve or deny affiliate payouts?
**Answer:** Navigate to Marketing > Affiliate Manager > Payouts. Under the 'Pending' tab, you will find all pending payouts. You can approve or deny payouts by selecting the relevant option for each payout. Approved payouts move to the 'Approved for Payout' tab, while denied payouts will be listed under 'Denied Payouts'.




**Question:** When are payouts generated?
**Answer:** Payouts are generated on the 1st of every month and appear in the 'Pending' tab. The 'Due' column shows the date by which the payout should be made to the affiliate based on the campaign's configurations.




**Question:** How are commissions linked to payouts?
**Answer:** Commissions are earned each time someone makes a purchase through an affiliate's unique link. The commission amount is based on the percentage set for the affiliate campaign. Payouts are then made based on these earned commissions.




**Question:** Can I use PayPal for affiliate payouts?
**Answer:** Yes, you can use PayPal for affiliate payouts. Ensure that your PayPal account is integrated into the payment’s integrations area. For detailed instructions on setting up PayPal Auto Payouts, refer to the article titled “How to pay your Affiliates using PayPal Auto Payouts”.




**Question:** How can affiliates set up their bank payout method?
**Answer:** Affiliates can go to their profile, navigate to the "Payout Methods" tab, click "Add Payout Method," choose "Bank Transfer," and enter their required bank details based on their country. They can also set the preferred payout method and manage multiple options.




**Question:** How do businesses process payouts to affiliates via bank transfer?
**Answer:** Businesses can approve payouts in the "Approved Payouts" tab, choose to pay affiliates individually or in bulk, download the payment CSV, adjust it if necessary, and upload it to their payment platform to initiate the payout. Once completed, they can mark payments as paid.




**Question:** Can I customize the payout terms for my affiliate campaigns?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize affiliate payout terms in the campaign settings. For example, you can set a NET-30 payout term to ensure that commissions are only paid after a 30-day refund window has passed. This gives you ample time to account for any adjustments, such as refunds or chargebacks.




**Question:** How are payouts generated, and when should they be processed?
**Answer:** This is explicitly discussed in the description under the section "How are payouts generated?" where it mentions that payouts are generated on the 1st of every month, and the 'Due' column reflects the date by which the payout should be made, based on the campaign configurations.  




**Question:** How can I control which payout methods (like PayPal or Bank Transfer) are visible to my affiliates?

**Answer:** You can configure this within the Affiliate Manager by navigating to **Settings > Affiliate Portal Settings**. Click on **"Configure Payout Methods"** to select the specific options you want to offer. Once you save your changes, affiliates will only see those selected methods in their portal.




**Question:** What happens if I move an "Approved" payout back to "Pending"?
**Answer:** The payout will return to the Pending tab, and the "Due Date" will remain the same. This is useful if you realize an affiliate’s tax information is missing or if you need to double-check a specific sale before sending funds.




**Question:** Does the system automatically adjust commissions if a customer requests a refund or cancels their subscription?

**Answer:** No. The platform does not track refunds for Stripe products. It is the responsibility of the administrator to monitor cancellations and refunds manually. To handle this, it is recommended to use payout terms (like 30 or 60 days post-sale) so you can deny commissions for transactions that were later refunded or canceled.
