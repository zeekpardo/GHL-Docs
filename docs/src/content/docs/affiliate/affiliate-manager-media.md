**Affiliate Manager: Media**

The Affiliate Manager’s Media Library has been enhanced to improve the management and sharing of media assets with affiliates. The process for uploading, downloading, and linking media to specific campaigns or making it available across all campaigns is now more efficient. This functionality ensures affiliates can quickly access the right assets, saving time and improving workflow. By uploading marketing media, such as images, videos, banners, and documents, affiliates can easily access and promote your products or services, boosting brand visibility and sales potential.

## **How will this feature help you?**

-   **Centralized Media Library**: Easily upload and organize various promotional materials like images, videos, banners, and documents.
-   **Streamlined Access for Affiliates**: Affiliates can quickly access media directly through the Affiliate Portal, saving time and improving focus on promotions.
-   **Campaign-Specific Media**: Link media to specific campaigns or make it universally available, ensuring affiliates have relevant content.
-   **Increased Brand Visibility**: Well-equipped affiliates can enhance brand exposure and drive more sales.
-   **Customizable Client Portal**: Create a branded experience by customizing the Client Portal with a custom domain for a professional affiliate environment.

## FAQ’s




**Question:** How can I upload media to the library?
**Answer:** To upload media, navigate to _Marketing > Affiliate Manager > Media_, then click on _\+ Add File_ to select and upload your asset. Choose whether to link the media to a specific campaign or make it universally accessible.




**Question:** Can affiliates access media directly from the portal?
**Answer:** Yes, affiliates can easily access, copy media links, or download assets directly from the Affiliate Portal. This makes it quick and convenient for them to get the resources they need.




**Question:** How do I organize the media I upload?
**Answer:** You can categorize and organize your media files into folders within the Template Library for better management and ease of access.




**Question:** Can I customize my Client Portal for a more branded experience?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize your Client Portal with a custom domain to create a more seamless, branded experience for your affiliates.




**Question:** Can I update or replace media after it’s uploaded?
**Answer:** Yes. You can update, replace, or reorganize media files at any time within the Media Library to keep content current and relevant for affiliates.




**Question:** Can media be shared across multiple campaigns?
**Answer:** Yes, you can choose to make media universally accessible, allowing affiliates to use the same asset across multiple campaigns for consistent branding and messaging.




**Question:** If I update a file, do I need to send a new link to my affiliates?

**Answer:** No. If you replace an existing file in the Media Library with an updated version, the change is reflected instantly in the **Affiliate Portal**. Any affiliate who goes to download the asset or uses the "Copy Link" feature will automatically receive the newest version.




**Question:** What types of files can I upload to the Media Library for my affiliates?

**Answer:** You can upload a wide variety of promotional materials, including images, videos, banners, and documents (such as PDFs or brand guidelines).




**Question:** If I delete a media file from the library, what happens to the affiliate's link?

**Answer:** Deleting a file will immediately break the link in the Affiliate Portal. We recommend "Replacing" a file instead of deleting it if you want to keep the same URL active while updating the content.
