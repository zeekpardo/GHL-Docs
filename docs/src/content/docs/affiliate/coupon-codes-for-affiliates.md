Revolutionize your affiliate game with a dynamic strategy! Craft exclusive links for enticing, with coupon discounts/ freebies. As affiliates champion these links, every user sign-up brings a surge of commissions. It's a win-win, turbocharging traffic and conversions while propelling both affiliate and service provider to new heights! 

## **Creating affiliate links for free services without requiring a coupon can have several advantages:**

**1\. No Barrier to Entry:** Since there's no cost associated with the service or product, potential customers don't need to make any financial commitment, making it easier to attract leads.

**2\. Higher Conversion Rates:** Users are more likely to try out a free service, as it doesn't require an upfront payment. This can lead to higher conversion rates compared to paid services.

**3\. Wider Audience Appeal:** Free services are generally more appealing to a broader audience, as they don't have to invest money to try it out.

**4\. Builds Trust and Credibility:** Recommending a genuinely useful free service can build trust with your audience, as they see you're providing value without any hidden costs.

**5\. Encourages Trial and Adoption:** Users are more likely to try out a service if it's free, which can lead to higher adoption rates and potential upsell opportunities later.

**6\. Easy to Promote:** Promoting a free service is often easier, as it doesn't require convincing potential customers to make a purchase.

**7\. Less Friction in the Sales Funnel:** Since there's no payment involved, the process of converting a lead into a user is streamlined, resulting in a shorter sales cycle.

**8\. Opportunity for Upselling:** Once users see the value in the free service, they may be more inclined to upgrade or purchase related products or services in the future.

**9\. Increased Brand Exposure:** A well-promoted free service can lead to increased brand visibility, as satisfied users may refer others or share their positive experiences.

**10\. Positive User Feedback and Reviews:** Offering a valuable free service can lead to positive feedback and reviews, which can further enhance the reputation of the service and your affiliate efforts.

**11\. Long-term Relationship Building:** By introducing users to a free service, you have the opportunity to build a long-term relationship with them. This can lead to continued engagement and potential future affiliate sales.

**_Note: Affiliates can set a 0% - 100% coupon for selling either at full price or as a free service._**

### **How does this work?**

1.  Go to Marketing on the left > Affiliate Manager on the top > From the dropdown Click on Affiliate
2.  In the Affiliate/Promoter module, click on the three dots next to the Affiliate to which you want to add the coupon code.

-   Note: The coupon will only work in the corresponding affiliate’s link. If the coupon is entered under another affiliate’s link, it will not grant the discount.

## **FAQ**




**Question:** How does setting a 0% discount help in tracking affiliates?

**Answer:** Setting a 0% discount allows you to track affiliates’ activities without offering a discount to customers. This is useful for monitoring performance and calculating commissions.




**Question:** Can I use both percentage and fixed coupons for the same affiliate?
**Answer:** Yes, depending on your promotional strategy, you can offer either percentage-based discounts or fixed dollar amount discounts through the affiliate management system.




**Question:** What are the advantages of using fixed dollar coupons over percentage discounts?

**Answer:** Fixed dollar coupons provide a clear and straightforward discount, which can be more attractive to customers. They also offer greater control for specific promotional campaigns, making them ideal for targeted marketing efforts.




**Question:** How can I track the effectiveness of affiliate campaigns using these coupons?

**Answer:** Coupons allow you to track conversions and affiliate performance by linking specific promotions to individual affiliates. This data can be analyzed to measure the success of campaigns and optimize future strategies.




**Question:** Can affiliates share coupon codes directly with their audience?

**Answer:** Yes, affiliates can share their unique coupon codes directly with their audience. These codes can be used to apply discounts when users sign up through the affiliate's link, helping to drive traffic and conversions.




**Question:** Can affiliates promote free services without a coupon code?
**Answer:** Yes, affiliates can promote free services without the need for a coupon code. This can be especially effective for attracting a wide audience, as it lowers the barrier to entry for potential customers, making them more likely to try out the service. Additionally, the affiliate will still be credited for any sign-ups or conversions.




**Question:** Can the same coupon only be used with a specific affiliate link?
**Answer:** Yes. Coupons are tied to the corresponding affiliate’s link. Entering the coupon under another affiliate’s link will not grant a discount or credit the affiliate.




**Question:** Can I set an expiration date for these affiliate coupons?
**Answer:** Currently, these coupons remain active as long as the affiliate campaign is live. To end a promotion, you can manually remove the coupon from the affiliate’s profile in the Affiliate Manager.




**Question:** Why is the coupon "Generate Code" step required?

**Answer:** Clicking **"Generate"** ensures that the system creates a unique hash for that specific affiliate. This prevents "Coupon Hijacking," where one affiliate tries to use another’s popular code. The system verifies that the code entered at checkout matches the Affiliate ID associated with the referral link.




**Question:** Can I offer a "Ten Dollars Off" coupon instead of a percentage?

**Answer:** Yes. The system supports **Fixed Coupon Support**, allowing you to choose a fixed dollar amount rather than a percentage. This is ideal for specific promotional campaigns where a clear cash discount is more attractive to the customer.
