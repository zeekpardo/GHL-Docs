The Affiliate Manager Dashboard is your go-to tool for managing and optimizing affiliate campaigns effortlessly. With its centralized interface, it provides real-time metrics, key insights, and actionable data to help you make informed decisions. Whether you’re tracking performance, rewarding top affiliates, or managing payouts, this dashboard ensures that you stay organized and efficient.

### **How-to Steps:**

**1: Access the Dashboard:**

-   Navigate to the marketing section on the left-hand side.
-   Select 'Affiliate Manager,' then from the dropdown choose 'Dashboard.'

#### **2: Create Campaigns and Add Affiliates**

### **FAQs**




**Question:** How do I add a branded domain to my affiliate campaigns?

**Answer:** Navigate to Settings > Business Profile > Branded Domain to add your domain. It will automatically reflect in all affiliate campaign URLs.




**Question:** How do I filter data by campaign?

**Answer:** Use the filter options on the dashboard to select specific campaigns and date ranges.




**Question:** Can I automate payouts for my affiliates?

**Answer:** Yes, use the auto-approve feature to streamline the payout process.




**Question:** What core metrics are available on the dashboard?

**Answer:** You can view total revenue, total customers, conversion percentage, and conversion trends.




**Question:** How do I track affiliate performance over time?

**Answer:** You can monitor affiliate performance by using the **Affiliate Leaderboard** to track top performers and by regularly checking the **conversion trends** and **total revenue** metrics. This will help you identify which affiliates are performing best and optimize your campaigns accordingly.




**Question:** Can I filter data by specific campaigns or date ranges?
**Answer:** Yes, use the **Campaign Picker** and filter options to select individual campaigns and define custom date ranges for insights.




**Question:** Can I export data for a single affiliate instead of the whole campaign?
**Answer:** Yes. While the "Export Sales CSV" provides a bulk view, you can use the search bar or filters to isolate a specific affiliate's data before exporting to get a tailored report.




**Question:** Can I manually attribute a sale to an affiliate if the customer forgot to use their link?

**Answer:** Yes. You can navigate to the **Commissions** tab within the Affiliate Manager, click "Add Manual Commission," and link the specific transaction to the desired affiliate. The dashboard metrics will update to include this manual entry.




**Question:** How can I handle new affiliate registrations and commission approvals efficiently?
**Answer:** You can manage these through the **Latest Updates** section of the dashboard. This area allows you to review, approve, or deny recent commissions and keep a real-time track of new affiliate registrations as they join your program.
