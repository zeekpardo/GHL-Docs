**Switching from FirstPromoter to Affiliate Manager**

This article will cover how to export your affiliates from FirstPromoter so you can start using the Affiliate Manager directly within . Follow along to get started.

### Step 1: Export your FirstPromoter Affiliate List

-   Within your FirstPromoter dashboard, click the Promoters section.
-   Then click the Export button.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763638640680-unnamed - 2025-11-20T193709.680.png>)

-   Your list will be exported to your computer.

### Step 2: Format the File 

-   Once the export is complete, **format your file as a CSV** with headers including “First Name”, “Last Name”, “Email-id”, “Phone Number”, “Industry”, and “Website”.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763638718361-unnamed - 2025-11-20T193829.189.png>)

### Step 3: Import your Affiliate List CSV File

-   Navigate to Affiliate Manager > Affiliates
-   Click +Add and “Upload Affiliates” to import a list

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763638879740-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194105.765.png>)

-   Select a list from your device, map the fields, and confirm the upload.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763638905281-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194134.770.png>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763638918443-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194146.887.jpg>)

-   Once the list is uploaded, you can then create Affiliate Campaigns.

For more details, see the article “**Affiliate Manager Part 2 - How to Add or Upload Affiliates**

### FAQ




**Question:** What should I do if the import process fails or shows errors?
**Answer:** Check that your CSV file is formatted correctly with the required headers and that there are no missing or incorrect data entries. Make sure that the file size does not exceed the platform’s limits and try re-importing after corrections.




**Question:** Can I add affiliates manually if I don’t have a CSV file?
**Answer:** Yes, you can add affiliates manually by navigating to Affiliate Manager > Affiliates and clicking the “+Add” button. From there, you can enter affiliate details individually.




**Question:** How do I handle duplicates when importing affiliates?
**Answer:** The Affiliate Manager may have built-in duplicate detection. However, it’s recommended to review your CSV for duplicates before importing. If duplicates are detected during import, you may need to manually resolve them or follow platform-specific prompts for handling duplicates.




**Question:** What happens if some fields are missing during the import process?
**Answer:** If mandatory fields are missing, the import process may fail or those specific affiliates may not be added. Ensure that all required fields like “First Name”, “Last Name”, and “Email-id” are present in your CSV and correctly mapped during the import process.




**Question:** How do I export my affiliate list from FirstPromoter?
**Answer:** Go to the Promoters section in your FirstPromoter dashboard and click the Export button to download your affiliate list.




**Question:** What should I do after successfully importing affiliates?
**Answer:** After the import is complete, you can create affiliate campaigns and assign affiliates as needed to start tracking referrals and performance.




**Question:** Is the "Email-id" field mandatory for every affiliate?

**Answer:** Yes. The email address serves as the unique identifier for each affiliate. Without a valid email, the system cannot create an affiliate profile, send login credentials for the affiliate portal, or track commissions accurately.




**Question:** How do my imported affiliates log in to see their commissions?

**Answer:** Once added to a campaign, you can send them the URL to your **Affiliate Portal**. They will use their email address to log in. If it’s their first time, they can use the "Forgot Password" or "Magic Link" feature to set their credentials and access their dashboard.




**Question:** What specific column headers are required in my CSV file to ensure a successful import?
**Answer:** To correctly format your file for the Affiliate Manager, your CSV must include headers for **"First Name", "Last Name", "Email-id", "Phone Number", "Industry", and "Website"**.
