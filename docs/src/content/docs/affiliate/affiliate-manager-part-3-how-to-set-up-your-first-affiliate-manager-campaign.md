**Affiliate Manager Part 3 - How to Set Up Your First Affiliate Manager Campaign**

Affiliate campaigns are a powerful tool for driving sales and rewarding your partners for their contributions. Whether you’re new to affiliate marketing or looking to optimize your strategy, this guide will walk you through the process of creating a successful campaign. By leveraging features like customizable commissions, multi-tier support, and direct affiliate assignments, you can build a robust system to incentivize affiliates and boost your business growth.

## **Navigation**

### **Step 1: Start by Clicking the "Add" Button**

-   Navigate to **Marketing** on the left > **Affiliate Manager** at the top > From the dropdown select **Campaigns**
-   Click +Add to create a new campaign

### **Step 2: Choose Your Campaign Source**

Select the source for your campaign, such as a funnel, website, or store. This defines where your products are listed and sales will be tracked.

The Affiliate Manager also supports external websites, allowing you to create campaigns, track sales, and manage commissions for websites hosted outside the platform. For more information please see the article “**Create Affiliate Campaigns using External Websites”**

**Please note: You can also edit the source of an existing affiliate campaign. This eliminates the need to recreate campaigns if a funnel or source is deleted, and ensures that old campaign data remains intact.**

**Please also note, changing the source will generate a new referral URL, and the old one will stop tracking.**

### How to add 2nd Tier Commission

Second-tier commissions in affiliate marketing refer to a system where affiliates can earn commissions not only for the sales they directly generate but also for the sales generated by affiliates they refer to the program. This creates a multi-level structure where affiliates can benefit not only from their efforts but also from the efforts of those they recruit.

### How does Second Tier Affiliate Marketing work?

-   Go to Marketing > Affiliate Manager
-   Navigate to Campaigns and Create new campaigns for running two-tier commissions.
-   While creating the campaign, there will be an option to add the first-tier commission and second-tier commission

## FAQ’s




**Question:** What is the purpose of multi-tier commissions?
**Answer:** Multi-tier commissions allow affiliates to earn rewards not only for their sales but also for the sales made by affiliates they refer to, fostering a cascading incentive structure.




**Question:** How can I customize commission settings for individual affiliates?
**Answer:** Navigate to the Affiliate Page, select an affiliate, and adjust their commission rates under the "Commissions" tab in the "Active Referral Link" section.




**Question:** Can I clone an affiliate campaign?
**Answer:** Yes, go to the Campaigns section, click on the three-dot menu next to the campaign, select "Clone Campaign," and choose whether to retain affiliates in the new campaign.




**Question:** How do second-tier commissions work?
**Answer:** Second-tier commissions reward the parent affiliate who referred another affiliate when that referred affiliate generates a sale.




**Question:** Can I set product-specific commissions?
**Answer:** Yes, product-level commissions can be customized to provide specific rewards for individual products.




**Question:** What happens if affiliate-specific commission rates conflict with campaign-level rates?
**Answer:** Affiliate-specific commission rates take precedence over campaign-level settings.




**Question:** What happens when I suspend an affiliate from a campaign?

**Answer:** Suspending an affiliate:

-   Pauses tracking (no clicks, leads, or sales)
-   Prevents access to the campaign
-   Disables manual lead assignments or backdated commissions

Unsuspending reactivates their access and links.




**Question:** How many tiers can I add in a multi-tier commission structure?

**Answer:** The Affiliate Manager supports up to **seven tiers**, offering flexibility for complex referral networks.




**Question:** Can I edit or remove affiliates from campaigns quickly?
**Answer:** Yes, from the Affiliate List Page, use the three-dot menu next to an affiliate to add, remove, move, suspend, or unsuspend them from specific campaigns.




**Question:** Can I move an affiliate to a new campaign without losing their existing customers?
**Answer:** Yes. When using the "Move to Campaign" action, the system gives you the option to either "Keep" or "Move" existing customers along with the affiliate, ensuring a smooth transition for their portfolio.




**Question:** Can I use an affiliate campaign for a website not hosted on this platform?

**Answer:** Yes. The Affiliate Manager supports **external websites**. You can track sales and manage commissions for sites hosted elsewhere by following the specific setup for external sources.
