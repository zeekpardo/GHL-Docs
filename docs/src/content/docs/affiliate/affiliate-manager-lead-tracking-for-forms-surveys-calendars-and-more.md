Affiliate campaigns are an effective way to track leads and manage referrals seamlessly. With robust tools for tracking forms, surveys, calendars, and much more, businesses can monitor performance and optimize campaigns efficiently. This guide will walk you through setting up campaigns and leveraging key features for lead generation and tracking.

### **How-to Steps**

1.  **Navigate to Affiliate Manager:**
    -   Navigate to **Marketing** on the left > From the Dropdown in **Affiliate Manager** \> select **Campaign**.
2.  **Create a New Campaign:**
    -   Click on the 'Add' button on the Campaign Page.

1.  **Select the Source:**
    -   Choose From:
    -   Funnels/Websites, Stores, Forms, Surveys, Calendars, or External Websites as your lead source.

**Note:** Currently we only support lead tracking for campaigns with Funnels/Websites, Stores, Forms, Surveys, Calendars, or External Websites. So Users will not be able to define commission settings for such campaigns

1.  **Configure the Campaign:**
    -   Assign Affiliates to the campaign.
    -   Add a description and review any additional settings as needed.

1.  **Publish the Campaign:**
    -   Publish your campaign to start tracking leads.
    -   Click on the 'Publish now' button

    -   Access the Source field in the Lead tab of the Affiliate Profile Page to monitor leads.

### **Benefits**

-   **Efficient Tracking:**
    -   Track leads directly from forms, surveys, and calendars without requiring purchases.
-   **Ideal for Service Industries:**
    -   Perfect for clients in service industries who rely on appointments, submissions, and leads.

### **Pro Tips**

-   **Embed/Iframes Limitations:**
    -   Custom Embed/Iframes are not supported for lead tracking. Ensure forms, surveys, and calendars are added as components inside a funnel/webpage.
-   **Branded Domains:**
    -   Users can add their own domain to reflect on the referral URL by going to Settings -> Business Profile -> Branded Domain. The new domain will automatically reflect on referral URLs.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** Why can't I use custom embeds for lead tracking?

**Answer:** Custom embeds are not supported to ensure accurate lead tracking. Add forms, surveys, and calendars directly to your funnel/webpage.




**Question:** How can I use my domain for referral URLs?

**Answer:** Go to Settings -> Business Profile -> Branded Domain to add your domain. Once added, it will reflect on the referral URLs automatically.




**Question:** What industries benefit most from this feature?

**Answer:** This feature is particularly beneficial for service industries that depend on leads generated from forms, surveys, and calendar bookings.  
This feature enhances your ability to track and manage leads, providing valuable insights and ensuring compliance across various lead sources.




**Question:** How do I assign affiliates to a campaign?

**Answer:** This topic is explicitly discussed in the "Configure the Campaign" section, where it mentions "Assign Affiliates to the campaign."




**Question:** What happens if I don’t add a branded domain?

**Answer:** If no branded domain is added, affiliate campaign URLs will default to the system’s standard domain. Adding a branded domain ensures all referral links display your company’s URL.




**Question:** Will the affiliate see the leads they have generated in their own portal?
**Answer:** Yes. Once a lead is successfully tracked through a form or calendar, it will appear in the Affiliate’s dashboard under their "Leads" or "Referrals" section, providing them with transparency on their performance.




**Question:** Can one affiliate be part of multiple campaigns simultaneously?

**Answer:** Yes. An affiliate can be assigned to multiple campaigns. However, each campaign will have a unique referral URL. The affiliate must use the specific link for the campaign they wish to be credited for.




**Question:** Which lead sources are supported for tracking within affiliate campaigns?
**Answer:** Currently, the system supports lead tracking for **Funnels/Websites, Stores, Forms, Surveys, Calendars, and External Websites**. Please note that because these are lead-based, users cannot define specific commission settings for these campaign types, and custom Embeds/iFrames are not supported.
