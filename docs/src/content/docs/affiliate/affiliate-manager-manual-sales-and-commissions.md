**Affiliate Manager | Manual Sales and Commissions**

Accurate tracking of affiliate sales is crucial for maintaining trust and transparency within affiliate programs. The Manual Sales and Commissions feature in the Affiliate Manager offers businesses the ability to record sales that may not be captured automatically. This ensures affiliates receive appropriate credit for all their efforts, creating a reliable and transparent system for tracking commissions. (Check out our other tutorials for thorough guides to get started.)

### **Key Features:**

1.  **Add Manual Sales:** Assign sales and commissions manually through the Affiliate Profile page.
2.  **Customer/Lead Filtering:** Search for existing customers or create new leads directly from the interface.
3.  **Optional Fields for Customization:** Add Event IDs for invoice or reference tracking and Event Dates for accurate transaction logging.

### **Step-by-Step Guide**

**1\. Access the Affiliate Manager:**

-   Navigate to **Marketing** on the left > **Affiliate Manager** on the top > From the Dropdown select **Affiliate**

**2\. Select an Affiliate:**

-   Find the affiliate you wish to credit.

**3\. Go to the Commissions Section:**

-   On the Affiliate Profile page, scroll down to the **Table Tab** under the Commission section.

**4\. Add Manual Sales:**

-   Click on **Add Manual Commissions** to open the manual sales pop-up.

### **Notes:**

-    Affiliate commission is tracked via a sale happening first in Stripe. The system does not currently track refunds for One-time products or Subscriptions.  
-   For subscription cancellations, the Affiliate Manager administrator can choose to pay 15, 30 or 60 days after the sale has occurred. This allows for sufficient time for the administrator to deny commission if the subscription gets canceled. Once canceled the lead will move into the churned category and can later be re-activated by initiating another checkout or enrolling them again in Stripe. 
-   The affiliate commission can be one-time or recurring depending on the Stripe product being sold. 
-   Payments are either a manual process that would need to be done by the Affiliate Manager Administrator or you can now also pay your Affiliates using Paypal Auto Payouts please see the article titled “**Automating Affiliate Payouts with PayPal: A Step-by-Step Guide**”
-   If a coupon is used during check-out, the discount value is considered revenue, and the commission is calculated off that discounted value

### **Use Cases**

### **Scenario 1: Offline Sales**

A customer purchases a product in-store. Use the Manual Sales feature to credit the affiliate who referred the customer.

### **Scenario 2: Post-Sale Adjustments**

An automated system missed recording a sale due to a technical issue. Add the sale manually to ensure the affiliate receives their rightful commission.

### **Scenario 3: Special Promotions**

Track and credit affiliates for sales made during a limited-time event where sales tracking was temporarily disabled.

**FAQs**




**Question:** What is the purpose of the Manual Sales and Commissions feature?
**Answer:** It enables businesses to track and credit affiliate sales that are not captured by automated systems, ensuring affiliates are fairly compensated.




**Question:** Can I add a new customer directly from the interface?
**Answer:** Yes, you can create a new lead by clicking on the **Add as Lead** option when entering manual sales.


**Question:  What is the Event ID field used for?
**Answer:** The Event ID allows you to add an invoice or reference ID for tracking purposes, making it easier to link transactions to specific documents.




**Question:** Can affiliates view their manually added commissions?
**Answer:** Yes, affiliates can see their commissions, including manually added entries, in their profile, maintaining transparency.




**Question:** Do affiliates get notified when a manual sale is added?
**Answer:** This depends on your notification settings. You can enable commission notifications so affiliates are alerted when new sales (manual or automated) are credited.  




**Question:** How can I approve or delete multiple manual commissions at once?
**Answer:** You can use the "Bulk Actions" feature to approve, deny, or delete multiple commissions at once. Simply select the desired commissions using checkboxes and then click the "Bulk Actions" button to perform the action.




**Question:** Can manual sales be used for offline transactions?
**Answer:** Yes, if a customer purchases a product offline, you can manually add the sale to credit the affiliate who referred the customer.




**Question:** How can I prevent paying commissions on subscriptions that get canceled early?

**Answer:** Administrators can set a payout delay of **15, 30, or 60 days** after the sale. This window allows you to deny the commission if the subscription is canceled before the payout occurs.




**Question:** Can I attribute existing Stripe transactions to an affiliate if they weren't linked initially?

**Answer:** Yes. By using the "Add Manual Sales" option from the three-dot menu on the Affiliates screen, you can enter a customer's email and select from their previous transactions to attribute them to a specific affiliate.
