**Adding Sub-Affiliates:**

You can add sub-affiliates directly in the affiliate section with ease.

**Key Aspects:**

-   No Purchase Necessary: Previously, customers had to make a purchase to become a sub-affiliate. Now, sub-affiliates can be added without any purchase, simplifying the process.
-   Streamlined Process: Adding and removing sub-affiliates requires just two clicks, making it faster and more efficient.
-   Direct Addition Option: Users can add a sub-affiliate directly, even if they are not already present on the platform.

**How to add sub-affiliates:**

1- Navigate to the Affiliate page in Affiliate Manager.

2- Select the affiliate and scroll down to the Sub-Affiliate tab.

3- Click "Add Sub-Affiliate" and choose the campaign.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** How can I bulk upload affiliates to ?
**Answer:** You can bulk upload affiliates using a **.CSV file**. Ensure the file includes headers like “First Name,” “Last Name,” “Email-id,” “Phone Number,” “Industry,” and “Website.” Navigate to **Affiliate Manager > Affiliates > Add > Upload Affiliates**, map the fields, and upload the file.




**Question:** Can sub-affiliates be added without a purchase?
**Answer:** Yes, sub-affiliates can now be added without requiring a purchase. Navigate to the affiliate’s profile, go to the **Sub-Affiliate tab**, and click “Add Sub-Affiliate” to assign them to a campaign.




**Question:** How can I manually add sales and commissions for affiliates?
**Answer:** To manually add sales, navigate to **Affiliate Manager > Affiliates**, select the menu dots next to an affiliate, and choose **Add Manual Sales**. Input the customer’s details, revenue amount, and event date to credit the affiliate accurately.




**Question:** Do affiliates receive system-generated emails when added?
**Answer:** No, affiliates do not receive a system-generated email when they are added to . However, they will receive an email with their unique Affiliate ID when an affiliate campaign is published or made live.




**Question:** How can I find the Affiliate ID for a specific campaign?
**Answer:** To find the Affiliate ID for a specific campaign, navigate to Marketing > Affiliate Manager > Affiliates. Scroll down and click on an affiliate's name, then toggle between your campaigns to see the unique ID associated with each campaign.




**Question:** How do I export a list of affiliates?

**Answer:** Navigate to the Payouts section of the Affiliate tool and click Export to download your affiliate list.




**Question:** Can I add a contact as an affiliate if they haven't bought anything yet?

**Answer:** Absolutely. Previously, a purchase was often required, but the updated system allows you to add anyone as an affiliate or sub-affiliate regardless of their purchase history. This is ideal for partnering with influencers or industry peers.




**Question:** How do I assign many affiliates to a specific campaign at once?

**Answer:** You can **bulk add** affiliates to a campaign during the initial selection process or while you are creating a new campaign within the Affiliate Manager.
