**Affiliate Manager | Adding Sub-Affiliates**

Managing your affiliate program is essential for effective marketing campaigns. Adding sub-affiliates is a streamlined process that saves time and enhances efficiency. This guide will walk you through the steps to add and manage sub-affiliates in your affiliate campaigns.

## How It Works

### Steps to Add Sub-Affiliates

1\. Navigate to the Affiliate Manager:

-   Go to the Marketing section and select the Affiliate Manager at the top.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912254079-unnamed \(74\).png>)

2\. Select an Affiliate:

-   Choose the affiliate for whom you want to add a sub-affiliate.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912290019-unnamed \(75\).png>)

3\. Add Sub-Affiliate:

-   Scroll down to the Sub-Affiliate tab.
-   Click on "Add Sub-Affiliate."

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912328741-unnamed \(76\).png>)

4\. Choose or Add a New Contact:

-   You can either select an existing contact from your database or add a new contact by filling in their email, first name, last name, company name, and the campaign to which they should be assigned.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912376482-unnamed \(77\).png>)

5\. Assign Campaign:

-   Ensure you have selected the correct campaign under the campaign dropdown.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912424781-unnamed \(78\).png>)

6\. Save Changes:

-   Click "Save" to add the sub-affiliate to the selected campaign.

### Managing Sub-Affiliates

Editing Sub-Affiliates:

-   Click on the three dots next to the sub-affiliate’s name.
-   Select "Edit Affiliate" to update their details.

**How to enable Sub Affiliate Sign ups for Affiliates:**

-   Go to the final step in the campaign creation or editing process.
-   Click on the "Additional Settings" option.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912470136-unnamed \(79\).png>)

-   Turn on the Sub-Affiliate Sign-Up Page by toggling the switch.
-   Choose the appropriate form for sub-affiliate sign-ups from the dropdown menu.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912488599-unnamed \(80\).png>)

-   Click "Finish" to save your changes.

**_Note: This feature requires campaigns to be tiered( 2 tiers or more), and the signup form must have the required fields (First Name, Last Name, and Email)._**

**_You can also configure this option globally in the Global Settings. Affiliates will see a sign-up link in their affiliate portal, which they can share to recruit sub-affiliates._**

**Setting it Up in Global Settings**

1\. Access the Affiliate Manager dropdown and select Settings.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912522080-unnamed \(81\).png>)

2\. Under Default Campaign Settings, enable the Sub-Affiliate Sign-Up Page toggle.

3\. Select the default form that will be used for sub-affiliate sign-ups in all future campaigns.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912547860-unnamed \(82\).png>)

**_Please Note: These changes will only apply to new campaigns created after the settings are updated. Existing or older campaigns will not be affected by these changes_**.

**Affiliate Portal:**

Once the settings are configured, affiliates will have access to a sign-up link in their portal. They can share this link to bring in potential sub-affiliates. The portal will also display all active sign-up form links and provide a list of sub-affiliates associated with each affiliate.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1761912576820-unnamed \(83\).png>)

Deactivating Sub-Affiliates:

-   Click on the three dots next to the sub-affiliate’s name.
-   Select "Mark as Inactive" to deactivate the sub-affiliate.

Deleting Sub-Affiliates:

-   Click on the sub-affiliate’s name.
-   Click "Delete" and then "Save" to remove the sub-affiliate.

## FAQs




**Question:** Can I add a sub-affiliate who is not already in my contact list?
**Answer:** Yes, you can add new contacts as sub-affiliates directly from the Affiliate Manager.  




**Question:** How do I know which campaign to assign a sub-affiliate to?
**Answer:**  Ensure you have selected the appropriate campaign from the campaign dropdown menu when adding a sub-affiliate.  




**Question:** Can I deactivate a sub-affiliate instead of deleting them?
**Answer:** Yes, you can mark a sub-affiliate as inactive if you do not want to delete them permanently.  




**Question:** Is there a limit to the number of sub-affiliates I can add?
**Answer:** There is no specified limit to the number of sub-affiliates you can add to your campaigns.  




**Question:** How can I keep track of changes made to sub-affiliates?
**Answer:** Regularly review the sub-affiliate tab within the Affiliate Manager to track and manage changes  




**Question:** How do affiliates access their sub-affiliate signup link?
**Answer:**  Affiliates can find their sub-affiliate signup link within their dashboard under the sub-affiliate tab or within the campaign itself in the affiliate section.  




**Question:** Can I configure the Sub-Affiliate Sign-Up Page globally for all campaigns?
**Answer:**  Yes, you can enable the Sub-Affiliate Sign-Up Page globally in the Affiliate Manager under Settings. This will apply the settings to all new campaigns, allowing affiliates to access a sign-up link in their portal to recruit sub-affiliates.  




**Question:** Can I edit the details of a sub-affiliate after adding them?
**Answer:** Yes. Click the three dots next to the sub-affiliate’s name and select **Edit Affiliate** to update their information.




**Question:** Can I approve or deny sub-affiliates before they can start selling?

**Answer:** Yes. If your global affiliate settings are set to "Manual Approval," every new sub-affiliate who signs up through a link will show as "Pending" until you review and approve them in the Affiliate Manager.




**Question:** What happens to a sub-affiliate if I delete the "Parent" affiliate?

**Answer:** If a parent affiliate is deleted, the sub-affiliates typically remain in the system as standard affiliates but will no longer have a "Parent" connection for override commissions. To maintain the structure, it is often better to mark the parent as **Inactive** rather than deleting them.




**Question:** How do I enable the sub-affiliate sign-up page for a specific campaign?

**Answer:** Go to the final step of the campaign creation or editing process, click on **"Additional Settings,"** toggle the "Sub-Affiliate Sign-Up Page" switch to ON, and choose your desired form from the dropdown.
