**Affiliate Manager - Customize Naming conventions for your Niche**

Managing an affiliate program requires a platform that is flexible enough to adapt to various business needs. offers such flexibility by allowing users to customize the names of modules in the Affiliate Manager. This article will guide you through the simple steps to rename these tabs and discuss the advantages of doing so.

## **Benefits of Customizing Tab Names**

**Customization**

Tailor the name of the tab to better reflect its purpose within your business operations.

**Clarity and Understanding**

A more intuitive name can reduce the potential for confusion among users who may not be familiar with the term "Affiliate Manager."

**Alignment with Branding**

Rebrand the tabs to align with your brand identity, creating a more cohesive and professional user experience.

**Increased User Engagement**

A more descriptive tab name can attract more attention and encourage users to explore and engage with the features within.

**Improved User Experience**

Enhance the overall usability and navigation of your platform by opting for a well-chosen tab name.

**Enhanced User Satisfaction**

Users are more likely to enjoy a positive experience when they can easily locate and use features, leading to higher satisfaction and retention rates.

## **How to Customize the Naming Conventions**

Follow these steps to rename the Affiliate Manager tabs:

**Go to Marketing > Affiliate Manager**

Navigate to the Marketing section on the left and click on 'Affiliate Manager' at the top.**Access Settings**

From the dropdown, select “Settings” and locate the Customizations sub-tab.

**Re-Name Tabs**

You will find options to rename the tabs according to your brand. For example, you could rename 'Affiliates' to 'Promoters' or 'Referrers,' 'Campaigns' to 'Funnels,' and 'Payout' to 'Payment.'

**Confirm Changes**

After making the desired changes, confirm to save them. Note that these new names will replace the old ones everywhere they are used within the Affiliate Manager. You can click on Restore Default to change everything back to the native default names.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I change the tab names back to the original default names after customizing them?
**Answer:** Yes, you can revert to the original default names at any time by clicking the 'Restore Default' option in the settings. This will reset all tab names back to their native defaults.




**Question:** Will changing the tab names affect any of the functionalities within the Affiliate Manager?
**Answer:** No, changing the tab names will not impact the functionalities. It is purely a visual change to improve clarity, branding, and user experience. All features and operations within the Affiliate Manager will continue to work as usual.




**Question:** Are there any limitations on the types of names I can use for the tabs?
**Answer:** There are no strict limitations on the names you can use, but it's best to keep them concise, relevant, and aligned with your business terminology for clarity and consistency. Avoid using special characters that may not be supported across all platforms or devices.


**Question:  Will the custom tab names appear for all users, or only for my account?
**Answer:** The custom tab names will be visible to all users within your affiliate program who access the Affiliate Manager. Any changes made to the tab names will be applied universally across your platform for consistency.




**Question:** Will renaming tabs affect reports or analytics?
**Answer:** No, renaming tabs does not affect data, reports, or analytics. All underlying functionalities and data remain intact.




**Question:** If I rename a tab, will the old name still appear in other parts of the Affiliate Manager?

**Answer:** No. Once you confirm the changes, the new names will globally replace the old ones everywhere they are used within the Affiliate Manager module.




**Question:** Is there a character limit for the new tab names?

**Answer:** While there isn't a strict hard limit, we recommend keeping names under **12–15 characters**. Longer names may get cut off on mobile devices or cause the navigation bar to look cluttered.
