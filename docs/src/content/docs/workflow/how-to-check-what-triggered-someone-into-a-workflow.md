### **FAQ**




**Question:** How do I check how a contact was added to a workflow?
**Answer:** To check how a contact was added, simply go to the specific workflow, click on _Execution Logs_, search for the contact, and click on the clipboard icon. The details, including the source, will be visible under the _Added From_ section.




**Question:** Can I see the exact trigger that added a contact to the workflow?
**Answer:** Yes, when you access the _Execution Logs_ and view the details of a contact, you can see the specific trigger or source that initiated their entry into the workflow, whether it was a tag, a manual add, or another method.




**Question:** What does the_Added From_section show?
**Answer:** The _Added From_ section provides detailed information on how the contact was added to the workflow, whether through a manual action, a trigger, or a direct addition from their contact record.




**Question:** Can I view the history of how multiple contacts were added to the workflow?

**Answer:** Yes, you can search for multiple contacts within the _Execution Logs_ of the workflow and view each contact’s source by checking their details through the clipboard icon.




**Question:** What should I do if I can’t find the source for a contact in the logs?
**Answer:** If you’re unable to find the source, double-check that you’re looking in the correct workflow and ensure you’re searching with accurate contact details. If the issue persists, it could be due to a system error or an incorrect workflow.




**Question:** Are all methods of adding contacts tracked in Execution Logs?

**Answer:** Yes, all actions such as manual adds, bulk actions, and triggers are logged in **Execution Logs**, allowing you to trace the source.  


**Question:Can I filter or narrow down the search results in the Execution Logs?

**Answer:** Yes, you can filter your search results within the Execution Logs to find specific contacts or sources more easily. Filters can help you narrow down the search based on date, action type, or contact details, making it easier to pinpoint how someone was added to the workflow.


**Question:Can I filter or narrow down search results in the Execution Logs?

**Answer:** Yes. You can use filters within Execution Logs to narrow results based on date, action type, or contact details, making it easier to find how a contact was added.




**Question:** What is the specific icon I should look for to see more details?

**Answer:** In the Execution Logs, look for the **"View Details"** hyperlink or the **clipboard icon** next to the contact's name. Clicking this will expand the sidebar containing the source information.




**Question:** What specific section of the Execution Logs displays how a lead was added to the workflow?

**Answer:** Once you click on "View Details" for a contact, you can find the source of their entry in the **"Added From"** section of the log details.
