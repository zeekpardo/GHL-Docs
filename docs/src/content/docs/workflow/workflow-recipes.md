**Workflow Recipes**

In this article, you will learn about the Workflow Recipes. 

Within the Workflow builder, you have several options for getting started in creating a new Workflow. You can start from scratch with a blank slate,  import steps from an existing campaign, or you may use a “Recipe” from the list of pre-made templates we’ve created for you.

When you click the "Create Workflow" button, you will see two options in a dropdown menu:

-   You can choose to start from a Recipe.
-   You can choose to start the workflow from scratch.

### **How to Use**

1.  Click the "Create Workflow" button in the Automation section.
2.  Select either "Recipe" or "Start from Scratch" from the dropdown menu

### **The Five Fast Workflow**

The Fast Five workflow automation is a pre-built template designed to streamline customer engagement immediately after they opt in. The Fast Five workflow consists of **five key touchpoints** designed to engage leads quickly:

1.  **Confirmation Email**
    -   Sends an automated email confirming the lead’s action (e.g., signing up or claiming an offer).
    -   Customize the email subject, body, and sender details if needed.
2.  **Confirmation SMS**
    -   Sends a text message to confirm the lead's engagement.
    -   Ensure a valid phone number is collected during the opt-in process.
3.  **Wait for Reply & Follow-Up Action**
    -   If the lead replies, the system may send a booking link or notify a team member.
    -   If no reply is received, the workflow progresses to the next step.
4.  **Call Connect (Optional)**
    -   The system automatically calls the lead and connects them with a sales or support team member.
    -   If the lead does not answer, the call redirects or moves to the final step.
5.  **Voicemail Drop (If No Answer)**
    -   A pre-recorded voicemail message is sent to the lead if they don’t pick up.
    -   You can upload a custom voicemail file or use a default system message.

### **FAQ's**




**Question:** What are Workflow Recipes?
**Answer:** Workflow Recipes are pre-made templates that help streamline the creation of workflows by providing ready-to-use setups for common tasks and processes. These templates can be customized to fit your specific needs.




**Question:** How can I start creating a new Workflow?
**Answer:** You have two options for starting a new Workflow:

1.  Click the "Create Workflow" button in the Automation section.
2.  Choose either "Recipe" or "Start from Scratch" from the dropdown menu.




**Question:** What should I do if I want to use a Recipe?
**Answer:**

1.  Click the "Create Workflow" button in the Automation section.
2.  Select "Recipe" from the dropdown menu.
3.  Browse and select the desired Recipe from the list.
4.  Click “+Create new workflow” to apply the Recipe.




**Question:** Can I customize a Recipe?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize any Recipe to fit your specific use case. This includes adding or removing features as needed.




**Question:** What are some examples of available Recipes?
**Answer:** Some available Recipes include:

-   **Appointment Booking:** Detect intent and send booking links or SMS for decisions.
-   **Appointment Confirmation + Reminder:** Confirm appointments and send reminders.
-   **Auto Missed Call Text-Back:** Automatically reply to missed calls and notify the assigned user.
-   **Birthday Template:** Set up workflows for birthday promotions.
-   **Email Drip Sequence:** Drip a series of emails over time with automated follow-ups.
-   **FAQ Auto Reply:** Automate replies to FAQs across multiple platforms.
-   **FB Messenger:** Manage and respond to inbound Facebook messages.
-   **Fast 5 Lite:** Nurture leads with a combination of email, SMS, calls, and voicemails.
-   **GMB Business Message:** Auto-respond to Google Business Messages.
-   **List Reactivation:** Generate leads with AI filters for positive responses.
-   **Webinar Registration Confirmation & Reminders:** Confirm and remind about webinars.
-   **IVR:** Streamline caller navigation and messaging.
-   **No-Show Nurture:** Recover from missed appointments.




**Question:** How do I apply a Recipe to a new Workflow?
**Answer:**

1.  Select the Recipe you wish to use (it will be highlighted in color).
2.  Click “+Create new workflow” to load the template into the Workflow builder.




**Question:** Do Recipes support automation across multiple communication channels?
**Answer:** Yes, many Recipes support multi-channel automation including SMS, Email, Facebook Messenger, and Google Business Messages.




**Question:** How does IVR improve customer service?
**Answer:** IVR optimizes the customer experience by **reducing wait times**, guiding callers to the right department, and ensuring messages are captured accurately.
