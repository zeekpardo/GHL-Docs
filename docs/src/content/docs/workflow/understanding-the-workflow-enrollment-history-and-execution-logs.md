**Understanding the Workflow Enrollment History and Execution Logs**

Managing workflows effectively requires a clear view of how contacts move through each step, track actions taken, and identify any potential issues. **Execution Logs and Enrollment History** provide the necessary insights into these processes, ensuring smooth operation and helping to resolve errors efficiently. Users can access detailed logs, track progress more easily, and handle issues promptly, resulting in an optimized workflow experience.

**FAQs** 

**Questions: How can I view detailed execution information?**  

**Answer:** You can quickly access detailed execution information by clicking the "View Details" hyperlink, which is more visible and intuitive.

**Questions: What happens if there is an error in the execution log?**
**Answer:** Errors are more noticeable, with key fields like Status or View Details highlighted, making it easier to resolve issues promptly.

**Questions: How do I track actions taken by contacts?**
**Answer:** When you open the details of a row, it will be automatically highlighted, allowing you to easily track which action you're analyzing.

**Questions: What does the "Action" column display?**
**Answer:** The "Action" column shows the name of the action you've assigned, and hovering over it provides additional context about the action type.

**Questions: How do I filter and sort actions?**
**Answer:** Filters are more streamlined and user-friendly, with actions linked to your workflow appearing at the top, making it easier to filter and prioritize actions.

**Questions: What statuses are included in the execution logs?**
**Answer:** You can view statuses such as "Workflow Completed," "Removed by Workflow Action," and "Removed by External Workflow Action," providing better insight into contact progress.

**Questions: Can I control how much data I see in the Execution Logs and Enrollment History?**
**Answer:** Yes, both sections support pagination, allowing you to choose how many rows to display per page.

**Questions: How do I track the contact's journey through the workflow?**
**Answer:** By selecting the "Highlight Contact Path" icon, you can instantly view the path the contact has taken through the workflow, with the path highlighted in green.

**Questions: What is the "Go To Action" button for?**
**Answer:** The "Go To Action" button allows you to quickly navigate to the specific action in the builder for adjustments.

**Questions: How does the Rate Limit Notification work?**
**Answer:** The Rate Limit Notification Banner informs you when contact enrollment is delayed due to rate limits, staying visible until the enrollments are executed.

**Questions: How can I see the exact time actions were taken?**
**Answer:** The "Executed On" field includes the timezone, providing clarity on when actions were performed across different time zones.

**Questions: Can I specify a custom date range for enrollment and execution history?**
**Answer:** Yes, you can select custom start and end dates for a tailored view, with a maximum date range of 30 days.

**Questions: How do I view a contact's detailed history?**
**Answer:** Click the "View Contact History" icon to access detailed action execution logs for any contact, with improved navigation breadcrumbs linking back to the logs.

**Questions: How can I access detailed execution logs for specific enrollments?**
**Answer:** Click the "View Execution History" icon to access detailed logs for actions taken during a specific enrollment, with enhanced navigation through clickable breadcrumbs.

**Questions: Can I adjust the data displayed in the Enrollment History section?**
**Answer:** Yes, you can control how many rows are displayed per page in the Enrollment History section, with options to display 10, 25, or 50 rows at a time.

**Questions: How can I track the exact execution time of actions?**
**Answer:** The "Executed On" field shows the timestamp along with the timezone for precise tracking.




**Question:** How can I tell what type of action was used if I have given it a custom name?

**Answer:** In the "Action" column, you can hover your mouse over the assigned action name to display a tooltip showing the actual action type for additional context.




**Question:** Can I export these logs to a CSV or Excel file?

**Answer:** While you can export a list of contacts from the main Contacts page, the detailed step-by-step Execution Logs are currently view-only within the platform. You can, however, use the **Custom Date Range** to narrow down your search results and take screenshots for reporting.




**Question:** How does the interface help me stay organized when I am looking at many lines of data?

**Answer:** The system uses "Highlighting Opened Rows," which means that as soon as you open the details for a specific action, that row is automatically highlighted so you don't lose your place while analyzing the data.
