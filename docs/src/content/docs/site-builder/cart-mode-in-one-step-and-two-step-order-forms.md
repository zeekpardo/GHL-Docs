_Cart Mode_ allows people to purchase multiple products through an order form. 

### **Key Features:**

-   **Support for Multiple Quantities**: Customers can purchase multiple quantities of any item within a single order.
-   **Cart Mode**: Multiple products can be added to a single order form, with the option to select different quantities for each item.
-   **Single & Multi-Product Options**: Easily switch between single-item or multi-product purchases within the funnel builder (multi-product select option).
-   **Customizable Quantity Limits**: Set a maximum order quantity per product, ranging from 1 to 999, directly on the funnel product page.

**How To Set Up Cart Mode:**

**Step One:** Navigate to your Funnel or Site

-   Go to Sites on the left > Funnels at the top.
-   Open the Funnel Step where you have your checkout (One-Step or Two-Step)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763750309071-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 133823.png>)

-   Click anywhere on the one-step or two-step order form element.
-   On the right-hand side, the editing sidebar will open.
-   Toggle on the "**Enable Cart Mode**" option to enable selling multiple products in a single order

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764874945116-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-04 140217.png>)

**Step 2:** Limit how many of a product someone can order:

-   Go back to the Funnel Overview
-   Click on Products
-   Click the dropdown menu and select the product you want to set a limit for
-   Click on additional options
-   Scroll down and toggle on “Allow multiple quantities to be purchased”
-   Save

Note: Maximum quantity per order can be set between 1-999 in the funnel product page.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763750385390-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 133939.png>)

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I enable Cart Mode in my order form?
**Answer:** To enable Cart Mode, navigate to your funnel step where the checkout form is located. Click on the one-step or two-step order form element, then toggle on the "Enable Cart Mode" option in the editing sidebar. This will allow customers to purchase multiple products through a single order form.




**Question:** Can I set a limit on the number of items a customer can purchase?
**Answer:** Yes, you can set a purchase limit for each product. Go to the Funnel Overview, select "Products," choose the product you want to limit, and enable the "Allow multiple quantities to be purchased" option. You can set a maximum quantity per order between 1 and 999.




**Question:** Can I sell multiple quantities of bump products?
**Answer:** No, selling multiple quantities of bump products is not currently supported.




**Question:** Does PayPal support multiple quantities?
**Answer:** PayPal supports multiple quantities for single-payment items and allows a single quantity for recurring payment items. However, it does not support multiple quantities for recurring payments.




**Question:** What is the maximum number of units a customer can buy for a single product?

**Answer:** You can set a customizable quantity limit for each product ranging from **1 to 999** directly within the funnel product settings.




**Question:** How are shipping costs calculated in Cart Mode?

**Answer:** Shipping is usually applied as a flat fee at the product level. If you are selling multiple quantities of heavy items, it is recommended to factor your average shipping into the product price or use a weighted average, as the cart does not yet dynamically calculate shipping based on quantity/weight.




**Question:** What happens if I toggle off "Enable Cart Mode" after a customer has already added items to their cart?

**Answer:** The form will revert to a **single-selection** layout. To ensure a smooth user experience, it is best to finalize your Cart Mode settings before sending live traffic to the funnel.
