**Hosted Video Element**

Adding videos to your page is a great way to provide more visual details of your business. You can add how-to videos, testimonials, and more. This article goes over using the Hosted Video Element in Funnels.

### Adding the Video Element

-   Navigate to Sites on the left > Funnels at the top
-   Click into the Funnel you want to add a video to.
-   Click on the plus sign icon at the top left to add elements.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763639110042-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194457.871.jpg>)

-   Click on the Video Element
-   On the right-hand sidebar, go to Video Type > choose Hosted Video

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763639136162-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194525.489.jpg>)

### Add the Video to the Element

-   Click on Select Video to be taken to your Media Storage. (​​If you do not already have the video that you need, upload it to the Media Storage)

**To learn more about Media Storage, check out our article “The Media Storage Tab.”**

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763639161772-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194552.162.jpg>)

-   Once the Video loads, double-click on it for it to encode.
-   Edit the playback controls as needed.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763639184804-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194615.715.jpg>)

**NOTE:** The video can be in any of the mainstream Video formats as long as it is under 4GB.

-   **Tracking Viewer Progress on a funnel-hosted Video**

This can help increase lead conversion by targeting viewers based on their interaction with your content.

-   Select the video element and disable the "Show Progress Bar" option.  
    **Please Note:** The accuracy of the video tracking depends on whether or not you have turned off the progress bar. Keeping it toggled on would interfere with the accuracy of the workflow's video tracking.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763639208210-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194639.827.png>)

**Create a Redirect Page:**

-   Build a blank page with a form for unregistered visitors to collect their contact information before they can continue watching the video. This ensures they get registered as contacts in the system so that you can include them in the workflow afterward!
-   **Ensure this page is part of the same funnel** and set it as the redirect destination for unregistered visitors.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763639236064-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194707.610.png>)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763639260809-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194729.863.png>)

-   Save and preview to make sure that everything is working.

Go to the automation settings and create a new trigger based on video tracking with the necessary filters.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763639285063-unnamed - 2025-11-20T194754.366.jpg>)

 If you want to learn more about the video tracking trigger, check out our article “**Workflow Trigger: Video Tracking”**

### Why do my videos start on mute?

This is due to the regulations that auto-playing videos on iOS, Safari, Chrome, Android, Firefox, and Edge must start muted. The browser manufacturers control this policy, which cannot be overruled. 

**Note:** Non-auto-played videos start unmuted.

**FAQ**




**Question:** How do I add a video to my funnel page?

**Answer:** Navigate to Sites > Funnels, select the funnel you want to add a video to, click the plus sign icon to add elements, and choose the Video Element. Then, select "Hosted Video" and upload or select your video from Media Storage.




**Question:** What formats are supported for videos?

**Answer:** The video can be in any mainstream format as long as it is under 4GB.




**Question:** Why should I disable the progress bar on my video?

**Answer:** Disabling the progress bar ensures accurate video tracking by preventing users from manually adjusting their progress, which could interfere with tracking automation.




**Question:** How do I create a redirect page for unregistered visitors?

**Answer:** Build a blank page with a form to collect contact information, then set this page as the redirect destination for unregistered visitors within the same funnel.




**Question:** Why do my videos start muted?

**Answer:** This is due to browser regulations requiring auto-playing videos to start muted on iOS, Safari, Chrome, Android, Firefox, and Edge. Non-auto-played videos will start unmuted.




**Question:** What types of actions can I automate based on video progress?

**Answer:** You can automate actions such as sending follow-up emails or SMS messages, tagging leads, or triggering other workflows based on viewer engagement.




**Question:** Why do I need to double-click the video after upload?

**Answer:** Double-clicking initiates **encoding**, which prepares your video for proper playback, quality selection (e.g., 480p, 720p, 1080p), and tracking within your funnel.




**Question:** How does video integration improve lead conversion?
**Answer:** By tracking viewer engagement and using redirect pages for unregistered visitors, you can collect contact information and trigger targeted workflows, improving lead conversion rates.




**Question:** Is there a limit to how many videos I can host in my account?
**Answer:** While there is no specific limit on the _number_ of videos, each individual file must be under **4GB**. For the best user experience and faster loading times, we recommend compressing your videos before upload whenever possible.




**Question:** Why does my video take a few minutes to appear after I upload it?

**Answer:** This is because of **Encoding**. When you upload a video, our system creates multiple versions of it (480p, 720p, 1080p) to ensure that whether your lead is on a slow 3G connection or high-speed fiber, the video plays smoothly without buffering. Higher resolution videos may take slightly longer to process.




**Question:** Does the video player allow viewers to change the playback speed or watch in a floating window?
**Answer:** Yes. The video player supports **adjustable playback speeds** (0.5x, 0.75x, 1x, and 2x) and includes a **Picture in Picture (PiP) mode**, allowing for a more customizable viewing experience.
