**Content AI for the Site Builder**

**Content AI** is your assistant to generate your content 10X faster so that you can invest your efforts in other things.

Use **Content AI** to create:

-   Headlines
-   SubHeadlines
-   Bullet points
-   Paragraphs

## **Navigation**

-   Click on the **"Sites"** section on the left to open the Site Builder.

-   Within the Sites section, select **"Funnels or Websites"** or **"Blogs"** depending on where you wish to use Content AI.
-   Choose an existing site or create a new one by clicking **"+ New Funnel"** or **"+ New Website"**.

-   Click on the desired page you wish to edit or create a new page within your chosen funnel or website.

The Content AI option will appear in the in-line editor. Select the text, and the in-line editor will appear. Then click on the AI button. 

The Content AI model will ask for a description of the content required and show more advanced settings with variation count and tone of writing.

### Using Brand Voice with Content AI:

**Brand Voice Toggle**: Easily enable/disable **Brand Voice** within the **Content AI modal**. It’s **ON by default** if a Brand Voice exists.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764098505061-image9.png)

**Smart Autofill**: When **Brand Voice is ON**, the **Writing Tone** field is automatically filled based on your selected Brand Voice.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764098538572-image4.png)

**Metadata Shaping**: **Target Audience**, **CTA**, **Brand Purpose**, **Values**, and **Promise** silently influence the AI prompt behind the scenes.

**Brand Voice Selector Dropdown**: Choose your preferred Brand Voice from saved options, with your default pre-selected.

## FAQs




**Question:** What is Content AI in the Site Builder?
**Answer:** Content AI is a feature in the Site Builder that helps you generate content up to 10 times faster, allowing you to focus your efforts on other tasks. It can be used to create headlines, subheadlines, bullet points, paragraphs, and more.




**Question:** How do I access Content AI in the Site Builder?
**Answer:** To access Content AI, click on the "Sites" section to open the Site Builder. Within the Sites section, select "Funnels or Websites" or "Blogs." Choose an existing site or create a new one, and then click on the desired page to edit or create a new page. The Content AI option will appear in the in-line editor; click on the button to start using it.




**Question:** What types of content can I create with Content AI?
**Answer:** Content AI can be used to create various types of content, including headlines, subheadlines, bullet points, paragraphs, and more.




**Question:** Can I choose the length of the content generated by Content AI?
**Answer:** Yes, Content AI allows you to select the length of the content in each module, such as paragraphs, within the Site Builder.




**Question:** What happens when I click on Content AI multiple times?
**Answer:** When you click on Content AI, it retains the previously generated content. You can generate new content by clicking “Generate new content” again.




**Question:** Will I be charged if Content AI produces fewer variations than I requested?
**Answer:** No, if you select multiple variations and Content AI is only able to produce fewer variations than desired, you will not be charged for the unproduced variations.




**Question:** Does Content AI retain text element properties like color and style when generating content?
**Answer:** Yes, Content AI retains text element properties such as color, style, and formatting when generating content to ensure consistency with your existing design.




**Question:** What settings can I adjust when using Content AI?
**Answer:** When using Content AI, you can adjust advanced settings such as the variation count and tone of writing. Content AI will prompt you to describe the required content to generate tailored suggestions.




**Question:** How do I generate content with Content AI?
**Answer:** Click on the Content AI button in the in-line editor, provide a description of the content you need, adjust any advanced settings as required, and click "Generate" to receive content suggestions.




**Question:** Can I edit the content generated by Content AI?
**Answer:** Yes, you can further edit and customize the content generated by Content AI to suit your specific needs before inserting it into your page.




**Question:** How can I manage the Content AI and view its history?
**Answer:** To manage the Content AI and view its history, navigate to the **Marketing** section on the left and click on the **Content AI** tab at the top.




**Question:** How does Brand Voice work with Content AI?


**Answer:** When using Content AI, the Brand Voice toggle is ON by default if a Brand Voice exists. The Writing Tone field is automatically filled based on your selected Brand Voice. You can enable or disable Brand Voice in the Content AI modal, and select from saved options in the Brand Voice Selector Dropdown.


**Question:How can I provide feedback on the content that the AI generates?

**Answer:** You can click on the Thumbs Up or Thumbs Down icons within the tool to provide feedback. This helps improve the quality of future generations and tunes the AI to better match your specific expectations.




**Question:** Can I use a different tone even if my Brand Voice is turned on?

**Answer:** Yes. While the **Smart Autofill** feature will automatically suggest a Writing Tone based on your Brand Voice, you still have the flexibility to manually override it or choose a different variation if a specific piece of content requires a unique style.




**Question:** Will I lose my existing text formatting (like color or font style) when the AI generates new content?
**Answer:** No. The system ensures that all existing text element properties, including color, style, and formatting, are retained in the generated text to maintain the design of your site.
