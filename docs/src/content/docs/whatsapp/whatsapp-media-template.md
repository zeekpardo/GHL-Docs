**WhatsApp Media Template**

WhatsApp has added support for Media Templates, allowing users to send various types of media content such as images, videos, documents, and location data. This article provides a detailed guide on how to use this feature.

### **Use Cases for WhatsApp Media Templates**

Improve your WhatsApp marketing campaigns with Media Templates:

-   **Product Launches:** Show off new products with striking images and videos.
-   **Promotions and Offers:** Share discount codes or special offers with eye-catching visuals.
-   **Event Announcements:** Promote events with location sharing and compelling imagery.
-   **Customer Service:** Provide helpful tutorials or guides in document format.

### Before we start:

**Important Update: WhatsApp Marketing Templates Pause in the U.S. (April 1, 2025)**Starting April 1, 2025, Meta will pause the ability to send WhatsApp marketing template messages to U.S. numbers. However, utility and authentication templates, along with responses within the 24-hour customer service window, will still be allowed. This change does not affect marketing messages in other countries.

**What’s Changing & How to Prepare**Meta is temporarily pausing marketing templates in the U.S. to focus on WhatsApp’s long-term growth. While marketing messages won’t be delivered, businesses can still engage with customers using Utility Templates and Click-to-WhatsApp Ads.

**What You Should Do Next**

-   **Create Utility Templates**: Transition essential messages to utility templates to avoid disruptions.
-   **Use Click-to-WhatsApp Ads**: Engage customers through ads triggering WhatsApp conversations.
-   **Maximize the 24-Hour Service Window**: Ensure real-time responses to customer inquiries.
-   Please scroll down to see the section “**Convert Marketing Templates into Utility Templates.”** 

**Permitted WhatsApp Business Activities**

-   Utility Conversations: Order updates, appointment confirmations
-   Authentication Messages: OTPs, account verifications
-   Service Conversations: Responses within the 24-hour window
-   Click-to-WhatsApp Ads: Drive interactions via ads

### **Convert Marketing Templates into Utility Templates**

Starting April 1, 2025, Meta will block the delivery of marketing templates to US phone numbers. With the Template Rewriter, businesses can convert their marketing templates into utility templates, allowing them to continue messaging US-based customers without disruption.

The AI automatically removes non-compliant elements (like "Stop Now" buttons) and adjusts the content to align with Meta’s utility template guidelines.

**Cost Efficiency:** Utility templates are more affordable and provide higher delivery rates compared to marketing templates, allowing businesses to reach a broader audience while reducing costs.

**Important Note:**

The converted template will automatically have "\_utility" appended to its name, and the original marketing template will remain intact for future use.

**To convert templates:**

-   Navigate to your existing WhatsApp Marketing Template.
-   Select “Convert to Utility Template.”
-   The AI will rewrite the content to meet utility messaging guidelines and automatically submit the new template for approval.

## FAQs


**Question:What are WhatsApp Media Templates?

**Answer:** These pre-approved message formats let you include images, videos, documents, or location information in WhatsApp messages. They streamline customer communication, particularly for marketing, support, or authentication purposes.


**Question:Do I need approval before using WhatsApp message templates?

**Answer:** Yes, all templates must be approved with WhatsApp (Meta) to ensure they meet specific guidelines.


**Question:**  **What factors can lead to a template being rejected?

**Answer:** Common reasons for rejection include:

-   Missing or incorrect variable formatting.
-   Content violating WhatsApp's Business or Commerce policies.
-   Requests for sensitive information (e.g., full payment card numbers).
-   Abusive or threatening language.
-   Duplicating an existing template.


**Question:What does it mean if my template is 'Paused'?

**Answer:** A paused template indicates it received negative customer feedback or low engagement. You cannot send a paused template until its status changes back to 'Active'.




**Question:** How does the AI Template Rewriter work?

**Answer:** The AI rewrites the content to match utility messaging guidelines and submits the converted template directly for Meta’s review.




**Question:** What happens if I upload the wrong type of media?

**Answer:** You will receive an error. For example, uploading a video in an image-only template will be rejected. Ensure the media matches the template type and size limits.




**Question:** Can I change the image in a template for different customers?
**Answer:** Yes. When sending a template via a **Workflow** or the **Conversations** page, you can provide a unique public URL for the media. As long as the file type (e.g., Image) matches the approved template category, the system will render that specific file for the recipient.




**Question:** How does the "Convert to Utility Template" feature help U.S. businesses?

**Answer:** It allows you to use an **AI Template Rewriter** to automatically adjust existing marketing content into a "Utility" format. This removes non-compliant elements (like "Stop Now" buttons) so you can continue messaging U.S. customers without interruption.
