**WhatsApp Media Retention & “Add to Documents”**

This article serves as a comprehensive guide to understanding the enhanced media storage and management capabilities within . It outlines how your business can ensure long-term access to critical files shared via WhatsApp, moving beyond standard platform limitations to maintain a complete and reliable customer history.  

**Key Features and Benefits**

The integration of advanced media retention ensures that your communication history remains intact, professional, and accessible. By addressing the standard expiration windows of third-party messaging, provides a more robust environment for client interactions.

-   **Extended Media Life:** Both inbound and outbound media are automatically retained for **one full year**, bypassing the typical 7-day or 30-day expiration cycles.
-   **Centralized Documentation:** The ability to save attachments directly to a contact’s profile ensures that all vital files—regardless of the original channel—are stored in one unified location.

## How to Manage Your WhatsApp Media

Managing your files is straightforward and integrated directly into your existing conversation workflows.

### 1\. Automatic Retention

You do not need to take any action to benefit from the one-year storage. Whether a customer sends a photo (Inbound) or your team sends a proposal (Outbound), automatically secures these files to prevent them from becoming "expired" or "unavailable."

### 2\. Saving to Documents

For files that are critical for long-term records (such as contracts or site photos), follow these steps:

-   Navigate to the specific **WhatsApp conversation**.
-   Locate the **media attachment** you wish to save.
-   Select the **"Add to Documents"** option.

-   The file will be instantly accessible within the contact’s **Documents section** for permanent record-keeping.
