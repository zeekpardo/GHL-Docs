**How to Enable the Chat Widget for WhatsApp**

This guide will walk you through the steps to enable and configure WhatsApp on your chat widget, allowing you to seamlessly connect with your visitors via WhatsApp.

**Please note:** You must have WhatsApp enabled and a phone number to use this feature. To do so, go to "Settings" > "WhatsApp" in the side menu.

### **How the WhatsApp Chat Widget Works**

1.  Embed the code in the landing page's header (via external providers) or enable it through the Funnel/Website Settings toggle.
2.  Once the chat widget is embedded, it will appear on the landing page when the page loads in the browser.
3.  After a contact visits the page, fills out, and submits the form, they will be redirected to the WhatsApp web app with a pre-filled message sent to the selected number.
4.  You will then receive a notification in the "Conversations" view and can begin messaging the contact directly.

### **Setting Up WhatsApp on the Chat Widget**

To enable WhatsApp on your chat widget, follow these steps:

**Navigate to Chat Widget Settings**:

-   Go to platform and navigate to "Sites" on the left.
-   Access the Chat Widget at the top.

**Select WhatsApp Chat Type**:

-   Click on New and under the "Chat Type" options, select "WhatsApp Chat."

-   After selecting WhatsApp, save your changes to ensure the configuration is applied.

### **Configuring Your WhatsApp Number**

In addition to selecting WhatsApp as your chat type, you need to configure which WhatsApp number will be used by the chat widget:

**Access Widget Window Settings**:

-   Within the chat widget settings, scroll down to the "Widget Window" section.

**Select Your WhatsApp Number**:

-   Choose the WhatsApp number you wish to use for the chat widget from the list.
-   Save your selection to finalize the setup.

### **Final Steps and Benefits**

Once these settings are configured, any visitor who fills out their name, phone number, and message in the chat widget and clicks "Chat with Us" will be redirected to WhatsApp. This integration is particularly beneficial for users in international regions or those who frequently use WhatsApp for communication.

### FAQs:




**Question:** Do I need a WhatsApp account to enable the chat widget?
**Answer:** Yes, you must have an active WhatsApp account and a phone number to use this feature. For more details, see the article “**Setting up WhatsApp**”  




**Question:** Can I customize the appearance of the WhatsApp chat widget?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize the chat widget’s window, including the text, colors, branding, and form fields for email/phone inputs. While the widget will default to WhatsApp’s signature green colors, you can adjust the color scheme to match your branding.  




**Question:** How does the WhatsApp chat widget work after a visitor submits the form?
**Answer:** After a visitor fills out and submits the form, they will be redirected to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message addressed to your selected WhatsApp number. You will then receive a notification in the "Conversations" view to start the chat.  




**Question:** Can I choose which WhatsApp number to use for the chat widget?
**Answer:** Yes, within the chat widget settings, you can select the specific WhatsApp number you want to use for communication. Ensure that the number is configured correctly in your settings.  




**Question:** Can I use multiple WhatsApp numbers with different widgets?
**Answer:** Yes. You can create separate chat widgets and assign different WhatsApp numbers to each one. Just ensure each number is properly configured in your WhatsApp settings before assigning it.  




**Question:** Can my whole team see the WhatsApp messages?

**Answer:** Yes. Once the visitor sends the message, it appears in the **Conversations** tab of the platform. Any team member with access to that can see the message and reply, ensuring your customers get a fast response.  




**Question:** Does the visitor stay on my website during the WhatsApp chat?

**Answer:** No. When the visitor clicks "Chat with us," they are redirected to the WhatsApp application on their phone or WhatsApp Web on their desktop. This is a major benefit, as it allows the conversation to continue even after they have closed their browser or left your website.




**Question:** Does the widget always have to be green?

**Answer:** While the widget defaults to WhatsApp’s signature green shades to be recognizable to users, the settings allow you to configure your own **custom color scheme** to match your specific branding.
