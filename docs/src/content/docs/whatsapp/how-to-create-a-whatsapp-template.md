### **How to Create a WhatsApp Template**

### Before we start:

**Important Update: WhatsApp Marketing Templates Pause in the U.S. (April 1, 2025)**Starting April 1, 2025, Meta will pause the ability to send WhatsApp marketing template messages to U.S. numbers. However, utility and authentication templates, along with responses within the 24-hour customer service window, will still be allowed. This change does not affect marketing messages in other countries.

**What’s Changing & How to Prepare**Meta is temporarily pausing marketing templates in the U.S. to focus on WhatsApp’s long-term growth. While marketing messages won’t be delivered, businesses can still engage with customers using Utility Templates and Click-to-WhatsApp Ads.

**What You Should Do Next**

-   **Create Utility Templates**: Transition essential messages to utility templates to avoid disruptions.
-   **Use Click-to-WhatsApp Ads**: Engage customers through ads triggering WhatsApp conversations.
-   **Maximize the 24-Hour Service Window**: Ensure real-time responses to customer inquiries.
-   Please scroll down to see the section “**Convert Marketing Templates into Utility Templates.”** 

**Permitted WhatsApp Business Activities**

-   Utility Conversations: Order updates, appointment confirmations
-   Authentication Messages: OTPs, account verifications
-   Service Conversations: Responses within the 24-hour window
-   Click-to-WhatsApp Ads: Drive interactions via ads

[**_Read more here_**](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/whatsapp/cloud-api/guides/send-message-templates/)

### Getting Started:

**What are the prerequisites for using WhatsApp at a location?**

**The location should have an active WhatsApp Subscription, and the Meta onboarding should be completed.**

**Setting Up a WhatsApp Template**

**Step 1:** Go to Settings -> WhatsApp -> Template tab -> Click on Create Template

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555628131-unnamed - 2025-12-13T000656.965.png>)

**Step 2:** A new section will open where you will need to fill in the Template Name, Category, Language, and Header the information

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555645067-unnamed - 2025-12-13T000718.914.png>)

To learn about Media Templates, please see our article, **WhatsApp Media Template**

Details around the fields available

**Template Name -** The name of the template should be set using lowercase characters.

**Category -** Select between the Marketing and Utility Category. 

**Language -** Select from the list of all the languages available.

**Header (Optional) -** You can add a static or a variable header. Only 1 custom variable is allowed in the header.

**Please note:** You can use the Media Library directly when creating or editing WhatsApp templates. When adding a media header, simply select your media type and choose to either upload from your computer or select an existing asset from the Media Library.

You'll find this within the WhatsApp template builder under the header media selection flow.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767106428588-image12.png)

**Step 3:** Fill in additional details Body and Footer

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555672301-unnamed - 2025-12-13T000742.566.png>)

**Details around the fields available**

1.  **Body -** The message that would be sent to the customer. You can add multiple custom variables while creating the template by clicking on Add Variable. 
2.  **Footer (Optional) -** You can add a static footer.

**NOTE: Please fill sample value for all the custom variables added as Meta need examples to be sent while submitting the Template.**

**Please note:**

You can also use Service Booking variables directly in WhatsApp Templates. This allows you to include dynamic details like appointment times and service information in your WhatsApp messages. Simply add these variables to your templates and flows to create personalized, timely reminders and notifications for your customers.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766610494590-image4.png)

**Adding Media:**

-   **Images/Videos:** Upload images or short videos (under 16 MB) to make your message more engaging.
-   **Documents:** Attach PDFs or other documents that recipients can download.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555696462-unnamed - 2025-12-13T000807.630.png>)

-   **Locations:** Add location details by inputting the latitude and longitude, which you can easily obtain from Google Maps.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555714375-unnamed - 2025-12-13T000827.056.png>)

**Step 4:** Verify the template message and hit on Create

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555745321-unnamed - 2025-12-13T000857.304.png>)

**Step 5:** Now we will send this template to Meta for approval and you can track the status in the Template Tab.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555766681-unnamed - 2025-12-13T000919.110.png>)

**Statuses for Templates**

1.  **Pending -** Template successfully submitted and sent to Meta for approval.
2.  **Approved -** The template is successfully verified and can be used for seeing messages to the customers.
3.  **Rejected -** The template was rejected by Meta and needs to be resubmitted. Read FAQ for resubmission

### Convert Marketing Templates into Utility Templates

Starting April 1, 2025, Meta will block the delivery of marketing templates to US phone numbers. With the Template Rewriter, businesses can convert their marketing templates into utility templates, allowing them to continue messaging US-based customers without disruption.

The AI automatically removes non-compliant elements (like "Stop Now" buttons) and adjusts the content to align with Meta’s utility template guidelines.

**Cost Efficiency:** Utility templates are more affordable and provide higher delivery rates compared to marketing templates, allowing businesses to reach a broader audience while reducing costs.

**Important Note:**

The converted template will automatically have "\_utility" appended to its name, and the original marketing template will remain intact for future use.

**To convert templates:**

-   Navigate to your existing WhatsApp Marketing Template.
-   Select “Convert to Utility Template.”
-   The AI will rewrite the content to meet utility messaging guidelines and automatically submit the new template for approval.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555840702-unnamed - 2025-12-13T001029.943.png>)

### Trigger Links Support for WhatsApp

The trigger links feature is also available for your WhatsApp templates. This allows you to track link clicks and trigger specific actions based on customer interactions. Here’s everything you need to know about this feature and how to use it effectively.

**Easy How-to-Use Steps**

**1\. Create Trigger Links:**

-   Create trigger link > Marketing > Trigger Links > Add Link

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555858463-unnamed - 2025-12-13T001051.476.png>)

-   Go to the settings section found on the bottom left of your platform.
-   Navigate to WhatsApp > Templates.
-   Create your desired template body and add variables as needed.
-   Select the option to add Trigger Links from the available options.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555880622-unnamed - 2025-12-13T001109.458.png>)

**2\. Incorporate Trigger Links into Templates:**

-   When designing your WhatsApp templates, choose the Trigger Links you have created.
-   Ensure the links are placed strategically within your message to maximize engagement.

**3\. Set Up Automated Workflows:**

-   Use the platform’s automation tools to set up workflows triggered by link clicks.
-   Define the actions that should occur when a customer clicks on a Trigger Link, such as sending a follow-up message or updating customer records.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775062620909-image2.jpg)

**Monitor and Analyze Engagement:**

-   Go to Marketing > Trigger Links and access the analytics section to monitor which links are being clicked and by whom.
-   Use this data to refine your communication strategies and improve future interactions.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555923278-unnamed - 2025-12-13T001153.449.png>)

**Trigger Link Buttons:**

You can also add clickable Trigger Links to buttons, giving you the ability to track clicks, measure engagement, and branch workflows automatically based on user interactions

**How to Use:**

1.  Edit or create a WhatsApp template.
2.  Add a button and choose "Trigger Link" from the options.
3.  Select the link you want to embed and save the template.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555946261-unnamed - 2025-12-13T001215.968.png>)

4\. In workflows, use the Button Click condition to route contacts based on the link they clicked.

**Limitations:**

-   You can have a maximum of two buttons per message.
-   Examples: Two Trigger Link buttons or one Trigger Link button and one quick-reply button.

### Interactive CTA Buttons for WhatsApp

The Interactive CTA (Call-To-Action) buttons are designed to create more engaging and visually appealing messages. This feature allows users to respond quickly, visit websites, make phone calls, copy offer codes, opt out of marketing messages, and format text for better emphasis.

**How to Use:**

1\. Navigate to Settings > WhatsApp > Templates.

2\. Create Template > Add Button.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555963314-unnamed - 2025-12-13T001236.255.png>)

3\. Select the type of CTA button you wish to add: Quick Reply, Visit Website, Personalized Website Link, Call Phone Number, Copy Offer Code, or Marketing Opt-Out.

**Quick Replies:**

-   Allow users to respond quickly with predefined options, enhancing the conversation flow and user engagement. This allows customers to reply without typing.

**Visit Website:**

-   Direct users to your website with a single click, increasing traffic and improving conversion rates.

**Personalized Website Links:**

-   Utilize dynamic “Visit Website” buttons to customize links for each customer, offering a personalized experience and boosting engagement.
-   Adding a variable creates a personalized link for the customer to view their info. Only one variable can be added to the end of a URL.

**Call Phone Number:**

-   Provide an option for users to call a specified phone number directly from the message, facilitating immediate communication and support.

**Copy Offer Code:**

-   Enable users to easily copy promotional codes or offer details, making it convenient for them to redeem offers.

**Marketing Opt-Out:**

-   Give users the option to opt out of marketing messages effortlessly, ensuring compliance with WhatsApp regulations and improving user trust.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1765555998581-unnamed - 2025-12-13T001311.855.jpg>)

**Note:** Only one variable can be added to the end of a URL for personalized website links.

**Question:** What happens if my WhatsApp template is rejected by Meta?
**Answer:** If your WhatsApp template is rejected, Meta will provide a reason for the rejection. You can review the feedback, make the necessary changes, and resubmit the template for approval.




**Question:** How long does it take for a WhatsApp template to get approved by Meta?
**Answer:** The approval process for a WhatsApp template typically takes between a few minutes to 24 hours, depending on Meta's review process. You can track the status of your template under the "Templates" tab.




**Question:** Can I include media in my WhatsApp templates, such as images or videos?
**Answer:** Yes, you can include media in your WhatsApp templates. You can upload images, short videos (under 16 MB), and documents (like PDFs) to enhance your messages and make them more engaging.




**Question:** How does the AI Template Rewriter work?
**Answer:** The AI rewrites the content to match utility messaging guidelines and submits the converted template directly for Meta’s review.




**Question:** Can I clone a WhatsApp template?

**Answer:** Yes, simply click the three-dot menu next to the template and select Clone Template. Give the copy a new name, make your changes, and save.




**Question:** Are there limitations on header and body variables?

**Answer:

-   Only **one custom variable** is allowed in the **header**.
-   Multiple variables can be added in the **body**, each with a sample value for submission.




**Question:** What types of WhatsApp messages are still allowed in the U.S. after April 1, 2025?
**Answer:** Utility templates, authentication messages (like OTPs), service conversations within the 24-hour window, and Click-to-WhatsApp ads are still permitted. Marketing templates to U.S. numbers will be paused.
