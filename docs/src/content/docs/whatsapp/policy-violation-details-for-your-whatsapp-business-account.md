WhatsApp Business provides a robust platform for businesses to connect with their customers, but it’s important to follow the platform’s messaging and commerce policies to ensure compliance. If a violation occurs, your WhatsApp Business account may be restricted. This article outlines how to view policy violation details and how to file an appeal if your account has been restricted.

**Important:** All policy violations can be appealed **within 90 days** of being received.

Learn more about **_H_**[**_ow to comply with WhatsApp Business Messaging and Commerce policies_**.](https://faq.whatsapp.com/933578044281252)

### **How to View the Policy Violation Details**

**1\. Go to Business Support Home**: Start by navigating to the **Business Support Home**.

**2\. Access My Accounts**: Click on "**View my accounts**" under the "**My accounts" section**.

**3\. Locate Your Business Portfolio**: Scroll down to find the business portfolio connected to your restricted WhatsApp Business account. It will be labeled as "**Assets restricted**."

**4\. Select Your Business Portfolio**: Click on the business portfolio to view its details.

**5\. Find the Restricted Account**: In the WhatsApp accounts section, locate the restricted WhatsApp Business account. It may show as "**Account issues**" or "**Account disabled**."

**6\. View the Policy Violation**: Under the "**Available for review**" tab, click on the policy issue to open the details of the violation, including:

-   A summary of the violated policy and a link to the full policy.
-   Active restrictions on the account and the consequences of repeat violations.
-   How to avoid future violations.
-   What actions you can take to resolve the issue.
-   How to file an appeal.

### **How to File an Appeal and Request a Review**

**1\. Access the Policy Issue**: Follow the steps above to go to the "**Available for review**" tab under the Activity issues section.

**2\. Select the Policy Issue**: Click on the policy issue that you want to appeal.

**3\. Request a Review**: Click on "**Request review**."

**4\. Select an Appeal Reason**: Choose the reason for your appeal.

**5\. Provide Additional Information (Optional)**: Optionally, add any details that may help the review team better understand your case.

**6\. Submit the Appeal**: Click "**Submit**" to file your appeal. Reviews typically take up to 24 hours. You can track the review status in the "In review" tab.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** How long do I have to appeal a policy violation?
**Answer:** You can file an appeal within 90 days of receiving the violation notification.




**Question:** How can I view the details of a policy violation?
**Answer:** Go to the Business Support Home, select "View my accounts," find the restricted WhatsApp account, and click "See details" next to the policy issue.




**Question:** How do I file an appeal?
**Answer:** Select the policy issue under the "Available for review" tab, click "Request review," choose your appeal reason, and submit the appeal.




**Question:** What happens after I submit my appeal?
**Answer:** Reviews typically take 24 hours, and the decision may result in the issue being reversed. You can track the review status in the "In review" tab.




**Question:** What information should I provide when appealing?
**Answer:** While optional, providing additional details that clarify your situation may help the review team make a more informed decision.




**Question:** How can I avoid future policy violations?
**Answer:** Review the **WhatsApp Business Messaging and Commerce policies**, follow best practices, and ensure that your messages and commerce activities comply with the guidelines provided in the policy violation details.




**Question:** What are the consequences of repeat policy violations on my WhatsApp Business account?
**Answer:** If your account continues to violate policies, it may face more severe restrictions, which could result in a permanent ban or the account being disabled. The "Available for review" tab provides details on the active restrictions and the consequences of repeat violations.




**Question:** Where can I see a summary of the specific policy I violated?

**Answer:** Within the "Available for review" tab, you can click on the policy issue to see a summary of the violation and a direct link to the full text of the policy in question.




**Question:** Where can I track the status of my appeal after I have submitted it?

**Answer:** You can track the progress of your review by navigating to the **"In review"** tab within your Business Support Home.
