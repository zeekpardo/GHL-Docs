**WhatsApp Coexistence Feature**

The WhatsApp Coexistence feature allows businesses to integrate their WhatsApp Business App with while maintaining access to both platforms. This dual-platform functionality enables businesses to manage messages, sync contacts, and streamline customer interactions efficiently.

This guide explains how to set up and use this feature, highlighting its key benefits, step-by-step instructions, and important considerations for a smooth experience.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Dual-Platform Flexibility** – Continue using WhatsApp Business App while leveraging advanced features.
-   **Seamless Chat Syncing** – Import up to six months of chat history and contacts into your account for a unified communication experience.
-   **Enhanced Messaging Capabilities** – Utilize bulk messaging, automations, interactive templates, and voice notes to engage with customers effectively.
-   **No Service Disruptions** – WhatsApp Business App remains fully functional during and after integration.
-   **Improved Customer Engagement** – Customers can message your business on WhatsApp directly from your Facebook Page.
-   **WhatsApp Coexistence Global Expansion** – The WhatsApp Coexistence feature enabling smooth communication across mobile and desktop while maintaining active chats and access to essential tools.

### **How to Link Your WhatsApp Business Account to a Facebook Page**

To connect your WhatsApp Business account with your Facebook Page, follow these steps:

#### **Prerequisites:**

-   A **Facebook Page** for your business.
-   A **WhatsApp Business App account** with the latest updates installed.

**Business Tip:** Connect your WhatsApp Business account to your Facebook Page so customers can message you instantly on WhatsApp with just one click.

#### **Step-by-Step Guide:**

**1\. Open the WhatsApp Business App.**

2\. On **Android**, tap **More Options**; on **iPhone**, tap **Settings**.

3\. Navigate to **Business Tools > Facebook & Instagram**.

4\. Tap **Facebook > Continue**, which will redirect you to Facebook login.

5\. Enter your **Facebook account credentials** and tap **Log In**.

6\. Select the **Facebook Page** you want to link and tap **Connect WhatsApp**.

7\. If you do not have a **Facebook Page**, follow the prompts to create one.

8\. Once linked, a **WhatsApp button** will appear on your Facebook Page.

9\. Confirm the connection in the WhatsApp Business App.

### **Important Notes & Limitations**

-   **WhatsApp Business App must remain open** during the synchronization process.
-   **Only one phone number can be linked** per account under the coexistence model.
-   **Template messages cannot be sent** via WhatsApp Business App; they must be sent through .
-   **Your WhatsApp Business profile can only be managed** within the app, not from .
-   A WhatsApp Business App **version 2.24.17** or later is required.
-   If you have previously integrated with LeadConnector, please remove the old WABA, reinstall the app, and sign up again before enabling Coexistence.
-   If you disconnect a number, you will need to wait 1-2 months and use the number actively before reconnecting it.

### **Unsupported Countries for WhatsApp Business Accounts**

#### **Unsupported Regions:**

-   European Union (EU)

**FAQs**




**Question:** What is the WhatsApp Coexistence feature?
**Answer:** The WhatsApp Coexistence feature allows businesses to continue using their WhatsApp Business App account while leveraging LeadConnector’s advanced tools powered by the WhatsApp Cloud API.




**Question:** What version of the WhatsApp Business App do I need?
**Answer:** You must have WhatsApp Business App version 2.24.17 or later.




**Question:** Is linking my WhatsApp Business account to a Facebook Page required?
**Answer:** Yes, you must link your WhatsApp Business account to a Facebook Page to enable this feature.




**Question:** Can I use this feature if I’ve previously integrated with LeadConnector?
**Answer:** No, if you have already integrated with LeadConnector, you’ll need to delete your existing WABA account, reinstall the WhatsApp Business App, and sign up again.




**Question:** How do I connect my WhatsApp Business account to a Facebook Page?
**Answer:** You can follow the step-by-step instructions in the article to link your accounts through the WhatsApp Business App.




**Question:** Can I send template messages through the WhatsApp Business App after integrating with ?
**Answer:** No, template messages can only be sent through , not directly from the WhatsApp Business App.




**Question:** Can I manage my WhatsApp profile within ?
**Answer:** No, your WhatsApp profile can only be managed through the WhatsApp Business App.




**Question:** Who is the Coexistence Feature designed for?
**Answer:** This feature is ideal for businesses that primarily use the WhatsApp Business App but need advanced tools to scale conversations, manage leads, handle payments, and streamline shipping processes.




**Question:** Is there a limit to the number of marketing messages a business can send via Cloud API under Coexistence?
**Answer:** Yes, standard WhatsApp messaging limits apply. You do not need Business Verification to exceed the 250 business-initiated conversations per 24-hour limit. Businesses that initiate 1,000+ conversations in a 30-day period using high-quality templates may qualify for an increased limit upon WhatsApp’s review.




**Question:** If I have a Meta Verified blue badge in WhatsApp Business App, will it transfer when I connect to a partner platform?
**Answer:** No, at this time, the WhatsApp Coexistence feature does not support transferring blue badge verification between the SMB app and the Cloud API.




**Question:** Why is my country not supported for WhatsApp Business accounts?
**Answer:** WhatsApp restricts certain country codes due to regulatory and compliance requirements. If your country is unsupported, you cannot register a WhatsApp Business account with a number from that region.




**Question:** Can I use a phone number from a supported country while operating in an unsupported region?
**Answer:** Yes, you can register a WhatsApp Business account with a phone number from a supported country and use it in an unsupported region. However, you must comply with local regulations and WhatsApp’s policies.




**Question:** Do I need admin access to enable the Coexistence Feature?
**Answer:** Yes, in most cases, only users with admin privileges can enable or modify this setting.




**Question:** Will enabling the Coexistence Feature affect my existing configurations?
**Answer:** No, activating this feature will not overwrite or disrupt your existing settings—it simply allows additional functionalities to coexist.




**Question:** Can I keep the same phone number when migrating from the WhatsApp Business App to the Business Platform?
**Answer:** Yes, businesses can retain their existing phone numbers during the migration process, ensuring seamless customer communication.




**Question:** How long does the onboarding process take?
**Answer:** The onboarding duration varies depending on business requirements, but it typically takes a few days to a week.




**Question:** How much chat history can I sync from my WhatsApp Business App to ?
**Answer:** You can import up to six months of chat history and contacts from your WhatsApp Business App into your account to ensure a unified communication experience.




**Question:** What are the specific requirements and steps for the synchronization process to work correctly?
**Answer:** To ensure a successful sync, the **WhatsApp Business App must remain open** throughout the process and you must have a stable internet connection. Additionally, you must grant full data transfer permissions when prompted. If you are a previous LeadConnector user, you must delete your old WABA, reinstall the app, and sign up again before enabling Coexistence.
