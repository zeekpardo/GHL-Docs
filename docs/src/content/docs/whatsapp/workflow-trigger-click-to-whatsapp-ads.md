Instant communication is key to engaging high-intent leads. The Click-to-WhatsApp Ads and Facebook Page Call-to-Action (CTA) workflow trigger offers a seamless way to turn ad clicks into immediate conversations. This powerful feature allows businesses to automate their response, assign leads, and trigger follow-up actions as soon as a user reaches out. By automating this process, businesses can increase engagement, improve customer service efficiency, and boost their return on investment (ROI) for ad campaigns.

**Important:** This workflow trigger activates **when a customer using an Android or iOS device messages you** via A Click-to-WhatsApp Ad or A Facebook Page Call-to-Action (CTA) button

### Key Features & Benefits

1.  **Instant Lead Engagement**:  
    As soon as a prospect clicks on a Click-to-WhatsApp ad or CTA button on a Facebook page and sends a message, the workflow is triggered. This allows businesses to respond immediately when interest is at its peak, ensuring that the lead is not lost due to delays.
2.  **Cost-Efficient Messaging**:  
    If you respond to the message within 24 hours, you unlock a 72-hour free messaging window, where all communications whether template or free-form are free of charge. This is a cost-saving benefit for businesses that want to engage leads without incurring additional costs.
3.  **Seamless Automation**:  
    The workflow can be further customized by adding filters like Ad ID, Post ID, or URL to control automation more granularly. Once a lead is captured, the system can automatically tag the lead, assign them to a specific pipeline stage, or initiate follow-up actions without manual intervention.
4.  **Efficient Follow-Up**:  
    Follow-ups are automatically triggered, ensuring that no lead is forgotten and that timely responses continue to nurture the customer journey.

### How to Set It Up

1\. Navigate to **Automation** on the left **> Workflows** at the top**,** and select **\+ Create Workflow > Start from Scratch**

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766073600896-1.png)

2\. Select **Add New Trigger** and choose the **Click-to-WhatsApp Ads** option.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775251561443-image2.png)

3\. (Optional) Add filters to narrow down the automation to a specific Ad ID, Post ID, or URL for more targeted engagement.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1775251579338-image1.png)

4\. Save the trigger and then select the desired actions, such as sending a message, assigning a pipeline stage, or firing a webhook.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766073754475-4.png)

5\. Review your setup and activate the workflow.

### **Free Entry-Point Conversation Rules**

-   The free 72-hour messaging window opens when a user taps your ad or page CTA on mobile (Android/iOS) and messages you.
-   You must respond within 24 hours to activate the free messaging window - no template fees, unlimited sends.
-   Once the 72-hour window expires, the normal messaging charges apply unless a paid template is used.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** What counts as a trigger event?
**Answer:** A trigger event occurs when a user taps your Click-to-WhatsApp ad or Facebook Page CTA and sends a WhatsApp message **on mobile.**




**Question:** What happens if I reply after 24 hours?
**Answer:** If you reply after 24 hours, the free messaging window will not open, and you must use a paid template to re-engage the customer.




**Question:** Can I mix template and non-template messages?
**Answer:** Yes, free-form messages are allowed within the 24-hour customer service window. After that, only template messages are permitted.




**Question:** How are charges handled?
**Answer:** Messages sent within the 72-hour free entry-point window incur no additional fees. After the window expires, standard WhatsApp conversation charges apply.




**Question:** Can I filter messages by Ad or Post ID?
**Answer:** Yes, you can filter the workflow based on specific Ad IDs, Post IDs, or URLs for more precise control over which ads or posts trigger the workflow.  




**Question:** What happens if a user doesn't message within 24 hours after tapping the ad or CTA?
**Answer:** If the user doesn't message within 24 hours after tapping the ad or CTA, the 72-hour free messaging window will not be activated. You would need to use a paid template to engage the customer.




**Question:** What happens if I use an automated bot to reply?  
**Answer:** As long as the automated reply is sent through the workflow within 24 hours of the lead's message, it counts as a valid response and will trigger the 72-hour free window. This is a great way to ensure you always capture the cost-saving benefit.




**Question:** Can I use a URL to trigger a specific automation?

**Answer:** Yes. In the trigger settings, you can add a filter for a **specific URL**. This is useful if you have multiple ads or pages leading to WhatsApp and want different automated responses for each.
