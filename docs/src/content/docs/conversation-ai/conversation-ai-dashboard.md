The Conversation AI Dashboard is a powerful tool designed to provide businesses with in-depth insights into their AI bot’s performance. This article explores the key features and benefits of this dashboard and walks you through the steps of using it effectively.

### Key Features and Benefits of the Conversation AI Dashboard

The Conversation AI Dashboard offers SMBs (small and medium-sized businesses) the ability to monitor and optimize their AI bot performance, ensuring better customer engagement and operational efficiency. Here’s a breakdown of its core features:

-   **In-depth Performance Tracking**: Access detailed metrics that track the effectiveness of your AI bot. Monitor unique contacts, actions triggered, appointments booked, average messages per contact, and time saved through automated processes.
-   **Data Filtering and Comparison**: Refine insights by using the Channel Selector and Date Range Selector to focus on specific periods or communication channels. Compare performance across different times to assess trends and improvements.
-   **Actionable Insights**: The dashboard provides tailored recommendations to help you enhance your bot’s performance, identify potential issues, and optimize customer interactions for greater satisfaction.

### How to Use the Conversation AI Dashboard

Using the Conversation AI Dashboard is simple and intuitive. Follow these steps to get started:

**1\. Access the Dashboard**:

-   Go to AI Agents > Conversation AI.
-   Open any bot you wish to track and monitor.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766487782903-unnamed \(32\).png>)

**2\. Open the Dashboard Tab**:

-   Once the bot is selected, open the Dashboard tab to access key metrics.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766487804070-unnamed \(33\).png>)

**3\. Monitor Key Metrics**:

-   Review the following data:
    -   **Total Unique Contacts**: The number of unique contacts engaged by the bot.
    -   **Actions Triggered**: Total actions completed by the bot, such as answering questions or scheduling appointments.
    -   **Appointments Booked**: The number of appointments successfully booked through the bot.
    -   **Average Messages per Contact**: This helps you gauge the bot’s efficiency in handling inquiries.
    -   **Time Saved**: An estimate of manual effort reduced due to automation.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766487827230-unnamed \(34\).png>)

**4\. Filter & Compare Data**:

-   Use the Channel Selector and Date Range Selector to filter the data.
-   Compare the performance of your bot across different time periods to spot trends and improvements.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766487848712-unnamed \(35\).png>)

**5\. Receive Actionable Insights**:

-   The dashboard will provide actionable insights and recommendations, helping you fine-tune the bot’s responses and improve efficiency.

### Pro Tips for Maximizing Bot Efficiency

-   **Refine Bot Responses**: Regularly review the metrics, especially the "Average Messages per Contact," to determine if the bot is providing concise and helpful answers. A high number of messages could indicate that the bot is too verbose or not effectively guiding users.
-   **Monitor Time Saved**: Track the time saved by the bot over time. Use this information to highlight the operational efficiency improvements that the bot brings to your business.

**FAQs**




**Question:** What metrics does the Conversation AI Dashboard track?

**Answer:** The dashboard tracks Total Unique Contacts, Actions Triggered, Appointments Booked, Average Messages per Contact, and Time Saved. These metrics offer a comprehensive view of your AI bot's performance.




**Question:** How can I filter data in the dashboard?

**Answer:** You can filter data using the Channel Selector and Date Range Selector, allowing you to focus on specific communication channels or periods.




**Question:** What are actionable insights, and how can they help my business?

**Answer:** Actionable insights are recommendations provided by the dashboard to help optimize your bot’s performance. They can suggest improvements in bot responses, efficiency, and customer engagement.




**Question:** Can I compare the bot’s performance over different time periods?
**Answer:** Yes, the Date Range Selector allows you to compare the bot’s performance across different time periods to assess improvements or changes.




**Question:** How do the "Tailored Recommendations" work?

**Answer:** The dashboard analyzes your bot's interaction data to identify potential issues. It then provides specific suggestions to help you fine-tune responses and optimize customer interactions for better satisfaction.




**Question:** How can I determine if my bot is being too wordy or failing to guide users effectively?
**Answer:** You should monitor the **"Average Messages per Contact"** metric. A high number of messages can indicate that the bot is too verbose or isn't effectively guiding users to a resolution, allowing you to refine its responses for better efficiency.
