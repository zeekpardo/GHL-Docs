**Mobile App: Conversations AI**

The integration of Conversation AI into LeadConnector Mobile App marks a significant step forward in enhancing communication management. Already available on desktop, this powerful tool is now accessible on mobile devices, making it easier for businesses to engage with customers from anywhere. Whether you're on the move or away from your desk, Conversation AI helps you respond quickly and intelligently, streamlining interactions and boosting productivity.

**Key Features and Benefits**The introduction of Conversation AI on mobile brings numerous advantages to improve customer communication:

-   **Two Smart Modes for Enhanced Flexibility**
    -   **Autopilot Mode**: In this mode, AI automatically responds after a preset delay, ensuring that no message is left unattended even when you're unavailable. It’s perfect for handling conversations when you are away from your device.
    -   **Suggestive Mode**: Here, the AI provides you with reply suggestions as soon as you begin typing. You can select, tweak, and send responses easily, speeding up your response time while ensuring accuracy and consistency.

-   **In-App Bot Controls**With direct access to AI settings within your mobile app, you can manage the bot's functionality with ease. This allows you to toggle the AI on or off, view the bot's status, and adjust reactivation timeframes. All these controls are easily accessible from any conversation screen, keeping you in charge of your interactions.
-   **Control AI & Costs On the Go**Managing AI bot settings directly from your mobile gives you the flexibility to pause, resume, or schedule reactivation anytime. This feature not only ensures you're always in control of your customer communication but also helps optimize costs by managing usage-based billing effectively.

**How to Use This Feature?**Follow these easy steps to get started with Conversation AI on mobile:

**1\. Set Up on Desktop (One-Time)**Start by configuring the AI settings on your desktop dashboard. Choose between Autopilot or Suggestive mode, train your AI to suit your communication style, and manage your subscription preferences.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** How do I change the AI settings on mobile?
**Answer:** To adjust the AI settings, tap the ellipsis menu on the top-right of the conversation screen and select "Edit AI Settings." From there, you can toggle the bot’s status and modify reactivation schedules.




**Question:** Can I pause the AI on mobile?
**Answer:** Yes, you can pause the AI at any time by accessing the AI settings from the conversation screen and toggling off the “Activate Bot” option.




**Question:** Is there a delay in the Autopilot mode?
**Answer:** Yes, Autopilot mode allows you to set a delay before the AI sends a reply. This delay can be customized to suit your response time preferences.




**Question:** Can I switch between modes?
**Answer:** Absolutely. You can easily switch between Autopilot and Suggestive modes by adjusting the AI settings whenever needed.




**Question:** How do I manage AI usage-based billing?
**Answer:** By controlling when the AI is active or paused, you can better manage your usage-based billing. Regularly monitoring the bot’s activity helps optimize costs.




**Question:** How often should I review my AI settings?
**Answer:** Regularly review and update your AI settings to ensure they match your business priorities and communication tone.




**Question:** Can I use Conversation AI on multiple mobile devices?
**Answer:** Yes, as long as you're logged into the same account, you can access and manage Conversation AI across multiple mobile devices, ensuring seamless communication management from anywhere.




**Question:** Do I need to use a desktop to set up Conversation AI before using it on the mobile app?

**Answer:** Yes. You must perform a one-time configuration on your desktop dashboard to choose your operating mode (Autopilot or Suggestive), train the AI, and manage your subscription preferences before it becomes active on mobile.  




**Question:** Which messaging channels can I manage with Conversation AI on my phone?

**Answer:** The mobile integration supports all standard platforms connected to your account, specifically highlighting **WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram**.




**Question:** If I have the AI set to Autopilot, can I still take over the conversation or change how long it waits before responding?
**Answer:** Yes. Even while on mobile, you have direct **In-App Bot Controls** that allow you to manage the bot's functionality from any conversation screen. You can specifically **adjust reactivation timeframes** or schedule when the bot should resume its operation, ensuring you remain in charge of the interaction timing.
