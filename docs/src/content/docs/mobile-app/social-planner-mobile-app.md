**Social Planner - Mobile App**

Whether you're using the app for business or personal use, you can effortlessly connect your favorite social accounts, create and customize posts, and schedule or publish content across different platforms—all from the convenience of your mobile device. Available on multiple mobile apps, this makes social media management on the go more efficient than ever.

**Note:**

Google Business Profile only supports **1 image per post**.

Google Business Profile supports a **minimum of 720x720** and a **maximum of 3000x3000 dimension**

### **Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Multi-Platform Support**: Manage posts across 6 platforms—**Facebook**, **Instagram**, **Google My Business**, **LinkedIn**, **YouTube**, and **TikTok**.
-   **Account Management**: Connect new accounts or access existing ones from within the app.
-   **Customizable Views**: Switch between **Monthly**, **Weekly**, or **List** views for better organization.
-   **Post Redirect**: Open published posts directly in their respective platforms for seamless management.
-   **Create & Edit Posts**: Easily create, edit, or delete posts, and customize them for each platform.
-   **Advanced Settings**: Organize posts with categories and tags, and add media from your device’s gallery, camera, or files app. Additionally, watermark your posts for branding and automatically format media through media optimization.
-   **Scheduling**: Publish posts instantly, save as drafts, or schedule them for later, including recurring posts.

### **How It Works**

**1\. Install the App**: Download and install the LeadConnector mobile app.

**2\. Open Your Account**: Access your account within the app to manage your social media accounts.

**3\. Navigate to Social Planner**: Open the left-hand navigation drawer and tap on the **Social Planner** tile to access all the features.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766597189489-image6.jpg)

**4\. Connect Accounts**: If you’ve used the Social Planner previously on the web, your connected accounts and posts will automatically sync. To connect new accounts, tap the **\+ button** under the Account(s) section and complete the authentication process.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764013559159-image.png)

**5\. Create and Customize Posts**: Choose the social accounts you want to post to, craft your message, add media, and customize it according to each platform’s specific requirements.

### **Image Optimization and Watermarking**

You can easily apply your branding to all media and have your images automatically optimized for each platform's specifications, saving time and ensuring consistency.

To make use of these features,

-   Open the Social Planner on your mobile app.
-   Upload your media when creating a new post.
-   Under **Advanced Settings**, toggle on:

-   Watermark to apply your branding.
-   Media Optimization to auto-format for every platform.

### **Media Library Feature – Centralized Media Management**

Having the **Media Library** integrated into the **LeadConnector mobile app** significantly enhances the **Social Planner** by allowing users to **quickly access, organize, and schedule content on the go**

The Media Library allows users to upload, organize, and access all media files, including images and PDFs, in one place. This simplifies management, boosts efficiency with quick search and sorting, and supports bulk actions. 

**How to Use:**

1.  Open the app and select **Media Library**.

### **Pro Tips**

-   **Organize Your Content**: Use categories and tags to sort your posts for easy access and better content management.
-   **Preview Posts**: Always preview your posts before scheduling to ensure they look great on each platform. This helps avoid formatting issues that may arise with different post types.
-   **Optimize Scheduling**: If you’re managing content for multiple clients or campaigns, take advantage of the recurring post feature to automate consistent content publishing.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I manage multiple social media accounts at once?
**Answer:** Yes, the Social Planner supports multiple platforms, so you can manage all your social media accounts from a single interface.




**Question:** How do I customize posts for different platforms?  
**Answer:** The Social Planner allows you to customize posts for each platform’s specific needs, such as adding follow-up comments or selecting between different post formats like reels or feeds.




**Question:** Can I schedule posts for later?
**Answer:** Absolutely! You can schedule posts for any time in the future, even setting them to repeat on a monthly or weekly basis.




**Question:** What if I want to edit a post after scheduling it?
**Answer:** You can easily edit or delete any scheduled post at any time before it goes live.




**Question:** Can I upload media for my posts?
**Answer:** Yes, you can upload images, videos, and files directly from your device’s camera, gallery, or file manager to enhance your posts.




**Question:** What are the image requirements for Google Business Profile posts?  
**Answer:** Yes, when posting to Google Business Profile, keep in mind that only one image is supported per post, and the image dimensions must be between 720x720 and 3000x3000 pixels.




**Question:** Does the app notify me if a scheduled post fails to publish?
**Answer:** Absolutely. If a post fails due to an expired token or platform restriction, you will receive a push notification on your mobile device. You can then open the app, check the error message, and re-schedule or fix the post immediately  




**Question:** What is the minimum app version required to use the Social Planner and Media Library?

**Answer:** The Social Planner requires version **3.88.1** or above, while the Media Library integration requires version **3.92.1** or above.
