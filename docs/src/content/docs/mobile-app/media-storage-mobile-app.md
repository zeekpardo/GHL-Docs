Welcome to the guide on using the **Media Storage** feature! This feature is designed to make managing all your media, from images to PDFs, easier and more efficient. No more jumping between different platforms or dealing with scattered files; everything you need is available in one centralized location.

Let’s walk through the key features of the **Media Storage** and how to use them effectively.

### Key Benefits of the Media Storage

-   **Centralized Media Management**: Upload, store, and access all media types images, videos, PDFs, and more, within one location.
-   **Enhanced Organization**: Organize your media with folders and categories, reducing the need to switch between apps or external storage.
-   **Cross-Module Integration**: The Media Storage connects with features like Social Planner and Conversations, allowing for seamless media use across the platform.
-   **Time-Saving Features**: With quick search, sort, and filter options, you can find your files instantly, improving productivity.
-   **Seamless Upload Experience**: Upload media directly from your gallery, file app, or camera with no interruptions.

### How to Use the Media Storage

Follow these simple steps to make the most of your **Media Storage**:

**1\. Open the Mobile App**:  
Navigate to the app and open the main menu (usually represented by three horizontal lines).

**2\. Select the Media Storage**:  
From the menu, tap on **Media Storage** to access all your media storage and management options.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770059308830-image2.png)

**3\. Upload Media**:

-   Tap on the **Upload** button.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766070032857-image1.jpg)

-   Choose media from your **gallery**, **camera**, or **files app** to upload.
-   Upload images, videos, or PDFs with ease.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766070055454-image2.jpg)

**4\. Organize Your Media**:

-   Create **folders** to keep your media well-organized.
-   Categorize media based on project, type, or any system that suits your needs.

**5\. Use Search and Sort Options**:

-   Use the **search bar** to quickly find specific files.
-   Sort your files by **name**, **size**, or **recently added** to easily locate them.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766070078475-image4.jpg)

**6\. Multi-Select Actions**:

-   Select multiple files by tapping and holding on to them.
-   Once selected, you can **move**, **delete**, or **organize** the files into folders.

**7\. Monitor Upload Status**:

-   Keep track of ongoing uploads with the **Upload Manager**, which shows the status of each file being uploaded.

### Special Requirements

-   **Availability**: This feature is available in versions **3.92.1 and above**.
-   **Cloud Storage**: Integration with Google Drive allows you to upload files directly from your cloud storage.

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I organize my media files?
**Answer:** You can create folders to categorize your media and keep everything neatly organized.


**Question:  Can I upload multiple files at once?
**Answer:** Yes, simply select multiple files and upload them together, or use multi-select actions for bulk operations.




**Question:** What types of media can I upload?
**Answer:** You can upload images, videos, PDFs, and other file types directly from your gallery, camera, or cloud storage.




**Question:** How can I find specific media files?
**Answer:** Use the **search bar** to quickly find specific files, or sort by **name**, **size**, or **recently added**.




**Question:** Is there a limit to how many files I can upload?
**Answer:** There may be limits based on your storage capacity or app settings, but the upload manager helps you track your files.




**Question:** Can I use Media Storage across other platform features?  
**Answer:** Yes, Media Storage integrates with tools like **Social Planner** and **Conversations**, allowing seamless use of media across different modules.




**Question:** Why can't I see the Media Storage feature on my mobile app?
**Answer:** The Media Storage feature is only available on app versions **3.92.1 and above**. If you cannot see it, please check your app store to ensure your application is updated to the latest version.
