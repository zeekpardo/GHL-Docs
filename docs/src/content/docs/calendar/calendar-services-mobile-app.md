This article explains how to use the mobile-first service booking feature to manage multi-service appointments efficiently on the go. It highlights key functionalities that help teams and businesses streamline scheduling, handle service variations, staff assignments, add-ons, coupons, and multi-location bookings, all from a mobile device.

**Key Features & Benefits**

-   **Multi-Location Booking:** Easily select available business locations during scheduling.
-   **Service Variants:** Support for multiple options within a service (e.g., different durations or packages).
-   **Add-Ons with Quantity Support:** Include optional extras or enhancements directly in the booking flow.
-   **Apply Coupons:** Enter promo codes for discounts during checkout.
-   **Date-Specific Staff Hours:** Reflects accurate availability for selected dates.
-   **Multi-Service Checkout:** Add multiple services to a single appointment seamlessly.
-   **Calendar Filtering:** View and filter booked appointments by services across Monthly, Weekly, and List views.

**How to Use (Step-by-Step)**

1.  **Open the Calendar Module:** Tap the + (plus) button and choose "Service Appointment."

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764847320209-unnamed - 2025-12-04T192154.058.png>)

1.  **Select Contact & Location:** Search for a contact and pick the desired service location.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770919729710-image4.png)

1.  **Choose Services:** Browse and select the services to include in the appointment.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764847439554-unnamed - 2025-12-04T192353.211.png>)

1.  **Select Variants & Add-Ons:** Choose service options (e.g., duration) and add optional extras with quantities.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764847461373-unnamed - 2025-12-04T192412.264.png>)

1.  **Assign Staff:** Assign a team member to each service; staff-specific pricing is applied automatically.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764847477410-unnamed - 2025-12-04T192432.585.png>)

1.  **Apply Coupons (Optional):** Enter a valid promo code to apply discounts.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764847499234-unnamed - 2025-12-04T192453.120.png>)

1.  **Pick a Time Slot:** Choose from available slots or select a custom time if enabled.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764847519774-unnamed - 2025-12-04T192511.576.png>)

1.  **Confirm & Schedule Booking:** Review the service, staff, pricing, and discount details, then tap "Schedule Booking."
2.  **View Booking Summary:** Access a full overview of the appointment, including customer details, service breakdown, duration, pricing, and applied discounts.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764847575194-unnamed - 2025-12-04T192607.052.png>)

**Pro Tips** 

-   Use the service labels at the top of the calendar to filter appointments by specific services for faster visibility.
-   Ensure staff and calendars are properly set up to avoid missing appointments in the calendar view.
-   Multi-service checkout can help reduce errors by combining bookings into a single workflow.

**FAQs**




**Question:** Can I book multiple services at once on mobile?
**Answer:** Yes, the multi-service checkout allows you to add multiple services to a single appointment seamlessly.




**Question:** Can I apply coupons or discounts?
**Answer:** Yes, you can enter promo codes during the booking process to apply discounts to the appointment.




**Question:** Can I edit or reschedule appointments on mobile?
**Answer:** Currently, editing, rescheduling, or duplicating appointments is not supported on the mobile app.




**Question:** Will all appointments show if calendars are not set up?
**Answer:** No, appointments may not appear if no calendars are configured, even if services and staff exist.




**Question:** Can I view only specific services in the calendar?
**Answer:** Yes, you can filter calendar views by selecting service labels for clearer visibility across Monthly, Weekly, or List views.




**Question:** Does the system automatically adjust pricing if I assign a specific staff member?

**Answer:** Yes, staff-specific pricing is applied automatically once you assign a team member to a service during the booking process.




**Question:** Can I add multiple quantities of an extra service or enhancement to a booking?
**Answer:** Yes. When selecting Add-Ons for a service, the system includes quantity support, allowing you to choose and include optional extras with specific quantities directly in the booking flow.
