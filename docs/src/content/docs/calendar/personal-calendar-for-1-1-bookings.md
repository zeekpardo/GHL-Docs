The Personal Booking Calendar offers an efficient way to handle one-on-one meetings without the complexities associated with multi-user setups. Ideal for users who wish to have a dedicated booking calendar for individual appointments, this feature enables seamless scheduling, helping to reduce confusion over calendar types and prevent unnecessary support queries. In this article, we will explore the Personal Calendar's essential features, outline its benefits, and provide step-by-step instructions on how to set up and use it.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

1.  **Streamlined Scheduling for 1:1 Meetings**The Personal Booking Calendar is simple and explicitly designed for one-on-one appointments. It ensures that bookings are focused on a single user, eliminating the need for more complex multi-user configurations.
2.  **Easy Setup and Management**Creating a personal booking calendar takes just a few minutes, and users can easily configure it to fit their needs. This makes it an ideal solution for those looking to quickly set up a calendar without dealing with the intricacies of a team-based scheduling tool.
3.  **Versatile and Fully Functional**The Personal Calendar shares features of a multi-user calendar, such as Round Robin, but is designed for individual use. It includes time slots, booking options, automated reminders, and integrations, making it a robust tool for solo professionals or anyone needing a dedicated booking calendar.
4.  **Avoids Common Confusion with Event Calendars**Unlike event calendars typically meant for physical events, the Personal Booking Calendar supports integrations with virtual meeting platforms like Zoom and Google Meet, making it suitable for remote and virtual appointments.

### **How to Use the Personal Booking Calendar**

**Step 1: Access Calendar Settings**Go to the main menu, select Calendar, and then "Calendar Settings." Here, you will find options for creating and managing different types of calendars.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773331796658-image3.png)

**Step 2: Create a New Calendar**Click on the "+ New Calendar" button. You will see different options for calendar types.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773331817394-image16.png)

**Step 3: Choose Personal Booking**Select the "Personal Booking" option. This type is intended for one-on-one scheduling and automatically sets you as the default team member.

**Step 4: Configure Your Calendar Preferences**After selecting "Personal Booking," you will be prompted to configure various settings:

-   **Availability:** Set your available time slots, days, and booking rules.
-   **Meeting Duration:** Choose how long each appointment will last.
-   **Buffer Time:** Specify any buffer time required before or after appointments.
-   **Integrations:** Connect virtual meeting platforms like Zoom or Google Meet for seamless online scheduling.

**Step 5: Save and Share Your Calendar Link**Once configured, save your settings. You will receive a unique booking link that contacts can share to schedule one-on-one appointments directly on your calendar.

### **Note**

By default, the user who creates the calendar is automatically added as a team member. However, this can be adjusted if necessary. Unlike the Round Robin calendar, which supports multiple team members and allows the distribution of bookings, the Personal Booking Calendar is designed for a single team member, focusing on individual appointments.

### **FAQs**




**Question:** What is the Personal Booking Calendar used for?
**Answer:** The Personal Booking Calendar is designed specifically for one-on-one appointments, offering an efficient way to handle individual bookings without the complexity of multi-user setups.




**Question:** Can I integrate virtual meeting platforms like Zoom with the Personal Booking Calendar?
**Answer:** Yes, you can integrate virtual meeting platforms such as Zoom and Google Meet when setting up your Personal Booking Calendar, allowing seamless online scheduling.




**Question:** How do I customize email and in-app notifications for appointments?
**Answer:** You can customize both email and in-app notifications by going to the Calendar Settings, selecting the calendar, and enabling or editing notification content. Notifications can be sent for appointment confirmations, cancellations, reminders, and more.




**Question:** Can I use the "Look Busy" feature with the Personal Booking Calendar?
**Answer:** Yes, the "Look Busy" feature is available for the Personal Booking Calendar. It hides a portion of available slots to create urgency and encourage faster bookings.




**Question:** How do I troubleshoot unavailable calendar slots?
**Answer:** To troubleshoot unavailable slots, go to Calendar Settings, select your calendar, and use the "Troubleshoot Calendar" option. This tool will show the reasons behind unavailable time slots.




**Question:** Can I adjust team members for the Personal Booking Calendar?
**Answer:** By default, the user who creates the Personal Booking Calendar is the team member. However, you can adjust the assigned team member if necessary. The Personal Calendar is limited to one team member, unlike Round Robin calendars, which support multiple users.




**Question:** How is the Personal Booking Calendar different from a Round Robin or Event Calendar?
**Answer:** The Personal Booking Calendar is designed for **individual appointments** with a single user, while Round Robin calendars distribute appointments among multiple users, and Event Calendars are intended for group or in-person events. This keeps scheduling simple and personal.




**Question:** Does the Personal Calendar sync with my phone's calendar?
**Answer:** Yes, provided you have integrated your Google or Outlook account. Once synced, any "Busy" event on your personal phone calendar will automatically block out that time on your booking widget, ensuring you never get double-booked.
