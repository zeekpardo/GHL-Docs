How to integrate Razorpay in Calendar Payments

The integration of Razorpay and the payment framework represents a significant advancement in our payment solutions. It offers businesses greater flexibility, improved management capabilities, and the ability to serve a broader customer base

### Key Features of Razorpay Integration:

**Comprehensive Payment Options:**

**All Channels Covered:** Accept payments across various channels, including order forms, invoices, payment links, and contact pages.

**One-Time and Recurring Payments:** Easily manage both one-time custom amounts and subscription-based payments.

**Subscription Management:**

**Flexible Management:** Handle subscription cancellations and refunds efficiently, addressing a major pain point for agencies with clients in.

## How to access

### 1\. Integrate Razorpay:

1.  **Set Razorpay as Your Default Payment Provider:**
    -   Navigate to the Payments tab on the left.
    -   Select Integrations at the top.
    -   Select Razorpay and set it as your default payment provider.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764169804937-101.png)

### 2\. Enable Payment Collection for Calendars:

To start collecting payments for bookings through your calendars:

1.  **Navigate to Calendar Settings:**
    -   Go to the Calendar Settings in your account.
2.  **Turn on the Accept Payments Option:**
    -   Locate the Accept Payments option and toggle it on.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1766184041421-image2.png)

### 3\. Set Payment Amount:

To specify the amount to be charged for bookings:

1.  **Enter the Desired Payment Amount:**
    -   In the payment settings, enter the amount you wish to charge for each booking.
2.  **Click Save:**
    -   Ensure you save the changes to apply the new payment amount.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1764169854618-103.png)

## Note

-   **Currency Restrictions:**
    -   If you are using a currency other than INR, only the 'Card' payment option will be available on the booking widget.
    -   Wallet, Net Banking, and UPI payment options are supported only in INR.

## FAQ




**Question:** What payment options are available with the Razorpay integration?
**Answer:** The integration supports both one-time and recurring payments, and you can accept payments through order forms, invoices, payment links, and contact pages.




**Question:** How do I set Razorpay as my default payment provider?
**Answer:** To set Razorpay as your default payment provider, navigate to the Payments tab, go to the Integrations section, and select Razorpay.




**Question:** Can I collect payments for bookings through calendars?
**Answer:** Yes, you can collect payments by enabling the "Accept Payments" option in the Calendar Settings under your account.




**Question:** How do I specify the amount to charge for bookings?
**Answer:** In the payment settings of your calendar, enter the desired payment amount for bookings and click "Save" to apply the changes.




**Question:** Are there any currency restrictions for payment options?
**Answer:** Yes, if you are using a currency other than INR, only the "Card" payment option will be available in the booking widget. Wallet, Net Banking, and UPI payment options are supported only in INR.




**Question:** What features are available for subscription management in the Razorpay integration?
**Answer:** The integration allows for flexible management of subscription cancellations and refunds, making it easier for businesses handling recurring payments.




**Question:** Can I use Razorpay and another payment provider at the same time?
**Answer:** You can integrate multiple payment providers, but only one can be set as the default at a time. All new payments will be processed through the default provider unless otherwise specified.




**Question:** What happens if I forget to hit "Save" after entering my booking fee?

**Answer:** Your changes will not be applied. You must click **Save** after entering your desired payment amount in the calendar settings to ensure customers are prompted to pay during the booking process.




**Question:** Does Razorpay support UPI and Netbanking for international customers?

**Answer:** No. As per Razorpay’s banking regulations, UPI, Wallets, and Netbanking are exclusively available for transactions in **Indian Rupees (INR)**. If your customer is paying in a different currency (like USD or EUR), they will only see the **Credit/Debit Card** option at checkout.
