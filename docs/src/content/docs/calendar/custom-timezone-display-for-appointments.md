Managing appointments across time zones can be challenging, especially when working with clients or team members in different regions. The _View Appointments in Your Preferred Timezone_ gives you full control over how you view scheduled meetings, helping you stay organized without affecting how others book time with you. This article explains how the feature works, its key benefits, and how to set it up easily.

**Key Features and Benefits**

**Flexible Timezone Selection**

You can now choose _any_ timezone to view your appointments — not just your user or business timezone.

**Personalized Viewing**

The selected timezone is user-specific, meaning it only affects how _you_ see appointments.

**Seamless Integration**

The chosen timezone will reflect across Calendar View, Appointment List, Contacts, and Conversations.

**No Impact on Availability**Your availability and how others book with you remain unchanged. Only the display is affected.

**FAQs**




**Question:** Will changing my timezone affect how clients book appointments with me?
**Answer:** No, this setting only changes how _you_ view appointments. It does not impact your availability or client booking experience.




**Question:** Where does the selected timezone apply?  
**Answer:** It applies across Calendar View, the Appointment List, Contacts, and Conversations — wherever appointments are shown.




**Question:** Can I switch time zones frequently?
**Answer:** Yes, you can adjust your preferred timezone at any time through Calendar Settings → Preferences.




**Question:** Does this affect notifications or reminders?
**Answer:** No, notifications and reminders are not impacted by this setting. They continue to operate based on the original appointment time.




**Question:** Can I set my calendar to match a client’s timezone temporarily?  
**Answer:** Yes. You can switch your view to any timezone temporarily to coordinate meetings or prepare for discussions, without changing your actual availability.


**Question:** Will switching to a different timezone change how I see my appointments in the calendar?  
**Answer:** Yes, your appointments will be displayed according to the selected timezone, allowing you to view them as per your preferred time zone without affecting the scheduling for others.




**Question:** If I see a meeting scheduled for 2:00 PM in the Conversations tab, is that in my "View" timezone?
**Answer:** Yes. Once you save your preferences, the system updates the timestamp for appointments across the entire platform. This includes the **Appointment List**, the **Contact Record**, and the **Conversations** thread, providing a "single source of truth" for your schedule.




**Question:** How do I apply the changes after selecting a new timezone?

**Answer:** Once you have selected your desired timezone from the "View Appointments In" dropdown, you must click **Save Preferences** for the display changes to take effect across the platform.




**Question:** Can I set different view timezones for different calendars?

**Answer:** Currently, this is a global user preference. Once you select a preferred timezone, it will apply to all calendars you have access to within that   to ensure a consistent viewing experience.
