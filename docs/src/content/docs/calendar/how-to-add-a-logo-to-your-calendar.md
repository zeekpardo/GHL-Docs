The Calendar widget logo is an important feature that can be customized to represent your client's company branding. 

By replacing the default logo with a custom logo that reflects your client's brand identity, the Calendar widget becomes more personalized and provides a more professional appearance to users.

The logo customization feature is easy to use and can be done by uploading a high-resolution image file that meets the required specifications. Once uploaded, the custom logo will replace the default logo on the Calendar Booking widget, providing a professional and personalized appearance representing your client's brand.

## **Navigation**

You can edit an existing Calendar or create one to add a logo. 

-   Go to Settings>Calendars
-   Click the **Pencil Icon** in front of the calendar you want to edit.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773334299522-image2.png)

-   In the **Meeting Details** tab, add a logo:
-   Upload or drag and drop the SVG, JPG, or GIF.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768264475656-image1.png)

**Note:** Max dimensions: 180x180px | Max Size: 2.5mb

After successfully uploading the image you can select a square or circle shape

## **FAQ’s**




**Question:** Why is it important to use a high-resolution image for the logo?
**Answer:** Using a high-resolution image for the logo ensures that it looks crisp and professional on the Calendar widget. A high-quality logo enhances the user experience and reinforces your client’s brand identity.




**Question:** Can I add different logos for different calendars?
**Answer:** Yes, you can add unique logos to each calendar by editing them individually. This allows each calendar to reflect the specific branding requirements of different clients or services.




**Question:** Where do I find the option to upload a custom logo for the Calendar widget?
**Answer:** The option to upload a custom logo for the Calendar widget is located in the Meeting Details tab when you edit a calendar through Settings > Calendars. Look for the section where you can upload or drag and drop your logo file.




**Question:** What should I do if my logo doesn’t look right on the Calendar widget?
**Answer:** If your logo doesn’t look right, check that it meets the required specifications (180x180px, max 2.5 MB) and is in the correct file format (SVG, JPG, or GIF). You can also try adjusting the shape between square and circle options or resizing the logo for better alignment.




**Question:** Will the customized logo be visible on all booking pages linked to the calendar?
**Answer:** Yes, the customized logo will appear on all booking pages associated with the calendar, providing a consistent and professional representation of your client’s brand across their booking interfaces.




**Question:** Can I preview the logo on the Calendar widget before saving it?
**Answer:** Typically, after uploading the logo in the Meeting Details tab, you should see a preview of how it will look on the Calendar widget. This allows you to make any necessary adjustments before finalizing the changes.




**Question:** Are there any limitations on the type of logo that can be used?
**Answer:** The main limitations are on the file type (SVG, JPG, GIF), dimensions (maximum 180x180 pixels), and file size (maximum 2.5 MB). As long as the logo meets these requirements, it can be used on the Calendar widget.




**Question:** Can I change or remove the logo later?
**Answer:** Yes. Logos can be updated or removed at any time by revisiting the **Meeting Details** tab in the calendar settings.




**Question:** Can I have no logo at all?
**Answer:** Yes. If you prefer a minimalist look or if your branding is already prominent on the website where the calendar is embedded, you can simply remove the logo. The widget will adjust its layout to put more focus on the meeting title and description.
