Learn how to temporarily deactivate a calendar service (e.g., a 30-minute consultation or demo) instead of deleting it. Deactivate calendars that are not currently offered and reactivate them later as needed.

## How to Deactivate Calendars

-   Navigate to Settings > Calendars.
-   Choose the one you’d like to deactivate and click the 3-dot dropdown menu.
-   Click “Deactivate calendar.”
-   In the pop-up window, click Save to deactivate the calendar.  


![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770750881161-image3.jpg)

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767692469385-unnamed \(56\).png>)

-   Back in the calendar settings area, you’ll be able to tell which calendars are active by the “Status” column that shows inactive and active calendars.

**NOTE:** Deactivating a calendar hides it but does not delete or affect existing appointments. They will be visible once the calendar is reactivated.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770750955241-image1.png)

## How to Activate Calendars

-   Click the 3-dot dropdown menu for the calendar you wish to reinstate or activate.
-   Click “Activate Calendar”.
-   The calendar will be instantly re-enabled.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770751007866-image7.jpg)

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I deactivate a calendar service?
**Answer:

-   Navigate to **Settings > Calendars**.
-   Select the calendar you want to deactivate.
-   Click the 3-dot dropdown menu and choose **“Deactivate calendar”**.
-   In the popup window, click **Save** to confirm deactivation.




**Question:** How can I tell which calendars are active or deactivated?
**Answer:** In the calendar settings area, check the **“Status”** column. It will show which calendars are active and which are inactive.




**Question:** How do I activate a previously deactivated calendar?
**Answer:

-   Click the 3-dot dropdown menu for the calendar you want to reactivate.
-   Select **“Activate Calendar”**.
-   The calendar will be instantly re-enabled.




**Question:** Can I reactivate a calendar at any time?
**Answer:** Yes, you can reactivate a calendar at any time using the steps outlined for activation.




**Question:** Will deactivating a calendar delete my existing appointments?
**Answer:** No, deactivating a calendar does not delete any existing appointments. It simply hides the calendar from view until you reactivate it.




**Question:** What happens to the appointments in a deactivated calendar?
**Answer:** Appointments in a deactivated calendar remain intact. They will reappear when you reactivate the calendar.




**Question:** Can I deactivate multiple calendars at once?
**Answer:** Currently, the system allows you to deactivate one calendar at a time. If you need to deactivate multiple calendars, you will need to follow the steps for each individual calendar.  


**Question:How long does it take for a calendar to become available again after I reactivate it?

**Answer:** The calendar will be **instantly re-enabled** once you click "Activate Calendar."




**Question:** If I deactivate a calendar, will it still show up on my website or funnel where I’ve embedded it?
**Answer:** No. Deactivating a calendar hides it from view. This allows you to temporarily stop offering a specific service (like a 30-minute consultation) without having to delete the calendar entirely. Once reactivated, it will become visible and available for booking again.
