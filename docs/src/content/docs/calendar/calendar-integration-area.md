**Calendar Integration Area**

_Within , you can seamlessly connect your user-level integrations, such as third-party calendars and video conferencing apps, as well as account-level integrations like Google Organic Booking and Local Services Ads, with ease and convenience._

### How to Use?

You can integrate your calendar from two locations:

#### 1\. Settings > My Profile > Calendar Settings

#### 2\. Calendars > Calendar Settings > Connections Tab

**Reconnect Flow for Calendar Integrations**

The Reconnect Flow simplifies managing calendar and video conferencing integrations by allowing you to resolve connection issues directly within the page. Instead of navigating to a different page, you can quickly fix any disconnection issues with just a few clicks, improving efficiency and minimizing downtime. Click the "Fix This" banner located at the top of the screen, select the relevant integration, and follow the authentication process to reconnect.

### FAQ/Use Cases




**Question:** How do I integrate my calendar?

**Answer:** Navigate to Settings > My Profile > Calendar Settings or Calendars > Calendar Settings > Connections Tab, click ADD NEW, choose your integration, and enter your credentials.




**Question:** What should I do if my calendar is not syncing?

**Answer:** Check for detailed feedback provided during the integration process and ensure all information is correctly entered. 




**Question:** Will calendar events update in real-time?

**Answer:** Yes, once properly integrated, your events will sync automatically. In some cases, syncing may take a few minutes depending on the platform.




**Question:** How can I monitor the sync status of my integrations?

**Answer:** Look for the processing tag in your calendar settings, which indicates when the sync is in progress or completed.




**Question:** What should I double-check to avoid sync issues?

**Answer:** Ensure all credentials are entered accurately, monitor the sync status, and verify that your third-party services are active and authorized.




**Question:** How does the system handle different Time Zones between my connected calendar and the platform?
**Answer:** The system automatically converts event times based on the time zone settings in your "My Profile" tab. To ensure accuracy, we recommend verifying that both your third-party calendar (e.g., Google) and your platform profile are set to the same time zone.




**Question:** Can I connect more than one Google or Outlook account to my profile?
**Answer:** Yes! You can connect multiple accounts from the same provider (e.g., a personal Gmail and a Google Workspace account). This allows the system to check for conflicts across all your busy schedules while booking new appointments on your preferred primary calendar.




**Question:** If one of my calendar or video conferencing integrations gets disconnected, do I have to delete it and start over?
**Answer:** No, you do not need to restart the process from scratch. You can use the **Reconnect Flow** to fix the issue directly on the page. Simply click the **"Fix This" banner** at the top of your screen, select the disconnected integration, and follow the authentication prompts to restore the connection quickly.
