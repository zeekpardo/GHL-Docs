**TikTok Integration**

TikTok can be integrated with to generate new leads and enhance your social media management. This article will guide you through the steps to connect both TikTok Business and Personal accounts to the Social Planner, as well as highlight the key features, benefits, and best practices for using these integrations effectively.

### **Connecting Your TikTok Business Account**

To start generating leads from TikTok, you need to follow these steps:

**Step 1: Authenticate TikTok as a Business Account**

1\. Navigate to settings> Integrations within your dashboard.

2\. Find the TikTok section and click on the **Connect** button.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769757311274-image8.png)

3\. A new window will appear where you can log in to your TikTok for Business account.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763747615731-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-21 125326.png>)

4\. Once logged in, confirm the permissions to allow to perform Creative and Lead Management.

5\. Select the desired Advertiser Account from the window (if you only have one Advertiser Account, this will be the default). Once the window closes, your account is integrated.

**Step 2: Set up to Capture Your TikTok Leads**

1.  Go to TikTok Form Fields Mapping in the Integrations navigation bar. This is where you’ll see existing pages and forms created inside your TikTok account.
2.  Click on **Map Fields** to map instant page fields with our custom fields feature to ensure the contact information comes into correctly.
3.  **NOTE:** If you add new questions to a Lead Ad form during a live campaign, you’ll need to return to the TikTok Form Fields Mapping tab to update the form to track responses. Click **Edit fields** on the relevant form.

By subscribing to specific pages, you can generate leads that align with your business niche, attracting potential customers who are likely already interested in what you have to offer based on the TikTok algorithm.

After completing these steps, leads from TikTok will start appearing in a smart list within your Contacts section.

### **Key Features and Benefits of Direct TikTok Posting**

-   **Efficiency:** Eliminate manual push notifications, saving time in content distribution.
-   **Consistent Scheduling:** Plan content ahead of time to maintain a consistent presence on TikTok.
-   **Enhanced Planning:** Strategize content by considering optimal posting times for better audience engagement.
-   **Reduced Stress:** Automate posting processes for a smoother content creation experience.
-   **Improved Engagement:** Reach your audience when they are most active, increasing engagement rates.
-   **Better Analytics:** Track post performance to optimize your content strategy.
-   **Multi-Platform Integration:** Streamline content distribution across multiple social media accounts.

### **PRO TIPS:**

-   **Custom Fields:** You can use the following custom fields in TikTok Lead ads inside : Text, Large Text, Numerical, Phone, Monetary, Single Options, and Date.
-   **No Pages Message:** If you encounter a message saying “No pages for this account,” ensure you have created an instant form in your TikTok account.
-   **Access Token:** If your Access Token expires, disconnect and reintegrate the TikTok account to refresh the token.
-   **Changing Advertiser Account:** You can change your Advertiser Account at any time by visiting the Integrations tab.

### FAQ




**Question:** Can I schedule posts for both personal and business TikTok accounts?

**Answer:** Yes, you can schedule posts for both personal and business TikTok accounts through the Social Planner.




**Question:** What kind of content can I schedule for TikTok using this platform?

**Answer:** You can schedule video content along with captions, hashtags, and interaction settings like duets, comments, and stitches.




**Question:** How do I set who can view my TikTok video when scheduling a post?

**Answer:** While scheduling your post, you can select the audience (Everyone, Friends, or Only Me) who can view the video from the drop-down options provided.




**Question:** Is it possible to manage multiple TikTok accounts from the same Social Planner?

**Answer:** Yes, you can manage multiple TikTok accounts, including both personal and business profiles, from the Social Planner.




**Question:** What happens if I need to edit or delete a scheduled TikTok post?

**Answer:** You can easily edit or delete a scheduled post from the Social Planner before it goes live. Simply navigate to the post in the planner and make the necessary adjustments.




**Question:** Are the posting settings the same for desktop and mobile?

**Answer:** Yes. Videos and interaction settings apply platform-wide and are not view-specific like website layout settings.




**Question:** Will my TikTok videos post automatically, or will I get a notification to hit "Publish"?
**Answer:** Unlike some older tools, this integration allows for **Direct Posting**. Once you schedule your video in the Social Planner, it will go live on TikTok at the exact time you chose without you needing to touch your phone.
