**Sync Your Invoices with Wave Accounting**

This guide explores how you can seamlessly integrate your invoices with Wave Accounting using the invoice sync feature in .

**Please note:** Currently, status changes such as “paid” or “partially paid” are not automatically synced due to limitations within Wave. These updates will need to be made manually.

### **What is Wave Accounting**

Wave Accounting is a popular accounting and bookkeeping software tailored for small businesses, providing tools for tracking income, expenses, generating financial reports, and managing invoicing and payments.

## FAQs




**Question:** What does the invoice sync feature do?
**Answer:** The feature syncs new or updated invoices from to your Wave account, eliminating the need for manual duplication.




**Question:** Can I sync invoice payment statuses?
**Answer:** Currently, payment statuses like "paid" or "partially paid" are not synced. You’ll need to update these manually in Wave.




**Question:** How do I set up the integration?
**Answer:** Navigate to **Settings (bottom left)** -> **Integrations** -> **Wave**, connect your account, select the desired Wave account for syncing, and click **Save**.




**Question:** Will existing invoices sync to Wave?
**Answer:** No, the sync only applies to new or updated invoices created after the integration setup.




**Question:** Can I disconnect the integration later?
**Answer:** Yes, you can manage or disconnect the integration anytime via the same **Integrations** section.




**Question:** Are there any limitations I should be aware of?
**Answer:** Yes, payment status updates are not synced, and only new or updated invoices after integration setup will be synced.




**Question:** Does the sync work in both directions between and Wave?

**Answer:** No, the integration enables **one-way syncing** specifically from to Wave.
