Need help with your Facebook Business Pages not appearing in your Page Selector dropdown? This is a frequent problem; we have compiled a step-by-step guide to help you resolve it.



### **Steps for Updating Facebook Pages in Integration**

**Step 1: Access Your Facebook Settings**

Log into your Facebook account and click on the avatar icon featuring your profile picture at the top right corner of your screen. From the dropdown menu, select "Settings and Privacy."

## **Part 2: Return to the app to integrate the Facebook page.**

**Step 1:**  Under Settings > Integrations > Facebook, click "Select a Facebook Page".

**Step 2:** A new window will load, and a dropdown will allow you to select which page you want to integrate.

**Step 3:** Click "Connect Page" to save.



### **FAQ:** 

**1\. Why aren’t my Facebook Business Pages appearing in thePage Selector dropdown?** This issue usually occurs if the necessary permissions and integrations haven’t been properly set up within your Facebook account.

**2\. Who can view the Facebook pages in the dropdown menu?** Only the individual who initially integrated the Facebook page and is the admin of that page can view it in the dropdown menu. Admin access is required for other pages to appear.

**3\. What should I do if I see some pages already selected?** If pages are already selected, do not deselect them, as this could disrupt their existing integration with other accounts.

**4\. What happens if I don’t save my changes after selecting Facebook pages?** Failing to save your changes means your selections won’t be applied, and the integration may not function as expected.

**5\. Can I integrate multiple Facebook pages with the?** Yes, you can select and integrate multiple Facebook pages by following the steps provided. Just ensure you have admin access to each page.
