**Local Services Ad - Reserve with Google Integration**

Providing a seamless booking experience for your customers can make all the difference in growing your service-based business. Integrating Local Services Ads (LSA) with Reserve with Google lets you streamline your appointment process, ensuring potential customers can book services directly through search results. This guide will walk you through setting up this powerful integration.

## **Overview of the Integration**

Local Services Ads and Reserve with Google work together to ensure your business is prominently displayed when potential customers search for services you offer. The integration also allows customers to schedule appointments without leaving the search results page, simplifying their journey from search to booking.

**Important Notes:**

-   This integration requires a Local Services Ads (LSA) account.
-   It supports all subcategories under "Home Services," "Real Estate," and "Legal Services" for local service providers.
-   Currently, "Reserve with Google" is only available in the United States.
-   "Reserve with Google" does not support payments.
-   Ensure that the Company Name and Address in **Settings > Business Profile** match the details in your LSA business account.

### Benefits of This Integration:

-   **Increase Visibility:** Your business appears at the top of local search results.
-   **Simplified Booking Process:** Customers can book directly from Google without phone calls or emails.
-   **Streamlined Management:** Appointments are automatically added to your calendar, reducing manual tasks.

-   Click on **Integrations** and select "Configure Google Organic Bookings" under the Reserve with Google option.

## FAQs




**Question:** How can I leverage this integration for my business?


**Answer:** By setting up this integration, your business gains more visibility and an efficient booking process that appeals to customers looking for immediate results.




**Question:** What if my integration status shows "Not Matched"?

**Answer:** Ensure that your business details in the settings match your Local Services Ads account. If needed, update your details and try integrating again.




**Question:** Can I manage multiple businesses or Google accounts?

**Answer:** You can link various Google accounts to different companies within the platform. Simply log in using the account associated with the respective business.




**Question:** How long does it take for the integration to be verified?

**Answer:** Verification usually takes 48 hours, but this can vary depending on the volume of applications Google is processing.




**Question:** Can I accept payments through Reserve with Google?

**Answer:** Currently, payments are not supported through Reserve with Google, even if the "Accept Payment" option is enabled in your calendar settings.




**Question:** Can bookings still be tracked if customers call instead of clicking the Reserve button?
**Answer:** Calls from LSA ads are tracked within if your number is connected. However, calls made from the Reserve booking button itself will create **calendar events**, not call conversions.




**Question:** How will I know when a new booking comes in from Google?
**Answer:** You will see the appointment appear automatically in your calendar. You can also set up an **Automation** in the platform to send you a text or email notification the moment a "Reserve with Google" booking is confirmed.




**Question:** Which types of businesses and locations are currently eligible for this integration?

**Answer:** The integration is currently available for businesses located in the United States that fall under the "Home Services," "Real Estate," or "Legal Services" subcategories.
