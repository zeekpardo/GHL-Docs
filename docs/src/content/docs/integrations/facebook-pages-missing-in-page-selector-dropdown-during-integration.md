**Facebook Pages missing in Page Selector Dropdown during integration**

Need help with your Facebook Business Pages not appearing in your Page Selector dropdown? This is a frequent problem; we have compiled a step-by-step guide to help you resolve it.

### **Steps for Updating Facebook Pages in Integration**

**Step 1: Access Your Facebook Settings**

Log into your Facebook account and click on the avatar icon featuring your profile picture at the top right corner of your screen. From the dropdown menu, select "Settings and Privacy."

_Important: If any pages are already selected, refrain from deselecting them. Doing so could disrupt their existing integration with other accounts, even if they differ from those you're currently working on._

## **Part 2: Return to the app to integrate the Facebook page.**

**Step 1:**  Under Settings > Integrations > Facebook, click "Select a Facebook Page".

**Step 2:** A new window will load, and a dropdown will allow you to select which page you want to integrate.

**Step 3:** Click "Connect Page" to save.

### FAQ's




**Question:** Why aren’t my Facebook Business Pages appearing in thePage Selector dropdown?
**Answer:** This issue usually occurs if the necessary permissions and integrations haven’t been properly set up within your Facebook account.




**Question:** Who can view the Facebook pages in the dropdown menu?

**Answer:** Only the individual who initially integrated the Facebook page and is the admin of that page can view it in the dropdown menu. Admin access is required for other pages to appear.




**Question:** What should I do if I see some pages already selected?

**Answer:** If pages are already selected, do not deselect them, as this could disrupt their existing integration with other accounts.




**Question:** What happens if I don’t save my changes after selecting Facebook pages?
**Answer:** Failing to save your changes means your selections won’t be applied, and the integration may not function as expected.




**Question:** Can I integrate multiple Facebook pages with the?

**Answer:** Yes, you can select and integrate multiple Facebook pages by following the steps provided. Just ensure you have admin access to each page.


**Question:What should I do if I don’t see all my Facebook pages listed in the dropdown menu?
**Answer:** If some of your Facebook pages are not appearing in the dropdown, it could be due to insufficient admin permissions. Make sure you have admin access to the pages you want to see. If the pages are managed by another individual or you are not listed as an admin, they won’t appear in the dropdown menu.




**Question:** Can I link a page that I am not the original integrator of?
**Answer:** No, only the individual who initially integrated the page and has admin access can view and link it in .


**Question:What should I do if I find that some permission toggles are turned off in the app settings?
**Answer:** You should scroll through all available toggles and ensure every single one is enabled. If any are turned off, switch them on to ensure the integration works correctly.  




**Question:** What is the first thing I should do if my page isn't showing up?

**Answer:** Locate the app in your Business Integrations and click **"View and Edit."** This allows you to check if all the necessary toggles and page permissions are active.




**Question:** Does deselecting a Facebook page in the Business Integrations settings only affect my current sub-account?
**Answer:** No. You should never deselect pages that are already checked, even if they are not the ones you are currently working on. Deselecting a page can disrupt that page's existing integration with other accounts or platforms entirely.
