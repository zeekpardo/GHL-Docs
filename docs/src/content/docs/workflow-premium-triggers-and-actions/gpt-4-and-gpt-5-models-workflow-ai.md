1.  **Access the Feature**: Click the "+" icon in your workflow automation tool and search for "GPT powered by OpenAI."
2.  **Select the Model**:  Choose from the available models—GPT-5, GPT-5 Mini, GPT-5 Nano, GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-4o, GPT-4o Mini—based on your specific needs.
3.  **Choose an Action Type**: You can select from pre-made action types or use the Custom action type to write your own prompt.

-   **Pre-made Action Types**: Includes options like Analyze Text Sentiment, Summarize Text, Translate, and Custom.
-   **Custom Action Type**: Allows you to write a specific prompt tailored to your needs.

**FAQ**




**Question:** What is Workflow AI?
**Answer:** Workflow AI is an advanced workflow action that integrates artificial intelligence to automate and enhance various business processes, providing intelligent responses based on predefined triggers and actions.




**Question:** How do I select a model?

**Answer:** When setting up Workflow AI, you can choose from GPT-5, GPT-5 Mini, GPT-5 Nano, GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-4o, GPT-4o Mini, each offering different performance levels suited to various tasks.




**Question:** What are the benefits of using GPT-4 and 5 models?

**Answer:** These models provide superior contextual understanding, faster response times, and improved processing efficiency, making them perfect for high-volume and complex workflows.




**Question:** How do I use a custom prompt?

**Answer:** Select the Custom action type when setting up your Workflow AI action. This allows you to write and input a specific prompt tailored to your unique workflow requirements.




**Question:** What are GPT-4 and GPT-5 models used for within workflows?
**Answer:** These models are designed to enhance automation by generating intelligent text outputs such as summaries, translations, or email drafts helping teams streamline operations and save time.




**Question:** How can I optimize the performance of GPT actions in workflows?
**Answer:** Continuously monitor results, gather feedback, and refine your custom prompts. Selecting the right model for your task and iterating on prompt design will improve efficiency and output quality.




**Question:** Can the AI see the entire conversation history?

**Answer:** By default, the AI only sees what you put into the "Prompt" box. If you want it to summarize a conversation, you must use a Custom Variable to pull the "Last Conversation Message" or "Conversation History" into the prompt so the AI has that context.




**Question:** Are GPT-powered actions limited to text-based workflows?
**Answer:** Yes. GPT-powered actions are designed specifically for text-based automation such as summarization, translation, sentiment analysis, and content generation.




**Question:** What is the best way to improve the quality of the AI's responses over time?

**Answer:** We recommend an **"Optimize and Iterate"** approach. Monitor the performance of your actions, gather user feedback on the outputs, and refine your custom prompts to better elicit the specific information or tone your business requires.
