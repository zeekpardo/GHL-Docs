Organizing your messages is easier than ever with Bulk Actions and Filters in Conversations. In this article, you’ll learn what you can do in the Conversations tab to ensure you don’t miss out on opportunities with your leads.

### Bulk actions  (limited to 100 conversations at a time)

-   Here, you can mark it as unread, read, starred (important to mark meaningful conversations, conversations to come back to, and personal categorizing), unstarred, and delete unwanted messages.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768569186633-unnamed - 2026-01-16T211259.871.jpg>)

-   Select individual contacts or all loaded contacts.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768569204855-unnamed - 2026-01-16T211316.422.jpg>)

### Filters

By using the Quick filter, you can filter by:

-   Assigned
-   Last Message Direction
-   Last Outbound Message Type
-   Last Message Channel, etc

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768569223876-unnamed - 2026-01-16T211337.614.jpg>)

-   Here, you can view messages from the Latest to the oldest or the oldest to the latest, or you can sort by the Engagement Score or SLA.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1768569247021-unnamed - 2026-01-16T211400.779.jpg>)

**Please Note**

This will also work with the Quick filters mentioned above to make Conversations more structured.

So, in essence, if you have filters applied and sort based on oldest or latest, the system will also account the filters you have selected

**Conversation Views: Save Filters, Work Faster**

Conversation Views allow you to save frequently used conversation filters as reusable views. This makes it easy to switch focus instantly without having to recreate filters each time.

It’s especially useful if you regularly review the same types of conversations, such as:

-   Unassigned conversations  

-   Conversations from specific channels  

-   High-priority chats based on selected tags

**How to use:**Go to Conversations, then open the Views section in the Inbox panel. Click + Create View to get started.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772574638171-image1.png)

Apply the necessary filters and assign a name to your view. Once created, the view appears in the Inbox panel and displays all conversations that match the selected filters.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772574658820-image6.png)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772574676305-image4.png)

Views are visible only to the user who creates them. Admins can share views with other users through the view menu, making it easier for teams to stay aligned while managing conversations.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1772574702426-image8.png)

**Custom Filters**

While the Quick Filters provide preset options, you can **customize filters** to meet your specific needs. This allows you to create a tailored workflow that suits your priorities, making it easier to locate and manage important conversations.

#### Benefits of Using Filters and Sorting

-   **Efficiency**: Save time by managing multiple conversations with just a few clicks.
-   **Organization**: Easily filter conversations by type, assignee, or status.
-   **Collaboration**: Use internal comments and mentions to improve team communication.
-   **Flexibility**: Customize filters to meet your specific needs and preferences.

### FAQs




**Question:** How many conversations can I apply bulk actions to at once?
**Answer:** You can apply bulk actions to up to 100 conversations at a time. Use the "Load More" option to select additional conversations within this limit.




**Question:** What actions can I perform using bulk actions in the Conversations tab?
**Answer:** Bulk actions allow you to mark conversations as unread, read, starred, or unstarred or delete unwanted messages. This helps you organize your conversations efficiently.


**Question:  How can I filter conversations to view specific messages?
**Answer:** You can use Quick Filters to filter conversations by criteria such as Assigned, Last Message Direction, Last Outbound Message Type, and Last Message Channel. This helps in narrowing down conversations based on your needs.


**Question:  Does sorting by oldest or latest affect my applied filters?
**Answer:** Yes, sorting by oldest or latest will work with your applied filters. This means your filtered conversations will be sorted according to the selected order, from the latest to oldest or vice versa.




**Question:** Can I apply bulk actions to conversations across multiple pages?
**Answer:** Yes, you can select up to 100 conversations per bulk action. To include more, use the “Load More” option to load additional conversations and apply actions in batches.  




**Question:** Can I customize filters to meet my specific needs?
**Answer:** Yes, you can create custom filters to tailor your workflow and prioritize important conversations, making it easier to locate and manage messages that matter to you.




**Question:** Can I filter for conversations that have not been assigned to anyone yet?

**Answer:** Yes. Use the **"Assigned"** quick filter and select "Unassigned." This is a great way for managers to identify "orphaned" leads and distribute them to the appropriate team members.
