**Managing Appointments In Conversations**

Keeping track of appointments is crucial for effective customer relationship management. Users can now manage appointment activities directly within the Conversations module, making it easier to view and update details of canceled appointments and identify deleted ones seamlessly.

**Use Cases for Appointment Management Features**

### **Canceled Appointments**

-   **Customer Follow-Up:** Easily access canceled appointments to follow up with customers and reschedule if necessary. This ensures that no opportunity is missed and helps maintain good customer service.
-   **Data Accuracy:** Update details of canceled appointments directly from the Conversations module to keep records accurate and up-to-date without switching between different sections.

### **Deleted Appointments**

-   **Audit Trail:** Maintain a clear record of all appointments, including those that have been deleted, to ensure an accurate audit trail. This helps in reviewing past interactions and understanding customer history.
-   **Error Correction:** Identify deleted appointments quickly to address any potential errors or misunderstandings. Viewing details of deleted appointments can help in resolving issues that might arise from accidental deletions.

## **How to Use**

### **Viewing and Updating Canceled Appointments**

-   Access Conversations Module:
    -   Navigate to the Conversations module in your dashboard.
-   Locate Canceled Appointments:
    -   Look for activities labeled as canceled appointments within the conversation thread.
-   View Appointment Details:
    -   Click on the "View Appointment" button associated with the canceled appointment activity.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762442502985-image1.jpg)

-   This will open a detailed view where you can review and update appointment information as needed.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762442555651-image3.jpg)

### **Viewing Deleted Appointments**

-   Access Conversations Module:
    -   Navigate to the Conversations module in your dashboard.
-   Identify Deleted Appointments:
    -   Find activities with the title "Appointment Deleted" within the conversation thread.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1762442584444-image2.jpg)

-   View Appointment Details:
    -   Click on the activity to view the details of the deleted appointment.
    -   Note that while you can view the details, editing is not possible for deleted appointments.

### **Add Notes Per Appointment**

Appointment-related Notes are also accessible through the Conversations > Notes Tab and are also visible within the Appointment Modal, ensuring information is centralized for better coordination.

**Important:** Notes added **in appointments** are synced to the associated **contact** within the Contacts, Opportunities, and Conversations tab. However, notes added **directly in the contact** will **not** sync back to the appointment.

To use, simply go to the Appointment Modal, navigate to the Notes tab, add your note, and save. These notes can be viewed, edited, and deleted.

Notes added via two-way sync from third-party calendars (such as Google or Outlook) will automatically appear in the system. Additionally, responses from bookers in the "Additional Information" field of the default booking form are displayed in the appointment notes section for quick and easy access.

**Question:** What should I do if I need to correct an error related to a deleted appointment?
**Answer:** Review the details of the deleted appointment in the **Conversations module** to understand the issue. Take appropriate action to resolve any errors or misunderstandings outside the deleted record.




**Question:** Can I restore a deleted appointment if it was an error?
**Answer:** No, a deleted appointment cannot be "undeleted." If an appointment was deleted by mistake, you should use the details visible in the Conversations thread to manually book a new appointment for the client.
