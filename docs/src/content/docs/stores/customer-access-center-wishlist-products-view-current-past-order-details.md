With the Customer Access Center, store owners can provide customers with an easy way to view their current order details, access fulfillment statuses, and review past orders—all in one place. Additionally, the Product Wishlisting feature lets customers save their favorite items for future purchase, with customizable button placement and appearance, and easy access to wishlists through the Customer Access Center.

**Key Features and Benefits**

-   **Instant Access to Order Information**: Customers receive an order confirmation email with a "View Order" button, directing them to the Customer Access Center. By entering an OTP, customers can quickly access their current order information.
-   **Fulfillment Status and Tracking**: Customers can view order fulfillment status, including tracking number, URL, and carrier details, giving them real-time insights into order shipping.
-   **Complete Order History**: In addition to current orders, customers can access all previous orders, making it easy to track past purchases.
-   **The Product Wishlisting** feature allows customers to save their favorite items for future purchase, offering a personalized shopping experience
-   **Streamlined User Experience**: The Customer Access Center is secure and user-friendly, allowing customers to easily navigate and access order information without additional assistance.

**Important Notes:**

-   When multiple contacts use the same email, the login defaults to the first contact created.
-   Ensure the domain is connected for the Customer Access Center to function correctly, and confirm the default domain path points to a page within the store.

### **Wishlisting Products in E-commerce Stores**

The Product Wishlisting feature allows customers to save their favorite products for later purchase, improving their shopping experience and encouraging repeat purchases. Store owners have full control over where the wishlist button appears and its customization, including colors. Wishlisted items are easily accessible within the Customer Access Center (CAC), where customers can view or remove items from their list.

**How to Use It**:

-   To enable the wishlist button, within the store builder select the Product List, Product Details, or Featured Product Element.
-   In General Settings, toggle on "Enable Wishlist Products" and customize the button’s appearance.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770756185092-image6.png)

-   To allow customers to access their wishlists, add a Wishlist menu item under the Customer Login dropdown and link it to the Customer Access Center.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1770756212571-image7.jpg)

-   Save and publish the changes, ensuring a valid domain is linked for login functionality to work.

**FAQs**




**Question:** How do I enable the order confirmation email for my customers?
**Answer:** Go to Payments > Settings > Notifications > Order Confirmation Email for Stores and toggle it on to enable access for customers through email.




**Question:** Can customers view past orders in the Customer Access Center?
**Answer:** Yes, customers can view both current and past orders within the Customer Access Center by selecting the "Orders" option in the navigation menu.




**Question:** What should a customer do if they cannot access the Customer Access Center?
**Answer:** Ensure the customer uses the same email address they used at checkout. If issues persist, check that the domain is correctly connected and points to a store page.




**Question:** How can customers track the status of their orders?
**Answer:** Fulfillment status, including tracking number, tracking URL, and carrier details, is displayed in the Customer Access Center for each order.




**Question:** What is the purpose of the OTP verification?
**Answer:** OTP verification provides a secure login for customers, protecting their order information by ensuring only authorized users can access the Customer Access Center.




**Question:** Can multiple customers use the same email for login?
**Answer:** If multiple contacts share the same email, login defaults to the first contact created. Unique emails are recommended for accurate order tracking.




**Question:** What is a critical technical requirement for the Customer Access Center to work?

**Answer:** You must ensure that a **valid domain is connected** and that the default domain path points specifically to a page within your store. Without a connected domain, the login functionality will not work.
