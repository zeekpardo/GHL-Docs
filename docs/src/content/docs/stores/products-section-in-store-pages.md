**Products Section in Store Pages**

Managing product availability across multiple stores can be a challenge, especially for businesses operating under a single sub-account. To simplify this process, offers a **Product Selection per Store** feature. This gives store owners complete control over which products appear in specific stores, making inventory management more **flexible, efficient, and customizable**.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

**Customizable Product Visibility** – Include or exclude products from specific stores to control inventory availability.  
**Automated Store Listings** – Products with the **"Include in Online Store"** toggle enabled will automatically appear in new stores.  
**Bulk Actions for Efficiency** – Easily include or exclude multiple products across stores at once.  
**Advanced Search & Filter Options** – Quickly find products by name or manage visibility based on **Collections**.  
**Seamless Updates** – Changes are saved instantly, eliminating extra steps.

## **Important Notes**

**"Include in Online Store" Toggle**

-   If this setting is **disabled**, the product **will not** be available for selection in any store.
-   When a **new store is created**, only products with this setting **enabled** will be available.

**Product Listings for New Stores**

-   New stores **inherit product visibility settings** based on the **"Include in Online Store"** option.
-   Any **newly created product** with this toggle **enabled** will automatically be listed in all stores.

**Editing Products**

-   Store owners can **click on individual products** to modify details directly.

## FAQs




**Question:** How do I make a product available only in specific stores?  
**Answer:** Go to Stores > Select Store > Products, then disable the "Include in Online Store" toggle for any product you do not want to appear in a particular store.




**Question:** Can I apply changes to multiple products at once?  
**Answer:** Yes! Use bulk actions to include or exclude multiple products from stores simultaneously.




**Question:** What happens when I create a new store?  
**Answer:** Any product with the "Include in Online Store" toggle enabled will automatically be listed in the new store.




**Question:** Can I edit a product’s visibility after adding it to a store?  
**Answer:** Yes! You can modify product visibility at any time through the Products section in the store settings.




**Question:** Will disabling a product remove it from existing stores?

**Answer:** Yes, if you disable the **"Include in Online Store"** toggle, the product will be removed from all store listings.




**Question:** Can I edit product details directly from the store view?  
**Answer:** Yes, store owners can click on any product to modify its details directly from the Products section of that store.  




**Question:** What happens if I disable the "Include in Online Store" toggle for a product?
**Answer:** If you disable the "Include in Online Store" toggle for a product, it will not appear in any store listings. Additionally, it will not be available for selection in any store until the toggle is re-enabled.




**Question:** How can I find products in a specific group or category to manage their visibility?

**Answer:** You can use the Advanced Search and Filter options to filter products by **Collections**, allowing you to manage the visibility of predefined groups of products efficiently.




**Question:** If I delete a store, what happens to the products assigned to it?

**Answer:** Deleting a store only removes that specific storefront and its unique settings. Your products remain safe in your master **Products** library. They will continue to be available for any other existing or future stores you create.




**Question:** If I exclude a product from a specific store, does it still count toward my total inventory?

**Answer:** Yes. Excluding a product only hides it from the **digital storefront** (the customer's view). The product still exists in your master library, and its total stock levels remain tracked globally unless you have specific per-store inventory settings enabled.
