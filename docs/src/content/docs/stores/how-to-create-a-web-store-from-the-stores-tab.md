**How to create a web store from the Stores tab**

With the Stores Tab under the Sites section and the ability to create stores directly from the app, managing your online business has never been easier.

### **Seamless Store Management**

Store owners can manage and view all their stores under a single tab called "Stores" in the Sites section of the app. This tab offers a seamless and user-friendly interface for displaying stores.

-   **Features:** The Stores tab includes sorting, filtering, and folder creation options, similar to what is available for funnels and websites.
-   **Visibility:** Both existing and newly created stores are listed in this tab.

### **Key Aspects and Points to Note**

-   **Unified View:** All websites converted into stores are listed under the Stores section. Conversely, once the store pages are removed from a store, they will only appear under the Websites section.
-   **Inherent Structure:** All stores are inherently websites and will be found under the Websites section as well. This means the same store will be displayed in both places.
-   **Deletion:** Deleting a store under the Stores section will also delete it from the Websites section.
-   **Folders:**
    -   Creating a folder in either the Stores or Websites section will make it visible.
    -   Folders in the Stores section will only contain stores, while folders in the Websites section can contain both stores and websites.
    -   Folders can only be deleted from the Websites section, regardless of where they were created.

**How to use:**

**Stores Tab Under sites**

1.  Navigate to your account > Sites on the left
2.  There will be a tab called Stores at the top
3.  Navigate to Stores, and you can see all your created stores present.

## FAQs:




**Question:** What is the "Stores" tab?
**Answer:** The "Stores" tab is a section under the Sites menu that allows store owners to manage and view all their stores in one place. It provides a user-friendly interface with sorting, filtering, and folder creation options.




**Question:** Can I view both existing and newly created stores in the "Stores" tab?
**Answer:** Yes, both existing and newly created stores will be displayed in the "Stores" tab for easy management.




**Question:** What happens if I delete a store from the "Stores" tab?
**Answer:** Deleting a store from the "Stores" tab will also delete it from the Websites section, as all stores are inherently websites.




**Question:** Can I organize my stores into folders?
**Answer:** Yes, you can create folders in the "Stores" tab. These folders will only contain stores. Folders created in the Websites section can contain both stores and websites.




**Question:** How do I create a new store?
**Answer:** To create a new store, go to the "Stores" tab under the Sites section, click the "New Store" button, and select either "From blank" to create a store from scratch or choose a template.




**Question:** What happens if I remove a store page from a store?
**Answer:** When a store page is removed from a store, it will only appear under the Websites section. This ensures that all store pages are still accessible, but not necessarily linked to the store.




**Question:** How do I access the Stores tab?
**Answer:** Go to your account, navigate to **Sites** on the left menu, and click on the **Stores** tab at the top to view and manage all your stores.




**Question:** What is the benefit of using the Store Setup Wizard for my e-commerce store?

**Answer:** The Store Setup Wizard guides you through six essential onboarding steps, such as adding products, configuring payment providers, and setting shipping rates. This ensures that you don't miss any critical configurations before your store goes live.
