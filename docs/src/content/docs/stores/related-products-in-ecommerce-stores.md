Store owners can enhance the shopping experience by suggesting related products on their product detail pages. This feature allows you to recommend products that are likely to interest your customers based on their current selection, improving product discovery and potentially increasing sales.

**Use Cases:**

**Boosting Sales for New Products:**

-   Highlight new arrivals by adding them to collections that frequently appear as related products.

**Cross-Selling Accessories:**

-   For main products like smartphones, use related products to suggest accessories such as cases, chargers, and headphones.

**Seasonal Promotions:**

-   Organize products into seasonal collections (e.g., "Summer Sale") to automatically suggest seasonal items.

**Note:**

-   Products from the same collections as the main product will be shown as related products. For instance, if you have an "iPhone 15" in the "Apple" and "Summer Sale" collections, other products from these collections will appear in the related products section, with a maximum display limit of 10 products.
-   The images for related products maintain a 1:1 aspect ratio to ensure a consistent and visually appealing display across your store.

**Pro Tips:**

-   **Optimize Collections:** Ensure your collections are well-organized so related products are relevant and useful to customers.
-   **Test Display Settings:** Experiment with different numbers of products and column settings to find the optimal display for your store’s layout.
-   **Engaging Headlines:** Customize the related products section heading to make it more engaging and tailored to your brand voice.

**FAQ:**




**Question:** How do I decide which products to show as related?
**Answer:** Products shown as related are automatically selected based on the collections they belong to. Ensure your products are organized into relevant collections.




**Question:** Can I manually select related products?
**Answer:** Currently, the feature relies on collection-based suggestions. For manual selection, you would need to create specific collections for desired groupings.




**Question:** What happens if a product belongs to multiple collections?
**Answer:** Products from the first collection will be shown initially, followed by products from the subsequent collections, up to the maximum limit set.




**Question:** Can I adjust the number of related products shown?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize the number of related products displayed on the product detail page. By default, 6 products are shown, but you can adjust this number to suit your store's layout and preferences.




**Question:** How can related products help boost sales?
**Answer:** Related products can:

-   Highlight new arrivals by placing them in collections.  

-   Cross-sell accessories for main products, such as cases or chargers.  

-   Promote seasonal items by organizing products into seasonal collections.




**Question:** Is there a limit to how many related products will appear?

**Answer:** While you can customize the display count in the builder (default is 6), the system will pull a maximum of **10 products** from the associated collections. This prevents the page from becoming too long while still offering plenty of variety.
