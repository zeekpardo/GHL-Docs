**Reviews & Ratings for Products in E-commerce stores**

Enhancing credibility and increasing engagement is essential for any online store. The Reviews and Ratings feature empowers store owners by allowing customers to provide feedback on products, which can then be displayed to future shoppers. This article provides a comprehensive guide on how to enable, manage, and display customer reviews and ratings on your store’s product pages, helping you foster trust and transparency.

## **Key Features and Benefits of Reviews & Ratings**

-   **Improves Credibility**: Displaying reviews builds trust with potential customers, as they can see authentic feedback from other buyers.
-   **Encourages Customer Interaction**: Enabling reviews and ratings encourages customers to share their experiences, increasing engagement.
-   **Enhanced Product Visibility**: Positive ratings boost the appeal of products, which can lead to higher conversion rates.

## **How to Use the Reviews and Ratings Feature**

### **Step 1: Enabling Reviews and Ratings**

1.  **Access the Dashboard**:
    -   Open the builder preview for your e-commerce store.
2.  **Enable Ratings and Reviews**:
    -   In the builder, toggle **Show Reviews and Ratings** to activate review and rating visibility on both the **Product Details** and **Product List** pages.
3.  **Display Ratings Count**:
    -   To show the number of reviews, enable the **Show Ratings Count** toggle on the Product Details and Product List pages.

### **Step 3: Customizing the Appearance of Reviews and Ratings**

1.  **Adjust Star Colors and Fonts**:
    -   Customize the star color, font family, and font color directly in the builder preview for consistent branding.
2.  **Font Configuration**:
    -   **Font Size**: Fixed to ensure a balanced content hierarchy.
    -   **Font Family**: Matches the content fonts used throughout the product page.
    -   **Font Color**: Maintains uniformity across reviews and ratings.

### **Pro Tips for Managing Reviews & Ratings**

-   **Keep a Consistent Approval Policy**: Establish guidelines for approving reviews to ensure authenticity and transparency.
-   **Encourage Honest Feedback**: Reach out to customers to leave reviews after purchases, fostering an active community of feedback.
-   **Monitor Reviews Regularly**: Regular moderation ensures that inappropriate content is quickly addressed and that customers feel heard.

### FAQs




**Question:** Can I customize the star color for reviews on eCommerce products?
**Answer:** Yes, you can modify the star color and other style elements directly in the builder preview to match your store’s brand.




**Question:** How do I respond to individual reviews on eCommerce products?
**Answer:** Select a review in the Reviews dashboard, where you can reply to each review individually.




**Question:** What does "Show Ratings Count" in eCommerce store do?
**Answer:** This toggle displays the number of ratings a product has received, enhancing transparency for potential buyers.




**Question:** Can I delete a review permanently from eCommerce store?
**Answer:** Yes, reviews moved to the Trash can be permanently deleted from the Trash section in the Admin Preview.




**Question:** Is there a way to filter reviews by-products in eCommerce store?
**Answer:** Yes, you can filter reviews by **Time Frame**, **Ratings**, **Product Name**, or **Store Name** for more efficient management.




**Question:** How can I moderate reviews before they are published in eCommerce store?

**Answer:** You can moderate reviews by navigating to the Reviews dashboard, where new reviews will appear in the Pending section. You can choose to approve, keep them unapproved, or move them to trash.




**Question:** How do Reviews and Ratings help my online eCommerce store?
**Answer:** They enhance your store’s credibility and increase customer engagement. By showcasing real feedback, you build a foundation of trust and transparency with future shoppers.




**Question:** If I delete a product from eCommerce store, what happens to the reviews associated with it?

**Answer:** Reviews are tied to the specific Product ID. If a product is deleted, the reviews will no longer be visible on your store. However, they may remain in your dashboard under the "Trash" or "All" filters unless you manually delete them permanently.




**Question:** Can I manage or respond to multiple customer reviews at the same time to save time?
**Answer:** Yes. For higher efficiency, the system includes "Bulk Actions" within the Reviews Dashboard. This allows store owners to select multiple reviews at once to approve, unapprove, or move them to the trash in a single action.
