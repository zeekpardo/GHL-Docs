**Billing Address Field for Ecommerce Store Checkout**

This Billing Address field for E-commerce Stores allows store owners to capture billing information separately from shipping addresses. This provides greater flexibility, compliance, and control over how customer data is managed during the checkout process.

#### Key Features:

-   **Enable/Disable Billing Address**: Store owners can easily toggle the "Enable Billing Address" option in checkout settings.
-   **Same as Shipping Address**: A checkbox under the Shipping Details section lets customers quickly use their shipping address for billing.
-   **Text Customization**: Customize the billing address headline text, checkbox text, and field labels, including Full Name, Phone Number, Address, Country, State, City, and Zip Code.

#### How to Use It:

**1- Open Store Builder**: Navigate to the Checkout Page in your store builder.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763589581258-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-19 165936.png>)

**2- Select the Checkout Element**: Click on the Checkout element and go to the **General** Section in Settings.  

**3- Enable Billing Address**: Toggle on "Enable Billing Address" under Form Options.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773256233590-image4.png)

**4- Customize Text**: Under Text Options in General Settings, customize the billing address headline and checkbox text.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773256253874-image1.png)

**5- Save and Publish**: Save your changes, and your checkout page will now capture billing address details.

#### Additional Information:

-   **Order Details Page**: Billing address details are displayed separately below the shipping address.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763589679234-Captura de pantalla 2025-11-19 170114.png>)

-   **Customer Access Center (CAS)**: Customers can see the billing address in their Order Details section.
-   **Packing Slips and Thank You Pages**: Both shipping and billing information are included.
-   **Order Confirmation Email**: The billing address is included in the order confirmation email sent to customers.

#### Important Notes:

-   **New Locations**: Billing address is enabled by default when a new store location is created.
-   **Existing Locations**: Billing address is disabled by default; enabling it creates custom fields automatically.
-   **Custom Fields**: Custom fields for billing are non-editable and non-deletable after creation. If any existing fields share the same keys, they will be used.
-   **Tax Calculations**: The billing address section is not yet connected to tax calculations.

### FAQs




**Question:** How do I enable the billing address field on the checkout page?
**Answer:** Go to Store Builder > Checkout Page > Advanced Settings > Enable Billing Address under Form Options.




**Question:** Can I customize the billing address text?
**Answer:** Yes, you can customize the headline, checkbox text, and field labels for the billing address in the Text Options section.




**Question:** Will the billing address be included in the packing slips?
**Answer:** Yes, billing details are included alongside shipping information on packing slips and thank you pages.




**Question:** Can I delete the billing address fields after enabling them?
**Answer:** No, the billing address fields are non-deletable and non-editable once created.




**Question:** Will billing information impact tax calculations?
**Answer:** As of now, the billing address section is not connected to tax calculations.




**Question:** Can I turn off the billing address after enabling it?

**Answer:** Yes, you can disable the billing address toggle, but the custom fields created will remain in the system and cannot be deleted.




**Question:** Is the billing address automatically enabled for new store locations?
**Answer:** Yes, the billing address is enabled by default when a new store location is created. However, for existing locations, it is disabled by default.
