Managing service-based bookings while on the move is essential for maintaining a responsive and efficient business. provides a comprehensive mobile interface designed to handle complex scheduling workflows, ensuring that staff and administrators can coordinate appointments, manage team availability, and service diverse client needs from anywhere.

## Key Features and Benefits

The mobile service booking suite is built to mirror the power of the desktop environment while prioritizing a fast, touch-friendly experience.

-   **Multi-Location Support:** Seamlessly select from different business branches during the scheduling process.
-   **Service Variants & Add-Ons:** Support different durations (e.g., 30 vs. 60 minutes) and include upsells or enhancements with quantity support.
-   **Dynamic Staff Pricing:** The system automatically adjusts the total cost based on the specific team member assigned to the service.
-   **Real-Time Availability:** Staff hours and date-specific schedules are reflected instantly to prevent double bookings.
-   **Multi-Service Checkout:** Group multiple services into a single appointment for a streamlined client experience.
-   **Advanced Filtering:** Organize your calendar view by filtering specifically for individual services or service groups.

## How to Book a Service on Mobile

Follow these steps to create a new service-based appointment using the mobile application:

**1-Initiate Booking:** Open the Calendar module and tap the **\+ (plus)** button, then choose **New Service Appointment**.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767104993940-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-30 092944.png>)

**2-Select Contact & Location:** Search for the customer record. If your business operates across multiple branches, select the appropriate location for this booking.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767105029060-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-30 093020.png>)

**3-Choose Services:** Browse your service catalog and select one or more services to include in the appointment.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767105056039-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-30 093048.png>)

**4-Configure Options:** Select the desired variant (e.g., duration) and any optional add-ons.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767105120171-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-30 093153.png>)

**5-Assign Staff:** Pick a team member for each service. The pricing will update automatically if staff-specific rates are configured.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767105141659-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-30 093214.png>)

**6-Apply Discounts:** Enter any valid promo or coupon codes to adjust the final price.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767105279517-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-30 093433.png>)

**7-Select Time Slot:** Choose from the available real-time slots or use a custom time if your permissions allow.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767105305496-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-30 093456.png>)

**8-Confirm:** Review the summary of services, staff, and pricing, then tap **Schedule Booking** to finalize.

![](<https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767105326907-Captura de pantalla 2025-12-30 093520.png>)

## Current Limitations

To ensure smooth operations, please keep the following requirements and limitations in mind:

-   **Payments:** Mobile-created service appointments currently do not generate automated orders or support direct payment collection within the mobile flow.
-   **Modifications:** Appointments created through this flow cannot be edited, rescheduled, or duplicated within the mobile app at this time.
-   **Financial Itemization:** Manual taxes and processing fees are not currently displayed or supported in the mobile booking interface.
-   **Visibility:** You must have at least one calendar configured for services to appear, even if staff and services are already created.

### Pro Tip: Quick Filtering

You can quickly clean up a cluttered calendar view by tapping the colored labels at the top of your mobile calendar. This opens the filter modal where you can navigate to **Calendars > Services** to toggle specific services on or off, allowing you to focus only on the appointments that matter most in the moment.

## FAQs


**Question:** Can I book multiple services for a single client in one go?  **

**Answer:** Yes, the mobile interface supports multi-service checkout, allowing you to add several different services into one appointment window.


**Question:** Will the app show if a staff member is unavailable on a specific day?  **

**Answer:** Yes, the booking flow reflects real-time, date-specific staff hours to ensure you only select times when the assigned team member is actually working.


**Question:** Can I use coupon codes when booking for a client on the app?  **

**Answer:** Absolutely. There is a specific step in the mobile booking process where you can enter and apply valid promo codes to the total.


**Question:** Why can't I see my services in the mobile calendar?  **

**Answer:** Ensure that you have associated your services with a configured calendar. If no calendars are set up in the system, service appointments may not be visible.


**Question:** Can I change the price of a service manually during the mobile booking?  **

**Answer:** While you cannot type in a random price, the system will automatically update the price if you select a staff member with a specific rate or apply a valid coupon.




**Question:** Can I change an appointment's date or time after I have finished scheduling it on the mobile app?

**Answer:** No. According to the current limitations, appointments created through the mobile flow cannot be edited, rescheduled, or duplicated within the app at this time.




**Question:** Can I book an appointment for a branch across town?

**Answer:** Yes; the app includes multi-location support, allowing you to select different business branches during the scheduling process.
