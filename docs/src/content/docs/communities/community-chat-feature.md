Welcome to Community Chats, where you can easily connect with your community through private 1-on-1 messaging. This guide covers the essential features, privacy settings, and how to get started, ensuring a smooth and secure chat experience for both users and admins.

**Important Note:**You need to join a group to start chatting with its members, so be sure you're a group member to access the chat feature.

**Chat Features:**

-   **1-on-1 Messaging**: Connect directly with any member, whether they’re an admin or a regular user.
-   **Media Sharing**: Share photos, files, GIFs, and lots of emojis to add a fun touch to your conversations.
-   **Push Notifications**: Enable real-time message notifications to stay in the loop instantly (with sound alerts).

**How to start**

**Accessing Groups**:

-   Click on **Memberships** on the left sidebar.
-   Go to **Communities** at the top, then select **Groups** to see all available groups.
-   As an admin, click **Login** to enter the desired group.

### **FAQs**  




**Question:** What is the Community Chat feature?
**Answer:** The Community Chat feature allows 1-on-1 messaging between members in a group, with options to share media like photos, files, GIFs, and emojis. It includes real-time notifications for messages to help you stay connected.




**Question:** How do I start chatting with other members in a group?
**Answer:** First, you need to join the group. Then, access the group chat by clicking **Memberships** on the left sidebar, going to **Communities** at the top, and selecting **Groups**. As an admin, you can click **Login** to enter the group and start chatting.




**Question:** How do I enable or disable the chat feature?
**Answer:** Click on your **Account** icon in the top right corner, select **Account** from the dropdown menu, then scroll down to **Community’s Chat** at the bottom. Toggle it **on** or **off** as needed.




**Question:** How can I access chat on the desktop?
**Answer:** Inside the group, go to **Members**. In the top right corner, find the chat icon to see all chats. Click on a member’s name, view their profile, and click **Chat** at the bottom right to start a conversation.




**Question:** How do I access chat on mobile?
**Answer:** Open the app and go to **Community**. In the top right corner, tap on the message icon to see all chats. You can search by member name or view unread messages to reply.




**Question:** Can I share media in chats?
**Answer:** Yes, you can share photos, files, GIFs, and emojis to enhance your conversations with group members.




**Question:** How do I manage my chat interactions?
**Answer:** Click the dropdown menu next to the member’s name in a chat. Options include **View Profile**, **Delete Chat**, or **Block User** for managing your interactions.




**Question:** Can I disable group chats for specific groups?
**Answer:** Yes, you can disable group chats by logging in to the community > **Profile > Account Settings > Community Chat** and turning off chat for specific groups.




**Question:** Are there notifications for new messages?
**Answer:** Yes, push notifications alert you in real time when a new message is received, optionally with sound alerts.




**Question:** Can I delete a chat conversation?
**Answer:** Yes, you can delete a chat from your chat window using the Options menu. Deleting a chat removes it from your view but does not delete it for the other participant.




**Question:** Can I send documents or videos through Community Chats?

**Answer:** Yes, the media sharing feature allows you to send videos and documents in addition to photos, GIFs, and emojis.
