**Community Importer for Skool (Group & Post Importer)**

Administrators can quickly and efficiently import both members and posts from their Skool groups into Communities. In this article, we’ll explore how the Skool Importer works, its key features, and provide step-by-step guidance on how to use it for an ideal transition.

### **Key Features and Benefits**

1.  **Easy Migration**: The Skool Importer refines the process of transferring your community by enabling the import of members and posts with just a few simple steps. No need for complex manual data entry or third-party tools.
2.  **Real-Time Stats**: The system provides real-time updates on how many members have been successfully imported, along with a count of members who may not have been imported, ensuring transparency throughout the process.
3.  **Seamless Community Experience**: Post-import, members will find themselves in the same community space within , with all their interactions intact, ensuring continuity for your community’s engagement.
4.  **Admin Control**: You’ll have full control over missing or incomplete data. If any emails are missing or inaccurate, the system will notify you to allow for necessary corrections.

### **How to Use the Skool Importer**

**Step 1: Enter Group Details** To begin the import process, you’ll need to provide some key details:

-   **Skool Group Slug**: This is a unique identifier for your Skool group.
-   **Admin Email and Password**: These credentials will authenticate and allow the system to access your Skool account.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1773329720757-image4.png)

Once these details are entered, you’ll be ready to move on to the next step.

**Step 2: Start Importing Members** Click the **Continue** button to begin fetching member data from your Skool group. The system will start collecting all relevant information about your group members, including their profiles and contact details.

**Step 3: Download and Prepare the CSV** The system will generate a CSV file that includes:

-   Profile Slug
-   First Name
-   Last Name
-   Email (Note: some emails may be missing)

It’s important to review and ensure all email addresses are complete before continuing. Missing email addresses must be filled in by the admin to ensure successful member import.

### **Note: Pro Tips for Successful Import**

-   **Verify Admin Credentials**: Before starting the process, double-check that the admin credentials provided are correct and have the necessary permissions to access the group data.
-   **Prepare for Missing Emails**: Some member emails may not be included in the import. It’s a good idea to clean up your member list beforehand, ensuring that all emails are valid and complete.
-   **Review the Imported Posts**: While importing posts, take a moment to check that everything has been properly transferred, including formatting and content.

### FAQs




**Question:** What happens if there are errors during the import process?
**Answer:** If errors occur, you will be notified. You can then fix any issues (e.g., missing emails) and re-upload the corrected CSV file.  




**Question:** Are there any limits on the number of members or posts I can import?
**Answer:** There is no specific limit to the number of members or posts you can import. However, larger groups may take longer to process.  




**Question:** Will my community members be notified once they’ve been imported?
**Answer:** Yes, members are automatically notified of the import through email and are provided with access details. However, it’s recommended to inform them beforehand about the migration to avoid confusion.




**Question:** Can I undo the import if something goes wrong?
**Answer:** While the import process is not reversible, you can manually remove members and posts if needed after the import.




**Question:** Can I import Skool posts along with members?
**Answer:** Yes, after importing the members, you can import the posts made by the community members. These posts will be accessible in the new Community group.




**Question:** Will community members be notified after the import?
**Answer:** Yes, members are automatically notified via email and provided with access details. It’s recommended to inform them about the migration in advance to avoid confusion.




**Question:** Does the import maintain member interactions and post content?
**Answer:** Yes. After the import, members will find themselves in the same community space with all previous interactions and posts intact.




**Question:** How can I track the progress of my member migration?

**Answer:** The system provides real-time stats, updating you on the total number of members successfully imported and flagging any that were not.
