## **Private and Public Groups**

### **Step 2: Configure Subscription Options**

### **One-Time Payment**

For groups requiring a single payment for unlimited access:

-   Go to your group **Settings** and select **Subscriptions**.
-   Choose "Add new price", enter the amount, and select 'one-time' as the payment type.
-   Click "Add", and then "Save" to implement the one-time payment for the group.
-   Optionally, enable Test Mode to trial the payment process.

### **Recurring Subscription**

For groups with ongoing subscription fees:

-   In your group Settings, go to Subscriptions.
-   Click "Add new price". Specify the Amount, set Type as 'Recurring', choose the Billing Period (Weekly/Monthly/Annually), and add Trial Days if applicable.
-   Click "Add" then "Save" to create the group with a recurring subscription.
-   Activate Test Mode for payment trials.

##    **Notifications**

1\.     **Admin Alerts**: Admins receive email notifications of user payments with details.

2\.     **User Confirmation**: Users get a confirmation email regarding their payment and membership approval.

**Cancellations and Refunds:**

### **Cancellation Process**

-   **Via Payments**: Users receive a confirmation email, and admins are notified. The user is removed from the group.
-   **Via Stripe**: Similar process as Payments cancellation.     

###  **Refunds**

-   1\.   **CRM Refunds**: Initiated from Payments > Transaction screen.
-   2\.   **Stripe Refunds**: Managed directly through Stripe.

## **Restoring Deleted Contacts**

 If a user’s contact card is deleted and restored, they should regain group access without additional payment.

## **Private Channel-Based Course Access Within Paid/Private Groups**

Group admins or course owners can also control who accesses specific course content by setting course visibility to “Private Channel.” This allows access only to members of selected private channels within a group. To use this feature, admins simply select the “Private Channel” visibility option in the Edit Course settings and choose the private channels from the list. Only members of those selected channels will be able to view and access the course, while others will not see it unless they join the specified private channel. A new tag will be added to the course for easy identification. This feature is ideal for offering premium content to select groups, exclusive training for moderators, or restricting access to internal materials.

## **Useful Tips:**

**Update Group Status**: Select the group you want to update and change its status to active or inactive as needed.  
**Benefit:** This gives admins the flexibility to manage group accessibility, ensuring only relevant groups are active.  

**Ensure Visibility**: Verify that new groups are visible on the builder side and that the list refreshes correctly in invite email flows.  
**Benefit:** This ensures new groups are immediately available to users for smooth onboarding, provides accurate listings in invite emails for informed choices, and reduces admin workload by automatically resolving visibility issues.  

**Check Aspect Ratio**: Ensure that the aspect ratio of uploaded GIFs is accurate and they display correctly.  
**Benefit:** Properly displayed GIFs enhance the visual appeal and professionalism of the community platform.

**FAQs**

**  
Question: How do I manage access to a private group?**
**Answer:** Access is managed manually by admins. After payment, filter for 'Requested' in the  
People Tab and accept or deny membership.




**Question:** Can users join a public group immediately after payment?  
**Answer:** Yes, users gain membership immediately upon payment in public groups.  



**Question:** How are cancellations processed for paid groups?  
**Answer:** Cancellations are handled via Payments or Stripe, with users  
receiving confirmation emails and being removed from the group.  



**Question:** What should I do if a user’s contact card is deleted?  
**Answer:** Restoring a deleted contact card will allow the user to regain access to the group  
without needing to make an additional payment.  



**Question:** How can I ensure new groups are visible to users?  
**Answer:** Verify group visibility in the builder and ensure the list refreshes in invite email flows  
for accurate and immediate availability.  



**Question:** What are the benefits of checking GIF aspect ratios?  
**Answer:** Properly displayed GIFs enhance the visual appeal and professionalism of your  
community platform.  
**  
Question: How do I add a promotional link to my community?  
**Answer:** To add a promotional link, go to "Settings" and select "Links." Enter the description  
and URL of your link, set its visibility (public or private), and then click "Add Link" to save it.  




**Question:** Are admins notified when a user makes a payment for a group?  
**Answer:** Yes, admins receive email notifications with payment details, and users receive a  
confirmation email regarding their membership approval.  



**Question:** How can I restrict specific course content so only certain members within my group can see it?  
**Answer:** You can set the course visibility to "Private Channel" within the course settings. This  
ensures that only members of the specific private channels you select will be able to view or  
access the course content.




**Question:** If I have a "Private" paid group, can people find it to pay for it?

**Answer:** In a **Private Group**, the admin must manually approve members even after they have paid. You can manage this by filtering for **"Requested"** in the Members tab. For a "buy and enter" experience, use a **Public Group** setting.
