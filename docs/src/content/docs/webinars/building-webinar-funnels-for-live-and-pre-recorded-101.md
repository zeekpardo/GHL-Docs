**Building Webinar Funnels for Live and Pre-recorded - 101**

Webinars are an effective tool for engaging audiences, sharing valuable insights, and nurturing leads. With both On-Demand and Live Webinar options available, you can choose the best format to suit your audience’s needs. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the features, setup process, and tips for hosting a seamless webinar experience.

### **Key Features & Benefits**

-   **On-Demand Webinars**: These are pre-recorded sessions that users can access anytime, allowing flexible viewing.
-   **Live Webinars**: Host real-time sessions where you can interact directly with your audience, set for specific dates and times.
-   **Template Options**: Choose from customizable templates for each step of the webinar funnel, including registration and confirmation pages.
-   **Automated Workflows & Reminders**: Use automated workflows for reminders, confirmations, and follow-ups, ensuring attendees stay engaged.
-   **Recurring Webinar Settings:** Easily schedule and manage recurring webinars with flexible frequency options to save time and enhance audience engagement.

## **How to Use the Webinar Feature**

### **Step 1: Access the Webinar Setup**

**1- Navigate to Sites**Go to the **Top Bar** and select “Webinar.”

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763749740403-image9.png)

**2- Select Webinar Type**Choose between **On-Demand** (pre-recorded) or **Live Webinar** and enter the basic details, such as title and time zone.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763749891522-image10.png)

### **Step 2: Set Up Registration**

**1- Choose Registration Form**Select a form to capture attendee details. Forms can be created and customized under **Sites > Forms** if needed.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763750153188-image2.png)

**2- Upload Content**

-   For **On-Demand Webinars**, upload the video to your media library and link it directly.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763750517230-image12.png)

-   For **Live Webinars**, include a streaming link (e.g., Zoom) that attendees will use on the day of the event.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1763751619196-image5.png)

### **Step 3: Customize Templates**

**1- Select Templates**Webinar Template Library streamlines event creation by offering a shared, organized library of templates. This reduces repetitive setup tasks, ensures consistency, and helps users select or customize templates with ease. The templates automatically integrate with the video selected in Step 3, simplifying the process for webinar creators.  

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767366013296-image4.jpg)

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769444992558-image13.jpg)

**Optimized Workflow:** You can use keyboard shortcuts to save, undo, redo, publish, and preview your pages instantly (press ‘Shift +’ or click the button at the top right and the keyboard shortcut window will appear).

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769445019860-image6.jpg)

**Advanced Navigation:** Use keys to jump between elements, parent containers, and sections, or to open Layers, Settings, Quick Add, and Custom Code panels without searching through menus.

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1769445055756-image2.jpg)

**1- Access Workflow Recipes**In the webinar setup, click on the three dots in the title and choose **Edit Workflow** to use a pre-built automation recipe.

# Recurring Webinar Settings

Recurring Webinar Settings allow hosts to schedule webinars on a repeating basis without manually duplicating sessions. This feature supports flexible recurrence patterns daily, weekly, monthly, or custom helping maintain consistency while reducing setup time.

**How to use:**

To use it, simply create or edit a webinar, enable the recurring option in scheduling settings, select the frequency, and save_._

![](https://assets2.modalsupport.net/1767366360416-image8.jpg)

## **Tips for Effective Webinar Hosting**

-   **Encourage Pre-Webinar Engagement**: Share valuable information or insights before the session to pique interest and increase attendance.
-   **Customize Templates for Higher Conversions**: Tailor your registration and confirmation pages with strong CTAs, speaker bios, and benefits to increase sign-ups.
-   **Utilize On-Demand Webinars for Flexibility**: On-Demand webinars work well for audiences with varying schedules and allow attendees to engage with your content at their convenience.
-   **Automate Follow-Ups for Conversion**: Use automated follow-ups after the webinar to convert attendees into customers or leads. Include relevant offers or next steps.

## FAQs




**Question:** What’s the difference between On-Demand and Live Webinars?  
**Answer:** On-demand webinars are pre-recorded and accessible at any time, whereas Live Webinars happen in real-time, enabling live interaction.


**Question:Can I customize the webinar templates?  **
**Answer:** Yes, you can fully customize the templates for registration, confirmation, and broadcast pages, adjusting colors, text, images, and layout.


**Question:How do I automate reminders for the webinar?  **
**Answer:** Automated workflows are available within the webinar setup. These workflows allow you to send reminders and follow-ups without manual intervention.


**Question:What types of content can I upload for On-Demand Webinars?  **
**Answer:** For On-Demand Webinars, you can upload video files (e.g., MP4) to the media library and link them in the webinar setup.




**Question:** Can I download attendee details after the webinar?  
**Answer:** Yes, attendee details are accessible in the dashboard and can be downloaded as a CSV for follow-up and tracking purposes.


**Question:Can I delete certain Webinar pages?  **
**Answer:** No, specific pages from the Webinar cannot be deleted.




**Question:** Is there a limit to how many webinars I can host?
**Answer:** Typically, there’s no limit to the number of webinars you can create. You can manage multiple an.  
On-Demand or Live Webinar funnels simultaneously based on your subscription plan.  




**Question:** How do I see all available keyboard shortcuts?

**Answer:** You can press **Shift + /** at any time while inside the Page Builder to view a comprehensive list of all available shortcuts.  




**Question:** Do keyboard shortcuts work for all page types?

**Answer:** Yes, shortcuts are available across all Page Builder experiences, including Webinars, Funnels, Websites, Stores, and Blogs.




**Question:** Can I switch a Live Webinar to On-Demand after the event is over?

**Answer:** **Yes.** This is a pro-level strategy. Once your live session concludes, you can upload the recording to your media library and update the webinar type to On-Demand. This allows you to continue collecting leads from the same registration page long after the live event ends.
